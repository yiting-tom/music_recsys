Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded deluxe two CD edition of this 1970 album from the British rockers. The album has been restored to its 1970 original 6 track running order and a second disc has been added. Compiled by Steve Winwood, the second disc contains alternate mixes of 'Stranger To Himself', 'Every Mother's Son' and the first ever version of 'John Barleycom Must Die'. Additionally. seven previously unreleased songs from the band's legendary 1970 show at the Fillmore East in New York have also been added.
