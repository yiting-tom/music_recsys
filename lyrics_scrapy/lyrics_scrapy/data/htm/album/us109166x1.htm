Album Intro：

For the past few years Transit have been at the brink of of perfectly blending the midwest emo they grew up on with the modern pop punk sound of their contemporaries. With their sophomore full length, that idea has become fully realized. 'Keep This To Yourself' is 14 blasts of fast, emotive, technical pop-punk, cohesively strung together so well its hard to believe Transit hasn't been releasing music since the early part of the 2000's. From the frantic speed of opener 'Dear Anyone' to the acoustic sing along of the last track 'Love____' one thing is clear; Transit have fully surpassed all expectations of what five young kids from Massachusetts are capable of creating. 'Keep This to Yourself' is a letter to anyone willing to listen, and when you do, the hopeful words and carefully coordinated music will stay with you for years to come.
