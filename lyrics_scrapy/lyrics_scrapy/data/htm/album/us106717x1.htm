Album Intro：

Joshua Ryan (Jake) Owen (born August 28, 1981 in Vero Beach, Florida) is a Grammy-Award nominated American country music artist. Signed to RCA Records Nashville in 2005, Owen released his debut album Startin' with Me that year. This album produced three singles ('Yee Haw', 'Startin' with Me' and 'Something About a Woman'), all of which reached Top 20 on the Billboard Hot Country Songs charts. A fourth single, 'Don't Think I Can't Love You', was released in 2008 as the first from his second album Easy Does It. Owen has also toured as an opening act for several country acts, including Brad Paisley, Little Big Town and Sugarland.

Easy Does It is the second studio album by American country music singer Jake Owen. It was released on February 24, 2009 on RCA Records Nashville. Its lead-off single, 'Don't Think I Can't Love You', has charted in the Top 10 on the Hot Country Songs chart. As with his first album, Easy Does It is produced by Jimmy Ritchey, with whom Owen co-wrote several of the songs on it.

JO(出生日期：1981.08.28，出生地：美國福羅裡達州 Vero Beach)，獲美國格萊美鄉村音樂藝人提名。05年，和唱片公司RCA Records Nashville簽約，同年發行首磚《Startin' with Me》，包括三首單曲《Yee Haw》《Startin' with Me》《Something About a Woman》。這3首歌都上了Billboard鄉村音樂榜單前20。第四首單曲，《Don't Think I Can't Love You》（2008）收在第二張專輯《Easy Does It.》（2009）。JO還做過以下藝人現場的嘉賓：Brad Paisley，Little Big Town，以及Sugarland。

《Easy Does It.》（2009）是美國鄉村音樂人Jake Owen的第二章錄音室專輯。專輯於2009.02.24發行，廠牌：RCA Records Nashville。先於專輯發行的單曲《Don't Think I Can't Love You》已進入鄉村音樂單曲榜單前20。兩張專輯的製作人都是Jimmy Ritchey，另外，專輯裡部分歌曲由JO和他合作創作。
