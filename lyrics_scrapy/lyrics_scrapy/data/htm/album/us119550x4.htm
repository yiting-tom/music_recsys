Album Intro：

The only Yardbirds LP recorded entirely with Jimmy Page as a member, this 1967 album (guest: John Paul Jones) is a Yardbirds classic and Zeppelin blueprint: Page's instrumental White Summer ; the heavy blues-rock of Drinking Muddy Water ; the swirling psychedelia of Glimpses ; the reflective ballad Only the Black Rose , and more. 180-gram vinyl sourced from the original UK mono masters! Includes two bonus songs!
