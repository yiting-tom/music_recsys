Steel Panther【 5 albums 67 lyrics 】
Steel Panther is an American comedic glam metal band from Los Angeles, California, mostly known for their profane and humorous lyrics, as well as their exaggerated on-stage personae that parody the stereotypical 1980s 'glam metal' lifestyle.

Band members

Current members

Lexxi Foxx – bass, backing vocals (1997–present)
Stix Zadinia – drums, percussion, keyboards, backing vocals (2003–present)

Former members
