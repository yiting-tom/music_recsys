Album Intro：

Australian pressing. The Sunny Cowgirls debut album Little Bit Rusty marks the arrival of Australia's newest and most exciting country act. Like their Compass Bros stable mate Adam Brand, who discovered them at the CMAA College Of Country Music in Tamworth this year, the Sunny Cowgirls hail from WA, drive utes and sing songs that connect with rural and regional based Australians. Compass. 2005.
