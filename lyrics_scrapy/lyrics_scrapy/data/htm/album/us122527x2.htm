Album Intro：

The Jersey shore power trio of Chris Kosnik (Godspeed), Bob Pantella (Monster Magnet), and Fin Ryan (Core) are back, combining their Rush-like riff mazes and dueling vocal harmonies, creating the wall of sound and precision TAB fans have come to expect. From the pulsing groove of the opening track 'Revival', to the inhuman speed of the instrumental 'Super Computer', 'TAB 4' will leave listeners and players alike wanting more.
