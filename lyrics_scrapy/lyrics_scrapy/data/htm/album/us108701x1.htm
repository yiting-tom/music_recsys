Album Intro：

This is a discography of songs performed by The Naked Brothers Band, a teenage pop rock group. It's featured in a documentary-rockumentary-mockumentary musical comedy television series on Nickelodeon. It's executive produced and created by, Polly Draper (who starred as Ellyn Warren from ABC's hit drama series thirtysomething), who also writes and directs most of the episodes. It stars her real-life sons, Nat and Alex Wolff who write, compose, and perform all the music in the show.

he Naked Brothers Band's new album, and season 2 soundtrack, I Don't Wanna Go to School, is due to come out April 15th with a 'fan pack' edition. The fan pack edition will contain two bonus tracks , as well a pull-out poster, ringtone, booklet with full lyrics and band photos, and also never-before-seen footage available only with the fan pack's enhanced Connected features.
