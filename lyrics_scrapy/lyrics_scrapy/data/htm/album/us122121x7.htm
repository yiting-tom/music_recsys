Album Intro：

This is a new, 24 track compilation compiled by Dan Treacy of rare and unusual material culled from his endless archives. Material featured include dates from 1983 right through to some of their latest recordings from 2004. The Television Personalities have just signed to Domino Records (Home of Franz Ferdinand amongst others) and will have a brand new album out in the near future. Damaged. 2005.
