Album Intro：

Terranova is Fetisch plus special guests. Born in Berlin, Fetisch left home at 16 to live in New York and London. Most of his youth was spent in nightclubs and he was soon swept away by DJ’ing. He then returned to Berlin in 1994 to form Terranova. After releases on Compost and R&S Records, Terranova quickly gained international recognition with their collaboration with Legendary Manuel Goettsching.

A deal with !K7 was signed and in the years to follow Terranova acclaimed international success with the release of an edition of the label’s acclaimed DJ Kicks series and full-length albums Close The Door, Hitchhiking Non-Stop, and Peace Is Tough. Guests on these albums included Tricky, Mike Ladd, Ari-Up (Slits), Cath Coffey (Stereo MC´S) and Einstuerzende Neubauten.

On Digital Tenderness, Terranova decided to limit the toys, tools and people involved, creating a clear, electronic song-orientated, romantic sound. This album should be listened to while getting dressed, undressed, making love, smoking, drinking, driving, ironing and as loud as possible.
