Album Intro：

Limited edition pressing. 2012 album from the Tacoma-based Progressive Metal band. Working closely with producer Acle Kahney on the sounds and textures of the album, Moments developed into a dynamic and immense listening experience. The album uniquely combines the ferocity and rawness of modern metal with delicate and expansive atmospheres. The result is a sound that is accessible while maintaining a detailed and complex framework.
