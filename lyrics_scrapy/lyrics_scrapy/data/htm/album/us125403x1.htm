Album Intro：

2008 sophomore album produced by Danger Mouse (Gnarls Barkley, Gorillaz), a like minded devotee of obscure Psychedelia. Replica Sun Machine radiates charm, imagination, and scintillating melodies. Word Magazine says; 'Replica Sun Machine is a revanchist's dream, a sun-kissed missive from Psych-Pop's golden age. Their way with a tune is undeniable and when they hit the spot (the gorgeous 'Yesterdays To Come' or the Flaming Lips-gone-glam euphoria of 'Now Til '69'), the effect is cathartic'. Wall of Sound. 11 tracks. 2008.
