Alisa Mizuki【 21 albums 144 lyrics 】
Alisa Mizuki (観月 ありさ Mizuki Arisa, born December 5, 1976) is a Japanese actress, singer, and model. Mizuki is represented by the talent agency Vision Factory. Born in Nerima, Tokyo to Japanese-American parents, Mizuki began modelling for magazines and appearing in commercials at the age of four.

On 21 March 2015, she announced that she married Koji Aoyama, a president of a construction company.
