Album Intro：

On the title track and opening song of their fourth album, Hawk Nelson urges its listeners to 「Live Life Loud.' That sentiment, founding member Daniel Biro notes, is 「as Hawk Nelson as Hawk Nelson can be.' Live Life Loud. It's not just a matter of volume, mind you; it's a philosophy. Part of living at the extreme that title suggests is embracing all of life, accepting and even celebrating a little pain and darkness and finding a way to let it enrich life. After seemingly endless touring, Hawk Nelson returns September 22 with their most accomplished record yet Live Life Loud featuring the hit singles 'Meaning Of Life', 'Live Life Loud' and 'Shaken'.

This powerhouse, pop rock foursome hailing from Canada has won the hearts of fans and media nationwide having sold over half a million albums since their 2004 debut release Letters to the President. Hawk Nelson has appeared on NBC's 'American Dreams,' made a cameo appearance in the hit Nickelodeon film 'Yours, Mine, & Ours,' where they played their original song 'Bring Em Out,' as well as had their songs featured on NBC's 2006 Stanley Cup promo spots, WB's Smallville, Summerland, & Laguna Beach. In 2008 their third full length studio album, Hawk Nelson Is My Friend debuted at #34 on the Billboard Top 200 selling more than 18k units. Hawk Nelson is My Friend was nominated for both a GRAMMY and Dove Award for Best Packaging.
