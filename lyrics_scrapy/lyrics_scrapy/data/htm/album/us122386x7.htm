Album Intro：

Technical heaviness from the Northeast that's heavily influenced by Coalesce, Dillinger Escape Plan, and Meshuggah. Strained low end growls and utterly massive repetition with all of the grooves going on, before busting out into parts that are much faster, chaotic, and metallic. Plus there are some pretty killer breakdowns on here as well. Produced by Adam from Killswitch Engage.First cd by the band that went on to bring you 3750 and The Dead Walk.10 Tracks of HEAVY!!!
