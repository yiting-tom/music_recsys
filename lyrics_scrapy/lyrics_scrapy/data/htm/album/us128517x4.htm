Album Intro：

Featuring members of Carnal Forge, Centinex, Theory In Practice, Unmoored & Incapacity, Scar Symmetry formed under the common goal to create fresh, brutal and catchy death metal that had the potential to transcend the boundaries of the current scene/genre. Recorded at Black Lounge Studio (Centinex, Incapacity), Symmetric In Design is Scar Symmetry's world-wide debut, and showcases the band's ability to incorporate a plethora of vocal styles & searing virtuoso guitar solos over technically complex, catchy rhythms. Scar Symmetry will appeal to fans of Children Of Bodom, Arch Enemy & The Haunted! Nuclear Blast. 2005.
