Album Intro：

A different kind of metal: The vocal genii of VAN CANTO present their back catalog including previously unreleased bonus material! Renouncing the usual assistance from electric guitars and keyboards, Van Canto performs the compositions a cappella. Only a lone drummer lends his support to the virtuous vocal acrobats making the resulting 100% metal sound even more amazing. 'Hero' includes an exclusive unreleased bonus track, as well as two video clips to 'Speed of Light' and 'Wishmaster', and an unreleased clip to the track 'Kings of Metal'.
