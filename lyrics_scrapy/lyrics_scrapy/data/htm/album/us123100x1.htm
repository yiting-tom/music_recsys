Album Intro：

The Cottars have the same kind of drive and energy The Chieftains had when we were first starting out. With their combination of incredible musicianship, amazing voices and wonderful dancing, what a future they have ahead of them!
- Paddy Moloney, The Chieftains

This exciting new recording by Cape Breton Island's, The Cottars, is a bridge from the deep Scottish and Irish beginnings of Cape Breton Celtic music to a new emerging roots music that blends American folk music with Celtic overtones as well as blending age old instrumental and vocal material with fresh new compositions that offer an early view into a very promising evolution for this young emerging group.
