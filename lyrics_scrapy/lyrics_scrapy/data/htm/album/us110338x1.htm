Album Intro：

Many listeners who have followed Shane & Shane from the beginning would agree that with the release 'Everything Is Different' the Shanes have clearly ventured towards a more mainstream approach. This release takes that one step further. The previous reviewers mentioned that they had never purchased a S&S album before and that makes sense. I'm sure this album will draw many new fans to the duo. As for the music, there is quite a bit less acoustic guitar on this album. Instead, there is more use of strings and even a few organ sections. This album is by far their most experimental release to date, whereas 'Everything Is Different' was more praise and worship driven.

I am one of the biggest S&S fans out there. I own every album, I see the guys every chance I get and I fully support their music and message. The way they convey Christ to our hearts is a blessing and so unlike many other Christian artists out there. This album doesn't connect with me the way the others have, but that's not to say that will be your case. Hopefully their music will continue to draw believers closer to Christ.
