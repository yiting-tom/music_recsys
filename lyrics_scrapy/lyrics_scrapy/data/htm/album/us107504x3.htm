Album Intro：

North American debut from Australian modern rock trio Sick Puppies. Features the single 'All The Same' (aka 'Free Hugs' video). The 'Free Hugs' video which accompanies 'All The Same' chronicles the true life adventures of a man who walks around holding a billboard that reads 'Free Hugs,' the police who ban his humanitarian crusade, and the petition that earned him back the right to provide hugs to citizens in need.

Dressed Up As Life is a heartfelt collection of exultant rhythms, propulsive beats and choruses that span miles. It's the kind of record that captures the beauty, pain and endless possibilities of LIFE.
