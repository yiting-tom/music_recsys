Album Intro：

Estar de Moda No Esta de Moda is Tommy Torres' second album.
The 2010 re-edition of the album, released under Warner Music Latina, has been revamped with new artwork, includes the hits: Vinito pa'l corazon, Dame esta noche, Besos y Sal, Un Poquito, and De Rodillas. All of the 11 tracks where composed and produced by Torres.

2. Sigo Aqui

4. Dame Esta Noche
5. Besos Y Sal
6. Un Poquito
7. De Rodillas
8. Meteoro
9. Mariana Y Angelina
10. Corazon Remendado
11. Me Cuesta Tanto Olvidarte
