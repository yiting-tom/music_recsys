Album Intro：

Ellekari Larsson formed The Tiny with cellist Leo Svensson and upright bassist Johan Berthling in 2002. The Swedish trio made their US debut in 2005, with the domestic release of Close Enough, a record created from the combo's use of only piano, upright bass and cello. When it was time for the group to make their second record, they explored making a sound true to life in a cinematic way, taking each idea to a new level whilst still keeping their tiny personal identity. This CD creates an entire new world both grandiose and simple, and undoubtedly unique.
