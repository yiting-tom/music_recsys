Album Intro：

2008 debut album from this exciting Australian band who have already been nominated for two Aria Awards. Having formed in 2005 to compete in a school 'Battle of the Bands' competition, they went on to play multiple festivals, including Reading and Leeds in 2007 and supported the likes of Bloc Party and Arctic Monkeys. Catchy, quirky indie anthems are complemented by violin and guitar hooks throughout. Featuring 12 tracks including the singles 'Just A Song About Ping Pong' and 'Get What You Want'. Brille.
