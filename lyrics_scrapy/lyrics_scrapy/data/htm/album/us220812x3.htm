Album Intro：

「等待觸不可及的答案，是否想要的都在遙遠的彼岸」


《尾跡雲》中都是講述發生在夏天的羅曼史，一首是追憶曾經擁有的美好，仍然在等待某個人；另一首則是對幸福誓言的憧憬。

綻放的花火、絢爛的繁星，這些夏日限定的情節都被書寫在歌中，是屬於MisterLady的色彩。

'Awaiting the unreachable answer, whether if you want is on the other side of the distance'


'Contrail' tells the story of Romance that happened in the summer. One is to remember the happiness that once existed, still waiting for someone; the other is the embarrassment of the vow of happiness.

Blooming fireworks, gorgeous stars, these summer-limited plots are written in the song, is the color of MisterLady.
