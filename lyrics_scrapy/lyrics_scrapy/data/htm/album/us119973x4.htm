Album Intro：

2010 album from the Blues guitarist From the origins of his career as a sideman for John Lee Hooker and John Mayall, through his tenure with Canned Heat and then successful solo career that has seen the release of 12 albums, Walter Trout has emerged as one of America's musical treasures. Trout's backing band on Common Ground consists of drummer Kenny Aronoff, bassist Hutch Hutchinson and pianist John Cleary. This lethal combination of talent will embed into your soul and ease into your heart as the shredding melds Blues and Rock.
