Album Intro：

Madina Lake is an American band formed in Chicago, Illinois in 2005. The band consists of lead vocalist Nathan Leone and his twin, bassist and backing vocalist Matthew Leone, guitarist and backup vocalist Mateo Camargo and drummer Dan Torelli.

Citing influences such as Smashing Pumpkins and Nine Inch Nails, Madina Lake released their debut album From Them, Through Us, to You through Roadrunner Records on March 27, 2007. Madina Lake won Best International Newcomer at the 2007 Kerrang! Awards

Attics to Eden is the second studio album by American band Madina Lake, and is scheduled to be released on 5th May 2009 for the USA and 4th May 2009 for the UK. The track listing was announced in January 2009 by the British music magazine, Kerrang.
