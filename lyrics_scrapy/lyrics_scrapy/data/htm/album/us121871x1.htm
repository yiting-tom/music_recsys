Album Intro：

6 NEW RECORDINGS! Grammy Award nominated recording artist Tamela Mann has established herself as a leading lady of Gospel. Her album, Best Days, has been top 5 since its release over a year ago, and #1 on Billboard s Gospel sales chart for 22 weeks. Her royal performance of the hit single, Take Me To The King, has set numerous records, holding on to Billboard s Gospel radio chart #1 slot for 25+ weeks, while continuing to climb the Urban AC Chart, currently in THE TOP 10! Her TV performances have astounded millions - including the unforgettable delivery of her hit song on the BET Awards this summer with the song s writer/producer, Gospel legend Kirk Franklin. To thank her fans for all their support, Tamela is set to release the Best Days Deluxe Edition, featuring new live versions of FOUR Best Days hits along with TWO MORE unexpected NEW surprises!
