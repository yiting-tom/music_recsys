Album Intro：

As a wise man once said: you don´t just become a musician, you are one! If you asked the members of The Traceelords seperately about their motivations, you would be getting corresponding answers and it would be like having asked a family of four about the reasons for their right to exist and their functionality. A rock band is not that much different from a family, as we know some of them even choose the same last name. The Traceelords don´t need that, they are brothers in spirit, and since Hagen can even outside of Northrhine-Westfalia be seen as a part of the infamous Ruhr Area in Germany, the quartet knows what down-to earth and strong company mean. Otherwise it would be impossible to explain why their third album is entitled „The Ali Of Rock'. Get up! Again and again...
