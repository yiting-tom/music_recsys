Album Intro：

Mexico's greatest living singer joins his namesake oldest son for a one-of-a-kind ranchera album, titled Los 2 Vicentes. Vicente Fernandez and Vicente Fernandez, Jr., team up for this all-new, 14-song treasure that features seven songs by the father, six songs by the son, and a historic duet. Titled 'Vamos a Quererla Mas' (We're Going to Love Her More), the duet features father and son singing to Dona Refugio 'Cuquita' Abarca, who is their wife and mother, respectively. Produced in its entirety by Vicente Fernandez himself, other standouts from Los 2 Vicentes include his hit 'Cuando Manda el Corazon' (When the Heart Rules), the theme song to the forthcoming telenovela Amor Bravio. In addition, Vicente Fernandez, Jr., is racking up significant Regional Mexican airplay with his single 'Sin Papeles' (Without Papers). With three songs already gaining lots of attention, and the news of Vicente Fernández's upcoming farewell tour which will feature his son, there is no shortage of interest in Los 2 Vicentes.
