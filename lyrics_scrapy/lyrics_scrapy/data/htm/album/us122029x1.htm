Album Intro：

A collection of tazenda's best hits recorded live. From 'spunta la luna dal monte' to 'pitzinnos in sa gherra' as well as recent hits such as 'madre terra' and 'domo mia'. Including also two special duets: one with marco carta in 'la forza mia', interpreted in the dialect of sardinia and one with gianluca grignani in 'piove luce'. The album also includes a cover of lucio battisti's song 'l'aquila', and a christmas song entitled 'e sar… natale'.
