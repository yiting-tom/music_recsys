Album Intro：

R&B legends George & Louis Johnson return with a super funked out concert performance on this special CD/DVD set, recorded live in Oakland, CA, Memorial Day 2003!

Includes magnificent performances of the Johnson's blockbuster hit singles such as 'Strawberry Letter 23,' 'Stomp!,' 'I'll Be Good To You' and many more!

The Brothers Johnson have worked with such legends as Bobby Womack, The Supremes, Quincy Jones and the late great Ray Charles!

A superb treat for fans of '70s R&B funk!

On Memorial Day 2003, an Oakland CA audience was treated to a very special, truly transcendent live performance by two legendary figures of 70s psychedelic funk, The Brothers Johnson! Nearly three decades after they formed as a band, The Brothers appeared as energetic as ever, a little less fro but a lot more soul, and rocked a set of their very best songs, including the smash hit singles Stomp!, I ll Be Good To You, Shuggie Otis Strawberry Letter 23 and a supercharged cover of Cameo s Word Up. Brother Louis groovin bass lines never sounded as clean and vibrant since he first laid em down and Brother George blew attendants away with his effortless, almost spiritual guitar work. Thank the Heavens then that the entire affair was captured not only on crystal clear audio but stunning, hi-fi digital video as well. Now, fans of these pioneering artists can enjoy the magic at home, at work or wherever the Funky Spirit leads! --Cleopatra Records
