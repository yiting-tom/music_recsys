No one nailed the Beatles sound more dead-on than this New Jersey band did, and never did they do it more convincingly than with their breakthrough 1965 hit Lies . The hits that followed, One Track Mind and High on Love , are also on this collection of their original Challenge tracks, plus Just One Girl; Stick with Me , and more!
