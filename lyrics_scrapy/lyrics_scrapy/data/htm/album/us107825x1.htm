Album Intro：

Wordshaker is the name of the second studio album by British girlgroup, The Saturdays scheduled for release on 12 October 2009 through Fascination Records.[1] It is the group's first album to feature at least one song that was co-written by them.

The album's lead single, 'Forever Is Over' was written by James Bourne and produced by The Runaways. It premiered on 23 August 2009 on BBC Radio 1's Switch and will be fully released on 5 October 2009.
