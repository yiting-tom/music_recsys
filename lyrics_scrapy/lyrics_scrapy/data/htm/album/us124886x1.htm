Album Intro：

Japanese pressing. 2008 album from UK Britpopster Miles Kane and his new outfit The Rascals (not to be confused with Felix Cavaliere's '60s band AKA The Young Rascals). Miles, formerly of the short-lived The Little Flames is perhaps more famous for his role in The Last Shadow Puppets with Arctic Monkey Alex Turner. Rascalize sees the band play a brand of raucous British Indie Rock inspired by the sounds of the '60s as well as contemporaries such as Arctic Monkeys, The Coral and The Zutons. 12 tracks including the single 'Freakbeat Phantom'. Hostess.
