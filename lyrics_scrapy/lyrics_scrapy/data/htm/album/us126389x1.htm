Album Intro：

Demolished Thoughts, the third solo album from Sonic Youth's Thurston Moore, was produced by Beck and features Mary Lattimore on harp, Samara Lubielski on violin, and Beck on various instruments. Demolished Thoughts is an equally beautiful and brooding work. While there are more than a few tonal similarities to some of your favorite older Moore compositions, the execution this time is nothing short of staggering.
