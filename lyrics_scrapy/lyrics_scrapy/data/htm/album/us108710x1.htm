Album Intro：

Powerspaces' debut CD The Kicks Of Passion see the band unleashing their own inimitable sound, a brightly colored mash-up of pure power pop and electronica-inspired invention. Tracks such as ' I Put The 'Abs' in Abstinence' and 'Right On, Right Now literally snap and crackle with the just-out-of-their-teens quartet's uncontainable charm and energy. The Kicks Of Passion is available everywhere on July 31st 2007. The band will be on tour non-stop throughout 2007, kicking off 'Kicks Of Passion' release with Hawthorne Heights and From First To Last throughout the summer on the 'Summer Must Go On' tour! Past dates include Red Jumpsuit Apparatus, Bamboozle 2007, This Providence, Amber Pacific, Monty Are I, as well as performing at SxSW 2007.
