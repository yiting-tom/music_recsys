Album Intro：

'Stormwarning' is TEN's ninth original studio album. Considered by many fans and critics to be the best expression of British Classic Hard Rock in the nineties, TEN have won several reader's charts in BURRN magazine (Japan's leading HR / HM publication) and entered the sales charts in Germany as well Japan. Ten started the recording of 'Stormwarning' in the summer of 2010, an album that, like the name itself tells, is an inspiration for tales of glory, war, and death and of course love. Any rock fan who digs the sound that Rainbow carved out with 'Difficult to Cure' or that Whitesnake brought to success with '1987' and that Gary Moore exalted with albums like 'Final Frontier' or 'Run For Cover' needs to have this record in their collection.
