Album Intro：

Deluxe CD/DVD edition includes bonus DVD. 2011 album from the singer/songwriter. Revelation Road is Shelby's most personal record yet. She wrote, recorded and produced the album, which leads off with the title track and first single, a stirring reflection on reckoning and redemption. From Country to Pop and back again, this is Shelby at her finest.
