Making of the Song

This song is about a story about a guy getting back his girl, apologising and tryna be the better one for her. Its definitely a personal experience, and its normal for someone to relate when they realised they screwed up, and they wanna get back their loved ones.

Song was recorded on a mellow beat at first, then later rearranged to a more upbeat style beat. The switch-up from a sadder to a happier beat presented a contrast in the delivery of melody, portraying that sometimes a sad song doesn't have to be really gloomy.

這是一個關於當男孩嘗試要回到女孩的故事，要道歉，並且對她更好。
人戀愛，搞砸，道歉，挽回，一個圈子。

歌曲首先錄製在一個柔和的節拍上，然後重新排列成一個更upbeat的節拍。
從悲傷到快樂節拍的轉換呈現出旋律傳遞的對比，描繪了有時思念的歌曲不一定非常陰沉。
