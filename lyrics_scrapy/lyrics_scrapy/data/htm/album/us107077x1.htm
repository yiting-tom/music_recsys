Album Intro：

Undeniable hooks, soaring choruses, and driving anthems have garnered THE HINT industry attention and critical praise, and top A&R sites are watching closely. This band is consistently a step ahead of the pack, and I AM THE HINT represents a new high point in the band's fusion of power and finesse, buzzing guitars and tasteful synths. 'The Maryland-based act could hang in the same crowd as Jimmy Eat World and Weezer.' - Dean, Kings of A&R. 'Timeless music that can be as catchy as a single or as moody as something The Cure would release. I'm expecting big things from this band. Big things.' - Tim Towner, The Daily Chorus. Producedby Paul Leavitt (All Time Low, VersaEmerge) and mixed by Mark Needham (The Killers, Metro Station).
