Album Intro：

These three releases comprise the final wave of The Triffids' re-issue program that Domino Records started in 2006 with the re-issues of 'Born Sandy Devotional', 'Calenture', and 'In The Pines', all re-mastered and released as deluxe CDs with additional music and sleeve notes. 'Beautiful Waste And Other Songs' comprises a large series of recordings the band made between 1983 and 1985. For the most part, these fully re-mastered songs are appearing on CD for the first time.
