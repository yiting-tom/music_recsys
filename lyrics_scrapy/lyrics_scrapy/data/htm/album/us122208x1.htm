Album Intro：

Wildly exciting new album from Norway's electric guitar master This large scale work was commissioned by the Natjazz Festival, and recorded live in Bergen. Crime Scene makes reference to John Coltrane, another formative influence, in the writing for large ensemble but also has sections of burning flat-out rock jamming.with some of Rypdal's hottest guitar work in years, certain to delight Terje's many followers. All the musicians have worked with Rypdal before, and they contribute passion, energy and Noir-ish flair to the music, with with hints of everything from Spaghetti westerns to inner city Mob movies...
