Album Intro：

In 1999, The Two Dollar Pistols frontman John Howie, Jr. and North Carolina native, Tift Merritt, joined forces to try out some country duets they were working on. After performing the songs to an enthusiastic audience at Chapel Hill venue Local 506, the duo decided to record a seven-song EP. Yep Roc Records released the EP on CD in October 1999. Now, more than a decade later, Yep Roc is releasing the record on vinyl for the first time.
