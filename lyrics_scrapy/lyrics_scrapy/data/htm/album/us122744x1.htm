Album Intro：

Burning Sky's award-winning flutist/guitarist Aaron White's latest project is one of my top contemporary Native American CD's of 2006. White (Dine/Ute) and bandmates Gary Rasmussen (Guitar, Bass) and Ed Michaels (Drums, Percussion) have teamed up to perform as The Blue Stone Project. A masterful mixture of contemporary instrumentation, soulful vocals and traditional Native flute, Blue Stone's songwriting is 'sweet', the arrangements tight and the playing, right on the money. White's flute playing has never been better and his vocals are, to die for! The music is a cornucopia of influences from all three members including rock, folk, blues and traditional Native. Highlights include the opening cut, 'Sacred Road', a marvelous track entitled, 'Songs of Freedom' and my personal favorite, 'Hold Onto Hope & Love'. Aaron White's Blue Stone Project and their CD, 'Blue Stone' are superb. --By Gene Bates - Whispering Wind / Issue #257 / Vol 37 #1
