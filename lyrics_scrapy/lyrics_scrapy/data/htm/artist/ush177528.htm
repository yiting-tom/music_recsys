Nicholas Gale, better known as Digital Farm Animals, is a British DJ, record producer, singer, songwriter and remixer.

Career

Digital Farm Animals has worked as a songwriter and producer with Dua Lipa, Alan Walker, Galantis, will.i.am, Little Mix, Louisa Johnson, R. Kelly, Nelly, Marlon Roudette, Anne-Marie, INNA, Blonde, Louis Tomlinson and Sigma. As a DJ, he has supported Knife Party, Chase & Status and Netsky on tour. In 2015, Digital Farm Animals signed a record deal with Simon Cowell’s record label Syco Entertainment.
