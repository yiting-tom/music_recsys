Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of this 1973 collection from the British Glam rockers featuring four bonus tracks including a previously unreleased studio version of 'Hear Me Calling'. Comes with the original gatefold LP packaging carefully reproduced. Includes no less than five UK #1 hits. 16-page booklet includes new sleeve notes by respected scribe and Slade aficionado Chris Ingham incorporating new interviews with Dave Hill and Don Powell.
