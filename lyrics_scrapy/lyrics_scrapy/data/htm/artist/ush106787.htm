Terius Youngdell Nash (born September 20, 1977), better known by his stage name The-Dream, is an American singer, songwriter and record producer. His co-writing credits include songs with Dear Jayne, '16 @ War' for Karina Pasian, 'Me Against the Music' (2003) for Britney Spears, 'Umbrella' (2007) for Rihanna, 'Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)' (2008) for Beyoncé and 'Baby' (2010) for Justin Bieber. He released five studio albums between 2007 and 2013: Love Hate (2007), Love vs. Money (2009), Love King (2010), Terius Nash: 1977 (2012) and IV Play (2013).
