Album Intro：

New Jersey's metal superstars, The Air I Breathe, release their mind blowing debut, Great Faith In Fools on June 7th, 2011. Full on tech-metal ala Between The Buried And Me and Dillinger Escape Plan. But, there's something a little different about TAIB. It's not all shred and screaming all the time in their camp, they know how to bring the hooky choruses. They mix up melody with technical death metal and it sounds bad ass! Mature, melodic and sonically mind blowing.
