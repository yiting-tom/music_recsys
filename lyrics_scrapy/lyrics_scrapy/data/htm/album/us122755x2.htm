Album Intro：

2007 collection featuring early recordings by this most excellent Britpop band, recorded before they signed to a major label in the early '90s. 10 tracks including demos of favorites like 'Are You Blue Or Are You Blind?', 'Carn't Be Trusted' and others. Cooking Vinyl.
