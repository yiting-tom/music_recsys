Album Intro：

With lyrics that often evoke feelings of solitude and images of the natural world, my room in the trees sonically progresses along the path begun with 2003's Birds of My Neighborhood, spacious and warmly grounded with baritone and nylon string guitars and upright bass, and lit from above by electric guitar and piano. After making three albums with A&M records, the innocence mission began self-producing their albums in 1999. This is their ninth LP.
