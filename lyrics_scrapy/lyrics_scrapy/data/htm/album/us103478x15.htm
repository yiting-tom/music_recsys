Album Intro：

Limited deluxe digipak pressing. with embossed cover. 2009 release from the Brazilian Heavy Metal band, an album inspired by the book A Clockwork Orange. Recorded in Sao Paulo at Trama Studios and mixed at Mega Studios, A-lex features 18 songs and is the first album recorded with the new drummer Jean Dolabella . A-Lex sounds different from any other work by Sepultura. As the band defines it, it was written and composed in record time because it's basically a recording of studio jams over a three month period.
