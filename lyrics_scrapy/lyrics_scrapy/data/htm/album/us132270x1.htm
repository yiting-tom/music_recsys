Album Intro：

Deluxe two CD edition includes a bonus CD that contains eight previously unreleased demo tracks plus two enhanced videos. 2011 album from the veteran rocker. The 11 tracks include four new Mike Chapman compositions, two self-penned tunes plus great covers of the likes of Goldfrapp's 'Strict Machine' and Rhianna's 'Breaking Dishes'. Also features the bonus track 'Singing With Angels'. Produced by Sweet's Andy Scott, this Suzi-penned tribute to Elvis Presley features the Kings' original guitarist James Burton and his legendary backing vocalists The Jordanaires and is a 'must have' for all Elvis collectors as well as Suzi's legion of fans. Cherry Red.
