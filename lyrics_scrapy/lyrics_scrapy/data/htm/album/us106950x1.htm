Album Intro：

Discussing his smash hit single 'While I'm Waiting,' 'I'm sure most people can relate to this song,' says singer/songwriter John Waller, 'but the important thing to remember while we're waiting on God, is to not just wait, but to actively wait. Serve, worship and be faithful with what you have, where you are.even while you wait.'

Indeed, this overflowing message of trust in the Lord is so important that it soon became the prevailing sentiment of Waller's sophomore album, also titled While I'm Waiting. With songs like 'Faith Is Living,' 'Cling to the Call' and 'The Lord Reigns Here,' this writer of declaration-style anthems for the body of Christ is once again giving listeners biblical words to claim as their own during trying and uncertain times. 'My calling is to go deeper into God's Word and to let that truth set me free,' John relates. 'When people sing the truth of God's Word it changes their lives. I don't know anything more powerful!'
