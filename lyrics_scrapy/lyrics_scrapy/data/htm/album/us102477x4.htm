Album Intro：

Brand new recordings from platinum selling artist Vanilla Ice returning to his roots with this collection of revamped hip hop classics!

Features a stellar old school version of his classic Ice Ice Baby plus covers of Public Enemy s Fight The Power, Sir Mix-A-Lot s Baby Got Back, Cypress Hill s Insane In The Brain, House Of Pain s Jump Around and more!

He s been through multiple transformations in his career but with this release Ice takes his rightful place among the pioneers of rap music, a wise veteran who still has plenty to offer the hip hop community!

Go ahead, call it a comeback, because Vanilla Ice is BACK - back to playing that funky old school hip hop music this white boy is known for. After his meteoric rise to fame in 1990 - achieving the first #1 rap single on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and a Grammy nomination for Best New Artist - through his recent turn as a reality show superstar (The Surreal Life, Celebrity Boxing), Vanilla Ice has proved himself a survivor. Now he s ready to take on the new role of elder statesman of hip hop with these phenomenal recordings of classic songs originally made famous by fellow pioneers Public Enemy, Sir Mix-A-Lot, Cypress Hill and others. This is Ice as he is known and loved by millions - to the max, to the hilt and to the extreme! --Official Press Release
