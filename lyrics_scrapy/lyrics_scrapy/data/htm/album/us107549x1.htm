Album Intro：

Adelitas Way is a hard rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada that broke into mainstream in 2009 for their song 'Invincible', which is the official theme song of WWE Superstars on WGN America. They also perform the song 'New Day' as the theme song for World Wrestling Entertainment tag team The Legacy. On July 14, 2009, their debut album, Adelitas Way (2009), was released.
