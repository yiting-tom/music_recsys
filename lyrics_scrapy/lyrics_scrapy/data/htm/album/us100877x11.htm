Album Intro：

2009 compilation from the Australian singer, songwriter and performer containing songs sung in both French and English. Over her two decade long career, Arena has won numerous awards, most notably five ARIA Awards (the Australian equivalent of the Grammy) and, in both 1996 and 2000, she received the World Music Award for the world's best selling Australian artist. Features 18 tracks including duets with Patrick Fiori ('Out Here On My Own') and Henri Salvador ('Et Puis Apres'). Columbia
