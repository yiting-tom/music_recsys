Album Intro：

Formed in 2005, the Graduate quickly amassed a dedicated fan base with their atmospheric, emotional brand of rock. Their self-released EP was the #1 most added album at CMJ. After hitting the Midwest tour circuit, the Graduate released their debut album Anhedonia in 2007. The band lived on the road for the next two years, touring with Jack's Mannequin, Secondhand Serenade, Mayday Parade, Simple Plan, plus stints on Warped and Lollapalooza. After their label imploded, the Graduate soldiered on and hit the studio with Brian McTernan (Thrice, Circa Survive, Senses Fail) to record Only Every Time.
