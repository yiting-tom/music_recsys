Album Intro：

Things happened very quickly for Syracuse, NY's HONOR BRIGHT. The band made their impact after self-releasing the 6-song EP If This Was A Movie, a jam-packed recording full of gang- vocals & pure pop-punk fun. They appeared on MTV's TRL & joined the Vans Warped Tour that summer. After signing to Triple Crown Records, the band spent the final months of '09 working tirelessly on their debut, Action! Drama! Suspense! The album shows off their talent for crafting super-catchy and fun-filled melodic rock anthems.
