Album Intro：

Vanna have emerged from New England's surging post-hardcore scene with intense brutality to stake their claim at the forefront of the genre. 'A New Hope' brings twelve explosive tracks that perfectly fuse emotive rock and perilous hardcore. An accessible mix of catchiness from their EP and the brutal heaviness of their debut full-length with an added edge of maturity. RIYL: Escape The Fate, A Day To Remember, Underoath, Norma Jean.
