Album Intro：

- Second album of Hannover/Germany based Heavy Metal/Prog. Metal band onto their stunning debut Kings of Pain''. Influenced by the 70's era of Metal there are burning guitar riffs and virtuoso solos laid over a pounding bass and drum section plus superb lyrics and an atmosphere that will lurk in your soul forever! A perfect blending of classic rock and mysticial imagery, musically the whole band is flawless on this release. - Descent From Hell'' was mastered by Michael Wagener (f.e. QUEEN, ACCEPT, OZZY OSBOURNE, GREAT WHITE) in his WireWorld''-Studios in Nashville.
