Album Intro：

Century Media is proud to announce the release of Rise To Remain's debut album. The highly acclaimed young British metal band will release City Of Vultures in the States before setting out on their first major tour of the States, playing 21 dates in total. Created with renowned metal producer Colin Richardson manning the controls, City Of Vultures is an explosive, assured and extraordinarily diverse first offering that marks Rise To Remain as extremely talented standard bearers for a new generation of metal bands.
