Album Intro：

Chimeric is the fourth full length album from the Austrian trio Radian after a five year absence. The members of Radian are all huge parts of the avant electronic music scene and have a very large cultish following. They all perform in other groups besides Radian such as Lokai, Trapist, and Kapital Band 1. Anticipation for Chimeric has been building for years and we are given something much more free.
