2009 release, remixed and reconfigured by Mike Rep (Guided By Voices, Mike Rep And The Quotas). What you have here is a collection of songs found on the various shrine cassettes. They've all been lovingly fucked with my Mr. Mike Rep, (the man behind the lo-fi genius of Guided By Voices' Propeller and Get Out Of My Stations among other things) and as the old saying goes the proof is in the pudding. 11 tracks.
