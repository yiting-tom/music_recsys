Album Intro：

'Fearless is the second studio album by American country pop artist Taylor Swift. It is slated for release on November 11, 2008 (see 2008 in country music) on Big Machine Records. 'Change', the first song from this album to chart, was also included on the AT&T TEAM USA Soundtrack, although 'Love Story' is the first official single. The tracklisting along with the album cover was released on her website.The music video for 'Love Story' made its premiere on Friday, September 12th at 8:30 P.M. EDT on CMT.Another track from this album, 'White Horse', was played during the season premiere of Grey's Anatomy on Thursday, September 25, 2008.
