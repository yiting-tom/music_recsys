Album Intro：

2010 album from the British singer/songwriter. Merchants And Thieves is an album that brilliantly reflects how Sandi has opened up a whole new channel for her creativity. Retaining her innate feeling for a powerful melody and a trenchant lyric, the record adds the new Blues sensibility that visitors to her famously powerful gigs have sensed for themselves in recent months. Thom was encouraged to bring her long-held love of such heritage music to the fore, and her respect for such guitar giants as Peter Green, as fate called for her to sit in as vocalist with Joe Bonamassa, when the modern-day Blues titan lost his voice. There's no better endorsement than his presence on the album as both singer and guitarist on the album's lead track, 'This Ol' World.' Guardian Angel Records.
