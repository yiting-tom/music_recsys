Album Intro：

To prepare for his tenth anniversary in the music business, T.M. Revolution is set to release his brand new album vertical infinity! The ever-changing T.M. Revolution adds a heavier rock sound to the sound-scape, adding importance to the instrumental sound. Compared to his previous record, there is a noticeable improvement to his sound. Some of the great tracks in this 12-track album include the extremely popular theme song to the anime series Mobile Suit Gundam Seed Destiny, 'Ignited', as well as the Japanese theme song to the Hollywood blockbuster Spider-Man 2 , 'Web of Light'. Tofu. 2005.
