Album Intro：

Limited two CD Tour Edition of their critically acclaimed 2008 debut album including a bonus six track live CD. Sparkadia have certainly had a busy year to date. They've cemented themselves in the UK as one of Australia's most valuable exports, winning over heavyweights like the NME, BBC Radio and The Guardian as well as wowing audiences at Glastonbury, V Festival, Oxygen and T In The Park. They've shared the stage with Jimmy Eat World, Vampire Weekend, The Thrills and Australia's newest favorite indie darlings Faker. If that wasn't enough, Sparkadia will also be doing a full national headline tour.
