Kosheen was a British electronic music group based in Bristol, United Kingdom. The group consists of Welsh singer-songwriter Sian Evans (born 9 October 1971, Caerphilly), songwriter-producer Markee Ledge (born Mark Davies, 1974, Springburn, Glasgow) and Darren Decoder (born Darren Beale, 1974, Bristol). The name Kosheen derives from the name of a North American Apache Warrior, Cochise. When in Japan, it was discovered that a combination of the Japanese words for 'old' (古, romanized transliteration 'ko') and 'new' (新, 'shin') would make 'Kosheen' in Japanese.
