Album Intro：

Reason To Believe is the confirmed title for the ninth studio album by the American punk rock band Pennywise, which will be released on March 25, 2008 as a free digital download on MySpace Records in the United States. The album, will be available in Europe on March 24th. Epitaph Records will release the album as a standard CD and a limited-edition vinyl version with two bonus tracks, not available in any other format. The working title for the album was Free for the People.The album will be free only for 2 weeks. The rules are not clear about the time frame regarding free MP3s and such. New rules now? Nuke if you don't like...but keep in mind it won't be released free for another 4 days. Nor do we know the quality of it.
