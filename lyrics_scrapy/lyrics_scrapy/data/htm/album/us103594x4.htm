Album Intro：

2010 release, the fifth solo album from the Hip Hop sensation. Trina is back with an updated sound and truly consistent album that proves she has grown up, and matured as an artist. Heavy weight producers who collaborated on the album include Jim Jonsin, JR Rotem and Justice League among others. The album includes guest appearances by Diddy, Keri Hilson, Nicki Minaj, Flo-Rida, Monica, Lyfe Jennings, and Lady Saw. Features the single 'Million Dollar Girl'.
