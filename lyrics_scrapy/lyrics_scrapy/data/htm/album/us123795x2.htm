Album Intro：

Limited digipak edition includes two bonus tracks: 'Attention' and 'The Reflection' (Live). 2011 release, the seventh studio album from the Swedish Metal band. The album was recorded with longtime producer Tue Madsen at his Antfarm Studio in rhus, Denmark. Moving away from their Thrash Metal beginnings, Unseen finds The Haunted staking out new territory that is equally potent but more unique than their peers.
