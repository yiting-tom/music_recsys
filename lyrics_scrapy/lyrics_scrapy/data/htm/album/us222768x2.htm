Album Intro：

「曾經我就像找不到方向的海浪般在海上飄盪，害怕大海的你遠遠的看著我不敢靠近、充滿疑慮，如今你已放心的讓我帶著你一起在彷彿汪洋般一望無際的未來流浪，就算害怕也能繾綣在彼此的身旁，而海浪什麼話也沒說，只是靜靜的用三拍子拍打，不再感到孤單。」

'I used to wander around the sea like a wave of no direction. I am afraid that the sea is far away from me. I dare not approach, full of doubts. Now you have assured that I will take you with you like a sea. Wandering in the future, even if you are afraid, you can lie on each other's side, and the waves don't say anything, just quietly beat with three beats, no longer feel lonely.'
