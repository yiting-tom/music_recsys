Finally on Vinyl Progressive Rock has got a new definition: SUBSIGNAL! After the huge success of their debut album BEAUTIFUL & MONSTROUS in 2009, they come back with TOUCHSTONES , yet more elaborate and more innovative than before a stately album which reinforces their well-deserved large reputation.
