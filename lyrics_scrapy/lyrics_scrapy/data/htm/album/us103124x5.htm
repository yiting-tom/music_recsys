Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded two CD edition of the 1987 debut album from the British Pop/Soul vocalist. Rick Astley burst onto the pop scene in the summer of 1987 with 'Never Gonna Give You Up', which stormed to #1 in the UK and the U.S., selling a million copies in the US alone, and finished 1987 as the UK's best selling single that year. The album reached #1 in the UK in November, while selling two million copies in the US. This expanded edition features bonus non album tracks plus a multitude of remixes of the album's singles. The booklet features full annotation by Tom Parker as well as the singles sleeves. Edsel. 2010.
