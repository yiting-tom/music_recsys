Album Intro：

If the movie Sister Act wasn't enough and Sister Act II left you wanting even more, then you'll be delighted by this soundtrack to the hit musical! Sister Act is a divine musical comedy now raising the roof at the London Palladium. Based on the smash-hit movie, Sister Act features a brand new score by eight-time Oscar winner Alan Menken whose credits include Little Shop of Horrors and Beauty and the Beast. Lyrics to Menken's excellent music have been supplied by none other than Glenn Slater. Warner. 2009.
