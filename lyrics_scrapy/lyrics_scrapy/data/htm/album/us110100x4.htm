Album Intro：

Orlando, FL's Sleeping With Sirens completed their highly anticipated debut full length album with producer Cameron Mizell (I See Stars, Oceana). Sleeping With Sirens are no strangers to the road having already toured alongside From First To Last, Drop Dead Gorgeous, Sky Eats Airplane, Confide, Eyes Set To Kill, We Came As Romans,Jonny Craig, Tides Of Man, and more. If you haven't already heard of Sleeping With Sirens, you will shortly.
