Album Intro：

New Again is the upcoming fourth studio album by American rock band Taking Back Sunday, scheduled to be released through Warner Bros. Records on 2 June 2009. It will be the first album to include their new guitarist Matthew Fazzi after the departure of Fred Mascherino in mid-2007. It will be produced by David Kahne, with a selection of tracks also produced by Matt Squire. Additional production on all tracks was done by Jamie Siegel.
