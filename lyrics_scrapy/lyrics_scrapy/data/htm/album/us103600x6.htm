Album Intro：

2010 album from the veteran Chicago Hip Hop star. Features guest appearances from Chris Brown, Ray J, Lloyd, Yo Gotti, Waka Flaka Flame and Raekwon. For 20 years Twista has been bringing his kinetic flows to the ears of millions of listeners, putting Chicago on the map in the music industry. Always relevant, Twista remains in the game despite a constantly shifting music industry and a new wave of rap artists. Deeming himself the Jason Voorhees of rap, Twista is resilient in the industry as he continues to slay tracks.
