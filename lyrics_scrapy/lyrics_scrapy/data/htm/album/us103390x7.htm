Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded two CD edition of the British Goth Rock band's 1995 album including a bonus disc that contains the non-album singles, mixes and rare tracks. Formed in 1986 by Wayne Hussey and Craig Adams when they left the Sisters Of Mercy, The Mission enjoyed a succession of hit albums and singles through the second half of the '80s and the first half of the '90s. The CD booklet contains all the lyrics, as well as brand new annotation by Tim Jones of Record Collector.
