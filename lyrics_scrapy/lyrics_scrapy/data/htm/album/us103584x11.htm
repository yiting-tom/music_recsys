Album Intro：

2012 release, the 19th album from the undisputed Godfather Of Bay Area Hip Hop. The album's 17 tracks are sprinkled with live instrumentation infused with a funky and bluesy sound, storytelling and old school flavor that made his name the stuff of legend. Includes guest features from Snoop Dogg, 50 Cent, Twista, E-40, Kokane and Wallpaper.
