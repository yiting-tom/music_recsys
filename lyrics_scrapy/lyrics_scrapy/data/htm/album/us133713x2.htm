Album Intro：

Ricky Dillard has been proclaimed one of the most dynamic and charismatic
choir directors in Gospel Music! Dillard has gained notoriety by bringing his
staunch, hometown Chicago Gospel stylings to the national stage, backed by
his award-winning, and equally energetic choir, New G.
Dillard presented his latest musical offering, KEEP LIVING, during a filledto-
capacity live recording in Detroit, Michigan. The new music is rife with
Dillard s signature energetic flare and musicalities, and is infused with Dillard s
consistently foundational and newly invigorated spirituality.
In the tradition of Dillard s biggest hits like The Light, and Oh How Precious,
Dillard scores another homerun with KEEP LIVING, replete with hit songs in the
making, including the lead single, God Is Great, He Turned It, and the title
track, Keep Living.
Ricky Dillard and New G. are, unquestionably, leaders in choral Gospel music,
and do not disappoint with KEEP LIVING. The music hits harder, the lyrics dig
deeper, KEEP LIVING lifts the listener higher!
