Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of the British Shoegaze quartet's 1994 album including bonus tracks. Ride were a British alternative rock band that formed in 1988 in Oxford, England, consisting of Andy Bell, Mark Gardener, Laurence 'Loz' Colbert, and Steve Queralt. The band were initially part of the 'shoegazing' scene and remain perhaps the most popular band of that genre.
