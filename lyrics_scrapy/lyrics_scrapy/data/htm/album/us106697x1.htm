Album Intro：

Robert Wilson (born on February 27, 1980), better known by his stage name Bobby Valentino, is an American R&B singer (his nickname being 'Valentino' because he was due to be born on Valentine's day) known for his tenor. He is known as Bobby V or Bobby V-Tino in the United Kingdom due to a lawsuit involving a British musician who has been recording under the same name since 1975.

The Rebirth is the third studio album from R&B singer Bobby Valentino. It is scheduled to be released on February 10, 2009, which was revealed in an interview on MyFox Atlanta's Good Day Atlanta morning show. The album will be Valentino's first to be released through his new record label Blu Kolla Dreams.
