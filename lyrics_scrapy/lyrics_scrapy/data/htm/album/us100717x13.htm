Album Intro：

This is a 2 x CD release, including the original 'Relapse' album - Which reached No.1 in the UK albums charts when originally released - with a bonus disc containing 7 more tracks. Five of these tracks have not previously been released. The other two tracks are 'Taking My Ball' (from DJ Hero) and 'Forever' from the 'Music Inspired by More Than A Game' album.
