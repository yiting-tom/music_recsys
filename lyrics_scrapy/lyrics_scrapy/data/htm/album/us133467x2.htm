Album Intro：

Revolution Renaissance is the new band and brainchild of former Stratovarius guitarist Timo Tolkki. Stratovarius were one of the leading pioneers of progressive power metal they have sold in excess of 3 million albums worldwide. As a guitarist, Timo is ranked alongside other genre greats such as Yngwie Mamsteen, John Petrucci (Dream Theater) and Michael Romeo (Symphony X). New Era is a powerful musical portrait of melodic beauty and musical brilliance. * Also featuring Michael Kiske (Helloween) on vocals.
