Album Intro：

''What You Don't See'' is The Story So Far's followup to the wildly successful and now scene classic ''Under Soil And Dirt.'' Produced by New Found Glory's Steven Klein and engineered by long-time friend Sam Pura, ''What You Don't See'' is the record that every pop punk kid is dying for. With aggressive and very personal lyrics mixed with a more mature and developed songwriting style, the record proves to be everything the last record was and a lot more. Sophomore slump be damned, ''What You Don't See'' is the album that solidifies The Story So Far as a band to be taken seriously for decades to come.
