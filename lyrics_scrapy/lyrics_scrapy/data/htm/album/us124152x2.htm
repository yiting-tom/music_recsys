Album Intro：

Import repressing of the UK band's 2004 sophomore album including one bonus track: 'Cyclops'. In the late '90s in the East London squat scene, two troubadours and dreamers, Peter Doherty and Carl Barat, met and bonded over music and a common romanticism. They resolved to form a band with a name that reflected their attitude: a libertine is someone who is unrestrained by convention or morality. The Libertines were born! By the time of this second album, the dream was unraveling but the music was still exciting, raw and passionate. 15 tracks. 2009.
