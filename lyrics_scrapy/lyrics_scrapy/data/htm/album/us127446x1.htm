Album Intro：

Explicit version. Trillville is a rap group from Atlanta. Trillville's sophomore album, Straight Up, No Chaser! With production from The Trillest Producer himself, Don P this album is sure to be a hit. Trillville is gearing up for the next level. The boisterous and brash Trillville began their bid for the title 'Gods of Crunk' in 1997 while attending ninth grade in Atlanta's public school system. Don P was rapping and working on producing beats with his keyboard, and Lil LA (aka Lil Atlanta) was an aspiring promoter, later to become a rapper himself. Lil LA guaranteed and delivered a packed house every time the group hit the stage as young Atlanta took to their new crunk sound. With Don P always producing the hits that consistently got the clubs crunk, Lil LA joined with his own raps and the group named themselves Trillville.
