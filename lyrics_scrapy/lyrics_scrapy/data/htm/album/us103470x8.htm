Album Intro：

180-gram pressing. Screaming Trees is considered to be one of the Godfathers of Grunge. Although they were one of the best `Seattle-bands' of the early nineties, they never achieved the commercial success of bands like Nirvana, Pearl Jam and Alice in Chains. Founded in 1985, the band had to wait till 1992 for their biggest success, the release of 'Sweet Oblivion'. The song `Nearly Lost you' became the Screaming Trees' biggest hit after being featured on the soundtrack of the movie 'Singles'. Lead singer Mark Lanegan started a very successful solo career after the Screaming Trees, working with the likes of Queens of the Stone Age, Greg Dulli (Afghan Wigs), Isobel Campbell (Belle & Sebastian) and UNKLE. Being out of print for years, this 90's milestone will be finally available again!
