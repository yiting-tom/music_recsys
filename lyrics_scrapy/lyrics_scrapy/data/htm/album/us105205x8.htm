Album Intro：

Fronted by Brooklyn-based siblings Eleanor and Matthew Friedberger, the Fiery Furnaces started turning heads in 2003 with their debut album, Gallowsbird's Bark. The following year's Blueberry Boat was where they truly came into their own, though, with complex, shifting song structures and a wildly eclectic sound incorporating Garage Rock, Glam, Post-Punk, Blues, Prog, and much more.
