Album Intro：

Boston sweethearts Therefore I Am formed in 2004 and after years of DIY, are releasing their debut full-length. The hardcore punk band with pop rock sensibilities already has a rabid northeast fan base and will conquer the rest of the US during this summer's Warped Tour. One of Alternative Press' 100 Bands You Need To Know In 2009. Produced by Michael Poorman (Piebald, Tsunami Bomb, Paulson). RIYL: Thrice, The Receiving End Of Sirens, Circa Survive, and Thursday.
