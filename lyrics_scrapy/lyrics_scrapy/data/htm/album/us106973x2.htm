Album Intro：

Ocean Eyes is the 2009 sophomore album of synthpop artist Adam Young, who goes by the name Owl City. The album features guest vocals by Relient K vocalist Matt Theissen on 'Fireflies', 'Cave In', 'The Bird and the Worm' and 'Tidal Wave.' It was released on July 14, 2009 to iTunes, and on July 28, 2009 to stores. On July 14th, 2009, the song 'Fireflies' was chosen as the Single of the Week by the iTunes music store.
