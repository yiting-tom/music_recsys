Album Intro：

Gavin DeGraw (born February 4, 1977) is an American singer-songwriter and musician. He is probably most well-known for his songs 'Chariot', 'I Don't Want to Be', which is featured as the theme song for the TV drama One Tree Hill, and 'In Love with a Girl'.

2009 release, the third album from the multi-platinum singer/songwriter. Free is a collection of songs that showcase the true spirit of his incredible talent. Following up on Gavin's self-titled sophomore release which included the hit 'In Love With A Girl', Free showcases Gavin's versatility and soulful roots to form a collection of new songs, previously unrecorded fan favorites and a stunning cover of Chris Whitley's 'Indian Summer'. Joining Gavin on this record are such virtuosos as Audley Freed on guitar (Black Crowes), and Charley Drayton on drums (Keith Richards, Johnny Cash) who help give Free a rich, organic depth and help take Gavin's natural ability to create memorable songs into a new direction
