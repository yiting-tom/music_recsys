Album Intro：

No Gift to Bring is a five song EP by the Christian Alternative Rock band The Almost to tide over fans until the release of their sophmore album in 2009. No Gift to Bring releases in 2008 through Tooth and Nail Records. Almost's Aaron Gillespie is a man obsessed. Whether he's out in front of The Almost, his celebrated rock-based band, or behind the drum kit for Underoath, the Clearwater, Florida bred songwriter, musician can't help but throw himself into everything he does. Per Aaron 'From a 12-year-old listening to her iPod on the school bus to a 38-year-old guy commuting on a train, I'm excited by the fact that this music can find an audience with a lot of different people.'
