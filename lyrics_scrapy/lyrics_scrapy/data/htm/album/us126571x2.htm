Album Intro：

Out of print in the U.S.! Platinum-selling 1997 debut album from Timbaland & Magoo, the Norfolk, Virginia Hip Hop duo featuring Timbaland, who would soon become one of the biggest producers in the R&B and Hip Hop field.. The album features a number of guest appearances from Timbaland & Magoo's regular collaborators at the time, among them Missy Elliott, Aaliyah and others. Universal.
