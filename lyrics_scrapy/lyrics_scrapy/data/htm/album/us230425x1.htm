Album Intro：

「我覺得我們好像都生錯星球了？」
火星人們常常無法理解地球上的人們到底嗑了什麼，才能活得這麼開心這麼好，活得這麼沒有靈魂沒有腦袋，但無奈的火星人們總是努力維持著地球人的外表，讓自己繼續在這顆險峻的星球上繼續生存下去。

「有沒有人收到求救訊號？」
火星人們覺得每天關在家裡喝酒嗑藥逃避現實也不是辦法，所以開始嘗試對外星球求救。地球這地方雖然有許多令人厭惡的奇異現象，卻也充滿著令人著迷的美妙事物，當然，音樂正是其中之最。於是火星人們著手開始了這個計畫，每日每夜對這個宇宙唱出他們內心最深的鄉愁，或許有一天吧，也能夠感動地球人們也說不定。

說這麼多，最終還是要補這句：儘管在這樣不友善的世界裡，我們仍然對音樂有一份愚蠢的執著，就算這世界上不再有人有興趣聽，還是會繼續唱下去。

「被地球耽誤的火星人們」是失眠星球頻道的第一張專輯，在這張作品中他們維持了一貫的陰鬱色彩，用各式各樣的聲響堆疊為一捲濃厚的灰色漩渦，將聽眾深深地捲入其中，久久不能自拔。歡迎上船，一同體驗這個長達52分鐘的火星人生活吧。

'It feels like we've all been born on the wrong planet?'
Martians can never understand what kind of drug Earthlings use to live so happily and well... and so soulless and mindless. Still, helpless martians always keep their camouflage as Earthlings carefully in order to keep surviving on this dangerous planet.

'Does anyone get our distress signal?'
Martians soon realized that they can't keep abusing alcohol and drugs and trap themselves at home to escape the reality. Therefore, they started to figure out a way to call for help to the outer space. Luckily, although this planet is filled with all kinds of odd and disappointing phenomenons, there still exist a lot of fascinating beings. Music is one of the most of course. As a consequence, Martians started on the plan, singing out their deepest homesick in the heart to the universe. Just maybe one day they can also touch some earthlings' heart.

With all of the saying, we still have this proposition to add on: No matter how unfriendly this world we live in is, we will always keep this silly obsession with music. Even if there's no one left on this world cares, we would still keep on singing them.


'Martians Stuck on Earth' is the first album of Insomnimal Planet. In the album they kept their grayscale color and progressive music style as always used to be. Welcome aboard! Let's experience this 50 minutes long life of a martian together!
