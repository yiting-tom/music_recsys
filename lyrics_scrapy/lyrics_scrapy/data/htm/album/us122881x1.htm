2010 two CD collection of fantastic rockin' Doo Wop workouts and ballads from one of the greatest groups of the era: The Cadillacs! This is the first time that the A and B sides of all the group's singles have been collected together and features such classic cuts as 'Speedo' and 'Gloria'. Fans of Doo Wop and especially The Coasters will enjoy this selection of witty songs from this fantastic vocal group. 43 tracks. Jasmine.
