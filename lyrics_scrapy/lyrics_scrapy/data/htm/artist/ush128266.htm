Akdong Musician( AKMU )【 6 albums 29 lyrics 】
Akdong Musician (Korean: 악동뮤지션, also known as AKMU) is a South Korean duo who participated on K-pop Star 2 in 2012 and debuted under YG Entertainment in 2014 after they won the second installment of the K-pop Star series. The duo consists of siblings Lee Chan-hyuk and Lee Su-hyun.

The group's 2014 debut album Play was an well received by the public and critics, having sold over than 6,9 million total downloads, as well as being awarded Best Pop Album in the 2014 Korean Music Awards. The follow up albums Spring (2016) and Winter (2017) furthered their success. As of March 2017, the duo has sold over 20 million digital songs in South Korea.

Members

Lee Chan-hyuk (Hangul: 이찬혁; Hanja: 李燦赫), born September 12, 1996 is the lead vocalist and rapper. He is also an acoustic guitarist and songwriter.
Lee Su-hyun (Hangul: 이수현; Hanja: 李秀賢), born May 4, 1999 is the main vocalist.
