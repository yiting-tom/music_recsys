Album Intro：

In just three short years, Hedley's tireless work ethic, massive hit songs, goofball personalities and dedication to its fans have made the rock band one of Canada's biggest new success stories.

The Vancouver rockers have two platinum-plus albums, multiple No. 1 radio hits, six Juno nominations (Canada's Grammy Awards), seven consecutive No. 1 videos, five Much Music Video Awards, 2 million + MySpace song plays, and sold out headlining tours from coast-to-coast. They even landed the coveted opening spot for Bon Jovi's national arena tour, putting them in front of 200,000 people.

Now, with Hedley's first-ever international release, titled Never Too Late, the rest of the world will get to hear what Canada has long known - that there has not been a front man more charismatic than Jacob Hoggard since Green Day's Billie Joe came on the scene or a band with a knack for penning catchy can't-get-them-out-of-our-heads rock songs and ballads with lyrics that are unique, cool and heartfelt.
