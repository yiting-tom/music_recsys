Album Intro：

2007 reissue of the debut album from one of the most over-looked American bands of the '60s now including 10 bonus tracks!. The Remains' long overdue reissue is released to coincide with the 40th Anniversary of the 'Summer Of Love'. Originally issued in 1966, The Remains toured with The Beatles during their final U.S. visit that year and continued to excite audiences with their hard-edged Rock sound for the next two years. 20 tracks.
