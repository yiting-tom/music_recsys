Album Intro：

Deluxe three disc (two CDs + NTSC/Region 0 DVD) edition including the original 1989 album plus bonus tracks and a bonus DVD. This deluxe expanded edition features all the non-album b-sides, the Sidewalking EP, outtakes, BBC Radio Sessions, live tracks and rarities such as the 1988 re-record of early b-side 'Just Out Of Reach'. The DVD features the promo videos for all the singles, the band-made film for Just Out Of Reach and previously unreleased archival TV appearances, including two complete live performances from SNUB TV, an interview at their rehearsal studio for Rapido, and two MTV features, including an entertainingly confrontational interview with the Reid brothers by a hapless VJ. Edsel.
