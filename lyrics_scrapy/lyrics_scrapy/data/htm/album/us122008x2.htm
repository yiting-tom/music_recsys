Album Intro：

For over 13 years The Jealous Sound have been inspiring fans and bands with their unique blend of indie and alternative rock. Rise Records is proud to re-release A Gentle Reminder. The new deluxe digipak version includes 4 bonus tracks including the 2008 Got Friends EP. The double vinyl LP version also includes 2 remixes of the song 'Got Friends.' One of the remixes created by Jimmy Tamborello of the Postal Service. A Gentle Reminder was recorded by John 'Lou' Lousteau in the Foo Fighter's 606 Studios.
