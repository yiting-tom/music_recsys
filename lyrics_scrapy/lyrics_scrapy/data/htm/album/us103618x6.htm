Album Intro：

Special edition of French vocalist Vanessa Paradis' 2007 release, her first album in seven years. Best known in the U.S. as Johnny Depp's long-time girlfriend, Paradis is too talented of a singer to be a footnote in someone else's career and this album proves it. Divinidylle is a mature, seductive leap forward for Paradis sounding unlike anything else she's recorded yet containing many elements of her prior releases. Features collaborations with M, Patrice Renson, Oliver Lude, Albin de la Simone and Jerome Goldet...Franck Monnet, Didier Golemanas, Jean Fauque, Thomas Fersen, Marcel Kanche, Brigitte Fontaine and Alain Chamfort. Universal.
