Album Intro：

2012 sophomore solo release from the Hip Hop star. For more than a decade and a half, Vinnie Paz has been a mainstay at the top of the independent hip-hop scene. He is the frontman of both Jedi Mind Tricks and Army of the Pharaohs and one half of the Heavy Metal Kings duo with Ill Bill. In 2010, he stepped out as a solo artist with his album, Season of the Assassin which featured the popular track, End Of Days. God of the Serengeti features guest appearances by Scarface, Mobb Deep, Immortal Technique, La Coka Nostra, Army of the Pharaohs, Kool G. Rap, R.A. the Rugged Man and more with production by DJ Premier, Havoc, Psycho Les (The Beatnuts), C-Lance and others. The 18-track album promises to add another chapter to the growing legacy of Vinnie Paz's uncompromising brand of hardcore hip-hop.
