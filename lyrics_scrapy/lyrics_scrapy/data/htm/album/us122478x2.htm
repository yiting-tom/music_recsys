Album Intro：

Special two CD edition of the Irish Blues rocker's 2009 sophomore release includes a bonus 14 track live disc. With great musicanship and songwriting, the band belie their youth to convincingly channel the spirits of '70s Hard Rock bands like Led Zeppelin, Deep Purple and Thin Lizzy. Their defiantly retro groove-based songs, wailing slide solos and powerful vocals have earned them legions of fans as well as accolades from the press, their peers and the bands who influenced them. 25 tracks total
