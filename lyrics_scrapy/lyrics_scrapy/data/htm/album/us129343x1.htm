Album Intro：

Shawn Camp originally signed to Reprise Records in 1993 as a recording artist. Camp charted two minor singles on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks charts that year. Prior to being signed, he worked in the backing bands of such acts as the Osborne Brothers, Alan Jackson, Suzy Bogguss and Trisha Yearwood. Since then, he has found success as a songwriter. Camp has continued to write hit songs that have appeared on the Billboard U.S. Hot Country Songs charts such as, 'Would You Go with Me' by Josh Turner, which peaked at No. 1, and 'Nobody But Me' by Blake Shelton, which peaked at No. 4. Camp also co-wrote Turner's 2007 single 'Firecracker.' His most recent hit includes the No. 1 hit George Strait single 'River of Love' which he performed with Strait at the 2008 CMA Awards.
Originally released on 10/22/93.
