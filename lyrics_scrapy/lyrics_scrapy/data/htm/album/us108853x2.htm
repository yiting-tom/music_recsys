Album Intro：

2012 album from the Sacramento-based Hardcore Punk band. This is the band's first release on Odd Future Records, making them the first act not part of the Odd Future Hip Hop collective and the first non Hip Hop artist to sign to the label. Features the first single 'F.E.B.N.'
