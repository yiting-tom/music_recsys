Album Intro：

Dirty King' their most adventurous album to date finds the band teamed with producer-engineer SylviaMassy (Red Hot Chili Peppers, Tool) at her Weed, California based Radio Star Studios. The band's incendiary live shows became the calling card by which they were known, and in short order,musical heroes Ian Astbury of the Cult and Cyndi Lauper hand-picked the band for their respective tours.
