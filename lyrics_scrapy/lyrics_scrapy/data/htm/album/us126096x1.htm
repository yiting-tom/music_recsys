Album Intro：

For the last three years, Theoretical Girl has been busy making waves and leaving a trail of swooning hearts in her wake, and finally she is set to release her highly anticipated 2009 debut album. Recorded last autumn in North London, Divided's 12 tales of unrequited love, mistakes and regrets are juxtaposed against twisting melodies and grooves, as evidenced on features recent singles 'Rivals' and 'Red Mist'. Memphis Industries.
