Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of this 1989 album including four bonus tracks. S'Express were formed by DJ/remixer Mark Moore and were one of the most successful exponents of the emergent Acid House scene. Assisted by Pascal Gabriel, S'Express utilized the increasing use of samples and beats to create a sound that was both popular and fresh. Original Soundtrack is the high point of the band's commercial and creative output. Contains a comprehensive booklet featuring sleeve notes and a full UK discography including many of the band's sleeves. Cherry Pop. 2010.
