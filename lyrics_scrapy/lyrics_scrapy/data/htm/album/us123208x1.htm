Album Intro：

Seattle-based band The Dead Science have created a majestic, ornate and soaring collection of post-punk avant-pop on this, their fourth full-length album, and their first for CONSTELLATION. Together the trio unleash a truly unique sound, intensely literate in both word and sound, with a controlled and commanding fury. The songs on 'Villainaire' range from epic orchestral pop to sinister jazz-rock to weary, soul-bleached balladry.
