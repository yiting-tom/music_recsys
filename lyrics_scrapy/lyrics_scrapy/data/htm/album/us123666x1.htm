Album Intro：

With a 50-year, two-generation career in show business, The Gaylords are a very popular pre-rock vocal trio. This is the most complete collection of The Gaylords work, 54 tracks, including 11 of Ronnie Gaylord's solo work. Including all their charting hits, Tell Me You're Mine, 'Ramona,' 'Spinning a Web,' 'The Strings of My Heart' and 'From the Vine Came The Grape . Extensive liner notes including an interview with the founding member Burt Bonaldi.
