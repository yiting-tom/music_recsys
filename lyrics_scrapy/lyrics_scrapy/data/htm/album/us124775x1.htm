Album Intro：

The Popguns blazed a trail in the early nineties, preparing the way for the likes of sleeper and echo belly. They managed indie chart success with Landslide , Bye Bye Baby and Lightning , but never broke into the national charts. A mid price CD. The album has been unavailable for a while and is being re-promoted due to demand. The album will be re-serviced to the music press and suitable websites.
