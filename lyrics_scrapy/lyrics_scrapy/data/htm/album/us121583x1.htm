Album Intro：

2010 solo CD EP from the former Slits member. Viv is returning to music after a break of 25 years. Her first band, The Flowers of Romance, was formed with her flatmate Sid Vicious. Sid went on to The Sex Pistols and Viv to become guitarist and main songwriter with The Slits. Viv's songs are cute and dark with personal, almost confessional lyrics. The result is beautifully uncomplicated, stripped down and raw. The Flesh EP contains four tracks: 'Never Come', 'I Don't Believe/In Love', 'If Love' and 'The False Heart'.
