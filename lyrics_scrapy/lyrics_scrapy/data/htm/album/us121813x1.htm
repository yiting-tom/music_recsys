Album Intro：

Take It Back's sophomore album Atrocities is a natural progression from their debut album Can t Fight Robots (2008, Facedown). As seen on their Rumors Of Revolt limited edition EP that was released and sold out in Aug. 09, The band seamlessly blends hardcore and punk rock music and ideals with catchy choruses and anthemic sing-a-longs. Take It Back! have toured the US several times and appeared at numerous festivals nationwide and were hailed as one of Alternative Press s 100 Bands To Know in 2009.
