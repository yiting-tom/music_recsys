Album Intro：

Survival Story is the third studio album (counting 2001's independently released Onomatopoeia) by the Flobots, released on March 16, 2010.It is the follow-up to 2007's highly successful Fight with Tools. Work on the album began in 2009, and it was produced by Mario Caldato, Jr. (of Beastie Boys fame).
