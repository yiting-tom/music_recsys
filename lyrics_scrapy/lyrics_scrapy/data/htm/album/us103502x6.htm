Album Intro：

Gainesville, FL's Sister Hazel returns will an all new album, RELEASE. RELEASE features 12 brand new songs, written or co-written by all band members, including co-writes with people such as Pat McGee and Stan Lynch (Tom Petty). With over 2 million albums sold, a devoted fan base, and strong touring, Sister Hazel's RELEASE is the next step in the band's career. RELEASE features the tracks 'Take A Bow', 'Believe In Me', and the soon to be fan favorite title track, 'Release'. On tour now.......and always!
