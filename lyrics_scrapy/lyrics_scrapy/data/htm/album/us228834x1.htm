Album Intro：

繼A-Mac新專輯《英雄本色》後第二支先行單曲《Faded》，這首歌邀請到兩位來自成都的“寶藏男孩”——COIN和Step.Jad，一位擁有性感的磁性嗓音，洋氣的韻腳，另一位是擁有極高辨識度音色的靈魂歌手，三人在一起產生的化學反應，是未知的，無法預測的。
Faded是一首很用心的歌曲，描述的是在奮鬥的路上，很遺憾有些朋友漸行漸遠，但希望彼此的感情不要褪色。

Following A-Mac's new album 'A Better Tomorrow', the second single 'Faded' featured two ”treasure boys' from Chengdu - COIN and Step. Jad - one with a sexy magnetic voice and chic rhythm scheme, another with an easily-recognizable voice that can go straight into your soul.
The chemical reaction among them three will produce something unpredictably beautiful. Lots of efforts were put into Faded to leave a message: it is inevitable that some friends are parting ways on the pursuit of happiness, but at least the feelings for each other would not fade.
