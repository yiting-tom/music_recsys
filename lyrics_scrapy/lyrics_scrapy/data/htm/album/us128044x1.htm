Album Intro：

2006 reissue of this long-lost 1985 debut album from former Classix Nouveaux vocalist, including five bonus tracks: 'Adoramus Te', 'Just A Feeling', 'How Was I To Know' and the previously unreleased 'Metanoia' and 'Drift Away'. Recorded after the demise of his band (although they are featured on one track) but before he began his career as a religious artist, this is a warm and inspiring album featuring keyboards, acoustic guitars, a choir and great songwriting. Cherry Red.
