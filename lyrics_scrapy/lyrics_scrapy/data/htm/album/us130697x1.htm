Album Intro：

He Actually Started Out as a Folk Singer (He Actually Opened Shows for as Frank Zappa, Jay and the Americans, Mitch Ryder and the Detroit Wheels, the Byrds, and the Lovin' Spoonful), but the Acting Bug Bit and He Landed a Plum Role on the Short Lived Series 'Here Come the Brides'. A Few Years Later, He Hit Big on the 'Starsky and Hutch' Series. Capitalizing on his Fame, He Decided to Start a Pop Singing Career and Hit the Charts with 'Don't Give Up on Us' and Recorded Five Albums after That. This Disc Collates the Best of Those Recordings.
