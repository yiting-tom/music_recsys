Album Intro：

Katrina's last album, 2003's Untucked, contained four hit singles that spent a combined year and a half on the Billboard and R&R Adult Contemporary charts. Here And Now was recorded in Los Angeles with producer Ron Aniello, best known for his work with Guster, the Barenaked Ladies, Lifehouse and Jars of Clay. The first single is a cover of the Howard Jones hit 'No One Is To Blame' featuring Howard on vocals and piano.
