Album Intro：

2012 debut album from the melodically brutal Metal sextet from Bresica, Italy. Upon This Dawning has been honing their brand of metal, touring incessantly in Italy. The band has shared stages with some of the biggest names in the scene including Asking Alexandria, Underoath, Dance Gavin Dance, blessthefall, Piece the Veil, Motionless in White and In Fear and Faith, and is endorsed by Truth Custom Drums.
