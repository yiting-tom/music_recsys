Anne-Marie( Anne Marie )【 14 albums 42 lyrics 】
Anne-Marie Rose Nicholson (born 7 April 1991), known professionally as Anne-Marie, is an English singer and songwriter. She has attained several charting singles on the UK Singles Chart, including Clean Bandit's 'Rockabye', featuring Sean Paul, which peaked at number one, as well as 'Alarm', 'Ciao Adios', 'Friends' and '2002'. Her debut studio album, Speak Your Mind, was released on 27 April 2018.
