Album Intro：

2009 debut album from this outfit led by Bootsy Collins and Buckethead. Whenever the bass legend and guitar shredder extraordinaire get together, it's a given that new sonic ground will be broken. And this certainly proves to be the case on their latest project, Science Faxtion. Also included is singer/guitarist/producer Greg Hampton, and longtime Bootsy/Buckethead ally Brain on drums, as well as contributions by Chris Collier and Bootsy himself on drums. Masscot.
