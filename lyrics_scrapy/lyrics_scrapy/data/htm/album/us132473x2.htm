Album Intro：

Systematic's debut album Somewhere In Between raised eyebrows throughout the world of hard rock as much for its crushing guitar riffs as its focus upon melody and songcraft. The San Francisco East Bay-area four-piece, who honed their live show on tours with Tattoo The Earth, Linkin Park and Union Underground, is now on tour with Godsmack and Staind. The latest contribution by Lars Ulrich's Music Company to the Elektra family, Systematic skillfully balances mainstream sensibility with an underground edge.
