Album Intro：

First time on CD for this 1976 Disco classic which spawned two top UK hits ('Love Me Like A Lover' and the Number 1 'I Love To Love'). A unique voice, 'powerful and piercing' . Charles became a massive artist in Europe. Features eight bonus tracks taken from her singles in the late '60s and early '70s including 'Rich Girl', originally a b-side but has become a huge club favorite. 18 tracks. RPM. 2007.
