Album Intro：

2011 release from the Alt-Country/Gothabilly band. Engaging, riveting, sad and humorous; demanding the audience to participate both live and while listening to their albums, Slim Cessna's Auto Club has been tagged with every musical description possible. This is fitting, as the group describes their music as American (Is that not what America is, a mixture of everything resulting in something new?); and after more than a decade of working and living as Slim Cessna's Auto Club, they have created their own genre. Unentitled is another high-water mark in the Auto Club's career. The nine songs here are each thoughtfully crafted with unique arrangements, executed by superb musicianship and fully realized via original, insightful and intelligent lyrics.
