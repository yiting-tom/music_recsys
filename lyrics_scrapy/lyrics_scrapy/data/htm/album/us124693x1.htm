Album Intro：

A few years ago, BMG s Buddah imprint embarked on an ambitious reissue campaign for the Partridge Family album catalog, but they stopped short of releasing the last Partridge Family album, 'Bulletin Board'. Which, naturally caused the group s fans to beseech us here at Collectors Choice Music to do something about it! And do something we have, reissuing the entire original album with new notes and two bonus tracks, both sides of the Bones Howe-produced Shirley Jones single Ain t Love Easy/Roses in the Snow from 1972. And speaking of Shirley, she s not actually on Bulletin Board; it was David Cassidy s show now, and under his direction the album took a decidedly more adult turn than the Family s previous efforts, which probably explains why it escaped reissue till now, and also why the now grown-up fans of the show want to hear it so badly! A Collectors Choice Music exclusive!
