Album Intro：

The Maccabees return next month with 'Wall Of Arms' and it's an

expansive, epic-sounding follow-up to their debut, 'Colour It In'. Over its eleven tracks, 'Wall Of Arms' is a thrilling fusion of indie

anthems, gentle lullabies and dark, stormy swirls, the quintet are not afraid to sound vulnerable, forceful and plain loveable at the flick of a guitar string.
