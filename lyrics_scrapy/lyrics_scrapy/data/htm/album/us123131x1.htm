Album Intro：

While many bands today have a strong focus on sacrilegious and blasphemous matters with an obsession for gore, The Crimson Armada look to be an exception to this rule by promoting a powerful message through their music and meaningful lyrics that go beyond the trend of broken relationships or pure brutality. Their debut release Conviction is backed by new management at the Artery Foundation and new label home at Artery Recordings. Produced by Brian Hood (A Plea for Purging), the album features the guests Levi Benton of Miss May I as well as Andy Adkins of A Plea For Purging.
Look for the Crimson Armada s own blend of uplifting lyrics, powerful melodies and heavy riffs on Conviction this June.
