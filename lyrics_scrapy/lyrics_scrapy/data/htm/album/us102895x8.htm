Album Intro：

Monuments and Melodies is a two-CD compilation album by Incubus which will be released on June 16, 2009 through Epic Records. The first disc will feature 13 previously released singles from the albums Make Yourself, Morning View, A Crow Left of the Murder..., and Light Grenades, along with two new Brendan O'Brien-produced tracks, 'Black Heart Inertia' and 'Midnight Swim'. The second disc will be a collection of rarities, including B-sides, soundtrack cuts, alternate versions, three previously unreleased songs, and a cover of Prince's 'Let's Go Crazy'. The album takes its title from a song that was originally released as the B-side to 'Megalomaniac' and as a bonus track on international releases of A Crow Left of the Murder...; it is also included on Disc 2 of this album. The album art was drawn by vocalist Brandon Boyd.
