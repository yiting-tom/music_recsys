Lights( Lights Valerie Poxleitner )【 16 albums 145 lyrics 】
Lights (born Valerie Anne Poxleitner; April 11, 1987) is a Canadian musician, singer, and songwriter. She is known for her gold-certified albums The Listening and Siberia as well as singles 'Drive My Soul', 'February Air', 'Ice', 'Second Go', 'Toes', and 'Up We Go'. She released her fourth studio album, Skin & Earth, in 2017.

Lights was awarded the 2009 Juno Award for New Artist of the Year. and two 2009 Astral Media Radio awards during Canadian Music Week.
