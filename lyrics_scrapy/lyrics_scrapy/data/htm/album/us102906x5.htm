Album Intro：

2009 collection from the Motown/R&B legends that uncovers gems from the vault that reflect an extraordinary history of one of the greatest groups of all time. This release includes superb quality unreleased tracks as well as unique alternate versions of well-known hits. Stunning vocals from Michael Jackson from age 10 through age 16. This release includes the first-time appearance of Stevie Wonder's 'Buttercup', announced as a J5 single in 1973 but never released. 12 tracks.
