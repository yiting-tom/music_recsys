Album Intro：

『North and South』 is the fourth studio album from Belgian singer-songwriter Milow and has been released on April 1, 2011. The album was produced by Jo Francken. The album was recorded in Belgium and partially mixed in Los Angeles by two-time Grammy-winner Tony Maserati (Beyonce Knowles, Jason Mraz, Robbie Robertson). The mastering was done in New York City by Dave Kutch (The Roots, Erykah Badu, Outkast).
