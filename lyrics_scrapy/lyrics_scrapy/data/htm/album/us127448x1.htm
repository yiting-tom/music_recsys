Album Intro：

Deluxe edition features five bonus tracks. 2011 release from the Grammy nominated, Dove Award and Stellar Award winning Gospel act, their first album as a duo. Trin-i-tee 5:7 had been the Best Selling Female Gospel Trio in music history. After more than a decade on the music scene, the New Orleans natives and original group members, Chanelle Haynes and Angel Taylor have transitioned Trin-i-tee 5:7 from a trio to a duo. Highly acclaimed and award winning producers such as Rockwilder, PJ Morton (India Arie), Stan Jones (Marvin Sapp, Brian Courtney Wilson), David Mullen (Nicole C. Mullen), Fred Jerkins & Darkchild (Brandy) and Sure Fire (Ne-Yo) contributed to making an astounding album
