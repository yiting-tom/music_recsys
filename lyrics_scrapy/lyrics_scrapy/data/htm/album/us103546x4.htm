Album Intro：

2012 two CD set containing the Kansas City Hip Hop star's E.B.A.H and Boiling Point EPs, which were previously released as digital only titles. E.B.A.H. ( Evil Brain Angel Heart) is an explanation of inner mind of the guy they call Dr. Strange. Always on his own thing and being judged for it. On Boiling Point, Tech N9ne bares the dark side of his soul yet again - showing you what makes him truly inside out. Tech N9ne just keeps creating - feeding the fans more and more music every chance he gets. Boiling Point is sure to be a fan favorite.
