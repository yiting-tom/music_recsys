Album Intro：

With their colliding music styling and experimental vocals, This Romantic Tragedy doesn't seek to stand out of the crowd; they tower above it. Following up their previous release Trust In Fear, This Romantic Tragedy releases Reborn an album that will not hold back. The definitive experimental screamo record of 2011 is now here.
