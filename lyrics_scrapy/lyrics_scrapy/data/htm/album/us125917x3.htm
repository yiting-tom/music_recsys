Album Intro：

Always shrouded in mystery and dressed in black, this once octet known for their signature sound and live shows and compared a million times to the Velvet Underground have always done the unexpected. The new trimmed-down line-up, once known for taking long, meticulous recording sessions into hell has banged out this new record within weeks. Recorded by Rod Cervera (Weezer, Lavender Diamond, Young People, Silversun Pickups).
