Album Intro：

'An absolute must-have.' -- Echo Weekly Led by college radio legend/celebrity marauder Nardwuar the Human Serviette, The Evaporators sling out one wildly infectious tune after another, luring listeners into their frenetic and slightly surreal world. Named after Vancouver pioneer Captain John Deighton, who got the nickname 'Gassy' because he talked nonstop, The Evaporators' new release attempts to school the listener on such topics as how to shake with the Shaggy Shaker, how to do the Eggbeater, and why it's important to care about E.J. Hughes and Evatone Soundsheets!
