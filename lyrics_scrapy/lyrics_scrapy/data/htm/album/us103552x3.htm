Album Intro：

This 1996 album brought Terrorvision to a new level in terms of fame and musicianship. It was produced by Gil Norton and is far more polished and professional in sound to the band's two previous outings. A hint of early 90's grunge is still evident on this release but the album is packed with accessible pop-rock. is packed with 'singles' ('Easy', 'Hide The Dead Girl', 'Perseverance' etc). EMI.
