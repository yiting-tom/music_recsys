Album Intro：

Thyrane: Blastmor (vocals, guitar, rums); Avatmer (guitar); Mannamari (synthesizer); Daemon (bass) Thyrane was formed in 1994 when Blastmor, the founding member of the band and two other guys began playing their first chords of blasphemy. This is their fifth album on the Spinefarm label. Their sound is as sharp and meticulously crafted as the finest razorblade and as destructive as an air bubble in your veins. 2003.
