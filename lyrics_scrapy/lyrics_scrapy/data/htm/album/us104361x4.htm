Album Intro：

'L-O-V-E' is a jazz song written by Milt Gabler and Bert Kaempfert for American singer-pianist Nat King Cole's 1965 album L-O-V-E (the last album released before his death on February 15, 1965). In 2007, English soul singer Joss Stone covered the song for the Joe Wright-directed advertising campaign for Chanel's Coco Mademoiselle fragrance. Stone's version was released as a digital single on September 18.
