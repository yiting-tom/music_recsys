Album Intro：

Reissue of the 1986 debut album by this cult English alternative rock group signed to Beggar's Banquet (issued on the I.R.S. label in the U.S. at the time). Out of print for years, this album has been highly sought after by fans & collectors. This is the U.K. edition, which contains nine of the cuts found on the deleted American CD, plus two left off of it, 'Waspy' & 'A Funny Thing'. Standard jewelcase.
