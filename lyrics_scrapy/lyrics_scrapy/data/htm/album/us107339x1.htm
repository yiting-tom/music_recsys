Album Intro：

Enter Shikari is a British post-hardcore band, formed in 2003 in St. Albans, Hertfordshire, which is where the band members are from. Their most notable feature is their unique blend of post-hardcore and trance, as well as Rou Reynold's signature voice. The band is named after the boat of one of the band member's relatives, and a character in a play Rou wrote before forming the band, both of which are named Shikari[citation needed]. Shikari also means 'Hunter', in Persian, Hindi, Nepali, Urdu, Punjabi and Bengali. Their debut album, Take to the Skies was released on March 19, 2007 and reached #4 in the Official UK Album Chart. The album was later certified Gold.

Common Dreads is scheduled to be Enter Shikari's second studio album, and is set to be released on June 15th, 2009 and June 16th for the US. Recording of the album began in September 2008 and ended in late February 2009. The album has recently leaked onto the Internet.
