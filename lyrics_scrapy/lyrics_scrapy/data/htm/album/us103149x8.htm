Album Intro：

Digitally remastered edition of this 2009 collection from the British Pop trio. London Conversations is the definitive best of collection by Saint Etienne--one of Britains's most enduring and endearing bands of recent times. Often described as a '60s-inspired '90s Dance band, Saint Etienne encompassed many influences and delivered sublime and soulful pop songs. Underrated but critically acclaimed, this timely compilation highlights the band's contribution to modern British Pop.
