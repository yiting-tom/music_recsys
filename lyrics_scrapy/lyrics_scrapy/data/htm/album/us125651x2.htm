Album Intro：

After touring non-stop for three consecutive years, Flint, Michigan's very own The Swellers are now set to release their major label debut 'Ups And Downsizing' on Paper + Plastick/Fueled By Ramen this fall. The acclaimed group best known for their merging of melodic hooks and hard-edged punk power caught the attention of Fueled By Ramen co-founder (and Less Than Jake drummer) Vinnie Fiorello at one of their many jam packed club shows. Fiorello then brought the group to John Janick (Fueled By Ramen's Co-Founder/CEO) who signed the band instantly.
