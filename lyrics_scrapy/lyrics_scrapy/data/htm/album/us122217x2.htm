Album Intro：

2009 sophomore release from the Christian rockers. Terra Terra Terra is a Lakeland, FL native band that has been working the Central Florida music scene for all it's worth. In the past two years they have concentrated their full efforts into making music that has style, depth, and mainstream appeal. In 2007 Terra released its first self-titled album which was quite well received. Soon after, those who were exposed to their music began to get a good vibe, and they made it clear that they wanted more. Thus, Terra made the choice to pursue music as fully as humanly possible. The road traveled was not easy, but through many trials, the boys have finally achieved the first of many steps they would like to take.
