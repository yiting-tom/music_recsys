Album Intro：

Whitby, Ontario is perhaps best known, musically speaking, for churning out punk by numbers bands in abundance, but one could never lump The Mark Inside with their regional compatriots. This feral rock machine has been hard at work honing their craft and shaping a brand of rock n'roll that ranks with the greats. In the way of all great rock bands, they have worked through their influences to find their own clear singular voice.
