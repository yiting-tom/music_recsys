Album Intro：

After a string of releases in Japan Secret returns to K-pop with their first Korean release of the year. Fusing saxophone sounds with hip-hop beats their slinky new title song Poison is again the work of Kang Ji Won and Kim Ki Bum who crafted all of Secret's previous hit singles. Secret's third Korean mini-album also features new tracks like Telepathy and Calling U.
