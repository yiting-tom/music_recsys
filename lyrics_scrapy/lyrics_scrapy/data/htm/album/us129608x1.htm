Album Intro：

This record bristles with frantic energy and blistering tones. Producer/artist extraordinaire SpankRock lends his rapping/vocals to the slickest album track, 'Kick Drum'. Impressed with their unique sound, fashion maven Stella McCartney requested the band to be the performers for her segment in the Fashion Rocks TV special that was broadcast throughout Europe to over 60 million viewers. Look for them on a US tour in May, and in European festivals this summer.
