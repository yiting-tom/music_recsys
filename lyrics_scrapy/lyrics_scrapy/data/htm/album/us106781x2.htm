Album Intro：

2009 release. Redemption. Paranoia. Guilt. And brief glimpses of a better tomorrow, all cloaked in Pop hooks that truly help the medicine go down. What is this? Another lost LP from Brian Wilson's sandbox phase? If only things were that simple. What we're really alluding to here is Passion Pit's first proper full-length, a bird-flipping break from the blogosphere by a 21 year old with much more on his manic mind than girls, girls, girls. You know, important matters, like the end of the world and learning to love someone other than the man in the mirror.
