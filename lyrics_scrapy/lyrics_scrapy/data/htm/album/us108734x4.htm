Album Intro：

Limited Edition double disc (CD + PAL/Region 0 DVD) pressing of the Helsinki Goth Rockers' 2007 album features a bonus DVD that contains The video clip to 'Perfect Skin', a short film about 69 Eyes and a live performance of 'I Just Want To Have Something To Do'. Angels is the band's most cohesive album today, combining the darkness of Sisters Of Mercy with the high-flying metallic sheen of Nightwish. Virgin.
