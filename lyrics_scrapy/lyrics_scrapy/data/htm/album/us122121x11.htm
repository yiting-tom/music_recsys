Album Intro：

The second full length from Television Personalities, also released in1981, is more psychedelic than the first, but still with plenty of dry and acerbic wit. Featuring the classic title track and and acid rock drone of 'David Hockney's Diaries,' a style and sound that Daniel Treacy would explore further on subsequent records. Eccentric post-punk and pop from one the UK's most original bands, reissued on180 gram vinyl.
