Album Intro：

The 2005 special deluxe edition re-issue of Razed In Black s devastating debut album!

Features killer industrial and dark wave tracks that have become club favorites such as Cyberium, Black and Just Let It Go!

Includes all new artwork PLUS 3 ultra rare bonus tracks, two of which were never released, recorded during the same time as Shrieks but not included on the album...until now!

Razed In Black has developed a large and devout following all across the U.S., especially on the West Coast, with numerous national tours!


Previously released in 1996, Shrieks Laments, and Anguished Cries is the first official full length album by Hawaii's own RAZED IN BLACK. Formerly known in the islands as Lost Souls, solo composer Romell Regulacion created quite an impact in Hawaii's alternative music scene. During the era in which bands like Nine Inch Nails, Ministry and KMFDM were in prime underground highlight, Lost Souls fit comfortably alongside, quickly gaining popularity. With tremendous local radio support, concerts and the preferred opening act of national bands who toured the islands (Filter, Tool, etc.), Lost Souls caught the eye of Los Angeles' gothic-industrial mecca label Cleopatra Records, who signed the artist as RAZED IN BLACK after discovering other bands with the Lost Souls name. Since then, the band have produced a handful of impressive albums, each progressively taking electronic music to the next level. With guest appearances as producer, remixer or performer on more than a hundred albums, RAZED IN BLACK's popularity continues to grow tremendously as their worldwide exposure continues.

Remastered and repackaged, we proudly represent Shrieks, Laments, and Anguished Cries with three previously unreleased bonus tracks from that early Razed In Black era. Listen, feel...experience where it all began! --Cleopatra Records
