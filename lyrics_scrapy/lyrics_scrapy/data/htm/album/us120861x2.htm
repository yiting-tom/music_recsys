Album Intro：

In 2008 UNK unleashes his second album, Second Season. Lacing the entire album with his signature chart topping sound once again is Big Oomp's resident hit-making machine DJ Montay (who produced Flo Rida/ T-Pain's 'Low,' Three 6 Mafia 'I'd Rather' and many more). The album features appearances from Sean Kingston, Ray J, Three 6 Mafia and more. Since the release of his debut album Beat'n Down Yo Block in October 2006, UNK catapulted into the spotlight as one of Atlanta's brightest new talents. With the inescapable hit 'Walk It Out,' a #1 song at Urban radio nationwide, and a Platinum certified single and ringtone, UNK quickly became a forced to be reckoned with.
