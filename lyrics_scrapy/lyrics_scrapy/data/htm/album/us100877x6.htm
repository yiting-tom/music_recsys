Album Intro：

Australian exclusive compilation album hot of the heels of the dance/pop diva's performance at the Sydney Olympics. Includes her duet with Latin superstar Marc Anthony, 'I Want ToSpend My Lifetime Loving You' along with many of her U.S. and French hits as well as some previously unreleased tracks.Packaged in a limited edition 'souvenirs' slipcase. Includesthe cd-rom videos for, 'Aller Plus Haut', 'Les Trois Cloches' & 'If I Was A River'. 2000 release.
