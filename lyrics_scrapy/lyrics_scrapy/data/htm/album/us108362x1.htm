Album Intro：

Twilight Theater is the fourth studio album by the Finnish rock band Poets of the Fall. It was released in Finland as well as on iTunes on 17 March 2010.Record Shop X allowed listening to the complete album online for free until 24 March.In spite of the free online version throughout its first week of release, the record went gold in its first week after jumping up to #1 in the Finnish album charts.
