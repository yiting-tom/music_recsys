Album Intro：

Southern Pacific was a country group bordering on the fringes of rock music. They made numerous albums for Warner Bros. Records through the 80's & 90's. We have paired their self-titled first album Southern Pacific from 1985 with their 1988 release Zuma fitting them nicely onto one CD. Wounded Bird. 2003.
