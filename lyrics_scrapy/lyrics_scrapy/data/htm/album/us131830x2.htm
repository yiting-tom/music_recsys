Album Intro：

Nearly a year in the making and 15 years after the original release of 'Atonement' on CD and cassette by Tooth & Nail Records, Blood & Ink Records offers this classic release on vinyl for the first time ever. Alongside fellow South Floridians and friends Shai Hulud, Strongarm forged an entirely new take on hardcore, combining the genre's power with more technical and melodic elements unlike any had done before. For fans of Shai Hulud, Poison The Well, Saints Never Surrender and Dependency. Limited edition of 400 copies.
