Album Intro：

The Black & Roll Brigade known as Vreid has built their sound on a unique blend of Norwegian Black Metal & Seventies Rock. On their fifth album V, this is still their formula, but now their exotic brew also has the flavor of the guitar based metal from the mid 80`s to mid 90`s era. The past illuminates Vreid`s present & future, and with the new album they are reunited with guitar maestro «Strom» from the Windir & Ulcus days (former Guitarist Ese left the band in late 2009). Their nine darkest, heaviest and most complex songs to date are wrapped in a powerful & crystal clear production provided by songwriter & bass player Hváll.
