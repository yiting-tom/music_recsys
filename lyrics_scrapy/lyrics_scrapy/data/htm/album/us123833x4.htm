Album Intro：

This EP consists of the early versions of the songs that became Mississauga Goddamn. These songs are unrefined, clearly unfinished, and stunning in their raw earnestness. This EP just goes to show that talented musicians and lyricists don't need hi-fi recording studio fine-tuning to make breathtaking music.
