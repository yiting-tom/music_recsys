Album Intro：

A long awaited repromotion of the band's final album, the much acclaimed 'Wooden Foot Cops On The Highway'. It was originally released by Rough Trade Records in 1988. The Woodentops, hailing from Northampton were very respected by the press and public alike for their unique brand of infectious pop songs and relentless rhythms. This album was co-produced by The Woodentops singer and writer, Rolo and former REM producer, Scott Litt. The CD features the 9 original tracks from the 'Wooden Foot Cops On The Highway' album including the single 'You Make Me Feel' plus the bonus tracks, 'Get It On', 'Why' and the extended mix of the single 'You Make Me Feel (Late Night Mix)' The CD booklet includes all the original artwork and a complete discography of the band incorporating all of the band's original sleeves by the legendary Panni Charrington.
