Album Intro：

2009 two CD collection from the Christian Alt-Rockers. Ghosts Of The Past features 31 tracks of covers, b-sides, demos, and non-album versions of songs. Jason Martin's haunting vocal display perfectly accompanies the atmospheric mood set by the dreamy guitar lines, creating a sort of glimmering magic that could only come from one so gifted. Over 10 albums deep, over 15 years time, from the early guitar-heavy Shoegazing era to the ethereal moodiness of The Fashion Focus years to the latest incarnations and recreations of themselves as keyboard-driven masters of chorus, this is and always will be a band who will deliver great songs with new sounds. Ghosts Of The Past stands out as the perfect addition to a Starflyer 59 fan's collection, and also a perfect starting point for someone interested in diving into the bands impressive and extensive catalog.
