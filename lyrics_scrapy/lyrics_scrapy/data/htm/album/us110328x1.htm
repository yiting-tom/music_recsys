Album Intro：

Cristian Castro announces the release of his upcoming album 'Mi Amigo El Principe' for the November 1st

'Mi Amigo El Principe' is the second installment of his tribute album to the Prince of Song, Jose Jose

The first single is 'Lo Dudo' one of the biggest hits in the career of the Prince of Song, a song that sold more than 3 million copies in 1983 and will be released to radio and online partners all over the U.S. and Latin America on September 27th

'Viva El Principe' is the bestselling Spanish language Latin pop album of 2011 having achieved double platinum sales in the U.S. and Mexico

Now, with ' Mi Amigo El Principe,' Cristian his tribute to his idol
