Album Intro：

A Hundred Million Suns is the fifth album by alternative rock band Snow Patrol. The album will be released in Ireland on October 24, 2008, in the UK and the rest of Europe on October 27, 2008, and in the US on October 28, 2008.The first single from the album will be 'Take Back the City' which will be released on October 10 in Ireland, Germany and Belgium. With a release for the rest of Europe on October 13, 2008 and in France on October 14, 2008.
