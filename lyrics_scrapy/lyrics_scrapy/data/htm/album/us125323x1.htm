Album Intro：

The Melody Tent in Hyannis in Cape Cod has been a celebratory venue for The Saw Doctors for a few years now. Every August we park the bus out the back, enjoy the sunshine and the hospitality and play in the spectacularly theatrical enclosure that is The Melody Tent, completely surrounded by banks of exceptionally enthusiastic and charged-up fans and friends. Often we ve said we should film the show for its unique atmosphere and intensity, and every time we forgot until the bus was parking up again, another twelve months and a hundred gigs past.


Well this year, we ve managed to get our act together and we ve recorded the August 2008 show for posterity.


While mainly making a dvd, we find, of course, that we have the makings of a new live album - a current one with a different line-up from the previous collection we released a few years back. We ve listened through the gig and picked the best performances so here they are as a record of how The Saw Doctors played and sounded across the Atlantic Ocean in the year of our Lord, 2008.
