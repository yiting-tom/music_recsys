Album Intro：

The first time on CD for the one and only album by Racey which was first released by RAK Records in 1979 Includes the hit singles Lay Your Love On Me , Some Girls , Boy Oh Boy , Runaround Sue , Such A Night and Rest Of My Life . This 27 track double CD features every track the band released for RAK Records including the original version of the mega selling 'Micky' by Toni Basil which Racey vocalist Richard Gower had penned as 'Kitty'. The booklet contains original LP artwork, stacks of European only picture sleeves and indepth liner notes by Steve Thorpe. Totally re-mastered by Tim Turan.
