Album Intro：

Superb 6th solo disc from this awesome, outstanding, world-class blues/rock axeslinger from Arkansas. An incredible dynamic mix of blues meets rock with tons of killer, soul-powered heavy guitar power trio grooves. Spinner digs deep on Rollin' & Tumblin' and produces an amazing disc that stands tall in the world of Classic blues/rock guitar magic. Highly recommended to fans of Rory Gallagher, Johnny Winter, Robin Trower, SRV, Hendrix, Eric Clapton, Walter Trout, Warren Haynes, Joe Bonamassa, Philip Sayce, Doyle Bramhall, Jeff Healey, Mato Nanji & Indigenous, Chris Duarte and the Black Crowes.
