Album Intro：

The second full-length album from Vancouver's Veal is self-appointed ''Pixiesquepsychedelia'' - think Dick Dale-meets-Jayhawks surf-splashed country rock. By 2003, Nik Kozub has formed Shout Out Out Out Out and Luke Doucet is well on his guitar-slinging way as a solo artist, but the trio presses pause on these other projects long enough to pull together a powerful collection of ''bent pop'' perfection.
