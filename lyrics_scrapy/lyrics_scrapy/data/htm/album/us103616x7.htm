Album Intro：

Shallow Believer is a digital EP by The Used

The EP is a compilation of b-sides from their first 3 studio albums. Seven of the songs have been previously released as bonus tracks on different album versions. The artwork was done by Bert McCracken and Alex Pardee. The EP gets its title from a lyric in the song 'Slit Your Own Throat'.
