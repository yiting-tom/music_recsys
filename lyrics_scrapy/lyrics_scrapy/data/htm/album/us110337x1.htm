Album Intro：

2011 sophomore release from this Electronic outfit, the brainchild of Joseph Mount. While the blend of modern Electronica and a slight '70s Prog Rock feel may linger from their debut, The English Riviera is a huge leap forwards, taking in breezy West Coast vibes while remaining distinctly British.
