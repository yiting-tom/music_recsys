Album Intro：

二胡被稱為“中國小提琴”，是中國最重要的樂器之一，已有4000多年的歷史，雖然只有兩條琴弦，但它可以傳達廣泛的情感。


The erhu (二胡) has been called the 「Chinese violin」 and is one of the most important Chinese instruments, with a history of over 4,000 years. Though it has only two strings, it can convey a wide range of emotions.
