B.A.P( BAP )【 26 albums 131 lyrics 】
B.A.P (Korean: 비에이피; an acronym for Best Absolute Perfect) is a South Korean boy group formed in 2012 under TS Entertainment. The group's six members are Yongguk, Himchan, Daehyun, Youngjae, Jongup, and Zelo.

B.A.P made their musical debut with the single 'Warrior' on January 26, 2012, followed by the release of their debut EP Warrior in February 2012.

Members

Yongguk (용국), born Bang Yong-guk (방용국) on March 31, 1990 is the group's leader
Himchan (힘찬), born Kim Him-chan (김힘찬) on April 19, 1990
Daehyun (대현), born Jung Dae-hyun (정대현) on June 28, 1993
Youngjae (영재), born Yoo Young-jae (유영재) on January 24, 1994
Jongup (종업), born Moon Jong-up (문종업) on February 6, 1995
Zelo (젤로), born Choi Jun-hong (최준홍) on October 15, 1996
