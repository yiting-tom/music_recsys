Album Intro：

ALL THE SONGS IN THIS COLLECTION WERE RECORDED BETWEEN 1991-1998. THEY HAVE BEEN PREVIOUSLY RELEASED ON VARIOUS COMPILATIONS AND 7 INCHES (INNOCULATOR, BREAKUP, AND 2). THERE IS ONE UNRELEASED SONG CALLED 'LONG DAY' WHICH WAS FOUND IN THE CORNER OF JOSH'S BASEMENT. THIS MAKES FOR A PERFECT COMPILATION OF THE GENESIS OF ONE OF THE MOST IMPORTANT PUNK BANDS OF THE 90'S. ENJOY THEIR HUMBLE BEGINNINGS.
