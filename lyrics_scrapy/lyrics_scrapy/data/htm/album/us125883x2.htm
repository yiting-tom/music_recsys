Album Intro：

The Vision Bleak return to the stage of Horror Metal, more ghoulish and more poetic than ever before on 'Set Sail To Mystery', their 4th full length release. Schwadorf and Konstanz are coming full circle in 2010 as they once again set the tattered, ashen sails of the deathship and head towards midnight, death, and doom. Available in Regular Edition and a Special 2CD Digipak with 7 bonus tracks! Set sails, cast a final glance ashore: whosoever hires on the deathship shall never return again.
