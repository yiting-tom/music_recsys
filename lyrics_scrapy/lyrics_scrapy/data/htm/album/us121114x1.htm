Album Intro：

The album spans a spectrum between the beautiful and moving ode 'Dear Friends' and the hypnotically pulsating tribal electro anthem 'Warriors' featuring Dan Millman. Vargo took care that you'll be in for a deep, touching, exciting and utterly positive experience. Vargo's aim is to communicate, to inspire and to stimulate. They want to take us on the most exciting of musical and philosophical journeys - the journey to our inner selves.
