Album Intro：

A collection of 18 of SCC's most gangsta and commercially successful songs on one CD and features the incredible '17 Switches', 'Gang Stories', 'Tha Bomb' and 'All Day Everyday'. Featured Rappers include Big People, Havoc and Young Prodeje rap real tunes that reflect the true life of a gangsta in South Central Los Angeles and Big Prodeje produces the awesome backbone of hardcore and G-funk style beats. It is quite clear why SCC has secured its place in history as one of the originators and most successful groups of West Coast Rap.
