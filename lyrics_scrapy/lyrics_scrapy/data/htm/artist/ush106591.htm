Mucc (Japanese: ムック Hepburn: Mukku?, officially romanized as MUCC) is a Japanese visual kei rock band, formed in Ibaraki in 1997. Named after a character from the children's TV show Hirake! Ponkikki,[citation needed] the band is also known by the nickname '69' since the numbers six and nine can be pronounced 'Mu' and 'Ku' respectively in Japanese. As of 2017, the band has released thirteen studio albums, two live albums, three EPs, thirty-four singles and seventeen home videos, some of which were also released in Europe.

Members

Tatsurou (逹瑯?) – lead vocals, harmonica

Also in the supergroup Karasu (カラス?), that formed in 2009 with Hiroto (Alice Nine), Mizuki (Sadie), Dunch (Jealkb) and Kenzo (Ayabie).

Miya (ミヤ?) – Guitars, backing vocals

Also in the group GeKiGaKutai (激楽隊?), that formed in 2003 with Nezaki (La Vie En Rose), Tsuyoshi (Jully) and Yoshida (Three 9).

Satochi (SATOち?) – drums, percussion, backing vocals
Yukke (YUKKE?) – bass guitar, contrabass, electric upright bass, backing vocals

Former members

Hiro – bass, backing vocals (1997–1999)
