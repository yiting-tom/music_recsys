Album Intro：

Burlington, ON's Silverstein formed in 2000. Their creative and busy first decade has seen them perform over 1,200 live shows while making countless friends and selling over 1,000,000 albums. The hard rock quintet finished up a successful US and Canadian Winterizer tour in preparation for the release of their highly anticipated, fifth studio album, Rescue. The debut Hopeless Records full length Rescue earned a spot on Alternative Press' Most Anticipated Albums of 2011.
