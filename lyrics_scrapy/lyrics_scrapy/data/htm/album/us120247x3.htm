Album Intro：

'Polarity' is loyal to The Wedding's rock and roll feel but also displays their musical expansion and creativity. This album contains an artistic use of increased instrumentation; from strings, to horns, to even bagpipes, this will surely turn ears as it builds an even larger fan base. In addition, the honest and heartfelt lyrics preach a message that encourages listeners to desire an authentic way of living that is rooted in faith and hope. This record is truly filled with excitement and surprise.
