Album Intro：

Two Tongues is an indie rock side project/supergroup consisting of members of Saves The Day and Say Anything. The side-project consists of Max Bemis and Coby Linder of Say Anything, along with Chris Conley and Dave Soloway of Saves the Day. This thirteen-track disc was self produced and recorded at Electric Lady Bug studios in California.

Before forming Two Tongues, Max Bemis, Chris Conley, and Coby Linder had previously collaborated on a cover song of Bob Dylan's The Man In Me for the compilation album Paupers, Peasants, Princes & Kings: The Songs of Bob Dylan, released by Doghouse Records in 2006. After the collaboration was a success Bemis asked Conley about potentially doing an album together, Conley responded positively by saying 'I would do it with no one else, nothing could be more epic!」
