Album Intro：

'Ballas Hough Band' (BHB), formally known as 'Almost Amy,' was formed in 2005 in London, England and in 2008 moved to the US, where they were signed to a record deal with Hollywood Records. In November 2008, the band changed their name to Ballas Hough Band due to conflict with their previous name, as stated on the band's official music myspace. BHB is made up of Mark Ballas, Derek Hough, Sam Marder, Harry Sullivan, and Emily Grace. Lead vocalists, Ballas and Hough, are well known for their Dancing With the Stars championships.
