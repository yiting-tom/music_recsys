Album Intro：

Out of the flourishing New England post-hardcore scene that produced Unearth, Killswitch Engage, Shadows Fall, and Converge, comes Vanna. This six-song debut EP weaves a sonic web of vocals that veer from soaring melodies to visceral growls and incensed screams, complimented by dual guitars attacking with merciless precision and bittersweet dissonance. Vanna has shared stages with The Chariot, Evergreen Terrace, Black My Heart, and more. Look for them on tour this summer.
