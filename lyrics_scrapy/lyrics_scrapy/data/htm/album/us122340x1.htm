Album Intro：

More so than Roky Erickson (who was mired in drug abuse and legal problems), guitarist Stacy Sutherland led the way on the 13th Floor Elevators' last studio LP ('68). It's a hypnotic, reverb-drenched, fuzzed-out psychedelic gem that's too often overlooked in favor of their earlier albums. The remastered stereo edition joins the mono version of their lost third album A Love That's Sound ; includes Livin' On; Street Song; Dear Dr. Doom; Scarlet and Gold; Wait for My Love , and more!
