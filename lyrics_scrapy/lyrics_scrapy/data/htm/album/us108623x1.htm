Album Intro：

Derived from various Arizona bands, Calling of Syrens was formed in March of 2006 with a common goal. That goal being to throw their name into the ever-growing music industry. With their new highly anticipated EP 「Victims of Foul Play」 coming out in August, Calling of Syrens is sure to continue to build their fan base locally, and nationally. The band has high hopes ahead of them, as they will be hitting the road in the fall, completing a national tour. The band loves to talk to fans, and does their best to answer every comment/message, so keep them coming! Keep checking back for tour dates and other news, and remember COS LOVES YOU!!
