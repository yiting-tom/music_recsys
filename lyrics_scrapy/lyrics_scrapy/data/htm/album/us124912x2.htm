Album Intro：

14 original demos and previously unreleased tracks for the power pop band best known for their hit, 'Starry Eyes'. Recorded in 1977 before they signed to Virgin it includes songs that were re-recorded for their 1979 debut album, 'Shades InBed'. 20 page informative booklet with rare band photos andnotes from band leader John Wicks. 2001.
