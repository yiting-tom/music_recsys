Album Intro：

2009 collection from the British vocalist including two brand new tracks. In a remarkable career that spans more than two decades, Seal has garnered countless accolades along with three Grammy Awards and has sold more than 15 million albums worldwide. Known for his one-of-a-kind soaring, husky baritone voice and classic songwriting, Seal has seen success across numerous genres of music. Features the hits 'Kiss From a Rose', 'Crazy', 'Prayer For The Dying' and more.
