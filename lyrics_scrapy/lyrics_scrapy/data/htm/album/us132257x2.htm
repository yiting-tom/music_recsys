Album Intro：

Repressing. 2009 release. Susumu Yokota returns with one of his most engaging albums to date, on which every track is a gently glowing gem. Featuring the vocal talents of Casper Clausen (Efterklang), Anna Bronsted (Our Broken Garden), Claire Hope (The Chap), Panos Ghikas (The Chap), Nancy Elizabeth, Caroline Ross, and Kaori, Mother is full of great songs, a mesmerizing web of sound made of subtle rhythms and deep tones. 13 tracks.
