2014 release, the fifth studio album from the Welsh Reggae/Metal band. Lead vocalist Benji Webbe says: ''We are more than excited to unleash this Skindred bomb on the world! Kill The Power is for all them that are sick of being the underdog, and wanna step up and destroy all those who oppress and hold them down! Use this album as a backdrop to overcome and take your life back! We ain't fighting the power no more - we are definitely killing it!!'
