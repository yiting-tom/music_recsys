Album Intro：

Like Comedy is the 2013 release from the Scottish Folk/Rock duo making this their 9th studio album since NME readers voted them best band in 1988. The inclusion of their then little known hit, Im Gonna Be (500 Miles) on the 1993 soundtrack to the film Benny & Joon, catapulted The Proclaimers to worldwide acclaim. What followed was a series of gold and platinum albums in the UK, Australia, New Zealand, USA and Canada.


Craig and Charlie Reid have been recording their unique brand of Folk Rock for over two decades, earning them hits, awards and a growing fan base all over the world. Feature tracks include the first single 'Love Can Move Mountains', the title track Notes and Rhymes, and It Was So Easy To Find An Unhappy Woman .
