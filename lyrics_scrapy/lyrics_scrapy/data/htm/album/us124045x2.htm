Album Intro：

Worship albums are quickly becoming a dime a dozen with contemporary Christian groups. While the idea may seem keen to many top-charting artists, the music often falls flat with a dull luster. But the Katinas' new worship release, Lifestyle, is more than just the latest marketing gimmick; it truly reflects their own lifestyle as evidenced by their numerous appearances at Promise Keepers events and Billy Graham crusades as well as worship-driven radio hits. Rest assured, Katinas staples 'You Are God,' 'Thank You,' and 'Draw Me Close' are ably covered in this live release. However, tracks like the opening 'Mighty River,' 'I Give You My Heart,' and the calypso-flavored alternate version of 'Trading My Sorrows' add spice to the more regular fare. Never forcing emotion, the Katinas' worship spirit is one of credibility. The brothers from Samoa tastefully blend their harmonies throughout the release, delivering a pleasantly believable product. It's reaffirming to hear a true worship release that encourages the practice well beyond the delivery of the final note. --Michael Lyttle
