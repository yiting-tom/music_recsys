Album Intro：

2010 album from the British rapper and so-called 'Prince of Grime'. Third Strike, Tinchy's third album, explores themes on a deeper level following on from success and the impact it has had on his life since the days of growing in Bow, East London. The album is a balanced collection of songs; including a mix of harder street tracks such as `Gangsta?' coupled with catchy, radio friendly singles that have become Tinchy's signature. Production comes courtesy of Fraser T Smith and Nathan Retro and features guest artists such as Melanie Fiona on `Let it Rain', Bluey Robinson on `Tomorrow' and `Game Over' featuring Tinie Tempah.
