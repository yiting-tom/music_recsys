Album Intro：

Presenting the worlds latest music sensation are teatro - 4 strikingly handsome young men who have forged their career on broadway and west end. They are londoners simon bailey, andrew alexander, stephen rahman-hughes and american jeremiah james. Their debut album sung entirely in english is a fine collection of superb show tunes we all know and love.
