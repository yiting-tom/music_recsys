Album Intro：

The 13 new songs on Memory Almost Full are performed entirely by Paul McCartney (excluding strings) and produced by Grammy Award-winner David Kahne (The Strokes, Sublime, Bruce Springsteen and more).

Memory Almost Full is the upcoming studio album by Paul McCartney set for release in the United Kingdom on June 4, 2007 and in the United States a day later. The album will be McCartney's first release on Starbucks' Hear Music label (it is also the first release on the newly formed label). The album was produced by David Kahne and recorded at Hog Hill Mill Studios, Abbey Road Studios, Henson Recording Studios, AIR Studios, and RAK Studios between October 2003 and February 2007.

The first taster, 'Ever Present Past', made its debut on radio in the United States on April 20, 2007. The first single is slated to be 'Dance Tonight', featuring a video directed by Michel Gondry featuring Natalie Portman and Mackenzie Crook. The album was leaked to the Internet on May 11, 2007
