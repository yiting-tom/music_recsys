Album Intro：

Vinnie Stigma has been a pillar in the punk community since the early 80s when he founded the legendary Agnostic Front. This is his first solo record. Chock full of punk rock anthems that'll have people pounding beers and singing along. Produced by Jamey Jasta (Hatebreed) and Phil Caivano (Monster Magnet). Mixed and mastered by Zeus (Hatebreed, Agnostic Front, Shadow's Fall).
