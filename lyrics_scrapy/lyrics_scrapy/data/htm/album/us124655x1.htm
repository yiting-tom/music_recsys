Album Intro：

The Osmonds celebrate their return to the recording studio
with the release of their outstanding new album, I Can't
Get There Without You, September 18, 2012.

Fifty years since the very first Osmond album and 12 years
since the last studio album, I Can't Get There Without You,
features Jimmy, Merrill and Jay on vocals with special guest
vocals from Donny, Marie, Alan, Wayne and Sophia Osmond
(Jimmy's daughter). The track Fall To Fly' also features
vocals from 2008 X-Factor sensation Ruth Lorenzo.

The Osmond family has collectively sold over 100 million
albums, earning them more than 59 gold and platinum records.
Their remarkable cross-genre success in pop, rock &
roll, country, and gospel music has enabled them to touch
audiences of all ages.

The Osmond Brothers recently returned from their largest
UK tour ever, where they sold out over 50 venues in just
two months, with 100,000 tickets sold. Five decades after
their start, The Osmonds remain one of the most enduring
musical dynasties in entertainment history.
