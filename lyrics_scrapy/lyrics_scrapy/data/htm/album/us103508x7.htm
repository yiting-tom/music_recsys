Album Intro：

The award winning ex-pat American who splits her time between Lodon and Colorado has become the definitive voice for a genre. By placing the art of her singing in perfect balance with the genius of the writers whose songs she performs, Stacey has placed herself at the forefront of a small group of singers who have caught the attention of mainstream media and to whom listeners are flocing in their millions. Miss Kent has an innate understanding of how to capture the essence of a song. Her interpretation is delicate and crisp, lacking any superfluous parts. With every note Stacey elegantly bequeaths both purpose and direction, as exmplified in her soothingly intimate singing on Hushabye Mountain.
