Album Intro：

Out of print in the U.S.! 2007 UK reissue of this 1998 album from the Norfolk, Virginia Hip Hop artist who would soon become one of the biggest producers in the R&B and Hip Hop field. Tim's Bio was Timbaland's first solo album proper and features guest appearances from his partner Magoo, Missy Elliot, Nas, Jay-Z and many others. Features 'I Get it On', 'Lobster & Scrimp', 'Put 'Em On', 'Wit' Yo' Bad Self' and others. Universal.
