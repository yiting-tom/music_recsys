Album Intro：

Top of the Fair is a hard-working band from Milford, CT that began its career in early 2003 while the members were attending high school. They quickly got hooked up with Trumpet Breaker Productions, who has helped them successfully book and play 200 nationwide shows in the past year. And, with the success of their recent tours, they have been fortunate enough to share the stage with national acts such as Homegrown, Alexis On Fire, Spitalfield, Socratic, Days Away, Halifax and many more!
