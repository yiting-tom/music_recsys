Album Intro：

Paddock Park was formed in the summer of 2007 with their in-your-face screams, brutal breakdowns and catchy melodies. They've have captured the ears of thousands of listeners and virtually exploded onto the scene. After the debut self released highly anticipated EP With False Hope, Paddock Park is ready to unleash first full-length album, A Hiding Place For Fake Friends and are more than ready to hit the road.
