Lynch.( Lynch )【 20 albums 161 lyrics 】
Lynch (stylized as lynch.) is a Japanese visual-kei metal band formed in 2004. The band's concept is 'uniting heavy music with beautiful melodies.' As of 2017, the group has released three EPs, nine albums, and 15 singles.

Members
Hazuki (葉月) – lead vocals (2004-present), bass (studio only, 2004-2010, 2017-present)
Reo (玲央) – lead guitar, backing vocals (2004-present)
Asanao (晁直) – drums (2004-present)
Yusuke (悠介) – rhythm guitar, backing vocals (2006-present)

Former members
Akinori (明徳) - bass (2010-2016)
