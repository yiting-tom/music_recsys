Album Intro：

Sleeping With Sirens did it the right. They toured non-stop and built their fan base one show at a time. Their debut had a steady build with no 'flash in the pan' gimmicks or trends. Having toured the US over 8 times in 14 months, the band put in their time and hard work supporting bands like Silverstein and Of Mice and Men. Their 2011 album, Let's Cheers To This, is definitely their best work to date.
