Album Intro：

2007 digitally remastered and expanded two edition of the 1990 sophomore album from former Microdisney vocalist Cathal Coughlan and his mates. Includes a bonus CD featuring 15 tracks for a grand total of 33 cuts. The uncompromising sound of the Fatimas was hard to pigeonhole and they were one of the few bands during the early '90s who actually took chances with their music, not concerned with any restrictions thrown at them by their label. Sony/BMG.
