Album Intro：

Heavy tones, soaring melodies that bleed sporadically into fervent screams, shot through with pop accessibility; Arkansas born Spoken have it all. This is their sophomore album and it was produced by Travis Wyrick (POD, Pillar). For fans of Coheed & Cambria and The Used, Spoken will have your emotions in a whirlwind with all their dynamic diversity! Tooth & Nail. 2005.
