Album Intro：

Ticia Miller is being hailed as the Jazz voice to watch and listen for. An alumna of the famed Julliard school, she has been performing in front of audiences since age 10 when she played Oliver. She is currently the #1 performer on ReverbNation's Jazz chart for Nashville and in the Top 20 nationwide. This CD reached the first round of the Grammy Awards for Best Jazz Vocal Album of the Year. Her life story reads like a Hallmark movie and she has had her share of tragedies. Eight years ago, she was struck down by a heart attack and a stroke within days of one another. But she has fought her way back.
