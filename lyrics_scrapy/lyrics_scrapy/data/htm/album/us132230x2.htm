Album Intro：

Every now and then, an artist arrives on the scene that captures your attention not with pyrotechnics, high fashion or cutting edge technology, but with an honest voice, an open heart and songs that touch your soul. Susan Justice just might be one of those rare artists that can speak to a generation. On her debut album Eat Dirt, soon to be released on Capitol/Kite Records, Justice delivers a collage of emotionally resonant songs that are deeply personal and refl ective yet connects with anyone who has ever tried to fi gure out who they are and where they belong. Eat Dirt is an inspiring story of survival and a wild joyful ride that puts a smile on your face in these most difficult of times.
