Album Intro：

Produced by Ian Catt (Saint Etienne, Trembling Blue Stars, The Field Mice) The School's songs, with their hummable choruses, their doodoo-wops and imaginative Spector-esque arrangements show the same sense of melody as the 60s girl-group greats but updated with an impressive pop vision & elegance inherent in the best pop.
