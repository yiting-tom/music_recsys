Album Intro：

Digitally remastered edition of the fifth Go-Betweens album from 1987 that cemented the band's move toward the inclusion of strings that had begun with their last album. With this addition, the interplay between instruments and vocals is even more lush and pretty than ever, even on vaguely unsettling tracks like 'The Clarke Sisters.'
