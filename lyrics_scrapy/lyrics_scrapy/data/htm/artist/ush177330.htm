Victon【 8 albums 35 lyrics 】
Victon (Korean: 빅톤; stylized as VICTON), an acronym for Voice to New World, is a South Korean boy group formed by Plan A Entertainment in 2016. The group consists of Han Seung-woo, Kang Seung-sik, Heo Chan, Lim Se-jun, Do Han-se, Choi Byung-chan and Jung Su-bin, and made their debut on November 9, 2016 with the extended play Voice to New World.

Members

Han Seung-woo (Korean: 한승우) was born on December 24, 1994 in Busan, South Korea. His older sister is actress and former member of Secret, Han Sun-hwa.
Kang Seung-sik (Korean: 강승식) was born on April 16, 1995 in Gyeonggi, South Korea.
Heo Chan (Korean: 허찬) was born on December 14, 1995 in Seongnam, South Korea. His older brother is Heo Jun of Madtown.
Lim Se-jun (Korean: 임세준) was born on May 4, 1996 in South Korea.
Do Han-se (Korean: 도한세) was born on September 25, 1997 in South Korea.
Choi Byung-chan (Korean: 최병찬) was born on November 12, 1997 in Jeonju, South Korea.
Jung Su-bin (Korean: 정수빈) was born on April 5, 1999 in Daejeon, South Korea.
