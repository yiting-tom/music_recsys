The Black Ghosts are Theo 'Fake Blood' Keating and Simon 'Skywave' Lord. It's a project that sees Simon's beautiful songwriting and Theo's incredible electronic production skills join forces to make one of the stand-out albums of 2011. The Australian version features 2 bonus tracks.
