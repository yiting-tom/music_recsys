Album Intro：

Expanded edition of the Tampa-based Alternative/Orchestral Rock band's 2012 album including four bonus tracks. During the early days of the band, our man Cody Carson and Set It Off fit more into a Synth Pop Punk genre. Now that the band has matured, they define themselves as orchestral Pop Punk. The band's influences include Fall Out Boy, Four Year Strong, My Chemical Romance, All Time Low, New Found Glory, A Day To Remember, Forever The Sickest Kids, Relient K, and We The Kings. Go, Cody, go!
