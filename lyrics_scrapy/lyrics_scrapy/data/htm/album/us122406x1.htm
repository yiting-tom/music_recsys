Album Intro：

LA's Adored create a sound that mixes danceable Brit-pop vibe with power-pop hooks over a classic punk backbone. With constant shows in the Southern California area, a critically acclaimed EP last year, and a growing MySpace buzz, The Adored have built up a loyal fan base that has made them one of THE bands to watch in 2006. 'Suggests Pulp and The Jam playing a dance party celebrating the British Invasion' - LA Times. '...super-clean guitars, elastic pop hooks, bracing harmonies, big choruses, and LA-centric themes exploring the city's shiny faces and facades-with a more late-70's Brit-stomper edge, The Adored maintain a groove that makes you move' - LA Weekly.
