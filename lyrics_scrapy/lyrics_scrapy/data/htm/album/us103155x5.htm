Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded two CD edition of the classic 1986 debut album from the British bombshell. Touch Me features four UK hit singles: the international smash 'Touch Me (I Want Your Body)' (#3 UK and #4 US), 'Do Ya Do Ya (Wanna Please Me)' (#10 UK), 'Hold On Tight' and 'I'm All You Need'. Immediately establishing Samantha's signature pop-rock sound, the album was certified gold on its original release, reaching #17 in the UK and #24 in the US. Remastered direct from the master tapes, this edition brings together the original 10-track album with an incredible 27 bonus tracks across two CDs. Presented together with Samantha's later work and on CD for the very first time are her early recordings for Lamborghini Records with her first band SFX - 'Rockin' With My Radio', 'Aim To Win' and 'Holding'. Bonus tracks also include rare B-sides and many sought-after 12' mixes, the majority of which have never before been released on CD. Cherry Pop.
