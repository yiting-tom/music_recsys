Album Intro：

David Crowder Band delivered their final album 'Give Us Rest or (a requiem mass in c [the happiest of all keys])' to a stellar #2 debut on the Billboard Top 200 Chart in January 2012. It marked the end of the era that brought songs to the church like 'How He Loves,' 'O Praise Him,' 'Everything Glorious,' 'No One Like You,' and many more. Releasing 6 albums, the group sold over 1.9 million albums while winning 8 Dove Awards and receiving two GRAMMY nominations.

While the band has called it quits, Crowder will continue to release music and tour the country. Crowder will be headlining a 20-city club tour in the spring of 2013 and will continue to work on new music as he launches his solo career. An all new record from Crowder is coming in the fall of 2013.

'All This For A King: The Essential Collection' features one new song and three new remixes from Crowder, along with the David Crowder Band's biggest songs like 'How He Loves,' 'O Praise Him,' 'Everything Glorious,' 'No One Like You,' and more
