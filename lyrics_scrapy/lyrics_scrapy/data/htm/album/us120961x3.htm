Album Intro：

Israel's favorite pop punkers, Useless ID, are back with their finest release to date. It's been said that the third time's the charm, and that's definitely the case with Useless ID. Redemption is a perfect example of the kind of quality pop flavored punk that Kung Fu has made famous with bands like Blink 182 and the Ataris. The Japanese get it, Useless ID outdraws all of their Kung Fu label mates when they tour there and Japan record sales are through the roof. Now it's time for the U.S. to see what they've been missing. Fontana. 2005.
