Album Intro：

New Jersey rock band, The Parlor Mob is set to release their sophomore album, Dogs via Roadrunner Records October 11, 2011. The first single, 'Into The Sun' will go to radio in late August. In the winter of 2010, they finished writing Dogs and recorded the album from February through mid-March 2011 at Wire Recording in Austin, Texas. Produced, engineered and mixed by Matt Radosevich (30 Seconds To Mars, The Hives, Taking Back Sunday) Dogs is the follow up to their acclaimed debut album And You Were Crow which iTunes hailed as '... the behemoth sounds of bands like White Stripes...Black Mountain slowly rising from the indie rock realm, showing emo-rockers and pop-punkers how to turn their amps to eleven...A top notch debut.'
