Album Intro：

Original soundtrack to the 2013 film composed by Thomas Newman. Side Effects is a psychological thriller film directed by Steven Soderberg. The film, which stars Jude Law, Rooney Mara, Catherine Zeta-Jones and Channing Tatum, follows a young woman who experiments with prescription drugs as she waits for her husband's release from jail.
