Album Intro：

Vicious Rumors was formed in 1980 in Santa Rosa CA, by guitarist Geoff Thorpe. They issued numerous albums throughout the late 80s & early 90s. Welcome To The Ball was originally released in 1990 and has been out of print for years. 2005 Wounded Bird reissue.
