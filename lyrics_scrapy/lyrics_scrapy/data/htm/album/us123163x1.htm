Album Intro：

2009 holiday reissue. Comedy stars The Dan Band are back to jingle your bells for the holiday! Their holiday album Ho: A Dan Band Xmas features original, bound-to-become classics like 'I Wanna Rock You Hard This Xmas' and 'Please Don't Bomb Nobody This Holiday.' The Dan Band is led by Dan Finnerty, whose classic performances have been featured in such films as Old School, Starsky And Hutch, and recently The Hangover.
