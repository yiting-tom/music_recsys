Album Intro：

We are excited to announce the release of this newly and spectacularly remastered 7 song EP - with a brand new bonus track Face The Facts. Los Angeles' The Little Ones have been taking the west coast by storm with their already hard to find self-released Sing Song EP and with a slew of sold out dates. Now signed by Astralwerks (in association with Heavenly in the UK and Europe), the band is poised for a proper release that will bring their unique brand of feel-good guitar pop to the masses. Produced by David Newton (The Mighty Lemon Drops), their Rollercoaster sessions yielded a collection of songs that showcase their love for subtle textures, rhythmic pulses, and sharp melodies. With each breath of the mellotron or shake of the shaker, the Little Ones create their own aural reality. And despite only a few months of performing, the band is already gaining a must see reputation live. If you can see this band and not be excited, euphoric and happy, there's a chance you might actually be dead.
