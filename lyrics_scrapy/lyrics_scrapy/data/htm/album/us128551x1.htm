Album Intro：

Scars on 45 present their major label debut with this self-titled album on Chop Shop/Atlantic Records. The band is comprised of singer/guitarist Danny Bemrose, vocalist Aimee Driver, bassist Stuart Nichols, pianist David 'Nova' Nowakowski, and drummer Chris Durling. Scars on 45 create music that is both aurally and musically captivating.


The band's single 'Heart on Fire' was featured on the Vol. 4 soundtrack to ABC's 'Grey's Anatomy,' and were subsequently named on of VH1's 'You Oughta Know' Artists of 2012. To kick of 2012, the band toured with Anya Marina and The Fray.
