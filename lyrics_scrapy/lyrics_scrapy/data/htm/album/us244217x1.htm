Album Intro：

《微醺大飯店：1980s》為驚喜製造 Surprise Lab. 與進港浪製作攜手推出的六號沉浸式體驗作品，專輯中收錄《微醺大飯店：1980s》團隊的兩首原創歌曲，前來體驗將觀賞由歌手梁如嫣帶來的歌曲現場演出。
The Great Tipsy 2 : 1980s is the sixth immersive experience project presented by Surprise Lab. and KingKong Wave Production in Taiwan. The album includes two original tracks produced by The Great Tipsy 2 : 1980s team. Watch the live performance of artist Liang Ru Yan in the experience.
