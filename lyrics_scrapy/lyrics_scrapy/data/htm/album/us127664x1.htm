Album Intro：

No strangers to the road, Tuuli’s has toured coast to coast in Canada and the USA, notably when they were included in the all girl package with The Donnas and Bratmobile.Since the band started at a young age, Tuuli has had to prove themselves as musicians. They’ve accomplished this task and are being taken very seriously, having been the house band for Comedy Centrals’ 'Premium Blend', opened for the likes of Cheap Trick, and having their songs featured on BBC Radio 1, K-ROCK in Los Angeles, CFOX, KXLU, CBC, and hundreds of college radio stations. Music critics have come out lavishing them with praise for their creativity and musicality, resulting in features in such publications as Now Magazine, LA Times, Toronto Star and many others.
