Album Intro：

So chock full of catchy songs that it could be any other band's greatest hits album. The Volcano Suns rose out of the ashes of Boston's Mission of Burma and released this masterpiece in 1985. Never before released on CD, this is a must-have for any fan of post-punk, indie rock, or hardcore music. Seamlessly merging noise, aggression, and tunefulness, it's a wonder the Volcano Suns weren't huge from the get go. Remastered by Bob Weston (Shellac, Nirvana, Sebadoh, Polvo, Superchunk, Archers Of Loaf). Contains nine bonus tracks.
