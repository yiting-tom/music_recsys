Album Intro：

The legendary Ray Davis returns with all your favourite songs by The Kinks backed by the spectacular Crouch End Festival Chorus. This must-have album includes brand new renditions of 'Waterloo Sunset', 'You Really Got Me', 'All Day and All of the Night', 'Shangri-La', 'Victoria' and many more phenomenal classics. This special edition includes a brand new track penned by Davis himself 'Postcard From London'.
