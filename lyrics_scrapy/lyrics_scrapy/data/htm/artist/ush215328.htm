Greta Van Fleet is an American rock band formed in 2012. The band members are Josh Kiszka, Jake Kiszka, Sam Kiszka, and Danny Wagner. In April 2017, the band released their debut studio EP, Black Smoke Rising. The debut single, 'Highway Tune', topped the Billboard US Mainstream Rock and Active Rock Rock stations in September 2017.
