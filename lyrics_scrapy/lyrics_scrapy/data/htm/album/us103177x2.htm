Album Intro：

2013 is the official return of Spacehog as they release their first album in 10 years entitled, As It Is On Earth on their own Hog Space Records on April 16th. As It Is On Earth carries forth the profound influences of T Rex, Iggy Pop, Queen, David Bowie & the Spiders, and by colliding these influences with a wall of distorted guitars and the stupendous daring of their lead vocals.
