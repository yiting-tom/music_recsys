Rachel Rhodes (レイチェル・ローズ Reicheru Rōzu?), also known as Rei Yasuda (安田 レイ Yasuda Rei?), born April 15, 1993 in North Carolina, United States, is a model and singer of Japanese and Caucasian descent.

Rhodes achieved early fame as the image model and vocalist for the band Genki Rockets. In 2013, she made her solo debut as a singer with 'Best of My Love.'
