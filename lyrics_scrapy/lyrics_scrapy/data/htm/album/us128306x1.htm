Album Intro：

Coleccion Definitiva (2 CD's + 1 DVD) is the absolute best collection ever made available from the Spanish superstar Alejandro Sanz. This collection contains every one of his hits spanning his twenty year career featured on two audio CD's. In addition, the featured DVD contains 34 music videos. The pacakaging is an 8 panel digipack with 20 page booklet included. Throughout the extensive list of Sanz' classics on Coleccion Definitiva, featured tracks (on both CD & DVD) include some of the biggest Spanish hits of all time.
