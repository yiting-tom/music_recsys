Album Intro：

2009 debut album from the hotly-tipped British Pop sensation. Written by Tommy and produced with Mike Crossey (Arctic Monkeys, Razorlight and Foals), this long player is full to the rafters with bite size nuggets of goodness. From the anthemic stadium fillers like `These Things Happen' to the Talking Heads inspired `Much Too Much', the euphoric future single `Brand New Love' and the intoxicating Chic, MGMT and Franz melting pot that is new single - `She's Got Me Dancing', Tommy's proves he's as versatile as he is brilliant. This debut album fits just as snugly in a ram-jammed super club as it would at the grimiest of rock festivals. Quite simply Tommy's tunes are here to remind people of the universal power of music - to make you move. Island.
