Album Intro：

Love: Part Two is the fourth studio album by alternative rock band Angels & Airwaves, released November 1, 2011 via the band's own label, To the Stars Records. It follows 2010's Love, and its release coincides with that of the band's Love feature film, which includes music from the album. The first single from the album and respective music video, 'Anxiety', was released on August 11, 2011. The album is the last featuring drumming from Adam Willard, who left the band shortly before its release in October 2011.
