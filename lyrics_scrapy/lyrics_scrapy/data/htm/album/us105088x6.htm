Album Intro：

Jeremy Thomas Camp (born January 12, 1978) is a contemporary Christian musician from Lafayette, Indiana. Camp has released six albums to date and is an ordained minister. The soundtrack album Music Inspired by the Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe includes his song 'Open Up Your Eyes'. His original music is a mixture of ballads and rock.

Speaking Louder Than Before is sure to be one of 2008/2009 Christian Music's biggest releases- Produced by Grammy & Dove Award winning producer Brown Bannister (Amy Grant, Michael W Smith). CD unlocks 40 minutes of downloadable 'video devotionals' where Jeremy will address each song on the album with music and application.

Jeremy Thomas Camp（生於1978年1月）是來自印度Lafayette的現今Christian音樂人。距今Camp已經發行了六章專輯，其本人是一個部長。這張專輯的靈感源自於Narnia的《歷王記》：獅子、女巫、衣櫥這些元素都融合在他的歌曲《Open Up Your Eyes》中。他最原始的意圖就是將民謠和搖滾融合。

《Speaking Louder Than Before》毋庸置疑是2008/2009年度最好的Christian音樂——由Grammy & Dove獲獎人同時也是音樂製作人Brown Bannister（Amy Grant, Michael W Smith）製作。CD涵蓋了40分鐘可供下載的「視頻禱告」，Jeremy將詮釋此專輯中每一首歌的含義。

By Lydia
