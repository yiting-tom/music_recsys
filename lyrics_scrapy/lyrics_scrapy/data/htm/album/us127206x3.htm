Album Intro：

Celebrating 25 years in the business, Toyah Willcox is releasing a mini album of brand new material. In the past year alone Toyah has performed in front of a sold out Wembley Arena, to a crowd of over 20,000 at Manchester Evening News Arena as well as performing on stage to combined audiences of over 140,000 in her award nominated production of Calamity Jane. The multi-talented singer-songwriter, actress and presenter whose musical credits include 13 Top 40 singles and a No.1 platinum-selling album 'Anthem', as well as a Brit Award in 1982. Velvet Lined Shell is a far cry from the Eighties pop that made her an international household name and reveals a much edgier side more akin to Marianne Faithful, Tanya Donnelly and Kirstin Hersh, with the bite of PJ Harvey.
