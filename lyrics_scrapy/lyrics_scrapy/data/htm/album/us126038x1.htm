Album Intro：

2009 debut album by The Yellow Moon Band. It follows two sold out seven inch singles and some crazy shows at the Green Man Festival. Eight tracks of instrumental wonk that's as darn exciting as discovering that rare side of wax that's sorta' Anatolian led rare groove in a bare chested wrestle with Be Bop Deluxe's Sunburst Finish and pressed up on Vertigo. Well it might exist in the car boot of your dreams. More Hillage than Gong, more Hacket than Genesis, more Thompson than Fairport and certainly more Velvet Jacket than Leather jacket. Gatefold papersleeve CD with artwork by Luke Insect. Static Caravan.
