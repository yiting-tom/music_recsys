Gnash( Garrett Charles Nash )【 16 albums 49 lyrics 】
Garrett Charles Nash (born June 16, 1993) better known by his stage name Gnash (stylized as gnash), is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, DJ and record producer. He released his debut extended play (EP), u, in March 2015 on SoundCloud and followed up with the me EP in December 2015. His third EP, titled us, was released in March 2016 and includes the single, 'i hate u, i love u', featuring Olivia O'Brien, which peaked at number 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 and reached number one in Australia.
