Album Intro：

Three albums on 2CD set featuring three of the band's four albums (Heartbeats And Triggers, No Time Like Now and Evening Of The Harvest) plus bonus tracks including a cover of the only George Harrison/John Lennon composition 'Cry For A Shadow'. Translator's brilliant mix of Byrds-like jangle, Punk-attitude and great songwriting has kept their fanbase growing since the band split in '87. Acadia. 2007.
