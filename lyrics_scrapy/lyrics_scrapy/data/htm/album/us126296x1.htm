Album Intro：

Progressive Metallers Thought Chamber are back with a 2013 release, following up on their acclaimed debut album Angular Perceptions from 2007. The U.S. based group named their second album release Psykerion. Next to guitarist and composer Michael Harris as well as vocalist Ted Leonard (also in Enchant, Spock's Beard and Affector), Thought Chamber's ranks of skilled players now also feature Bill Jenkins (also in Enchant) on keyboards, Jeff Plant on bass and Mike Haid on drums.
