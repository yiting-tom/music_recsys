Album Intro：

Strawbs 40th anniversary album Dancing To The Devil's Beat is a record of its time - in its production values, and in reflecting Strawbs' evolution from sixties folk club origins, through the hits, on to stadium rock, and now as one of Britain's best loved Heritage bands. Dave Cousins' lyrics convey the maelstrom of current political events - from the machinations of climate change, to greed and corruption in the public sector, and the dignity of the armed forces in combat.
