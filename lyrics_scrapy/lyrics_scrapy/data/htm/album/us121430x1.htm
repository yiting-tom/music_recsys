Album Intro：

Known for an inventive and versatile technique that has supplied the pulse for dozens of fine albums over the last decade, acoustic bassist Viktor Krauss gathers a superb group of players around him for his solo debut Far From Enough. Bill Frisell is on the front-end of a line-up that also features slide guitar master Jerry Douglas, veteran drummer Steve Jordan, and vocalist/violinist Alison Krauss.
