Album Intro：

Canadian-only two CD fan-pack edition of the veteran band's 2009 album includes a bonus live CD and a bonus T-Shirt. The Tragically Hip are back with their most accessible release ever. We Are the Same, their first album since 2007, was produced by Bob Rock (Metallica, Aerosmith, Bon Jovi) and features some of the most memorable songs the band has ever written. Universal.
