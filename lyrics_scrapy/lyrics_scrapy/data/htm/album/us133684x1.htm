Album Intro：

2007 release of the debut solo album by the longtime collaborator and producer of singing star Lara Fabian, who also collaborated on some of these tracks. Other collaborators include Didier Golemanas, Zoe and Richard Seff. The album was recorded at his studio in Montreal. Allison wrote all the music played piano and guitar. He was accompanied by Remy Malo, Benoit Clement, Rejean Lachance, David Lfleche and Donald Meunier.
