Album Intro：

2009 release. Dimensions is The Lovetones' fourth studio album and the latest chapter in the Indie Psychedelic Pop legacy of frontman Matthew Tow, who remains one of Australia's most underrated songwriters. The album was recorded in the Blue Mountains, west of Sydney, mixed in Los Angeles by Rob Campanella (Brian Jonestown Massacre/Quarter After/Black Angels) and mastered by William Bowden (one of Australia's finest mastering engineers). 11 tracks.
