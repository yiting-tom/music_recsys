Album Intro：

Todd Rundgren first broke into the biz with his Philly rock band the Nazz, who looked to the British Invasion for inspiration and debuted with this 1968 LP. Here's their hit Hello It's Me (later a solo smash for Rundgren) plus Crowded; Open My Eyes; Lemming Song , and the rest of the LP plus 12 bonus outtakes, demos, live cuts and more!
