Album Intro：

Co-vocalist for Zero 7, Tina Dico has created a name for herself based on talent and talent alone. It took her beautiful voice, talented song writing skills, and personal wit to become a big success in her home country of Denmark. Word of the gifted singer spread like wild fire throughout Europe & North America and Tina's first EP, Far, garnered the kind of buzz most artists only ever dream about. One year later, Tina releases her highly anticipated first North American full- length, In the Red, an emotionally honest record, full of finely wrought tunes and riveting, bold choruses. Defend. 2006.
