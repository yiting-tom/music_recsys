Album Intro：

THE MIGHTY ORQ (pronounced Ork) are a power trio from Houston,Texas known for their unique blend of emotionally charged vocals, soaring guitars, and powerful drum lines.
THE MIGHTY ORQ have quickly risen to be one of Texas biggest bands by combining the energy of classic rock with undeniable pop hooks and melodies. Influenced by legendary Texas outfits such as ZZ Top, Soulhat, and Kings X, the band has been able to reach out to a wide-ranging audience that continues to grow and diversify. The band have become well known for their high-energy live shows where through their music they take the listener to the extreme edges of rhythm, melody and dynamics. They have been actively touring through Texas and the South for four years maintaining a rigorous 200+ shows per year. This daunting schedule as well as the addition of Spin Doctors Mark White on bass, has allowed the band to fine tune themselves into a razor-sharp trio. The release of their new album TO THE BONE coincides with an international tour which looks set to propel THE MIGHTY ORQ to stardom.
