Album Intro：

The Outsiders is the upcoming third album from Christian rock band Needtobreathe. It is slated for release on August 25, 2009

The album's first single, 'Lay 'Em Down', was released to Christian radio at the beginning of June 2009 and was released on iTunes on June 16.[5] According to drummer Joe Stillwell, the album will be released near 'the end of August' 2009. The next single from the album, Something Beautiful, was released in August.
