Album Intro：

Direct from Venezuela emerges a promising new vocal sextet group Voz Veis. This talented young and gifted sextet is slowly but surely building a solid foundation self producing their own works and achieving hits while building a solid fan base throughout Latin America. Their recent production 'Como Se Llega A Belen', will soon be considered to be a jewel within the Christmas albums, truly a beautiful cd that has recently reaped 2 Latin Grammy nomiations.
