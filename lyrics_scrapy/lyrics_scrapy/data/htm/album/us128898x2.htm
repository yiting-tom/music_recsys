Album Intro：

Five time Dove Award winning group Selah returns with their most spectacular release to date - Hope Of The Broken World. The new album features the group's stirring first single and title track offering Selah's harmonious signature sound. Stand out tracks include solo tracks from each member of the group, (Amy 'I Look To You', Todd 'Moments' and Alan 'Coat Of Many Colors') drawn from each of their own personal life experiences. Hope Of The Broken World is in stores Tuesday, August 23rd.
