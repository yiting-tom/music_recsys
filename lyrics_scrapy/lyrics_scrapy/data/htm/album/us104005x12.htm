Album Intro：

Import hybrid-SACD pressing. Features DSD remastering. Digitally remastered edition of the 1978 debut album from the British trio. Initially thought of as just another 'New Wave' band from the UK, The Police became one of the biggest acts of the '80s and remained on top of their game right up until their unofficial break up in '84. Features 'Roxanne', 'Can't Stand Losing You', 'Next To You' and more. Universal.
