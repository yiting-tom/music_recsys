The Unseen ''Explode''...with their second full length on BYO! The Unseen are Boston's premier street punk band with a national following that ranks them among the elite in the U.S. street punk scene. You've never heard of them? That's hard to believe considering that they've appeared on every street punk comp that matters! (Over 25 in all!) Not only that, but their debut full length, LOWER CLASS CRUCIFIXION on A-F Records has also sold over 4000 copies.
