Album Intro：

The Cardigans羊毛衫樂隊主唱Nina Persson的第二張個人專輯，呃，前一張是在2001年。主打曲Stronger than Jesus在去年十一月份已經先行公佈，掀起了歌迷們的絲絲躁動盼望著新專輯的到來。

2009 sophomore album from this outfit featuring Nina Persson, vocalist from Swedish Pop heathens The Cardigans. Originally formed as a Swedish musical duo by Persson and Atomic Swing musician Niclas Frisk, A Camp has since evolved into a trio with the addition of American film composer, Shudder to Think member, and Nina's husband, Nathan Larson. As the title Colonia suggests, the new album is a journey through a myriad of themes, events and ideas which have been prevalent throughout history and remain true today. 12 tracks. A Camp.
