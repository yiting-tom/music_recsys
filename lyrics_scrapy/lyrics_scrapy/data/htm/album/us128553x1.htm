Album Intro：

Upon forming in 1993, French deathsters Scarve birthed an intricate, dark and brutal brand of extremity. After being handpicked by Swedish masterminds Meshuggah to support their most recent European tour, these purveyors of modern brutality are back with The Undercurrent. Erupting track after unique track of ferocious, weaving, blastbeat-fueled madness, this mind-blowing slab of modern death metal sees Scarve explode their art to the max.
