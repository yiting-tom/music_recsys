Album Intro：

2008 release. On their hotly anticipated third album, Australian kings of Funk, The Bamboos, take the beat-laden deep party Funk of their previous LPs and push the boundaries of their sound with an injection of Northern Soul and `60s Mod Soul style. An amazing selection of guest vocalists (Kylie Auldist, MC Ty, Megan Washington and Swedish Soul sensation Paul MacInnes) add their own unique flavors to the brew. Tru Thoughts.
