Kaity Dunstan (born 1996), known by her stage name Cloves, is an Australian singer-songwriter.

Background
Early life and The Voice

Dunstan began performing at age thirteen with her sister in local bars and pubs. She wrote her first song at age eleven. She appeared in season two of The Voice Australia in 2013, singing Melanie's 'Brand New Key' during her audition. The song peaked at #40 on the ARIA singles chart. She was later eliminated during the battle rounds after performing 'Girls Just Wanna Have Fun'.
2015-present

In 2015, under the new moniker Cloves which was inspired by a trip to Bali, she released her debut single 'Frail Love' as well as her EP XIII through Duly Noted Records. Her song 'Don't Forget About Me' was featured on the soundtrack of the 2016 film Me Before You. She performed at the Coachella and Lollapalooza music festivals in 2016. In November 2016, Cloves released a new song titled 'Better Now'. Her song 'Everybody's Son' was featured in The Vampire Diaries, Season 7, episode 20. She released the song 'California Numb' in May 2017.
