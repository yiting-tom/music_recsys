Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2013 album from the Texas-based symphonic/choral Pop Rock band led by Tim DeLaughter, formerly of Tripping Daisy. Yes, It's True is the follow-up to the band's 2007 album The Fragile Army and their 2012 holiday release. The album was produced by DeLaughter and Julie Doyle, with Eric Drew Feldman (Captain Beefheart, PJ Harvey) as well as some help from Tim Palmer (Pearl Jam, U2).
