Album Intro：

2010 sophomore release from the Philadelphia-based producer/artist. With musical arrangement skills that make film score composers red-faced with jealousy, plus a knack for impressively bass-heavy music that works in clubs and on headphones, Starkey returns with Ear Drums & Black Holes, an album that takes an incredibly open-ended vision of Dubstep and runs with it. 15 tracks.
