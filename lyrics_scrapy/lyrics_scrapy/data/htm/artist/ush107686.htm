Josh Wilson( Joshua David Wilson )【 9 albums 73 lyrics 】
Joshua David Wilson (born November 14, 1983) is a contemporary Christian musician from El Dorado, Arkansas. He is best known for the radio singles 'Savior, Please' from his 2008 album Trying to Fit the Ocean in a Cup and 'Before the Morning' from his 2009 EP, Life is Not a Snapshot.
