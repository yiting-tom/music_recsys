Album Intro：

The debut album by the Canadian group that supports the songs of resident Moe Berg in all his wiley humour, sardonic wit, angst and frustrations. What should have been a chart topper when it was originally issued in 1997 has come to be revered as a pop classic, featuring the singles 'She's So Young', 'Hard To Laugh' and their all time best known tune, 'I'm An Adult Now'.
