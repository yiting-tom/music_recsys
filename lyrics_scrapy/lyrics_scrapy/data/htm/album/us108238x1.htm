Album Intro：

2010 release from the Kansas City, MO-based Alt-Rock quartet fronted by vocalist Kristen May. Stages is a stunning collection of dreamy Pop/Rock melodies which address such themes as loss, devotion, betrayal and love. Lead singer May describes Stages as 'our blood, sweat, and tears over the past five years as a band on the road. It's about our relationships to one another and the changes we've gone through'
