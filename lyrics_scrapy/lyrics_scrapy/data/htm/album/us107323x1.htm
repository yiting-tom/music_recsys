Album Intro：

A Skylit Drive is a 6-piece American Post-hardcore band from Lodi, California. The band has so far released one EP album, one studio length album, one DVD, and a 2nd studio album is expected to be released in 2009.

Adelphia is the second studio album by American post-hardcore band A Skylit Drive. It will be the band's debut album on their new record label Fearless Records, to be released on June 9, 2009.
