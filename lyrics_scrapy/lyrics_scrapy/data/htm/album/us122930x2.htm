Album Intro：

Limited edition collectors boxset from the Australian band containing the Live At The Bowl two DVD set and the Live On Earth double CD release plus a passport style booklet which contains a tour diary written by the Ryan (The Cat Empire bassist), four fan badges and a The Cat Empire bag and, finally, a set list signed by the entire band. Fans worldwide have created the demand for The Cat Empire to release their personal archive of live show recordings and footage from their career so far spanning from 2001-2008. Featuring tracks recorded at shows all around the world, Live On Earth has all the fan favorites including 'Hello', 'The Chariot', 'Sly', 'The Car Song' and 'No Longer There'.
