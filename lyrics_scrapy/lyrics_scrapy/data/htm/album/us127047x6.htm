Album Intro：

His fourth full-length is a collection of carefully crafted, poetically soulful songs that pay homage to the unforgettable singer/songwriter spirit of Laurel Canyon that thrived in the late '60s and '70s. Upcoming tour plans include a series of west coast dates with Josh Kelley and Joe Firstman, and a national headlining tour in the fall. From the folk-friendly venues of NYC's Lower East Side to the hype-crazed clubs on the Sunset Strip, Lucca has made an impressive name for himself as an artist not to be missed.
