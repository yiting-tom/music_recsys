Album Intro：

Leave Richie to remind us all once more of how good it used to be and how great it can be again. It is not just a good feelin' to know, it's a great feelin' to know-it's the best feelin' to know, and we all-old, new and still to come, owe Richie Furay a huge debt for that. Thanks Richie! --Pete Fornatale-Radio Programer (liner notes)

Absolutely one of the finest voices of our generation, and one of the most respected pioneers of the country rock sound, which he developed alongside his good friends Stephen Stills,Neil Young, Jim Messina, Dewey Martin and Bruce Palmer in Buffalo Springfield, and later with his pals in Poco.

This collection of hits, album staples and radio and fan favorites covers all of his long and still growing career.

This is one for the history books folks! --Joe Reagoso-Executive Producer
