Album Intro：

2007 reissue of the Irish trio's belated second album, released four years after their 1992 debut. Comprised of vocalist/bassist Paul Linehan, his guitarist brother Niall and drummer Ashley Keating, the group made their recorded debut in '91 just as the Madchester/Baggy scene was beginning to implode. Their straightforward Rock/Pop attack, clever lyrics and undeniable energy made them favorites with the critics. This sophomore release was more sophisticated than their debut and featured another fine batch of tunes. FIFA.
