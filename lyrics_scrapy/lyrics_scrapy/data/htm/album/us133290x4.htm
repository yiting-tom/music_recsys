Album Intro：

The story of ReinXeed takes place in a town called Vindeln in the north of Sweden. ReinXeed is Tommy ReinXeed's musical creation and band vision. The band was formed in 2002 and worked very hard since then to develop their own style of melodic power metal and image to create what ReinXeed call adventurous power metal. Big bombastic music! In late 2007 Christian Liljegren former singer and founder of NARNIA also owner of LILJEGREN RECORDS/DOOLITTLE GROUP got a demo from Tommy ReinXeed. He was so impressed of what he heard and signed ReinXeed direct to his label.
