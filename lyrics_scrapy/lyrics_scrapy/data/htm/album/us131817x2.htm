Album Intro：

The Canadians open their second album. Armed to the Teeth, with the perfect mix of melody and energy. Striker ignites a firework in which rigorous riffing, playful solos and brilliant refrains are clearly the prime focus. Inspired by the originators of the scene like Saxon, Iron Maiden, Annihilator but also Exciter, the band stays hungry and wild over the full duration of the album. With every note, Armed to the Teeth breathes the spirit of the glorious 80's and revitalizes this particular era of music with a boost of fresh energy.
