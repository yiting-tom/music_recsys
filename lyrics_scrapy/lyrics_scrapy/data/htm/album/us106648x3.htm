Album Intro：

Limited two disc (CD + DVD) edition of this 2007 live/archive release from the Punk rockers . Relive the story of Thursday with this fantastic collection of film and music that traces the band's humble beginnings in the basements of America all of the way to international acclaim. This is the essential DVD and CD collection for any fan and a must-have for true believers of Punk Rock. Hear the band tell their story from the early days of touring America in a van, releasing the groundbreaking Full Collapse on Victory and touching the lives of Punk fans world-wide. In addition to an incredible live performance at the Starland Ballroom in their hometown of New Brunswick, NJ, this DVD/CD package features candid interviews and rare footage from the band's archives. Plus, hear three brand new studio tracks, recorded especially for this historic release. Victory.
