Album Intro：

Japan only pressing. Commemorative release of the Kaiser Chiefs Lap Of Honour album includes seven unreleased and live tracks, plus remixes from Japan's own wonder-band Polysics! Universal. 2006.

Lap of Honour is an EP released in January 18, 2005 as a Japan-only release by British rock band Kaiser Chiefs, though it is available for import. The EP mostly consists of b-sides featured on the single releases from their debut album Employment, plus live sessions and an exclusive remix of 'Na Na Na Na Naa'. The album cover depicts the Royal coat of arms of the United Kingdom.
