Packing a mighty punch, WE THE KINGS, one of the most engaging and exciting acts to emerge: talent, ambition, hard work and with a knack for writing citrus-sweet hooks, they display an energy that never fails to be infectious. 'Check Yes Juliet' was this bands first major Australian hit shooting to the top of the charts, it is well on the way to Platinum + sales. This track appears as the first of two very special bonus tracks on the album alongside the song 'Summer'.
