Album Intro：

來自德克薩斯的The Secret Handshake差不多是Luis Dubuc的個人樂隊，之前另有作品-2專輯2EP，充斥著合成器的跳動和嶄新元素的嘗試，含雜著爵士情結和R&B式醒目悅耳旋律。

Luis Dubuc, a.k.a. The Secret Handshake, returns with his sophomore effort; an album that combines elements from an array of genres resulting in a unique sound that's equal parts electronic, pop, hip-hop, and alternative. Described by Alternative Press as 'straight out of a video game and echoing Hellogoodbye hopped up on fun dip, TSH is one solo artist who doesn't bitch or appear to use meds to solve his love woes'. RIYL: Metro Station, Fall Out Boy, The Maine, Gym Class Heroes, Daft Punk.
