Album Intro：

2013 release from bassist, songwriter, and vocalist Stephen Bruner, AKA Thundercat. As Thundercat, Bruner takes his Jazz roots and works with a mix of artists that suit his wildly experimental sensibilities. Thundercat teamed up with executive producer Flying Lotus once again, to form a profound body of work for his second album, Apocalypse, The album straddles lines and pushes genres further, blurring the confines of Pop, Funk, Electronica and Prog Rock, and creating something else entirely. Both vulnerable and fearless, a comedy and tragedy, Apocalypse is an intimate portrait of an artist who will continue to take music to a new place; the beyond.
