Johnny Orlando (born January 24, 2003) is a Canadian pop singer, most noted for receiving a Juno Award nomination for Breakthrough Artist of the Year at the Juno Awards of 2019.

Orlando first received attention on social media by posting covers of pop songs by artists such as Austin Mahone and Justin Bieber to his YouTube channel.

He signed with Universal Music Canada in May 2018, and released the singles 'What If' and 'Last Summer' through the remainder of 2018. In November, he performed with Carly Rae Jepsen at the public unveiling of the Christmas display at the Hudson Bay Company's flagship store in downtown Toronto.

In 2018, he starred in the first 2 seasons of Brat drama series, Total Eclipse. His full debut EP is slated for release in 2019.
