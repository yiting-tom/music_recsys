Album Intro：

Collection of tracks from the late '50s female vocal trio, best known for their hit 'Born Too Late'. While still in their late teens when they first started recording, The Poni-Tails were one of Cleveland's brightest girl groups. After signing to ABC/Paramount, they achieved great success with this compilation's title track, which was originally the b-side of their second single for the label. By 1960, with a new contract in front of them, the girls decided against pursuing a career in music and left the business entirely. This collection is testament to their talents.
