Album Intro：

On February 20th, The Ataris will release their first full-length album since 2003's blockbuster hit So Long, Astoria. Welcome the Night is a record that showcases the band's musical growth. This time around, the band has delivered a more mature and diverse record. While they haven't lost their roots, there's something here for everyone; artists like Coldplay and Interpol can just as easily be referenced as the band's more obvious punk influences. It's an album that's guaranteed to satisfy Ataris fans, and to win over the hearts of new listeners.
