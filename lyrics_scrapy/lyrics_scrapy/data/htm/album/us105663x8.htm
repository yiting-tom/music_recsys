Album Intro：

曾經想過很多方式來描述我的新作品，但都差那麼ㄧ點。

於是，我回歸到這張專輯的初衷。

當知道宇宙很大，甚至有外宇宙的時候，小小的一顆地球裡的ㄧ座小小的台灣的ㄧ個小小房間裡，做著自己想做的事，享受在這宇宙裡。

別懷疑自己。

I have imagined many ways to describe my new work.
But all fall short.

So I reach back to the original intention of the album.

The universe is immense, with universes beyond. Inside a small room, in small Taiwan, on this small planet…
Do what you yearn to do.
Delight in this world.

Do not doubt yourself.
