Album Intro：

RANDY KOHRS is the consummate music man singer, songwriter, master resophonic guitarist and Grammy ® winning producer. A #1 video on CMTPure proved that the mainstream country audience welcomes his progressive acoustic sound and an appearance on the Rachael Ray Show further introduced him to the mainstream audience. 'QUICKSAND,' is his 5th album and showcases every one of those skills. The single, 'Devil of the Trail' begins the album which contains styles ranging from hard driving bluegrass to blues, transcending genres solidifying Randy as a pioneer in progressive acoustic music.
