Album Intro：

2011 album from the Metal maniac and former Skid Row frontman. In early 2011, Bach headed into the studio to record under the direction of Bob Marlette (Black Sabbath, Shinedown, Atreyu, Filter). The result is a high-energy, hard rock album along the lines of Skid Row, Slave To The Grind, Subhuman Race and Angel Down, but the songs, performances and sound quality are all heavy, interesting and completely Rock `n' Roll. The album cover, which was created by artist Richard Villa, reflects the lyrics as well as the brutality of the music. If you like rockin' guitar riffs, high-energy songs and performances, deadly drums of doom, and ear-shattering screams, we got your Rock `n Roll fix right here!
