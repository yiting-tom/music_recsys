Album Intro：

Innovative and gorgeous arrangements of some of the most beloved songs in popular music, as well
as two debut recordings of new songs by Chicago songwriters.

Previous CDs, Sailing On and Written in the Stars,
received international acclaim including CD of the
Year from WDVR-FM Radio and After Dark Chicago,
as well as Show Business Weekly, album spotlights
from Billboard, and international radio airplay.
One of today s premier male cabaret vocalists.
Listed as one of Cabaret Scenes magazine s Top 100
Cabaret Acts.
Features jazz guitarist virtuoso Fareed Haque, who
has worked with Cassandra Wilson, Ramsey Lewis,
and Kurt Elling; saxophonist Jim Gailloreto, who
has played for Mel Torme, Diane Schurr, The
Manhattan Transfer, Johnny Mathis, and Garth
Brooks; and nationally recognized cabaret recording
artist Beckie Menzie, who supplied the arrangements
and also vocals for three duets.
