Album Intro：

UK vinyl LP pressing. 2009 sophomore album from the British rockers. The Rifles burst onto the U.K. music scene two years ago with the rebellious and attitude laden debut, No Love Lost, an album revealing the hopes and dreams of working-class suburban drudgery. Since then, The Rifles have been catapulted up the ladder to their rightful place of music colossus. With Jan 'Stan' Kybert (Weller, Oasis, Bjork) on production duties, singer Joel Stoker, guitarist Luke Crowther, bassist Rob Pyne, and drummer Grant Marsh's spiky guitar sound has been refined to a sharp point of perfection. Features the singles 'Fall To Sorrow' and 'The Great Escape'. 679 Recordings.
