Album Intro：

Known for his work with The Beatles circa 1962-65, Norman 'Hurricane' Smith's additional recording credits include works with Pink Floyd, Stevie Wonder, Little Richard and more. Transitioning from producer to recording artist, he found immediate success with his #1 hit 'Oh Babe, What Would You Say.' From Me to You, includes never-before-seen photos of Norman and the Beatles, messages from members of Pink Floyd, and a personal tribute from Sir Paul McCartney. It further contains in-depth interviews in which he discusses his time with The Beatles, Pink Floyd and chapters of his remarkable musical career.
