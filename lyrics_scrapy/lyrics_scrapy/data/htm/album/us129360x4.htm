Album Intro：

Explicit Version of this 2008 album from the Atlanta rapper and member of D4L. Carlos Walker was raised in West Atlanta's notorious Bowen Homes. He was known in the hood as Shawty Lo, a natural-born hustler. Looking for a new hustle he became the CEO of D4L Records in 2003. They released Down for Life in partnership with Dee Money Entertainment and Asylum Records, with Shawty Lo as Executive producer in 2005. The group consisted of Shawty Lo, Fabo, Stuntman and Mook. Before they knew it, 'Laffy Taffy' and 'Betcha Can't Do It Like Me' had taken the airwaves by storm. Down For Life went on to become a certified gold album. 'Laffy Taffy' broke records as the most downloaded song in the history of music according to the 2007 Guinness Book of World Records plus becoming a multi-platinum ring tone and winning an ASCAP award.
