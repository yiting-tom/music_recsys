Album Intro：

Someone Still Loves You Boris Yeltsin are prolific songwriters despite only having released three albums in their 10-year history. In the summer of 2012, the band went through their archive of over 100 songs and picked out the cream of the crop for a truly unique double album that encapsulates the entire band's career.
