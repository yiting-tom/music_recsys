Album Intro：

Our Last Night emerge as the fresh new face of rock with their explosive debut, The Ghosts Among Us. Produced by Steve Evetts (Senses Fail, Every Time I Die), the dynamic new album pushes boundaries with its blood pumping cocktail of brooding hardcore and cathartic rock. Lush melodic soundscapes interlaced with taut thrashing riffs on tracks like the irresistibly catchy 'Escape' and the discordant anthem 'Recovery' are a brash declaration of the genre's next heavyweights.
