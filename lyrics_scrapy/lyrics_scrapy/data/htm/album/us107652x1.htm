Album Intro：

Bomb in a Birdcage is the second album from singer-songwriter Alison Sudol aka A Fine Frenzy. This album features 11 infectious, bubbly fun tracks infused with gorgeous lyrics and danceable beats. It is set for release on August 28, 2009 in Germany and on September 8, 2009 in the United States. The album's first single, 'Blow Away', was released digitally via iTunes in July 2009, its second, 'Happier', was released in August 2009, also through iTunes, as well as it's third, 'Electric Twist'
