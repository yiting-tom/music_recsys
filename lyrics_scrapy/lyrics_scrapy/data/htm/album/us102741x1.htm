Album Intro：

Limited vinyl LP pressing includes a bonus seven inch single plus a CD edition of the album. 1982 album from the Australian Alt/Goth Rock band featuring singer/songwriter Nick Cave. The bonus seven inch features 'Release The Bats' and 'Blast Off'. The vinyl LP uses the 2000 remaster while the single is a new remaster taken from the original analog studio tapes.
