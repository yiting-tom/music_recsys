Album Intro：

The CRUXSHADOWS fifth studio album is a concept disc that transcends the goth-industrial genre by merging elements of pop, new wave and electronic music to produce a unique musical signature. Discarding the negativity often associated with the genre, Ethernaut is dark and powerful, but also positive and hope filled. Featuring new cover art, digitally remastered audio and presented in a clean, new digipak design with lyric sheet, the album that broke all the rules in 2003 is available once again.
