Album Intro：

International edition features six bonus tracks including two non-album cuts and four remixes. 2009 album from the '60s influenced Pop trio, The Postmarks recorded Memoirs between August 2008 and February 2009 in their home recording studio, taking a collaborative approach to the process. The resulting album is a sublime marriage of cinematic melodies, sonic experimentation, classic Soul harmonies and female vocalist Tim Yehezkely's sultry, world-weary vocals. In short, it's that rare beast, an album that oozes instant appeal as well as rewarding repeated listens.
