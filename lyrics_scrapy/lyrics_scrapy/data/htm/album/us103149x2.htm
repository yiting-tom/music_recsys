Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded deluxe two CD edition includes bonus CD with 15 rarities and unreleased tracks. Back in 1997 Saint Etienne addressed their quintessentially British typecasting by writing an album full of songs about a fantasy US of A, recording it in Sweden and making videos in Havana. The resulting album, Good Humor (named after the US ice cream company) is timeless pop that is full of possibilities. It is home to Top 30 hits 'Sylvie' and 'The Bad Photographer'. The album itself reached #18 on its release in 1998. Universal.
