Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded two CD edition of the British band's 1998 album including a bonus CD that contains 12 additional tracks. Originally known as the Spoilt Bratz, Bradford's Terrorvision (named after an '80s US comedy horror movie) signed to EMI Records in 1991 under their very own imprint label, Total Vegas Recordings and there was no looking back for the party-rock-four-piece of Tony Wright (vocals), Leigh Marklew (Bass), Mark Yates (Guitar) and Ian 'Shutty' Shuttleworth (drums). Terrorvision were also becoming known for their 'never fail to satisfy' live performances and in so doing became the default festival band during this mid 90s period. Cherry Red.
