Album Intro：

Limited UK vinyl LP pressing of their 2005 album. Crazed bullet belt and high-top Thrash Metal from the fiery pits of America. Toxic Holocaust carries on in the grand tradition of Destruction, Kreator, Whiplash, and Bathory, with songs about nuclear war, Satan, and other wholesome themes. This is the soundtrack to getting high on magic marker fumes and cheap domestic beer in your parents' basement circa 1986. 12 tracks. Relapse.
