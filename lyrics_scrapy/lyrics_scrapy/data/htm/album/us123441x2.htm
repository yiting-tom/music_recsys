Album Intro：

'The Exploders are the hottest young band out of regional Australia since Spiderbait. They've been refining their raw rock over the past year, on stages in Melb & regional Vic., & gained recognition for their ability to tame beats and guitar hooks with a driving groove of Motown soul. Features a rhythm section that you'd swear knows every Cream & Hendrix tune ever penned, The Exploders have an urgent and electrifying guitar sound that'll hit ya smack in the face. With a love for the raw rock, this classic 3 piece are set to take Australian rock devotees by storm, as they get set to release their self titled album.'
