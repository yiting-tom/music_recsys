Album Intro：

The View are an indie rock band from Dundee. They also include styles such as punk and pop in their music.

The band are currently signed to 1965 Records and their debut single, 'Wasted Little DJs', was released on August 7, 2006. The single entered the MTV Two/NME charts on July 9, 2006, eventually reaching number one on August 13, 2006. On the same day, the song reached number 15 in the UK Singles Chart.

The View released their debut album Hats Off to the Buskers on January 22, 2007, which entered the UK Album Chart at number one the following week. It was nominated for the 2007 Mercury Music Prize.

Which Bitch? is the second album from Scottish band The View and is scheduled for release 2 February 2009.Paolo Nutini guests on the track 'Covers'.
