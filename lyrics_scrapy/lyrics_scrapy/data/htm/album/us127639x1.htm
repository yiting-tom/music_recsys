Album Intro：

Originally formed in Oslo, Norway in 1989, Turbonegro returns in 2012 with their 9th album ''Sexual Harassment''. Not only one of Scandinavia's leading mainstream rock bands, but Turbonegro is recognized as international punk rock legend. Based on a musical blend of hardcore punk, metal and classic rock, Turbonegro is approachable on many levels - as a masterclass in nonconformist punk-rock attitude; a veritable orgy of twisted humour; an exploration of dark, self-destructive philosophy; as formularized performance a la AC/DC/Ramones/Slayer; as ritualized sexual deviancy; and, of course, as ass-shakin' rock & roll, second to none.
