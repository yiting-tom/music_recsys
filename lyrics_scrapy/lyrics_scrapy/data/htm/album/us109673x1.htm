Album Intro：

Glaswegian rockers Glasvegas follow-up 2008's highly-acclaimed self-titled debut with Euphoric Heartbreak, which was recorded in London and produced by Flood (Smashing Pumpkins,Sigur Ros). Starting off with spoken word piece 'Pain Pain,Never Again', the album features emotional, anthemic choruslines and reverb-drenched guitars backed by pounding drums,while singer James Allen's mother gives her own spoken word performance on final track, 'Change'.
