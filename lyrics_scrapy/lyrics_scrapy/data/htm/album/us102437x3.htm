Album Intro：

Musical troubadour RICK PRICE possesses one of the most celebrated and soulful voices in contemporary Australian music. By popular and constant request from his most loyal fans, REVISITED, a collection of Rick's greatest hits re-recorded in an intimate acoustic style, sees Rick revisiting and recasting the best loved songs of his career, including all his classic hit singles - 'Not A Day Goes By', 'Heaven Knows', 'River Of Love', 'Walk Away Renee' and many more mainstays of classic Australian radio.
