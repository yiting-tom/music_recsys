Album Intro：

Fly Yellow Moon is the debut album from Guillemots singer-songwriter Fyfe Dangerfield. Written over twelve months and recorded in just five days in the aftermath of his band's 2008 Red album, Fly Yellow Moon features ten tracks penned by Dangerfield, including 'When You Walk in the Room' and the single 'She Needs Me'
