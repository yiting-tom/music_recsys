Album Intro：

Sambora has an appealing persona, an inoffensive vocal style and an eager embrace for every cliché in the rock-and-roll thesaurus.... The songs are mostly love and power anthems full of vague imagery and emotion.... Sambora makes music that you can't really dance to, although it would be a great accompaniment to swaying back and forth on the floor of a concert hall.... -- People
