Album Intro：

'Desert Lights' follows 2003's #1 seller 'The Official Fiction' and is a powerful mix of discord and melody, sophistication and beauty and signals a band reaching a heightened level of creativity. Overseen by respected American engineer / producer Brad Wood (whose extensive credits take in Liz Phair, Tortoise and Ben Lee), 'Desert Lights' puts aside the tone of the band's previous hit albums, 'The Official Fiction' and 'Echolalia' and the change is both apparent and appealing, setting a new standard for the trio.
