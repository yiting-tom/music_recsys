Album Intro：

那天，災厄降臨之時，烙下永恆的傷痕。所有的一切，都毀滅殆盡。
心靈彷彿被什麼枷鎖著，試圖找到一條，能夠逃離的道路，卻看不到任何的光，看著殘破不堪的一切，我迷失了。
我甚至無法看清，是甚麼將我給綑綁，歇斯底里的嚎叫著，發狂般的拉扯著，終於我看見了那綑綁著我的，是自己的懦弱。
我朝著他怒吼著，醒來吧，從懦弱中解放吧，抬起頭面對自己，起身前行吧，就算前方一片黑暗，也必須前進。
即使遍體鱗傷，也要拋開膽怯，試著穿越黑暗，只要深信，終會找到屬於自己的樂園，沒有痛苦沒有謊言沒有戰亂的新世界。

On that day, the disaster befell. It left a permanent scar on us, leaving our world in ruins.
My soul, somehow imprisoned, strove to find a way out, but all I could see was darkness. Looking at this mess, I was totally lost.
I couldn’t even see clearly what had imprisoned me. Howling in hysteria and struggling in despair, I finally realized one thing—it was my cowardice that imprisoned me.
I growled at him, “Wake up! Liberate yourself from cowardice! It’s time to face the music and move on.”
Beaten black and blue as you are, you still have to get rid of timidity and get through the darkness.
As long as we believe in ourselves, we will eventually find our Eden, a utopia without agonies, lies, and wars.
