Album Intro：

Charismatic front man Alex Roy belts out lyrics that tell an animated story of hopes, dreams, lost and found love, and the desire to make music his life. This is their debut. They've shared stages with Saosin, Forever The Sickest Kids, and All Time Low, and played over 200 shows since forming in 2005.
