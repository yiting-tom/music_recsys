Album Intro：

Digitally remastered 10th Anniversary edition of the Indie/Emo Rock band's 2003 full length debut album including five bonus tracks. Kill Them With Kindness was recorded with producer Tim O'Heir and originally released on Better Looking Records. The album was rated at #39 for Spin magazine's '40 Best Albums of 2003' list. The magazine's review in June 2003, by Andy Greenwald, rated the album as '9 of 10'.
