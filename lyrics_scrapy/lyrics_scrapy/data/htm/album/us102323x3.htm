Album Intro：

The international version of the Ms. Kelly re-release will be in stores on May 12, 2008, re-titled as Ms. Kelly Deluxe. The physical album release will remove eight tracks from the original release, including the album versions of both 'Work' and 'Comeback' along with 'Still In Love With My Ex', 'Flashback', 'The Show', 'Ghetto', 'Interlude' and 'Gotsta Go', replacing them with six of the seven Diva Deluxe tracks. The Redline remix of 'Like This' will be replaced by Freemasons radio edit of 'Work'.
