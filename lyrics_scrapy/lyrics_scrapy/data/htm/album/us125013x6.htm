Album Intro：

Raleigh, NC's purveyors of mod style pop, The Rosebuds, are back with six songs of perfect melody, harmony and beat stylings! The 'Rosebuds Unwind' are the first new songs from Ivan Howard and Kelly Crisp since 2003's critically acclaimed full length debut The Rosebuds Makeout and the duo are in top form, delivering hooks galore and lush romantic ballads, too. Merge. 2005.
