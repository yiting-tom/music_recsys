Album Intro：

US only edition features a bonus disc with eight bonus live tracks; Wild Mountainside, Send For Henny - (featuring Eddi Reader), All the Dark Horses, Easy Read, Safecracker, Weightlifting, Hayfever and Earlies. 2009 album from the Scottish Pop maestros. In the five years since we last heard a studio album from Trashcan Sinatras, things have been looking up. After more than two decades in the business the guys behind Cake, I've Seen Everything, A Happy Pocket and 2004's Weightlifting have decided to throw caution to the wind and get happy. The result, In the Music, is a collection of love songs, full of relaxed harmonies, which were set to arrangements in a back room in the South Side of Glasgow over the summer of 2007, and recorded in New York and Marthas Vineyard over a crisp, cold winter in 2008. With highlights including 'Should I Pray' featuring special guest vocals by the iconic Carly Simon, and 'Oranges and Apples', a seven minute long tribute to Syd Barrett. 18 tracks.
