Country Musume( Country Girls )【 8 albums 54 lyrics 】
Country Musume (カントリー娘。 Kantorī Musume) is a Japanese female idol music group that is a part of the Hello! Project.

On November 5, 2014, Country Musume was renamed Country Girls (カントリー・ガールズ Kantorī Gāruzu).
