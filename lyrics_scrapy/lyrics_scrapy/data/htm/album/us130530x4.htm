Album Intro：

Somegirl presents the band's second album, featuring the new dynamic vocalist, Anada. The songs are catchy, dreamy, emotional, and sometimes even playful, wandering a wide range of emotions and sounds. From the romantic cover of the old blues hit 'I Only Have Eyes for You', to the poppy 'Feel Free', to the brooding 'Forgive You', Somegirl displays solid talent, excellent musical production, and contributes their own special touch to the modern synthpop scene.
