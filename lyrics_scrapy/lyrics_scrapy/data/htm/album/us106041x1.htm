Album Intro：

2007 debut album from the Canadian/British Electro-Pop outfit. Dragonette make sharp, sardonically witty pop songs (think Goldfrapp hanging out with their best friend Gwen Stefani at a Scissor Sisters party). They are ¾ Canadian (Dan, Martina and Joel) and ¼ British (guitarist Will). The band have been playing shows and making friends across the UK since decamping there in 2006. More than anything, Dragonette is a band having fun. 11 tracks including the singles 'Take It Like a Man' and 'I Get Around'.
