Album Intro：

Sleepy LaBeef is a force of nature, exclaims Eddie Gorodetsky in his liner notes. One listen to this disc will transport you to the mossy swamps of South Arkansas, into a land where John Lee Hooker and Hank Williams are friendly neighbors. Backed by an ace band -- including guitar wizard Duke Levine and barefoot drummer extraordinaire, Lisa Pankrantz -- Mr. LaBeef is a rock 'n' roll bulldozer, charging through the album's 11 choice songs with little regard for the safety of his listeners.
