Album Intro：

Here's the first ever legitimate CD from this pop/doo wop vocal group who rose to fame backing Nat King Cole on a string of '50s hit 45s. This collection includes their UK hit 'I Get So Lonely (When I Think About You)' which reached #5 on the NME charts in 1954. The whole CD was sourced from original EMI master tapes and compiled at Abbey Road Studios in London. Acrobat. 2004.
