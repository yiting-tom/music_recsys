Sultry and quiet defines NAKED WITHOUT YOU – THE EXPANDED EDITION from Taylor Dayne. Taylor has added a softness and a mellowness that allows her to sing with a sense of nurturing and empathy that would put her in the company of angels and is sure to please any devotee of great music.
