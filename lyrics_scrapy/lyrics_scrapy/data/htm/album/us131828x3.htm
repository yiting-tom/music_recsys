Album Intro：

Following their 2010 collaboration with Madlib on In Search of Stoney Jackson, Strong Arm Steady has teamed up
with Brooklyn-based producer/DJ Statik Selektah for their second Stones Throw release, the 14-track album Stereotype. The Album features appearances from the extended Strong Arm family Planet Asia and Chace Infinite,plus West Coast newcomers Casey Veggies, Schoolboy Q and the Black Hippy crew and southern rap veteran
David Banner.
