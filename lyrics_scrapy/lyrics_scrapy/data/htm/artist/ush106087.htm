Framing Hanley was an American rock band formed in Nashville, Tennessee in 2005. They released their first studio album in August 2007, titled The Moment.

Members

Final Line-Up

Kenneth Nixon - lead vocals, piano, rhythm guitar (2005–2015)
Ryan Belcher - lead guitar, backing vocals (2007–2015)
Jonathan Stoye - bass, backing vocals (2013-2015)
Brandon Wootten - rhythm guitar, lead guitar, backing vocals (2005–2015)
Chris Vest - drums (2005–2015)



Former members

Tim Huskinson - lead guitar (2005–2007)
Luke McDuffee - bass, backing vocals (2005–2013)
