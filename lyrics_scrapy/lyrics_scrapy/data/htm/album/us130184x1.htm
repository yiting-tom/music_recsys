Album Intro：

1996 compilation for James Moore from the vaults of the Nashville-based Excello label features 25 tracks recorded between 1967-1969, highlighted by the r&b hits 'Tip On In', 'Tee-Ni-Nee-Ni-Nu', 'I'm Gonna Keep What I've Got' & a cover of Johnny Cash's 'Folsom Prison Blues'. Includes 8-page booklet with liner notes & memorabilia. Ace.
