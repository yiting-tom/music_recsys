Album Intro：

2009 Holiday release. David Seville and his original Chipmunks have continued to be a phenomenon, appealing to each and every generation since their inception in the late '50s. This collection includes 20 cuts lifted from their original Christmas albums released during the height of their '60s popularity. Listen to Alvin, Theodore and Simon sing your favorite Holiday tracks like 'Silver Bells', 'Rudolph The Red Nosed Reindeer', 'Deck The Halls' and many others.
