Album Intro：

Over their tenure as the progenitors of the metal-infused punk sound, strung out have yet to get complacent. Instead, like a championship fighter that gets stronger with every round, they continually unleash a battering assault of technical guitar work and gripping melodies. Agents of the underground with blisteringly fast riffs, crushing rhythms, and powerful vocals, it is quintessential strung out, yet the band manage to evolve their sound. Enlisting the producing talents of cameron webb (motorhead, social distortion, danzig, pennywise), the success of the collaboration is overtly apparent.
