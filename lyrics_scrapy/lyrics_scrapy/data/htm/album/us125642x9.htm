Album Intro：

Digitally remastered edition of this 1982 album from the British Rock/Glam outfit, released shortly before the band went their separate ways. One of the most successful bands of the entire 70's Glam-era, Sweet had hits on all sides of the globe and their influence has been acknowledged by the likes of Def Leppard, Kiss and The Damned amongst many others. This re-issue comes with original LP artwork and detailed liner notes. 7Ts. 2010.
