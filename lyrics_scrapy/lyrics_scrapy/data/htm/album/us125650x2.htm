Album Intro：

Available for the first time on vinyl, from the Oscar winning duo of the movie Once. The Swell Season was released prior to the Once soundtrack. When it came time to put the soundtrack together, the band re-recorded four of the songs for the soundtrack. The other six songs are specific to this release and not available elsewhere.
