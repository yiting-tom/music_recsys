Album Intro：

Based out of Sacramento, CA, this four-piece punk band Trash Talk release their first album of new material in two years. Long a staple of indie retail and the hardcore circuit, Trash Talk step up to the plate on Eyes & Nines with a specially priced album ready for the national stage.
With their live chops maximized and their studio skills optimized, Trash Talk is poised to take that next big step. Featuring influences ranging from American punk bands Bad Brains and Black Flag to legendary jazz musicians John Coltrane and Miles Davis, Eyes & Nines is without doubt a crucial addition to the American hardcore discography. With a history boasting a myriad of sold out releases, an unparalelled touring history with bands like Alexisonfire, Coliseum, and Paint it Black (among other), Trash Talk carry the DIY spirit of punk into the digital age.
With Eyes & Nines, Trash Talk keeps up their pace while introducing more structure making this album their most focused, cohesive to date. From the sludge influenced Hash Wednesday to the anthemic, blast-beat laden Explode , this album features ten brand new crushing tracks for hardcore and metal fans the world over!
