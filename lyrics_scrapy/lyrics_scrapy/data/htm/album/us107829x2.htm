Album Intro：

2013 release, the third album from the Scottsdale-based Punk Pop band, the follow-up to their second official album Everything's Fine. After a few years of playing music together, Jess Bowen and brothers John and Stephen Gomez, set out in the fall of 2007 to form The Summer Set with newfound vocalist Brian Dales. The band's debut album, Love Like This, was released in 2009. They re-released their debut album, re-titled Love Like Swift, in 2010, featuring covers of five Taylor Swift songs.
