Album Intro：

The Blackout an alternative rock band from Merthyr Tydfil (Wales) inspired by the likes of Lostprophets. The name was taken from a t-shirt that read 'I survived the blackout' - referring to the New York City blackouts in 1977. 2CD - Deluxe limited edition with bonus disc of additional content (tracks TBC) and enhanced packaging.
