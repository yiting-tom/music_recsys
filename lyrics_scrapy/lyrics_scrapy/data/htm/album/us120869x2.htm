Album Intro：

The battle call has been raised! With their unstoppable new album, Sweden's mighty Unleashed have forged the blood and spirit of their Viking ancestry into a modern weapon of Death Metal mastery. A true rallying cry for the dedicated metalhead legions around the globe, Unleashed have done what previously seemed impossible: they raised the intensity level without losing their trademark skill of melding uncompromising brutality with extreme listenability. Unleashed have not only worn their Viking heritage on their sleeve for years, they've also proven to be one of history's most important flag bearers for metalheads around the globe. On their Nuclear Blast debut, they go above and beyond and prove that, even 20+ years into their career, Unleashed do it bigger and better than anyone else!
