Album Intro：

LTM are proud to announce the first reissue on CD of the entire works of The Field Mice, the cult South London band who recorded for the Sarah label between 1988 and 1991. Skywriting + Singles combines the second Field Mice album Skywriting (released in June 1990) with all tracks from the EPs The Autumn Store and So Said Kay. For the album the band had expanded from a duo to a three piece, and drawing influence from the dance and ambient scenes. Over 90 minutes over two discs, features 21 remastered tracks, and contains detailed biographical notes in the booklet. 2005.
