Boyfriend【 23 albums 135 lyrics 】
Boyfriend (Korean: 보이프렌드) is a South Korean boy group formed by Starship Entertainment in 2011. They are the first boy band to have twin members. The group consists of Kim Donghyun, Shim Hyunseong, Lee Jeongmin, Jo Youngmin, Jo Kwangmin, and No Minwoo. They debuted on Mnet's M!Countdown on May 26, 2011 with 'Boyfriend'.

Members
Donghyun, born Kim Dong-hyun (Hangul: 김동현) on February 12, 1989.
Hyunseong, born Shim Hyun-seong (Hangul: 심현성) on June 9, 1993.
Jeongmin, born Lee Jeong-min (Hangul: 이정민) on January 2, 1994.
Youngmin, born Jo Young-min (Hangul: 조영민) on April 24, 1995.
Kwangmin, born Jo Kwang-min (Hangul: 조광민) on April 24, 1995.
Minwoo, born No Min-woo (Hangul: 노민우) on July 31, 1995 .
