Album Intro：

With over 22 million plays on MySpace, three sold out U.S. tours and over 60,000 copies sold of their last album His Last Walk (2006), the story of blessthefall is already an impressive tale. Witness, the band's second album is a brain-blisteringly epic aural assault, combining their trademark stabbing riffs and soaring melody with a whole new energy. blessthefall has wowed crowds at Bamboozle, Warped Tour and Taste Of Chaos, and on the road with August Burns Red, Avenged Sevenfold, Atreyu and Silverstein. Their live attack continues throughout the Fall by co-headlining the Atticus Tour.
