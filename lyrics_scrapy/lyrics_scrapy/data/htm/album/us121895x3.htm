Album Intro：

Part three of the Five Atomic Seasons pulls together strands from Zen Buddhism and western psychology to reflect both the trauma and healing that took place in the wake of the atomic attack.

Packaged with liner notes that tell the story of Mr. H.T., the Japanese businessman who commissioned the piece, seeking to record and commemorate his personal experience in both cities at the conclusion of World War II. --Official Press Release
