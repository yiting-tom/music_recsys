Album Intro：

From Cleveland via New York and LA, Joshua Radin is the uniquely word-of-mouth global success story that's just about to get a whole lot bigger with the release of his third album The Rock and The Tide, due September 7th on Mom + Pop.

Radin only turned to music after college-a friend gave a demo to a TV producer who promptly used it in Scrubs in early 2004. Other Hollywood types found his music just as evocative, and soon various Radin songs were being heard 75 times on TV shows (Grey's Anatomy, Brothers and Sisters, American Idol), as well as movies (The Nanny Diaries, The Last Kiss) and ads.

His debut We Were Here (Columbia) drew critical acclaim, and 2008's follow up Simple Times saw Radin collaborating with producer Rob Schnapf (Beck, Elliott Smith), selling over 140,000 copies in the US and 500,000 downloads worldwide.
