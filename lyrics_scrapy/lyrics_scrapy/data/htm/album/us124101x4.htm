Album Intro：

Is an exclusive 4 song cd ep by The Ladybug Transistor released on Spanish label Green UFOs. It's a conceptual record about the arrival of the fall, with four covers of songs originally performed by Grin, Trader Horne, John Cale, and Kevin Ayers. The Ladybugs are joined by several special guests throughout, with contributions from: Alicia Vanden Heuvel (Aislers Set) Gus Franklin, Isobel Knowles, Keli Sutherland (Architecture in Helsinki), Jens Lekman, Kevin Barker (Currituck Co) and Paul Niehaus (Calexico, Lambchop). The artwork is printed on the rough side of the cardboard, giving the record an irrisistible oldies look!
