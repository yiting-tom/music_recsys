Fishmans【 8 albums 111 lyrics 】
Fishmans (Japanese: フィッシュマンズ Hepburn: Fisshumanzu?) was a Japanese dub music band formed in 1987, known for their unique dub-based sound and the distinctive vocals of lead singer Shinji Sato.

Members

Shinji Sato (佐藤 伸治?) – vocals, guitar, trumpet
Kin-ichi Motegi (茂木 欣一?) – drums, sampler, backing vocals
Yuzuru Kashiwabara (柏原 譲?) – bass (1988–1998)
Hakase (ハカセ?) – keyboard (1990–1995)
Kensuke Ojima (小嶋 謙介?) – guitar, backing vocals (1987–1994)

Support members

Darts Sekiguchi (ダーツ関口?) – guitar
Honzi – keyboard, violin, accordion, backing vocals
Zak – sound mix
