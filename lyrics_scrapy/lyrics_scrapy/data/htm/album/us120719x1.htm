Album Intro：

Highly anticipated 2013 release, the fourth album from well-respected German electronic artist and DJ Ulrich Schnauss. Well known for his unique blend of melodic Electronica, this album continues the multilayered, texturally rich sounds of past works, with a new updated contemporary production. Ulrich s music has been heavily used in syncs and film, and as a well known remixer he has mixed artists from Coldplay to Depeche Mode.
