Album Intro：

Sandra is the shortened name for successful German singer Sandra Cretu (vocals), who usually works in collaboration with Michael Cretu as writer and producer. After enjoying some success with the disco trio Arabesque Sandra began performing under her solo name in 1984. Other members of her team include Hubert Kemmler (singer and song writer), Markus Löhr, Klaus Hirschburger, Armand Volker, Frank Peterson, Andy Jonas (singer) and Jens Gad (song writer, co-producer).

She has sold over 12 million albums to date. Sandra and Michael Cretu are also well known for their work under the name 'Enigma'.
