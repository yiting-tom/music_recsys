Album Intro：

Unholy, from Finland, formed in 1988, were among the first doom metal bands from the country. This DVD/CD set includes the band's second studio album, and continued the boundary breaking approach to experimental doom metal which the band had established with their debut album 'From the Shadows,' mixed with strong atmospherics and unusual structures. This edition is a dream come true for fans, as the band themselves have provided footage of a rare live performance in Imatra, Finland, from 1994, featuring classic Unholy tracks.
