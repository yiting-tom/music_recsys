Album Intro：

Through his work in Thin Lizzy, Ultravox and of course Band Aid, Midge Ure became a household name in the UK. His second solo album, Answers to Nothing, originally released in 1988, was his first following the break up of Ultravox, and includes the track 'Sister and Brother', a duet with Kate Bush. This edition is a brand new digital remaster of the album, complete with an extra disc containing 15 bonus tracks (six of which are previously unreleased), including new mixes and recordings of a Wembley Arena live show from 1985.
