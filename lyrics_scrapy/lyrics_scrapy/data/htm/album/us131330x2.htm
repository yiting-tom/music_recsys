Album Intro：

The release of their 21st studio album, Cogs, Wheels & Lovers, is a record that will typically see Steeleye Span buck their own trend and go back to their roots; the interpretation of traditional song. This though, has always been the Steeleye way. Tied not to the past, the present or the future - simply timeless. The band has seldom been more active in its long career and the current lineup of Maddy Prior, Peter Knight, Rick Kemp, Liam Genockey and Ken Nicol has in fact become the longest serving of any across the years.
