Album Intro：

'Weights & Wings' is the fourth album by American singer/songwriter Matt Wertz. It was released on March 15, 2011 and was preceded by the single 'Feels So Right', which was released in January.

The album was named before any of its songs were written, and the concept of Weights & Wings not only acted as a compass for the project, but also helped guide Wertz personally, as well. Wertz explains: 'I got the title from a daily meditation book that I read. It's the idea that the heavy things in life and the transcendent times are both equally important in propelling us on and through life.'

All of the songs on Wertz's album are tied to relationships, and each track delves into what the singer has learned about himself and others during that last few years.
