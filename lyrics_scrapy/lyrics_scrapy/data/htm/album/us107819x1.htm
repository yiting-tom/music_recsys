Album Intro：

Humanoid is the third German studio album and the second English studio album by German band Tokio Hotel. The album was recorded in both German and English with both versions set for a simultaneous worldwide release bearing the same title, Humanoid. The German version of the album will be released on October 2, 2009 in Germany and other European countries while the English version will be released in the United States on October 6, 2009.

The first single, 'Automatisch' ('Automatic' in English speaking territories), is set for release in mid-September.
