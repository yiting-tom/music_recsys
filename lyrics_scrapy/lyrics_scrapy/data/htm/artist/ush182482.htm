James Maslow( James David Maslow )【 3 albums 22 lyrics 】
James David Maslow (born July 16, 1990) is an American actor, and singer. He played the role of James Diamond on Nickelodeon's Big Time Rush and was a member of the boy band with the same name. He also starred as Kevin Mohr on the Sony Crackle original series Sequestered.

In 2014, Maslow competed as a contestant on the eighteenth season of Dancing with the Stars, in which he reached fourth place. In 2018, Maslow was cast as one of the celebrity houseguests on the first-ever American version of Celebrity Big Brother where Maslow finished in 6th place.
