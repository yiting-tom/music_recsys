Album Intro：

Limited two disc (CD + PAL/Region 2 DVD) edition. 2010 release, the third album from the Swedish Death Metal band, the first to feature new vocalist Nathan J. Biggs. After having multiple chart entries all over Europe with their last album Love And Other Disasters, Sonic Syndicate shocked the world with their intense shows while supporting Nightwish on their US tour. The six piece then went on to support In Flames, Deathstars and others. We Rule The Night is a result of everything that Sonic Syndicate has been and has become.
