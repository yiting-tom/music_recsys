Album Intro：

With an infectiously energetic sound that draws equal comparisons to pop and arena rock, The Bigger Lights have made significant strides since forming in 2007. This is the physical follow-up to their digitally released debut EP 'Fiction Fever', from October 2008. It boasts tons of extra content such as videos, exclusive photos, wallpaper, buddy icons, and bonus acoustic/remix versions of all album tracks.
