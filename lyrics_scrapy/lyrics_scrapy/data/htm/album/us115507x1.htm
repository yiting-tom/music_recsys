Album Intro：

European pressing of the debut album from Japanese Hip Hop outfit The Teriyaki Boyz, released through Def Jam Recordings/Ape Sounds. Featuring some of Japan's dopest MC's (Ilmari and Ryo-Z from Rip Slyme, Verbal from m-flo plus Wise and Nigo from Bathing Ape), this is a tongue-in-cheek tribute to '80s rap with the help of The Neptunes, Daft Punk, DJ Premier, DJ Shadow, Adrock (Beastie Boys), Cornelius, Mark Ronson, The Alchemist, Dan The Automator, Pharrell and more. The Teriyaki Boyz have caused quite a stir in the industry lately with their contributions to the soundtrack to The Fast And The Furious: Tokyo Drift.
