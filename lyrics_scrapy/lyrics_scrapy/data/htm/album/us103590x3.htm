Album Intro：

2013 release from the Country superstar. The Calm After is actually an updated version of Tritt's last album, The Storm, which got mired in litigation between Tritt and his former label. The album contains Tritt's duet with his 15-year-old daughter, Tyler Reese, on the Patty Smyth/Don Henley classic 'Sometimes Love Just Ain't Enough'.
