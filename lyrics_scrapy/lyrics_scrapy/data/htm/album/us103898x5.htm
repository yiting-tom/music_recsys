Album Intro：

American Saturday Night is the eighth studio album by American country music artist Brad Paisley. It will be released on June 30, 2009 by Arista Nashville. Like all of his previous studio albums, it is produced by Frank Rogers.The first single, 'Then', has become his fourteenth Number One on the Hot Country Songs chart. iTunes is releasing songs from the album weekly as part of the countdown to the album's release. 'Water', was released on June 9, 2009, followed by the title track on June 16th. 'Everybody's Here' was released on June 23rd. The second single is 'Welcome to the Future.' In an interview with USA Today, Paisley says he's taking a more direct approach with this album: 'I'm not so worried about making the songwriters in town sit and take notice,' he says, 'as I am wanting people to feel like I really meant what I said on this record.'
