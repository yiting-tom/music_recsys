Album Intro：

Dedicated to the throng of fans that has been down with the group since they emerged on the hip-hop scene in 2009, FROM DAY 1 will feature guest appearances by Tyga, 2 Chainz, Jeremih and Mike Posner, along with production by Diplo, Justice League and more.

Hailing from Decatur, GA, TRAVIS PORTER (comprised of Strap, Quez and Ali) has garnered a collection of notable hits, including two Top 15 songs: 'Make It Rain' and Bring It Back, as well as 'College Girl' which helped earn them a 2011 BET Awards nomination for Best Group.
