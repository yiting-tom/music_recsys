Album Intro：

The Afters made a grand entrance at Christian radio with their landmark Light Up The Sky album which brought two smash hit singles ('Light Up The Sky' and 'Lift Me Up'.) The band has partnered once again with producer Dan Muckala to raise the bar higher with this, their latest record. The first radio single, 'Every Good Thing,' is a captivating pop tune that celebrates the truth that every good thing is from God. With an attentive blend of upbeat songs and lyrically rich anthems like 'Broken Hallelujah,' this release creates a balance that is assured to keep listeners engaged from start to finish.
