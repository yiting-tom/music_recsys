Album Intro：

2009 sophomore album from the British Alt-Rock outfit (knows as The Enemy UK in the States). Music For The People is described by the band as having a more 'raw and punkier sound' than their debut effort, We'll Live And Die In These Towns. The album was recorded on traditional analogue technology, rather than digitally, which the band says makes it 'sound huge, like a proper old-school Rock record.' Features the first single 'No Time For Tears'. Warner.
