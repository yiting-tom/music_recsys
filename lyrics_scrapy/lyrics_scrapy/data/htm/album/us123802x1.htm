Headpins were a hard rock group from Canada featuring the very capable female lead vocalist Darby Mills. They issued only a handful of albums in the 1980s, one of which was Turn It Loud. Previously only available as an expensive and hard to find import, we now make it available at an affordable price. Wounded Bird. 2006.
