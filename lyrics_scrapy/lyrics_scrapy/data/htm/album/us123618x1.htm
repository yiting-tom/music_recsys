Album Intro：

Bridging the gap between metal and pop, The Fully Down is one of the most dynamic and exciting young bands to burst onto the scene. The Fully Down’s unique cross-genre appeal and explosive live show is converting show-goers from city to city. Their three-guitar attack delivers Iron Maiden-like riffs combined with enough melody and hooks to leave you singing along. In 2004, Alternative Press Magazine selected The Fully Down (from thousands of entries) to play Vans Warped Tour dates in the North-Eastern U.S. & Canada. The band recently finished up a series of Vans Warped Tour 2005 dates throughout North-Western U.S. & Canada. An extensive U.S. tour is in the works for Fall/Winter 2005. The Fully Down band has tallied over 50,000 + song plays via their PureVolume.com & MySpace.com websites - a great indicator of the amount of interest in this band. With a strong emphasis on touring and live performances, expect those numbers to continuously climb. For Fans Of: Thrice, Coheed & Cambria, Anberlin
