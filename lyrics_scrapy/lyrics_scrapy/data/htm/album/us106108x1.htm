Album Intro：

Fight Fair is a pop punk/Hardcore band from San Diego, California, signed to Triple Crown Records. Their lastest CD entitled Settle The Score was released on November 18, 2008. It is a re-release of their first EP bearing the same title.

Spawned from the infamous San Diego State University party scene, Fight Fair's members came together with a wide range of influences from pop-punk, hip-hop, hardcore, emo, and more.
