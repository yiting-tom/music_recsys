Album Intro：

Deluxe two CD edition of the guitarist's 2009 album featuring one bonus track on the main disc plus a bonus CD that contains a previously unreleased 1990 concert by the Steve Morse Band, recorded in Germany. In 2009, Steve Morse joined forces again with Dave LaRue and Van Romaine to record a new Steve Morse Band album. The result was an album as multi-faceted as Morse`s career. The entirely instrumental album clearly reflects the remarkable talent of Steve Morse and distinguishes the influences of the artist's musical history. Earmusic.
