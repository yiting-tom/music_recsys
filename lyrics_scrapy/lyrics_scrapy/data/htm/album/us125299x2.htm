Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2012 album from the Boston-based Alt-Rock band. They play an authentic style of Emo, combining influences from the original Emo sound with the more chaotic sounds of the later Screamo' bands of the '90s. Recent activity includes a split with Pianos Become The Teeth, and another with We Were Skeletons. They did a West Coast tour with calculator summer of `11 and a Euro Tour with We Were Skeletons.
