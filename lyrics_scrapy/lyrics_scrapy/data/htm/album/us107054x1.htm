Album Intro：

Their first full-length is a polished and wise endeavor brimming with a poise that belies the band's years. Love, life, death, the past, the future...it's all there in the vein of the honest and clear-cut rock of Jimmy Eat World, Third Eye Blind, and Foo Fighters. One of AP.net's Most Anticipated Albums of 2009. RIYL: Jimmy Eat World, The Starting Line, Taking Back Sunday, The Maine, We The Kings.
