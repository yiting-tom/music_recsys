Album Intro：

RIVAL SONS are a raucous, maximum-blues-infused, hard rock band from Los Angeles, California, who explodes with the rhythm and roughness of some of the greatest rock acts of all time.

Hailed as The unlikely saviors of American Rock(Classic Rock) Lead Vocalist Jay Buchanan, guitarist Scott Holiday, bassist Robin Everhart, and drummer Mike Miley were brought together by a love of music, but also, in their own words, they want to give the people the rock and roll they deserve by keeping it honest, visceral, and dangerous.

From the first moment the band got together there has been a special artistic chemistry, landing them opening slots with legends like AC/DC and ALICE COOPER, several USO shows with KID ROCK, and a TV performance from The Indianapolis 500.

This new Deluxe album, Pressure and Time features a bonus DVD including 3 live tracks filmed from the High voltage Festival 2011, A very special backstage live performance of Pressure and Time, 3 official promo Videos and an Official EPK promo Trailer.
