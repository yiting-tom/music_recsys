Shin Seung-hun (Hangul: 신승훈; Hanja: 申昇勳; born on 11 April 1966) is a South Korean musician, singer and songwriter whose popularity peaked in the 1990s. He is remembered as one of the most important figures in South Korean pop music scene. He is also popularly known as 'Emperor of Ballads'

The newest single is 'For Our Dreams', which he composed and performed. It was released in January 2009 and was used by Korean Air to commemorate its 40th Anniversary. Both Korean and English versions have been released.
