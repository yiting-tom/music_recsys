Album Intro：

2006 release from the Australian trashy Blues Punk band. The band recorded and released their debut and sophomore albums within a span of a year and still had material left over that didn't make the albums. Miller's Daughter collects those even more primitive and raw recordings that were left over and compiles them as The Drones' third full-length album. Fuse.
