Album Intro：

Silverstein's main theme in Arrivals and Departures is, 'taking positive inspiration from the negative aspects of life, and taking advantage of whatever circumstance you have to deal with.' Silverstein has crafted a remarkable and engaging album that will most certainly inspire their fans and undoubtedly attract a whole new audience as well.


在新磚Arrivals & Departures中Silverstein闡述的主題是：」把消極的生活態度轉變為積極樂觀的態度，無論你遇到什麼問題都要樂觀積極的去面對！！「。Silverstein 精心設計了專輯標籤，相信新專輯一定會大大激勵他的FANS,當然也會毫無疑問的會吸引一大批的新歌迷！！
