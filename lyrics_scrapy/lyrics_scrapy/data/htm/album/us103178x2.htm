Album Intro：

Have you ever been to Spain? Wait, I m not talking about the hit song from
the 70s. Have you ever been to Spain? No, not the country although that s
quite magnificent.
Spain, the musical brainchild of Josh Haden, released 3 albums in the
late 1990s into the early 2000s releases that were universally lauded and
groundbreaking. Featuring his sisters Tanya Haden (Silversun Pickups),
Petra Haden (Beck, that dog), and drummer Joey Waronker (R.E.M.,
Beck, Smashing Pumpkins) among other world-class players.
Collaborators aside, Spain is about Josh, who created 3 amazing
releases that may have slipped through the populous cracks the first
time around, but will be fully realized on limited-edition colored vinyl for the
band s run on LP.
Experience Spain as it was meant to be heard. On vinyl! (Limited-edition,
180-gram colored vinyl for the first run.) Drop the stylus. Handle the jacket.
And prepare yourself for what you might have missed the first time around
or relive how you thought it should be.
