Album Intro：

UK tour edition includes two bonus tracks; Dance The Night Away (Live) and All You Ever Do Is Bring Me Down (Live). 2010 release from the former leader of The Mavericks. Lucky One is his first album of original material in seven years. Drawing on his deep love of Country, Rock, Jazz and Latin music. Malo displays not only his eclectic taste but a crystal clear voice unmatched by any other singer today. 14 tracks including the first single 'Hello Again'.
