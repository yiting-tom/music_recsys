Album Intro：

The Debut Album from the UK all Female Pop Punk Trio of Molly, Cherisse and Toy. The Girls Quickly Made a Big Splash in the Us Via their Careful Placement (And Appearance) on the Upn Television Series 'Veronica Mars' and by 'no Sleep Tonight' Being Heard on an Episode of the Hit Abc Series 'Grey's Anatomy'. The Girls Draw Inspiration from Nirvana, Led Zeppelin's John Bonham, Jimi Hendrix and all Three Love Adam Ant, Having Been 'Raised by Wolves' (So Claims Toy). You Might Be Surprised to Know Though that They have a Backgrounds at the Brighton Institute of Modern Music and Years in Prior Jazz Bands. They Shaped their Sound Modeling on the Buzz Guitar Sounds of the Runaways and Undertones and Write Smart Lyrics with a Great Sense of Humour and Wit...and Turning Heads, Engaging Fans Daily.
