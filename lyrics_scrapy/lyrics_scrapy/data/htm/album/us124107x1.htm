Album Intro：

The Lashes are here and now and have validity in this era of new 'new wave' music. With the enjoyable work of Interpol, Bloc Party, and others that artistically plunder the gold mine of past sounds, The Lashes are as legitimate as those previously mentioned and can grasp the potential love of a much wider audience that include those that miss the old days and that younger audience that embraces the new mix of old culture in an updated styling. Columbia. 2006.
