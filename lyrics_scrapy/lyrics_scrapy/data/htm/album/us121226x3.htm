Album Intro：

Sisters of the Red Death is the awaited follow-up to Vendetta Red's critically acclaimed 2003 major-label debut, Between The Never And The Now, which most notably featured the rock radio and MTV hit 'Shatterday'. The writing process took two years and contained numerous unexpected twists. The thrashy 'Banshee' and 'Joyless Euphoria' should satisfy fans of the VR's earlier, heavier material, while tracks such as 'Dark Heart Silhouette' and the first single, 'Silhouette Serenade', share a symphonic pop sensibility, akin to The Cure or Smashing Pumpkins at their most grandiose. Epic. 2005.
