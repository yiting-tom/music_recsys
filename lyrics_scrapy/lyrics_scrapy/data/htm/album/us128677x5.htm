Album Intro：

2011 debut album from the American Idol winner. Scotty has been well-received by the Country music industry since winning Season 10 of American Idol in May 2011, as evidenced by the fact that some of the best writers in country music wrote songs specifically for this project, which can be unheard of for a new artist. ' The album is a collection of songs in the same straight-ahead Country style that captured the nation's hearts - and votes--on AI. It's the next step in a promising career that is already breaking records.
