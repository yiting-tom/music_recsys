Diamond Yukai【 18 albums 116 lyrics 】
Diamond Yukai (ダイアモンド✡ユカイ Daiamondo Yukai?, born March 12, 1962) is a rock singer and actor. He was born Yutaka Tadokoro (田所 豊 Tadokoro Yutaka?) in Tanashi, Tokyo, Japan. He is the vocalist for the Japanese rock band Red Warriors. He has acted in the Hollywood films Tokyo Pop and Lost in Translation and is often seen on television variety shows.
