Album Intro：

有著「英國第一民謠夫人」美譽的英國著名歌手Kathryn Williams，憑借她清新甜美的民謠吉他彈唱在2008年又和我們見面了，這次她與被譽為「英國民謠之父」的Ewan MacColl的兒子Neill MacColl共同合作來演繹這張唱片，肯定會給我們帶來不一樣的驚喜。

Mercury Music prize nominee Kathryn Williams & songwriter/producer Neil MacColl first met at the Daughters of Albion concert (part of the BBC's Folk Britannia season) where they were paired to perform 'The First Time Ever I Saw Your Face'a song which Neill's father, Ewan MacColl, wrote for his mother. Peggy Seeger.They clicked musically & this album is a record of their collaboration. The album was written, recorded & mixed in a little over 2 weeks ?a miniscule timescale in these times of recording, re-recording & digital manipulation of every note. 'Two' was recorded and mixed by & with friends includingengineer/producer Phill Brown (credits include Led Zeppelin, Zero 7, David Gilmour &Jarvis Cocker). Kathryn & Neill are doing a short tour of the Highlands & Islands of Scotland in February with a full UK tour (including dates with David Gray) between March & April.
