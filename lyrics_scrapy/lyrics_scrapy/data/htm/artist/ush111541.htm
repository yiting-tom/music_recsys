Nobuhiko Okamoto (岡本 信彦 Okamoto Nobuhiko?, born October 24, 1986) is a Japanese voice actor and singer from Tokyo, affiliated with the Pro-Fit agency. He won the Best New Actor Award at the Third Seiyu Awards and Best Supporting Actor Award at the Fifth Seiyu Awards.

Nobuhiko has voiced many roles, including his involvement in character CDs and insert songs (soundtracks) for the anime, that on his 25th birthday, he announced his singing debut for the year 2012. On April 18, 2012 he made his debut with the single 'Atchi de Kotchi de' (あっちでこっちで?, 'Here and There'), sung together with Rumi Ōkubo, Hitomi Nabatame, Kaori Fukuhara, and Shintarō Asanuma, which was used as the opening theme to the 2012 TV anime Place to Place. On May 23, he released his first EP, Palette which reached number 9 on the weekly Oricon chart. On November 28, 2012, he released another single, 'Retrospective World' which was used as the opening theme to the 2012 anime Aoi Sekai no Chūshin de, sung in duet with Hiro Shimono. On June 5, 2013, he released his second EP, Enjoy Full. On March 19, 2014, he released his first single, 'Shunkan Beat' (瞬間BEAT?).
