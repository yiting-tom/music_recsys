Album Intro：

Following an eventful four years which found Tilly & the Wall rising to the national stage with appearances on Sesame Street and 90210, the beloved quintet presents a fierce album, Heavy Mood, on Team Love. Produced by Mike Mogis and featuring collaborations with Nick Zinner (Yeah Yeah Yeahs), Clark Baechle (The Faint) and Shane Aspegren (The Berg Sans Nipple), Heavy Mood is the band's most adventurous album to date.
