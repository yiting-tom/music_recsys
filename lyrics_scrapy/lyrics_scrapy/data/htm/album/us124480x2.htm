Album Intro：

2010 album from the Swedish Hard Rockers. With some experienced musicians (Daniel Flores of Mind's Eye, Johan Niemann of Therion and the guitar hero Daniel Palmqvist) and the vocalist Angelica Rylin, TMOMS are all pumped and loaded to create a major impact on the musical scene. With the recent release of the debut single, 'Bleed Me Dry', the band has taken the Swedish national single charts by storm. The music style is heavy, symphonic, grandiose and totally contemporary. First edition in digipak including bonus videoclip. Frontiers Records.
