Album Intro：

The Casual Dots are Christina Billotte (Slant 6, Quix*o*tic), Kathi Wilcox (Bikini Kill, Frumpies), and Steve Dore (Deep Lust), all with a shared history in the underground music scenes of Washington DC and Olympia, WA. Their minimalist sound, anchored by a propulsive backbeat and weaving melodic interplay between the guitars, provides a sonic platform for Christina's cool and distinctive vocal style. The result is a raw pop sound with a uniquely angular and lean sensibility.
