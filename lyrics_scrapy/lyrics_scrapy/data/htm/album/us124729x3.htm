Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2011 album from the New Zealand outfit. The Phoenix Foundation are music legends in their home country and rank up there with Wilco, The National and Arcade Fire. This, their first album to be released in Europe, looks like being their break out, blessed as it is with superb songwriting evidenced on stand out tracks 'Buffalo', 'Pot', 'Flock Of Hearts' and 'Bitte Bitte'. Memphis Industries.
