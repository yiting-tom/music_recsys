Album Intro：

2007 album from Alternative Rock band Verdena. 'Requiem' is their fourth album and was recorded mainly at their Henhouse Studio, all songs are produced by the band with a little from Italian musician-producer Mauro Pagani, he co-produced two songs and helped them playing keyboards. The first single, 'Muori Delay' is a genuine hitmaker in the style of bands like Smashing Pumpkins, White Stripes and Queens Of The Stone Age.
