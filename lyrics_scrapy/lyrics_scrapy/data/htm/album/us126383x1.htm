Album Intro：

Double vinyl LP pressing of this 2009 release. Who could have predicted that an accidental MP3 upload would birth a phenomenon? Never having met in person, but electronically connected through common musical friends, producer Grahm Zilla and Nigerian-born rapper/vocalist Isis began Thunderheist to throw a wrench into static Hip Hop conventions and explore their love for the dancefloor. 13 tracks including the singles 'Sweet 16' and 'Nothing 2 Step 2'.
