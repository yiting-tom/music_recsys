Album Intro：

美國合唱團(America)是由三位美國駐英國空軍軍官的兒子，1970年在倫敦成軍的民謠搖滾經典樂團。他們清新的木吉他伴奏、出色的和聲加上精心的製作，獨樹一幟。此張專輯是他們的第16章錄音室專輯，也是自1984年以來的首張專輯。

Here & Now is the sixteenth original studio album by American folk rock duo America, to be released by Burgundy Records in January 2007. This is the first major-label full-length studio album released by the group since Perspective in 1984.

The recording sessions were completed in July 2006, but a scheduled September release was eventually pushed back to January 2007. This was first known occasion on which America recorded in a studio in New York City.

The first disc is comprised of brand-new studio recordings, while a second disc features a live performance of the entire History: America's Greatest Hits album as recorded for XM Satellite Radio.
