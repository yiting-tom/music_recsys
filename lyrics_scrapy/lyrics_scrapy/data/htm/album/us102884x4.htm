Album Intro：

Originally released in 1983, Hysteria was the much-anticipated follow-up album to the Gold Record Dare! album. Now Hysteria is released for the first time in the US on CD. It has been expanded to include 5 highly sought after extended mixes and non-album tracks (over 24 minutes of additional music) that have never before been released on CD anywhere. Includes the three Top Twenty UK singles 'The Lebanon', 'Louise' and 'Life On Your Own'.Caroline. 2005.
