Album Intro：

The Outline are a four-piece indie rock outfit from LA with an expansive sound that has a knack for catching audiences off guard before converting them into zealous fans. Since early 2004, the band has been crafting their undeniably driving sound to encompass anything and everything, including fist-pumping anthems, electronic soundscapes, and a rousing musical number that ends their soon to be released rock triumph with a bang. Their debut was produced by Chris Fudurich (Nada Surf, Simple Minds). Boldly unexpected yet still familiar, the sound of The Outline is the fury of Recover and the keyboard melodies of Interpol meshed with the dance flavor of Head Automatica.
