Album Intro：

Digitally remixed and remastered edition of this 1990 album from the British trio including four bonus tracks. The album features the #8 UK smash 'Hippychick' which also reached the Top 20 in the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and the US Hot Dance Club Play chart, outselling Dee-lite's 'Groove Is In The Heart' which was released at the same time. The CD booklet includes the story of the making of the Goddess album as written by the band. It also features many repro's of the band's sleeves and a full UK discography. The CD has been made with the full co-operation of the band. Cherry Pop.
