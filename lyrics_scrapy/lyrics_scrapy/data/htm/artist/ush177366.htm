Momoland (Korean: 모모랜드; often stylized as MOMOLAND or MMLD) is a South Korean girl group formed by Dublekick Company. They debuted on November 10, 2016, with the EP Welcome To Momoland. The members are the winners of survival program Finding Momoland on Mnet. The group is composed of nine members: Hyebin, Yeonwoo, Jane, Taeha, Nayun, Daisy, JooE, Ahin, and Nancy.

MOMOLAND announced to have their first comeback with a single sometime in April 2017, with a new member and former JYP Entertainment trainee, Daisy. On April 10, 2017, former Produce 101 contestant, Kim Taeha was announced to have been added to the group as well.
