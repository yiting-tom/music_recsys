Album Intro：

Montgomery, AL is 280 miles from Nashville, TN. But in the early 1960's, that wasn't too far for the AM radio waves of the Grand Ole Opry, where in the back seat of a 1958 Chevy, a young Tommy Shaw lost - and found - himself in every note, and where the seeds of a life in music were planted.

Though Shaw went on to become one of the brightest stars in rock `n roll as the singer and guitarist of Styx, his early love of roots music always burned bright. The sense of direction instilled in him at that early age has led him on a remarkable path of musical discovery and storytelling, and ultimately, has led him home, to The Great Divide
