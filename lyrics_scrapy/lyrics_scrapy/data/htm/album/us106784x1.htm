Album Intro：

The Duke Spirit are an English rock band, with an affinity to the sound of My Bloody Valentine, Sonic Youth and the Pixies (a band The Duke Spirit has covered live in their early shows) and also display a primal take on Rock and roll, blues and soul music, allying them to groups such as Patti Smith Group, Spiritualized, and the 60's Girl Group / Spector Wall of Sound.

The band are currently actively touring back and forth between the USA, Europe and Australia whilst fitting in time to write new material for their next, and third, album.

The Duke Spirit released a collection of past and present material exclusively through Target Feb 24th 2009. The collection was a component to the McQ Fashion collection. See below.
