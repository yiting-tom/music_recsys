Tritonal (band)( Tritonal )【 9 albums 106 lyrics 】
Tritonal is an American music duo consisting of producer/DJs Chad Cisneros and David Reed from Austin, Texas, United States. They host a radio show called Tritonia.


Biography

Formed in 2008, Tritonal, the Texas-based production and DJ duo consisting of Chad Cisneros and Dave Reed, are #65 on DJ Mag's Top 100 DJs of 2012 and one of MTV Clubland's '10 Artists to Watch in 2013'.

Their 2011 debut artist album Piercing the Quiet produced eight top 20 Beatport singles, including five that held the #1 position on the trance chart for over three weeks. In September 2012, they released the remix album Piercing the Quiet Remixed, which landed in the top 5 on the iTunes dance albums chart. Throughout 2013, Tritonal released a 3 EP series entitled Metamorphic. The first EP, Metamorphic I, was released on March 25, with remixes of their track 'Bullet That Saved Me' featuring Underdown released in April 2013. As of June 2014, Tritonal have produced over 200 original productions and remixes and have been supported by other DJs like Armin van Buuren, Above & Beyond, Tiësto, Steve Aoki, Nicky Romero, Cash Cash, Ferry Corsten, Markus Schulz and more.

Tritonal has a worldwide fan base who have self-titled themselves 'Tritonians'. In 2013, they played festivals like Electric Daisy Carnival, Escape From Wonderland, Ultra Music Festival, Electric Zoo Festival, and Creamfields Australia. In summer 2013, Tritonal embarked on a major, 40+ city tour throughout North America. In April 2013, they announced that they would join Will Holland as co-owners of the UK record label, Enhanced Recordings. After 100 episodes of their radio show Air Up There, they ended it with a sold out, live performance in their hometown of Austin, Texas. A new program called Tritonia airs on Sirius XM Electric Area every Saturday at 2pm EST, with rebroadcasts on Tuesdays at 8PM. In 2015, their song 'Untouchable' with Cash Cash made Billboard's Year-End Top 100 for Dance/Electronic songs.
