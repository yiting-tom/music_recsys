Album Intro：

Considering the eldest band member is only 19, Ten After Two have come a long way in a short period of time. Their peers walked in ceremony for graduation while the band was touring supporting their debut EP entitled, If You Don't First. Having a sound that kids want and desire, Ten After Two does it like the best of them. Not unlike A Day To Remember and Attack Attack!, the band somehow mixes melodic choruses with brutal breakdowns.
