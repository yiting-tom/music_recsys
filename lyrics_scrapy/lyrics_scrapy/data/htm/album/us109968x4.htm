Album Intro：

What better way to continue your Fourth of July celebration than with the hottest new album this summer 'Peligro' (Danger), from REIK. Reik's past albums have sky rocketed to the top and not only have won them multiple MTV Music Awards (Best Group, Best new Artist, Best North American artist and Best Pop Artist) but in 2009 also winning them their 1st Grammy. Their success continued with a successful US tour selling out in major cities across the United States. REIK now returns and steps out of their comfort zone and have taken their music to the next level with a new feel to it yet maintaining what always made them stand out as artists. This new album showcases REIK's growth in pop music and contains 14 songs with a combination of dance songs, electronic beats, and their always popular ballads. Their first single also titled Peligro, has reached top 10 in the Latin pop charts in ONLY two weeks. This great success on the first single so quickly has caught the attention of radio, media and fans across the country. With featured articles this summer in Latina and Cosmopolitan En Espanol as well as various other events already planned across the country like VH-1 Save the Music and with a presentation at the Hispanic Heritage Awards, REIK is going to be a force to be reckoned with this summer.
