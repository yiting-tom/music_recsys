Album Intro：

As the last album from their hit making 1966-70 era, The Monkees Changes is one of their most collectable efforts. Featuring several hit singles with 'Oh My My' and 'I Love You Better,' this pop masterpiece features both Micky Dolenz and Davy Jones superstar vocals and first class musicianship. Produced by the great sixties producer/writer Jeff Barry (Monkees,Neil Diamond,Phil Spector) Changes is now mastered from the original Colgems tapes by Joe Reagoso & features 3 bonus tracks including 'Do It In The Name Of Love' + orig. artwork.
