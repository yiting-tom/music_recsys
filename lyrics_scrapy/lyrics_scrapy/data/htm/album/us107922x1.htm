Album Intro：

Fight Like a Girl is the debut album of American country music duo Bomshel. It was released on October 20, 2009 via Curb Records. Its lead-off single, the title track, peaked at #30 in June 2009, thus becoming the duo's first Top 40 country hit. '19 and Crazy' was released as the album's second single, and debuted at #55 on the same chart.
