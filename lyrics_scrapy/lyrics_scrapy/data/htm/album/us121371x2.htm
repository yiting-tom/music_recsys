Album Intro：

Vicki Yohe has been one of the most successful Gospel singers of the past decade. She has been nominated for Gospel Music's Dove Award. 'Reveal Your Glory' delivers a set of all new material (with the exception of one song) recorded live at The Cathedral in Atlanta. Produced by Kendall Duffie, the album captures the excitement and charged spirituality of Vicki's ministry in action.
