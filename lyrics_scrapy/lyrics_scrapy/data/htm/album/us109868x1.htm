Album Intro：

Be Here is New York City singer-songwriter-pianist Rachel Platten's collection of wise-beyond-their-years, piano-driven songs that echo elements of Alanis Morissette, Carole King, Tori Amos and Regina Spektor, while retaining a singular sense of self. Be Here is the culmination of a lifelong passion for music for Rachel who collaborated with a variety of production teams over the course of the process, including Fredrik Thomander ('N Sync), Jerry Abbott/Grant Black (Craig David) and the Wizardz of Oz (Vanessa Hudgens). Her live shows have earned raves from the Boston Globe and NY Post.
