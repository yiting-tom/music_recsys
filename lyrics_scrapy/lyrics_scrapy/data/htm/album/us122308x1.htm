Album Intro：

Former WWE music sensation and current TNA star trademarc has appeared in front of thousands and thousands of fans. Now he's ready to work the mic in a whole different fashion with this, the release of his highly anticipated solo debut 'Inferiority Complex.' The record brings you 'Marc's unique blend of high energy beats complemented by his deep social commentary. In addition to solid beats and trademarc's verbals, this CD includes two custom cut Kurt Angle theme songs; favorites of the TNA champ as featured on Spike TV. Only here will you find a trademarc track linking the violent action of Angle's MMA and the hardcore Bumpy Knuckle stylez of Hip Hop's Freddie Foxxx. Production handled by Freddie Foxxx, 7L (7L & Esoteric, The Bladerunners, Army of the Pharaohs) and Chaos & Order (Juelz Santana, Jim Jones, Peedi Crack). The release is being actively promoted by Marc as well as his close associate, one of wrestling s greatest, Mr. Kurt Angle. Pick this one up lest Mr. Angle pay a visit and hit you with a suplex.
