NU'EST( NUEST )【 14 albums 68 lyrics 】
NU'EST (Hangul: 뉴이스트; acronym for New Establish Style Tempo) is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2012. The group debuted on March 15, 2012, with their digital single 'Face'. The group consists of JR, Aron, Baekho, Minhyun, and Ren. They have released two studio albums, one Korean and one Japanese, as well as five EPs.

Members
