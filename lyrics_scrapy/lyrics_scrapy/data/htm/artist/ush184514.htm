Samm Henshaw( Iniabasi Samuel Henshaw )【 5 albums 13 lyrics 】
Iniabasi Samuel Henshaw, professionally known as Samm Henshaw (born 22 February 1994), is a singer, songwriter, and record producer of Nigerian descent, signed to Columbia Records. Henshaw has garnered mainstream radio support from BBC Radio 1 and was handpicked by James Bay and Chance the Rapper for tour support.
