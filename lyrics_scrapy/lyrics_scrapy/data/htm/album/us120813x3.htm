Album Intro：

Unholy, from Finland, formed in 1988 and were among the first doom metal bands from the country. In the bands earlier years, starting under the moniker of Holy Hell, their style began as more a black/death/doom metal mix but progressed to a style of atmospheric, experimental doom metal. 'Gracefallen' was the bands fourth and final album, and was originally released in 1999. 'Gracefallen' contains the trademark heavy, slow and often unusual Unholy arrangements and heavy guitar. This edition of 'Gracefallen' also includes a rare bonus track called 'Gone', recorded prior to the album.
