Album Intro：

For Bitter Or Worse is going to be the sixth studio album from the Dutch singer Anouk. The album was released at September 18, 2009 via the record label EMI.

The first single from the album, 'Three Days In A Row' was released in August. It reached the top of the charts in September 2009, making it Anouk's first number 1 in The Netherlands. In June of the same year, one of the songs recorded for the album, 'Today', was released as promo material. It was so successful that, despite never being released as an official single, the song reached number 50 in the Dutch chart.
