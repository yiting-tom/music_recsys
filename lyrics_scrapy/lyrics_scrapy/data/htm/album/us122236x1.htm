Album Intro：

Grindcore legends Terrorizer, once again spearheaded by legendary drummer Pete Sandoval (Morbid Angel), are back with the beastly new full-length 'Hordes of Zombies'. A massive riff avalanche, 'Hordes...' blazes a merciless path of sonic destruction through the genre they helped to create over 20 years ago. 'Hordes...' also sees the return to the fold of bassist/vocalist David Vincent (Morbid Angel) which ensures that 'Hordes...' is as vital as 'World Downfall' was so many years ago.
