Album Intro：

Terry Knight & the Pack were a garage band out of Flint, Michigan that had a number of regional Midwestern hits but never broke nationally, and would probably be destined for eternal if undeserved obscurity but for one thing: this is the band that gave Mark Farner and Don Brewer of Grand Funk Railroad their start.

Liner note writer Jeff Tamarkin tells the story while you dig these fuzz-laced sounds. The complete 1966 and 1967 releases! (Note: the 1966 album is in mono; it never came out in true stereo. Reflections is in stereo!)
