Album Intro：

Shine is the tenth studio album from country singer Martina McBride. It is scheduled to be released on March 24, 2009. The album's lead-off single, 'Ride', was released in November 2008. The album was co-produced by McBride and Dann Huff.

A cover of the Leo Sayer song 'I Can't Stop Loving You' is available as a pre-order bonus track on iTunes.
