Album Intro：

'Wait For Me' is the debut album from Leeds quintet The Pigeon Detectives. Produced by Will Jackson (Kaiser Chiefs/Embrace) and mixed by Cenzo Townshend and Steve Harris, the album sees the band deliver an urgent mix of up-tempo British indie rock, taking influence from the likes of The Clash, The Libertines and The Kinks. The singles 'I'm Not Sorry', 'I Found Out' and 'Romantic Type' are included.
