The Neighbourhood( THE NBHD )【 14 albums 102 lyrics 】
The Neighbourhood (sometimes rendered as 'THE NBHD') is an American rock band formed in Newbury Park, California in 2011. The band is composed of vocalist Jesse Rutherford, guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, bassist Mikey Margott, and drummer Brandon Alexander Fried. After releasing three EPs, I'm Sorry..., The Love Collection and Thank You, The Neighbourhood released their first full-length album, I Love You., on April 23, 2013, via Columbia Records. In November 2014, they released a mixtape titled #000000 & #FFFFFF. The band's second album, Wiped Out!, was released in October 2015. They released their EP Hard in September 2017 which briefly charted on the Billboard 200.
