Album Intro：

Brutha is an R&B group of brothers, from Los Angeles, California. They are currently signed to Def Jam Records/Good Fellas Entertainment & currently have a show on BET called 'Brothers To Brutha'.

2008 release of their self titled debut album. Anthony, Jared, Jacob, Cheyenne and Grady make up the five Brutha Brothers, ranging in age from 19 to 25. This group signed to Island Def Jam/Goodfellas Entertainment drawing their inspiration from the Jackson 5, New Edition and Boyz II Men. The album was recorded in Atlanta and it includes the single 'I Can't Hear The Music' featuring Fabolous. Brutha has a reality show which premiered in November 2008 on BET called Brother to Brutha which shows the brothers' struggles to fulfill their musical dream without compromising their close family ties.
