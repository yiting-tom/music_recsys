Album Intro：

Jasmine has done it again! A fantastic collection of 45 tracks recorded between 1954 and 1961 by one of the greatest doo wop groups, The Five Satins. Features the early singles as The Scarlets and their entire collection of chart hits as The Five Satins. Of course this includes, In the Still of the Night which is regularly voted as the greatest doo wop song ever. Fully detailed liner notes cover their entire career. This is yet another superb addition to Jasmine s ever growing doo wop collection.
