Album Intro：

Twelve inch vinyl pressing of this 2012 EP. Eight years after their partnership began with the debut of Dig Yourself, Times New Viking and Siltbreeze join forces again for the band's 2012 outing: six tracks, the result of a recent creative (albeit, modest) outpouring of ideas. What's interesting to note here is how the trio tempers the primal panic-punk of their salad days and fuses it with the sustained, keen pop skill found within their later catalog. It's hard to know where Times New Viking goes from here, but for right now, this perfectly distilled elixir of sound shows a band firing on all cylinders. What more can you ask for?
