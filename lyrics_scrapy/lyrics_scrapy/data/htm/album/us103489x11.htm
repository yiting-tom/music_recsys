Album Intro：

Released originally by Lost Highway Records in 2008, Just A Little Lovin' is Shelby Lynne's stirring, emphatic tribute to the late British singer Dusty Springfield. It is to date the highest charting album of Lynne's career, reaching No. 41 on the Billboard 200. The producer is veteran Phil Ramone - the engineer for Springfield's original recording of 'The Look Of Love.' In addition to the Springfield tracks, Lynne also covers Tony Joe White's 'Willie and Laura Mae Jones.' 200 gram vinyl.Tracks: 1. Just A Little Lovin' 2. Anyone Who Had A Heart 3.
