Album Intro：

With zealous faith and a love of music beyond her years, nineteen-year-old Alabama native Sarah Reeves is an artist whose voice seems to break through directly to the heart of each listener. Sarah』s vision and heart to lead listeners in worship no matter the setting is revealed through her fresh, honest and redemptive sound. Creating music that will be sung in churches and heard on radio across the U.S. for many years to come, it is clear after experiencing her music how Sarah is quickly becoming a new voice for worshippers around the globe. Lyrics that are authentic, engaging and humble, Sarah's music finds a way to reach people exactly where they are at – either on an intimate level or on a congregational level. Sarah』s Sparrow Records debut Sweet Sweet Sound hits stores April 21st.
