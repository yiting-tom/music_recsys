Album Intro：

The best record from the 90s best interpreters of classic rock sounds. With a Faces and Stones-like soulful swagger, Chris Robinson and Co. blaze through 9 originals and one cover on this, their second full length. Featuring the hits Remedy and Thorn In My Pride, The Southern Harmony And Musical Companion debuted at #1 in the Billboard Top 200 back in 1992. The best southern rock record since Eat A Peach and Lynyrd Skynyrd's classic debut.

Reissued on 180 gram vinyl, this pressing has turned up a small number of defective copies (as customer comments have noted!). Though the defect was serious, overall copies affected have accounted for less than 1% of stock. The Plain label (sister label of 4 Men With Beards) nearly always presses at a more dependable, higher quality manufacturer but on this one the licensor required they use a different plant. It is believed that all defective copies have now been weeded out, and that copies available through Amazon from this point forward will be free of defects.
