Album Intro：

This is it: The ultimate edition of TIAMAT masterpiece 'Wildhoney'. When the album was released back in 1994 neither the band nor the label was aware of the massive impact 'Wildhoney' would have in the entire metal scene. Now, 13 years later, 'Wildhoney' is considered one of the most important and influential gothic metal albums of all times.
