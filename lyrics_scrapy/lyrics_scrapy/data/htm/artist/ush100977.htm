平井堅( ひらい けん )【 54 albums 379 lyrics 】
Ken Hirai (平井 堅 Hirai Ken?, born January 17, 1972) is a Japanese R&B and pop singer. Since his debut, Hirai has worked as a model, actor, composer, lyricist, singer, and spokesperson.

During his career, Hirai has released 32 singles and 11 albums until October 2010. According to Oricon, his single Hitomi o Tojite (Close Your Eyes) became the best-selling single of 2004 in Japan. While his compilation album Ken Hirai 10th Anniversary Complete Single Collection '95–'05 'Utabaka' became the best-selling album of 2006 in Japan.
