Album Intro：

Veil Of Maya are a group of talented musicians who play melodic metal the way it should be played. Hailing from just outside Chicago IL they have been playing around the Mid West for about a year and have gained a following of metal and hardcore fans alike playing shows alongside Dead To Fall, Hewhocorrupts, Animosity, Ed Gein, Tony Danza Tap Dance Extravaganza, The Warriors and more. The band's self released demo moved hundreds of copies with just word of mouth and online sales. The memebers of VOM have quit jobs and school to hit the road full time. Thus far already touring alongside HARLOTS, CARNIVALE, and AFTER THE BURIAL. The band is quickly creating a strong fan base with their live shows, strong work ethic and of course the internet buzz.
