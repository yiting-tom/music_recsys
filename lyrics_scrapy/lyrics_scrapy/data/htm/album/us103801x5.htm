Album Intro：

As a pioneer of Nashville s new traditionalist movement, North Carolina-born
Randy Travis changed the face of country music and helped reconnect
the genre with its authentic roots. It all started with STORMS OF LIFE,
the 4X GRAMMY®-winner's 1986 #1 debut LP and country s first-ever
multiplatinum album. Rediscover the riches of this country music
masterpiece, including its four Top 10 hits: the #1 smashes On The
Other Hand and Diggin Up Bones plus No Place Like Home (2),
and 1982 (6).
