Album Intro：

Deluxe CD/DVD live release from the Norweginan Metal marvels Their debut, Shining, shocked the music world with its genre-defining and now classic Black Jazz /Krautrock. This release is a live album and a concert DVD recorded during one single concert in Norway, Shining set a new standard in the kingdom where they are already dictating the agenda. For the first time ever, the raw energy of their legendary live performances has been captured on tape in a setting organized solely by the band itself. Produced by the multi-talented front-man Munkeby, and backed by the same mixing and mastering team as on their previous Blackjazz studio album, we could not get a truer version of what Blackjazz is meant to be.
