Album Intro：

2013 debut from this hotly-tipped Heavy Metal supergroup. The band features Alan Averill (Primordial, Dead Sovereign, Blood Revolt) on vocals, Nick Barker (Ex-Cradle Of Filth, Ex-Dimmu Borgir, Ex-Testament, Ex-Exodus) on drums, guitarists Rune Eriksen (Aura Noir, Ex-Ava Inferi, Ex-Mayhem) and Patrik Lindgren (Thyrfing), and Frode Glesnes (Einherjer) on bass.
