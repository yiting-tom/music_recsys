Album Intro：

Coming off his debut album 'I'm Alive, I'm Dreaming,' which produced top single ,Love Like Woe,. The Ready Set is preparing to release a brand new EP, 'Feel Good Now.' According to Jordan Witzigreuter, the creative force behind The Ready Set, the first song from the EP, ,Hollywood Dream. is 'pretty much a story about being a kid from a small town with a simple life, who falls in love with a famous, Beverly Hills-style girl. I think everybody has, at some point in time, been or wanted to be a part of that sort of world, or that sort of relationship. It's always a fun thing to think about, if that's what you're after!'
