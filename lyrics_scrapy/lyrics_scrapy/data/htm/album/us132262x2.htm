Album Intro：

Sutton Foster is one of Broadway s most prolific and dynamic leading ladies. Her resume includes her star turn in the Tony-winning hit Thoroughly Modern Millie, for which she also won a Tony Award for Best Actress, and has since starred in
Little Women, The Drowsy Chaperone, Young Frankenstein, Shrek The Musical, and she will be
starring in the upcoming Broadway revival of Anything Goes in March 2011. The multi-awardwinning
artist has also been seen on television in Johnny and the Sprites, and HBO s Flight of the Conchords. As a solo artist, Sutton has graced the stages of Carnegie Hall, Feinstein s, Joe s Pub and many more, as she continuously tours the
nation whenever possible. Her debut solo album Wish is also available through Ghostlight Records, this album was captured live at New York
City s legendary Café Carlyle at The Carlyle Hotel. The album features her long time collaborator and music director Michael Rafter on piano.
