Album Intro：

Asia's Acoustic Sweetheart SABRINA joins forces with popular radio and TV personality Joe D'Mango in 'Acoustic Love Notes' which is inspired by the latter's show 'Love Notes' aired over DZMM 630 khz and DZMM Teleradyo (broadcast over The Filipino Channel in the United States, Europe and Middle East). The album features Joe D'Mango's famous 'love messages' coupled with Sabrina's versions of classic love songs like the carrier single 'Somewhere Down The Road', 'I Don't Want You To Go', 'One Last Cry', 'The Way You Look At Me' 'Officially Missing You', 'Insensitive' and many more. It also includes her version of 'The Way You Look At Me'-a song popularized by another Filipino Asian sensation Christian Bautista. For the first time, Sabrina recorded 4 original songs, 2 of which she composed herself ('One True Love' and 'Over For Now But Not Forever'), 1 featuring up and coming singer Anj Sulit ('Again') and a Filipino song ('Sa Bawat Paghinga'-'In Every Breathe') which will surely touch the hearts of many once released. Songs of heartbreak, love lost, letting go or falling in love again? It is here in 'Acoustic Love Notes' where you will hear a different SABRINA.
