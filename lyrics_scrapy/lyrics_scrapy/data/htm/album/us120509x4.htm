Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing including bonus CD edition. 2012 full-length debut album from the Indie quintet. The album was recorded with Dan Carey (Hot Chip, The Kills) in South London. Toy's musical influences incorporate a wide range of genres including punk, Psychedelia, Krautrock, Post Rock to name a few and they explore experimental recording techniques in the studio.
