Album Intro：

Vanilla Sky have made a big impression in Europe and Japan having sold over 20,000 copies between the 2 territories. May 31st, 2005, marks the release of their first release in the United States and we are certain that it won't be long til fans of their brand of catchy emo/punk follow suit. They have toured or played shows all over Europe with bands like the Ataris, Maxeen, Yellowcard, and Franz Ferdinand; they plan their first tour of the States during the summer of 2005 with Militia Group recording artist, Brandston and will be supporting their release with a number of other tours. Had this album been released on Drive Thru or Vagrant, Vanilla Sky would be a household name. Since this release isn't on either of those labels, the band knows and plans to tour and work their asses off to find an audience. We have a lot of faith in the band and are certain great things are to come for Vanilla Sky in the states.
