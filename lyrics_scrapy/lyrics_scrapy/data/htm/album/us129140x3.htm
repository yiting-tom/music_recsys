Album Intro：

Sha Stimuli is a Brooklyn native who can arguably be said to be the next rapper from New York, period. Originally signed to Virgin Records Sha was poised to release his debut album but due the usual major label politics Sha opted to control his destiny. Over the last 3 years he has released a series of mixtapes that have hundreds of thousands of downloads. Sha has been featured on tracks with the likes of Nas, Maino, Uncle Murder and other mainstream New York rappers. Platinum Producer Just Blaze, DJ KaySlay (Hot 97) and DJ Enuff (Hot 97) and Statik Selektah have co-signed the Sha Stimuli movement. My Soul to Keep features artists such as Freeway, Young Chris and Torae and production by the likes of Just Blaze, Lord Digga, Chopz, J. Cardim, DJ Victorious and G-Clef da Mad Komposa. Whether it be Hot 97, BET or the Internet, it's hard to escape Sha Stimuli's rising presence in hip hop!
