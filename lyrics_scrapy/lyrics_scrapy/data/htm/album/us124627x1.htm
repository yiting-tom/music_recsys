Album Intro：

Although you might have just become familiar with their music, The Only Normal has been jamming together for quite sometime. Formerly, Eight Cents Short, a three piece pop punk band, The Only Normal has matured into a fine specimen of rock and roll. With the addition of Paul “Potsie” Dutton, The Only Normal incorporated technical guitar harmonies to go along with their melodic vocal lines. Over the years their songwriting has become astoundingly tight, crafty, and mature for their average age of seventeen. We hope for big things for this scrawny bunch of guys.
