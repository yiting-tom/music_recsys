Album Intro：

我寫過的歌裡，這首Lyrics最少，我現在只想說這幾句話。關注在豐縣的姐妹，願所有女性兒童平安。
This song has the shortest lyrics in all my songs. There is nothing else I want to say at present, besides worrying about the sister in Feng County and wishing all women and children safe.


感謝樂隊與我一起演唱：貝斯手也是製作人李強老師、鍵盤手也是編曲吳永紅、和聲周甜。
Thanks to my band for singing with me: bass player and producer - Mr. Li Qiang, keyboard player and music arrangement - Mr. Wu Yonghong, back vocalist - Ms. Zhou Tian.

謝謝侄女旋歌與我一起設計歌曲封面。謝謝侄女與同和我哥幫我校對英文。愛你們。
Thanks to my niece Xuange for designing the cover of this song with me. Thanks to another niece Grace Yutong and my brother for polishing the English translation. Love you all.
