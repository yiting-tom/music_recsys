Kyosuke Himuro【 18 albums 215 lyrics 】
Kyosuke Himuro (氷室 京介 Himuro Kyōsuke?, born October 7, 1960 in Takasaki, Gunma, Japan) is a Japanese singer-songwriter and musician. He was a lead vocalist of the rock band Boøwy from 1981 to 1988. After the group disbanded he started a successful solo career, becoming one of Japan's best-selling artists. In 2003, HMV Japan ranked Himuro at number 76 on their list of the 100 most important Japanese pop acts. He currently lives in Los Angeles, California, where he purchased a palatial residence in Beverly Hills in 2004, which was previously owned by Shaquille O'Neal.
