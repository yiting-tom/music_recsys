Album Intro：

First time on CD in it s own right for the band s third and last album featuring ex-Last Resort legend Roi Pearce on vocals. Recorded live in front of a selected audience this album highlights the 4-skins at their best....live in action. Roi s version of older 4-Skins classics like 'Acab', 'Chaos' and 'Wonderful World' gave the 4-skins a fresh breath of air. Sadly this was to be their last ever album.
