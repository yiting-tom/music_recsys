Album Intro：

Before coming back with a brand new studio album in 2013, Stratovarius are now releasing a CD recorded November 19th, 2011 in Tampere, Finland. This is one of the very last shows with drummer and key-member Jörg Michael.
Under Flaming Winter Skies - Live In Tampere marks the end of an era as well as a new beginning.
Hits and classics like Under Flaming Skies , Speed Of Light and Hunting High And Low makes it a must-have for every fan and a real collector's item not to miss. This CD is being released simultaneously with a live DVD of the show.
