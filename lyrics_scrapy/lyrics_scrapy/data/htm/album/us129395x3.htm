Album Intro：

2010 release, the fifth solo album from New York rapper Sheek Louch best known for his roles as D-Block entrepreneur and founding member of Hip Hop super trio The L.O.X.. Donnie G: Don Gorilla is the follow-up to independently released Life on D-Block. Sheek Louch is always the eye of the storm - just like Jadakiss and Styles P, his long time partners in The L.O.X. and D-Block. The road to Def Jam began for all three as kids growing up rhyming in Yonkers, New York. Fellow Yonkers hip-hop first lady Mary J. Blige hooked them up with Bad Boy in 1996, where they became known as The L.O.X. (Living Off Xperience). Sheek's solo career began with Walk Witt Me (2003), and then went the indie route with After Taxes (2005), Silverback Gorilla (2008), and last year's Life on D-Block.
