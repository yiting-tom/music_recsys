Album Intro：

The unmistakable voice that is Tina Arena, will release her new album 'Songs Of Love & Loss 2'. This album follows on from the hugely successful platinum selling 'Songs Of Love & Loss' which: Sold in excess of 120,000 re-establishing Tina as an Australian Music Icon. The tracklisting for 'Songs Of Love & Loss- Volume 2' is a continuation of the Love and Loss theme and will include Tina's unique interpretations of Call Me ( Blondie), Oh Me Oh My (Lulu), Only Women Bleed (Alice Cooper), I Hope I Never (Split Enz) and Downtown (Petula Clark) among others.

Recorded LIVE at Air Studios in London alongside the London Studio Orchestra, Tina has once again teamed up with famed arranger/conductor Simon Hale who praised Tina, saying, 'She's amazing,.. she is not intimidated by the live set up or the orchestra and rises to the challenge of having everything going to straight tape.
Many artists would crumble in these conditions but Tina seems to shine. Nobody records like this anymore. This is how it should be done!'
EMI. 2008.
