Album Intro：

Refering to Vreid as black & roll is simplyfing it, this is black metal plus. Milorg got the production, the riffs, the solos, the concept, whats not to love? --Terrorizer Magazine

Milorg might just be the most proudly Norwegian album you will ever hear. The lyrical focus is on WW2. Finally a band has been able to treat such epic subject matter with equally epic music.

Vreid s style shifts between dramatic blast beats and black and roll head-nodding grooves that are cemented together with the folk metal melodies.

After this brilliant piece of work I can only hope that the rest of 2009 has releases as absorbing and unique as this one. The war might be over but Vreid are going to bring the fight to you! --Metalsucks.com
