Album Intro：

2010 debut album from this band featuring members of Fall Out Boy (Joe Trohman, Andy Hurley), Anthrax (Scott Ian, Rob Caggiano) and Every Time I Die (Keith Buckley, Josh Newton). The album was produced by Trohman and Caggiano and combines elements of classic rock with the best bits of the members' respective bands.
