Album Intro：

These gospel giants continue to blend their voices in four-part harmonies showcased in The Gospel Music of the Statler Brothers: Volume Two hosted by Bill Gaither. It's a special tribute to their endearing career that takes you through memorable tracks such as 'This Ole House,' 'The King is Coming,' 'Daddy Sang Bass' and more intimate moments shared with the legendary performers. Also available as a 18-track CD.(90 minutes)
