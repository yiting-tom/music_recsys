Album Intro：

Sometimes staying in touch with high school friends pays off-it did for these guys from Jamaica High School in Queens. They struck doo-wop gold after signing with the Gee label, and doo-wop collectors have struck gold here: these two original albums include the hits Heart and Soul; You Baby You; Little Girl of Mine , and For Sentimental Reasons plus 20 other Cleftones classics!
