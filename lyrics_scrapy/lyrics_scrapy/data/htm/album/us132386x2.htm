Album Intro：

One Man Band Man is the solo debut album from record producer Swizz Beatz. Guests included Drag-On and Chris Martin of Coldplay. Swizz Beatz himself has produced the majority of the album, with other producers contributing such as Needlz, also new-comers Young World Music and more. The first single off the album is called 'It's Me Bitches'.
