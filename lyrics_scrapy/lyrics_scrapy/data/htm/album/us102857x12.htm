Album Intro：

This special 2cd retrospective captures one of metal/hard rock's most unique and successful bands. With over 150 minutes of archived songs (some previously unavailable), Downfall is truly a must-have for fans of this long-running and widely respected Dutch band. Assembled from archive studio and rare live recordings, Downfall contains all the missing pieces for fans. All material mastered and restored by Mike Wead (King Diamond).
