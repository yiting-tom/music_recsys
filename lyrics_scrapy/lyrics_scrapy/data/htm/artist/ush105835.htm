Anri Kumaki (熊木 杏里 Kumaki Anri?, born January 27, 1982 in Chikuma, Nagano) is a Japanese pop female singer-songwriter currently signed on unBORDE Records, a division of Warner Music Group Japan.

Anri Kumaki started writing songs when she was 17 years old and won the audition of a TV program which was broadcast by Nippon Television in 2001. She debuted with her Single 'Madoe'(窓絵) arranged by Ryo Yoshimata (吉俣良?).

The singer whom she respects is Yōsui Inoue because she thinks his expression is very courteous.
