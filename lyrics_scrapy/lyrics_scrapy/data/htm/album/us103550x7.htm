Album Intro：

Rising star, Terri Clark, is quickly emerging as a singular voice on the country music landscape. As a dynamic, no-holds-barred live performer, she is one of the rare female country artists capable of throwing down impressive guitar work. The Alberta, Canada native has toured with superstars such as Brooks & Dunn, Reba McEntire, and George Straight on her way to becoming and eight-time, fan voted Canadian Country Music Association Entertainer of the Year.
