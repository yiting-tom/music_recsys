Album Intro：

Epic yet intimate, this Kansas City-based quintet weave future folk, euphoric psychedelia, and exuberant symphonic pop with intricately crafted electronic textures and uncommon emotional depth. The result is a bold and beautiful collection of strikingly human music that holds an aural mirror to our technologically integrated society. The sound is half organic, half synthetic.
