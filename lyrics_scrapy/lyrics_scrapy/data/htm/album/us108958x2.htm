Album Intro：

These days it's hard to find a band that's grown organically, by word of mouth and non-stop touring, winning kids over one show at a time. Man Overboard is that band. They have toured the world with Senses Fail, Polar Bear Club and Set Your Goals. The buzz reached New Found Glory's guitarist Steve Klein who fell in love with their infectious sound. He was so impressed with the band that he offered to produce the bands Rise debut. Man Overboard is humbled by their success, and it's only the beginning.
