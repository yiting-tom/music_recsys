Album Intro：

2009 CD/DVD release. Since the Chicago-based praise team began in 2000, Shekinah Glory Ministry has built a substantial fan base, ministering with their melodic ballads and contemporary praise. Now for the first time ever, the platinum selling artist offers their greatest hits in one collection, including the worldwide hits 'Yes', 'Praise Is What I Do' and 'Jesus'. In addition to the group's impactful hits, The Best of Shekinah Glory Ministry includes a bonus DVD incorporating the 'Praise Is What I Do' concept video, 'Touch Your Sons' video and a never released video segment from Apostle H. Daniel Wilson.
