Album Intro：

The Glorious Unseen began in a living room with a Sunday night Bible Study. Ben Crist began leading worship at the Bible Study and his friends were touched by his passionate songs and urged him to continue writing and follow his passions. Two records later and numerous tours under his belt, Ben is back from the drawing board with yet another fresh and passionate take at a worship record. Having worked with Christian music heavy hitters Steve Hindalong and Marc Byrd (Leeland, Jeremy Camp, Third Day), The Glorious Unseen is greatly respected in both the pop and alternative scenes, having been on tour with the likes of Anberlin, Underoath, The Almost, mewithoutYou, and more! This release is sure to please even those who do not necessarily enjoy mainstream worship music.
