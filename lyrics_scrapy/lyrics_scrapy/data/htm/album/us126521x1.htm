Album Intro：

A landmark collaboration between two prog metal giants, guitar genius Tim Donahue and Dream Theater vocalist James LaBrie!

Donahue s fretless guitar work puts the crunch in these 10 magnificent compositions and provides the perfect backdrop for LaBrie s intense singing!

As the leader of perhaps the most well-known prog metal band around, LaBrie s presence on M&S guarantees this album will be generating a lot of press!

Massive ad campaign planned with ads in Revolver, Metal Edge, Brave Words & Bloody Knuckles and others plus full service to press and radio!

Over two years in the making, the Madmen & Sinners project is the result of an awe-inspiring collaborative effort by guitar viruoso Tim Donahue and accomplished vocalist James LaBrie, recognized worldwide as the lead singer for progressive metal giants Dream Theater. Guitarist and singer met for the first time in July 2002 to disuss Donahue s musical vision and ideas for Madmen & Sinners. Over the course of their brainstorming sessions, LaBrie suggested bringing drummer Mike Mangini (Extreme/Steve Vai) on board, having worked with the skinbasher on LaBrie s MullMuzzler side project. With complex arrangements, chanting Gregorian choirs and exceedingly brilliant guitar playing, M&S looks to be one of the most amibitious prog metal releases this year and certainly a high point in the careers of both of these incredibly talented artists. Press ourtlets have already begun heaping praise on the album, calling it well-written, brilliantly executed and features some fine moments for those that like their music a little more complex than a simple 3 minute pop song and one to add to the collection of any self-respecting prog metal fan. --Cleopatra Records
