Album Intro：

The Maine wraps up the year with a special Holiday EP just for the fans. They've written original holiday tunes exclusively for this EP, plus a cover song performed by singer John O and special guest vocals from Lights. The Maine have been bringing their infectious pop rock to audiences in the thousands each night touring the US and UK with Boys Like Girls, Metro Station, and Good Charlotte.
