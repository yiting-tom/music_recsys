Album Intro：

Though she's only 25, Canada-based songstress Serena Ryder is already a world-traveled artist, hailed by No Depression for her 'Pipes. Presence. Potential.' Elle noted that 'When Serena Ryder sings about love, the most jaded world weariness dissolves.' Now the singer/songwriter Billboard declared to be 'Canada's Most Promising' is poised to present herself to American audiences with her new Atlantic Records release, 'is it o.k', available September 15th. Serena Ryder is a truly eclectic spirit, whose passion for music spans a wide array of styles, from folk and country to jazz, blues, and rock 'n' roll. At 15, Serena left her home in Ontario, Canada and began playing countless shows, both as solo artist and as singer in a number of local combos. Ryder toured across her native Canada, Australia and France. She has toured extensively with such great acts as Steve Earle, Randy Bachman and Burton Cummings of The Guess Who, The Corrs, Blind Boys of Alabama, Eric Hutchinson, Paolo Nutini, Stephen Kellogg, Matt Duke and many more! With the release of her Atlantic Records debut, Serena Ryder continues on her path of becoming one of the most revered female singer/songwriters of her generation and at this rate, it doesn't look like she's slowing down anytime soon.
