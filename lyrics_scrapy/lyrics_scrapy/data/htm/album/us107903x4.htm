Album Intro：

Southern Weather is the debut album of Aaron Gillespie s (formerly of Underoath) side project The Almost. Released in 2007, Southern
Weather debuted at #39 on the Billboard Chart and at #1 on the Billboard Christian Chart! The Almost then hit the road on Vans Warped
Tour, receiving rave reviews for the record and their live performances. Featuring the hit songs Say This Sooner, Amazing Because It Is,
and Dirty And Left Out, Southern Weather captured the ears and the hearts of their fans alike. The Almost will be teaming up with Tooth
& Nail Records to release Southern Weather in a limited-edition double-sided vinyl
