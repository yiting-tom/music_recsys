Album Intro：

No-one can dispute that Ultravox were one of the most influential groups of the 80's, they reached the top of the charts with such classic singles as Vienna and Dancing with Tears in My Eyes. Many of their songs are now regarded as anthems of the 80's. Ingenuity is a fusion of old and new styles, a must for past and present Ultravox fans and an exciting reminder of what good music sounds like.
