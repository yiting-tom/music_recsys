沼倉愛美( ぬまくら まなみ )【 8 albums 92 lyrics 】
Manami Numakura (沼倉 愛美 Numakura Manami?, born April 15, 1988) is a Japanese voice actress and singer from Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. Her best-known role is in The Idolmaster video game franchise where she voices Hibiki Ganaha from the rival group known as Project Fairy. Other major roles include Riko in Love Lab, Takao in Arpeggio of Blue Steel, Machi Tokiwa in Hanayamata, Saya Endō in Dagashi Kashi. Some of her character albums have charted on Oricon.
