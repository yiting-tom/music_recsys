Album Intro：

2011 sophomore album from the Swedish Death Metal band. Incomparable is the follow-up to the band's acclaimed self-titled debut. The album was produced at Big Island Studios in Stockholm by Swedish studio-meister Jacob Hellner, best known for his work with the mighty Rammstein. Features the single 'Lost'. Universal.
