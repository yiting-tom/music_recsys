Album Intro：

Emma-Lee Moss, known by her stage name of Emmy the Great, is a London based singer-songwriter. Moss was born in Hong Kong, and as a child she emigrated with her family to London. She has toured with Tilly and the Wall, Martha Wainwright, Jamie T and Kimya Dawson amongst others and collaborated with several other artists, most notably Lightspeed Champion. She has played numerous festivals including the Truck Festival, Green Man Festival, Latitude Festival, Glastonbury, and she played the inaugural S?n Festival in Cardiff in 2007. Her debut album, First Love, is expected to be released in early 2009. 'We Almost Had a Baby' is the first single from the new album and was released on November 10. In the same month it was announced that she would be releasing her debut album 'First Love' in February 2009.
