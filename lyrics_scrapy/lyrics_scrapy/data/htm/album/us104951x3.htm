Album Intro：

Red (USA): is an American Christian, Post-Grunge/Nu Metal band formed in Nashville, Tennessee in 2004.

Musically influenced by artists such as Linkin Park, Chevelle, and Muse, Red brings a unique blend of roiling guitars, epic string arrangements, strong melodies, and passionate vocals. The songs on End of Silence deconstruct struggles that listeners face, while painting a picture of redemption in the midst of brokenness.
