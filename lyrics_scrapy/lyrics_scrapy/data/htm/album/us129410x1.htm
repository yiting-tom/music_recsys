Album Intro：

2010 two CD release. The world-renowned Shekina Glory Ministry is not your average choir. In fact, technically, the aren't a choir at all. They are a dynamic retinue of psalmists, minstrels, karar, encouragers and banner wavers who have become the paradigm for urban praise and worship music in the young millennium. Over the last decade, they have taken their place alongside Fred Hammond and Israel & New Breed as the trendsetters in contemporary praise and worship music.
