XYLO( XYLØ )【 9 albums 18 lyrics 】
XYLO (stylised as XYLØ) is an American alternative pop duo consisting of lead vocalist and songwriter Paige Duddy and songwriter and musician Chase Duddy. They are best known for their debut single, 'America', which was self-released through NoiseTrade in February 2015. A year later, this single became the lead single of their debut EP America.
