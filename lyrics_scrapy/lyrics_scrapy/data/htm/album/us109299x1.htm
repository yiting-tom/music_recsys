Album Intro：

Thriving Ivory is back with the follow-up to their breakthrough self-titled debut. As a VH1 You Oughta Know artist, the band found instant fans through the single Angels on the Moon , which became a Top 40 radio hit. On Through Yourself & Back Again, Thriving Ivory takes their signature sound one step further on piano-driven tracks like Love Alone, On Your Side, and the album s first single Where We Belong. Frontman Clayton Stroope s vocals are at once uplifting and heartbreaking, making Through Yourself & Back Again a memorable collection of rock anthems.
