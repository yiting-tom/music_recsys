Ane Brun( Ane Brunvoll )【 16 albums 200 lyrics 】
Ane Brun (Norwegian pronunciation: [ɑːnə brʉːn]) (born Ane Brunvoll on 10 March 1976 in Molde, Norway) is a Norwegian songwriter, guitarist, and vocalist. Since 2003 she has recorded eight albums, six of which are studio albums (including a collection of duets) and one live DVD. She has lived in Stockholm, Sweden, since 2001, where she writes, records, and runs her own label (Balloon Ranger Recordings) when not on tour.
