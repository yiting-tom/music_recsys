Album Intro：

...the Southern California quintet offers up a tightly executed attack that takes no prisoners on Wounds, mixing the melodic bent of its predecessor with a punky energy that propels tracks such as Black Earth and Devourer. The title track is a nearly nine-minute epic filled with ambitious dynamic shifts, while These Ties That Bind and arches of Existence flaunt some vigorous guitar flash. Let s hope these Wounds never heal. --Revolver magazine

They re American but you d never know it. With a real melancholic swirl deep in the darkest pits of the forest, Nordic metal assault The Funeral Pyre are unique in a way that s a joy to hear.... An astonishingly likeable album, free of cliché, that ll worm into your blackened heart. --Rock Sound magazine
