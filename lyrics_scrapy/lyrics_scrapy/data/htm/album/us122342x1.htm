Album Intro：

2012 debut by this House duo consisting of Hot Chip's Joe Goddard and Raf Rundell. The eclectic pair take their love of old school House music and add their own unique twist to the genre. A London record through and through, Be Strong ducks and weaves to the myriad sounds of the city as The 2 Bears pursue the perfect party record.
