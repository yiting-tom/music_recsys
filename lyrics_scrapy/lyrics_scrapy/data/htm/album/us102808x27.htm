Album Intro：

2008 release from Mark E. Smith and his ever-changing line-up of The Fall. Though band members have come and gone in the band, Mark E. Smith has managed to maintain a clear and focused musical vision. There's no other band quite like The Fall and once you've heard them, you'll never forget them. Smith has never been one to hold back on releasing new music and The Fall usually release six albums in the same span of time that a less interesting band will release one album and a single! Sanctuary.
