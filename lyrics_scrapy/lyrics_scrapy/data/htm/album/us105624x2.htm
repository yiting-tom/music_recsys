Album Intro：

Following the release of her debut album 'Over The Rainbow' [2007], Connie returns in 2008 with her 'Christmas Album'; including versions of the christmas classics 'Let It Snow', 'Rocking Around The Christmas Tree', 'When A Child Is Born', 'Merry Christmas Everybody', 'Jingle Bell Rock', 'Walking In A Winter Wonderland', 'I Wish It Could Be Christmas Every Day' and more; plus the 3 Bonus Recordings 'Silent Night', 'White Christmas' & 'Walking In The Air'. Pebble Beach Records.
