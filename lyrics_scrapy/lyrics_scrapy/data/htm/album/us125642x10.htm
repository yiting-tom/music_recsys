Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of the British Pop/Glam Rock band's 1979 including three bonus tracks. This edition of Cut Above The Rest, their first album after the departure of vocalist Brian Connolly, comes with it's original UK sleeve. On it's original release, Cut Above The Rest hit the Top 50 chart in Germany. Includes the German Top 20 single 'Call Me' plus the 'Big Apple Waltz' 45 .Now comes with three rare bonus tracks. Detailed liner notes and pictures of associated singles sleeves are a feature of the artwork. 7Ts. 2010.
