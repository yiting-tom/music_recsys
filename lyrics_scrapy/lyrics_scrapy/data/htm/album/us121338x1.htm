Album Intro：

Vic had no less than 14 hits, so it is high time somebody put together a CD featuring this beloved '60s crooner! And this collection's a model of its kind, featuring notes, color photos and 16 tracks remastered from the original stereo tapes. Includes Red Roses for a Blue Lady; Shangri-La; I Love You Drops; More; Little Altar Boy; If I Never Knew Your Name; I Will; Crystal Chandelier; Moonlight and Roses (Bring Mem'ries of You); Love Is All We Need; Bring a Little Sunshine (to My Heart); A Million and One , and more, many never before on CD!
