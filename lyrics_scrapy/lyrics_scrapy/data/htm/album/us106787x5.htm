Album Intro：

2012 release, the fourth album from the Soul/Hip Hop singer, songwriter and producer. Terius Nash 1977, titled after his real name and year of birth, was originally intended as a stop-gap release following the delays in the release of his intended fourth album Love IV MMXII. Terius Nash 1977 was initially released as a download-only album, but has been reworked and has become his fourth official album after the cancelled release of Love IV.
