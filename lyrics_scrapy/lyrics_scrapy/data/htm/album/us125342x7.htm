Album Intro：

The Biz was The Sea and Cake's third album released in October 1995. Out of print on vinyl for nearly a decade we are happy to finally offer it again. This 2012 edition was remastered to vinyl by Roger Seibel at SAE Mastering and pressed on high quality virgin vinyl. Included is an artworked inner sleeve and for the first time a download coupon!
