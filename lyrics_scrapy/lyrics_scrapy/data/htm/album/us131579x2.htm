Album Intro：

Steven Wilson is a twice Grammy-nominated producer, writer and performer, best known as founder and front man of British rock band Porcupine Tree. He has also produced and /or mixed albums for artists as diverse as Swedish metal band Opeth, Norwegian chanteuse Anja Garbarek, and progressive rock institution King Crimson. Last year, he also co-wrote and sang a track from Pendulum s number one album Immersion .



This 3 disc set contains his debut solo album, Insurgentes, along with the companion release NSRGNTS RMXS.



Insurgentes was described as 'an incredible musical journey... yet another masterpiece' (Classic Rock Prog) and this set features the album on CD and on DVD-A (5.1 surround mix).



NSRGNTS RMXS features tracks from the album remixed by David A. Sitek (TV On The Radio), Dälek, Engineers, Pat Mastelotto (King Crimson) and Fear Falls Burning.
