Album Intro：

Despite being an accomplished guitarist and songwriter, Sera preferred to stay behind the drum kit (drumming for the likes of Carissa's Wierd, LA musician Patrick Park, and on the Band Of Horses' debut, 'Everything All The Time') until one tour across the US with Park in 2006. She found herself inspired by the landscape and she wrote an album in her thoughts as they drove along the blue highways and back roads of the expansive west and the plains. '...a breathtaking collection of sad and dusky songs that reveal an artist of remarkable depth as well as a truly stunning voice' - The Stranger.
