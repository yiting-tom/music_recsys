Album Intro：

Revolting Cocks Are God! Al Jourgensen/Ministry & RevCo Founder



In 2006, After a hiatus of nearly a decade, Al Jourgensen resurrected his delinquent side project The Revolting Cocks with the star-studded release Cocked & Loaded, which included appearances by members of ZZ Top, Cheap Trick, the Butthole Surfers, and Jello Biafra.



RevCo s 2009 sophomoric release on Jourgensen s 13th Planet indie imprint Sex-O Olympic-O features the incarnation of RevCo s live touring musicians from the hugely successful 2006 MasterBaTouR, for which The Revolting Cocks served as direct support to Ministry. With Jourgensen at the helm as producer and mixologist, Sex-O Olympic-O features the unique talents of vocalist Josh Bradford, guitarist Sin Quirin, and keyboardist Clayton Worbeck.



States Jourgensen: I ve found these three young knuckleheads that capture the true essence and spirit of being a Revolting Cock.



Sex-O Olympic-O is positioned to be RevCo s most cohesive and focused album to date. Stand-out tracks include the dance floor single I m Not Gay, the futuristic Hookerbot3000, the devilishly demented Cousins and the dark, dub-based, electro-funk fused Wizard of SexTown featured on the Saw V Soundtrack.
