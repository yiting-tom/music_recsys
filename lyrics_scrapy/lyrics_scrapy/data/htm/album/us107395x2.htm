Album Intro：

AMG、Guardian 四顆星、PopMatters 9分、Pitchfork 7.5 分高評價

『這是一張具一貫性，才氣十足且大膽的唱片，值得一聽』…Amazon網站

這位出生於莫斯科但成長紐約布朗克斯的創作女聲Regina Spektor，在主流唱片重新發行「Soviet Kitsch」專輯後，獲得傳媒包括滾石、浮華世界、紐約時報一致推薦。聽過她的聲音後，想必你會馬上被她那神似Tori Amos、Fiona Apple與Bjork式，但卻更溫暖、天真與純粹，不受約束的自信嗓音所吸引。整張全新專輯充滿驚奇，不似「Soviet Kitsch」只有鋼琴伴奏，這次更加進吉他與輕快的絃樂編曲等元素。如同開場曲'Fidelity'，如天使的歌聲，加上大膽而奇異的Lyrics馬上勾住聽者的耳朵，這股魔力，一直持續至'Better'、'On The Radio' 等歌曲。除了這些天馬行空的創作外，還有如'Samson'、'Field Below'的抒情作品和'Hotel Song'等流行佳作。值得你一聽再聽。

Regina Spektor's last album, 2004's Soviet Kitsch, garnered praise from Time, Rolling Stone, Spin, Vanity Fair, The New York Times and many others. But this Russian-born, Bronx-bred singer-songwriter-pianist, who emerged from the NYC cafe circuit, continues to expand her vision. On Begin To Hope, produced by David Kahne (The Strokes, Sublime, Sugar Ray), she broadens here palette with electric guitar, drum machines and seductive electronic loops, finding new canvases for her provocative vocal style. Hope for pop has arrived with Regina Spektor.
