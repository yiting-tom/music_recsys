Album Intro：

Coming to you from sunny southern California and working at Saddleback church, Jadon Lavik has built up quite the radio story that is respected amoungst the 「worship genre.」 After releasing three albums—Moving on Faith (2004), Life on the Inside (2006) and the latest worship album Roots Run Deep (2008)…Jadon is now set to release 「The Road」 Acoustic this October. Lavik makes singing and playing guitar look so effortless as he takes the stage. You are sure to enjoy the simple acoustic sounds that only Jadon Lavik can produce in such the southern Californian fashion.
