Album Intro：

Deluxe two CD edition includes a bonus CD containing 11 remixes. 2011 collection from the German Hard/Industrial rockers spanning their entire career, 1995-2011, and including one brand new track, 'Mein Land'. Rammstein have never been about subtlety and this compilation of their most popular tracks is proof that the band are capable of mixing genres and pushing boundaries without raising a sweat.
