Album Intro：

Permission to Fly is the second studio album by American singer-songwriter, Jordan Pruitt. On July 22, the album will be released to all Limited Too stores only and in August the album will be released on iTunes. In September the album will be given a physical release to all other stores. This is confirmed on her official MySpace. The album is available for preorder on Amazon.com and is expected to be released on August 26, 2008.
