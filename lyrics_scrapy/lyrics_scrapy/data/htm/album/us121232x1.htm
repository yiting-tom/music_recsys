Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing of this 2009 release. This is the best acid record ever made! Venetian Snares actually made sweet love to his 303s while watching Top Gun on repeat. The end result is something that Goose would have been proud to die listening to. Heavy, ripping, unconventional, off-timed beats blast away any winter cobwebs and are 100% guaranteed to remind the listener why some things are best left to the professionals. 10 tracks.
