Arjun Kanungo【 1 albums 1 lyrics 】
Arjun Kanungo is an Indian singer, composer, actor, and entrepreneur. By 18, Arjun had set up a professional recording studio - Promethean audio. In the next few years, Arjun caught the attention of industry legend, Asha Bhosle, who he then toured with all over the world as her opening act and male lead.

A classically trained singer, Arjun's first commercial success came with 'Khoon Choos Le' in the 2013 film- Go Goa Gone, starring Saif Ali Khan, which rocked the charts Go Goa Gone (2013). Since then he has churned out 3 hit singles in 2 years- Baaki Baatein Peene Baad ft. Badshah, Fursat, and Ek Dafaa (Chinnama). Arjun has lent his voice to several Bollywood films. His Tamil Debut came with 'Sirrikadhey', composed by Anirudh Ravichander, from the film 'Remo'.

Arjun Kanungo's single 'Gallan Tipsiyaan' which literally translates to 'interesting conversations when drunk'. The party number has garnered massive appreciation online.

His song 'La la la' with Neha Kakkar, which released on February 7, 2018, is currently at 50 million views on YouTube and No. 7 on the Apple music pop charts.

His latest song 'Aaya Na Tu' with Momina Mustehsan, was released on June 19, 2018, and has gathered more than 7 million views on YouTube in 2 days.

Arjun won the GIMA Award for best debut in 2015. He was featured in Times of India's 100 most desirable men on 2016 at no. 31 beating huge names like Saif Ali Khan and Arjun Kapoor.

His fans proudly call themselves 'Fanungos'.
