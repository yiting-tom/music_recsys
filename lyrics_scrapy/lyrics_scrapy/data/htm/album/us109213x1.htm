Album Intro：

'Love Revolution' is Natalie's 6th studio album and was produced by award winning producer and husband, Bernie Herms. The songs on 'Love Revolution' were written by the likes of Natalie Grant, Jason Ingram, Jordin Sparks, Matthew West, Matt Bronleewe, Reuben Morgan, Stu G, Dan Muckala and others.

The deluxe edition of Love Revolution includes 3 exclusive bonus tracks: 'Held,' 'I Will Not Be Moved' and 'In Better Hands.'
