Album Intro：

Skin wa formed from the ashes of respected Brit rock outfits Kooga and Jagged Edge and led by guitarist extraordinaire Myke Gray. Thier self titled release, originally issued in 1994, produced by Keith Olsen (Whitesnake, Scorpions, Ozzy Osbourne) is big on hard hitting guitar riffs, killer hooks and meticulous production. Of course, the icing on the cake is vocalist Neville McDonald, a veritable iron lunged howler in the finest Coverdale/ Rodgers mold
