Album Intro：

The Ready Set's debut album 'I'm Alive, I'm Dreaming,' reflects the extent of this wunderkind's abilities - he plays every instrument on the record, synthesizing a lifetime of influences into one remarkably diverse album. 'I hate buying a twelve-song CD and having twelve of the same songs,' Witzigreuter laments. 'So I made sure no one song on my album sounds like any other. There's pop stuff, techno, rock - there's everything.'
