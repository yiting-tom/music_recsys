Album Intro：

Australian pressing of the 2008 debut album from the Irish/Aussie House/Electro quartet. Both DJs were born in Dublin, Ireland. During 2006 the DJ/Producers began recording songs in their Darlinghurst terrace with two vocalists MC Blu originally from Brisbane and now based out of Sydney and Ilan Kidron. 13 tracks including the massive hits 'Don't Hold Back', 'Are You With Me' and 'Trouble Trouble'. Universal.
