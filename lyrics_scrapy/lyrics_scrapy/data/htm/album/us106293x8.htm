Album Intro：

UK enhanced edition of their 2008 album includes two bonus tracks: 'I Must Be Dreaming' (Acoustic) and the enhanced video for 'Into Your Arms'. Can't Stop, Won't Stop is the 2008 studio album by the band, The Maine. They create a new sound by drawing influences from '90s radio Alternative bands and meshing styles like Indie, Pop and Rock. The Maine's mix of a wide variety of genres just sounds natural. These guys love their job because they love music. The Maine are out to capture your soul with their jaw dropping melodies and amazing hooks.
