Mike Will Made It( Michael Len Williams II )【 9 albums 69 lyrics 】
Michael Len Williams II (born March 23, 1989), known professionally as Mike Will Made It (often stylized as Mike WiLL Made-It) or simply Mike Will, is an American record producer, rapper, singer and songwriter from Atlanta, Georgia. He is best known for producing trap beats for several Southern hip hop artists as well as for producing several singles, such as 'Black Beatles' by Rae Sremmurd, 'Mercy' by GOOD Music, 'No Lie' by 2 Chainz, 'Bandz a Make Her Dance' by Juicy J, 'Pour It Up' by Rihanna, 'Love Me' by Lil Wayne, 'Body Party' by Ciara, 'We Can't Stop' by Miley Cyrus, 'First Day Out the Feds' by Gucci Mane, 'Formation' by Beyoncé, and 'Humble' by Kendrick Lamar. He has released six mixtapes.
