2009 debut album from the singer/songwriter. Sitting next to Angel Taylor, her charisma is as palpable as her music itself. Talking with her about the passionate swirl of her debut release, Love Travels, she is quick to share stories about her inspirations, but those stories fast become secondary to the true magic of her talent as a songwriter: The 12 tracks on Love Travels are more than just personal missives that Angel has set to music, they are living and breathing experiences that we have all shared. The names may change, but the stories remain the same.
