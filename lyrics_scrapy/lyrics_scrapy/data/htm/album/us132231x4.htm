Album Intro：

Susan Kay Wyatt is a richly diverse entertainer, songwriter and motivational speaker. The many faces of her music include inspirational pop, her work with children, and most recently a return full circle to Jazz Standards, which fit her like a glove. Her passion and message that remains constant in all of her work is to empower and inspire people of all ages to find their own unique gifts and live a life of joy that expresses them.
