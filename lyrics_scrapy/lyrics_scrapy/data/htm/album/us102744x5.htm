Album Intro：

'Believe Again' is a pop song written by Delta Goodrem, Brian McFadden, Stuart Crichton and Tommy Lee James, produced by Stuart Crichton and Marius de Vries for Goodrem's third studio album Delta (2007). The single, released in Australia on December 8th, 2007,[1] features exclusive remixes and the unreleased B-Side track, 'Fortune And Love'.[2] The single was released to radio on November 12, 2007, although it had its premiere on commercial radio in late-October, 2007.
