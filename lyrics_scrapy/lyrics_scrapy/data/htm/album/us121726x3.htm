Album Intro：

The spearhead of self-coined musical genre 'Hip-Pop', T. Mills combines the energy of Alternative Rock, with rap oriented lyrical structures, melodic pop hooks and a punk rock aesthetic to deliver a unique and cutting edge sound that's fast becoming the de-facto soundtrack for today's suburban youth.

A self proclaimed internet addict, Mills has used that medium to win over fans nationwide, with almost 3 million Myspace plays to date, and over 8,000 followers on twitter. That combined with this energetic live act- which utilizes live drums, keyboards, DJ and hype man- has steadily built his loyal following.

From his first shows on the entire 2009 Warped Tour (in the Babycakes tent) to his 2010 appearances at both Bamboozle and Bamboozle Left, as well as current tour with Bizzy Bone, T.MILLS has continues to move seamlessly through musical genres, appealing to rock fans, scene kids, hipsters, rap fans, punk rockers and hardcore kids alike.
