Album Intro：

2010 X-Factor winner Matt Cardle releases his highly anticipated debut album Letters. The Essex crooner has previously tried his luck with rock bands Darwyn and Seven Summers before entering the TV talent show, in which he subsequently triumphed. The winning song 'When We Collide' - a cover of Biffy Clyro's 'Many of Horror' - is featured here alongside second single 'Run for Your Life' which was penned by Gary Barlow. Cardle has co-written the rest of the album, working with James Walsh (Starsailor) and Eg White (Adele, Duffy, James Morrison) to complete the 13-track record.
