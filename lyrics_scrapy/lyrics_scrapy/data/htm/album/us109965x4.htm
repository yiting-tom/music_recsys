Album Intro：

2011 release, the third album from the British Popsters. Together We Were Made is a Feeling record that sounds, unashamedly, like The Feeling, but with a host of new twists and turns to explore. The album was recorded in frontman Dan Gillespie Sells' home (and the band's studio). The band worked alongside The Freemasons to produce the album, with the Brighton dance duo adding 'fairy-dust' to the these new tracks.
