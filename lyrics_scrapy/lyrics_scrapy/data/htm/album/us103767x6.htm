Album Intro：

如果葛萊美的評審加上巴黎服裝設計師，同時瘋狂討論同一個人，你是不是該仔細研究一下，此人是何方神聖？她就是關史蒂芬妮！擁有No Doubt不要懷疑女主唱的頭銜，全球1500萬張銷售基礎、葛萊美獎告示牌音樂獎得主、時尚雜誌票選全球50大性感人士，還有她是服裝品牌L.A.M.B.設計師，在今年紐約時尚周獨領風騷；記住她的名字，等於一手掌握世界流行資訊。

首張個人專輯【Love. Angel. Music. Baby/愛.天使.音樂.寶貝】在美國上市2周創下百萬銷售風潮，2005年2月底葛萊美頒獎典禮上，她以No Doubt的作品'It』s My Life'和個人專輯首發單曲 'What Your Waiting For'雙重身份入圍並擔任壓軸表演；同一個月，她在李奧納多的電影「神鬼玩家」露臉客串'珍哈露'一角，並且忙於推出2005年秋季LAMB新品。而真正創造大流行的，是關的【L.A.M.B.】跨季蟬連Top20榜上40周，美/英/加拿大/澳洲等10國白金銷售成績。

Gwen Stefani's second solo album, dance-oriented THE SWEET ESCAPE, is due on December 5th, 2006. The Neptunes produced the first single, 'Wind It Up,' and the record will also include tracks produced by Nellee Hooper, Dave Stewart, and Swizz Beatz.

It is speculated that the album will contain songs originally written for her 2004 debut solo album Love. Angel. Music. Baby. that did not make the final track listing and new material recorded during 2005 and 2006. The Sweet Escape's first single is 'Wind It Up', which Stefani premiered on her Harajuku Lovers Tour in 2005. Unlike Stefani's first solo record, this one carries a Parental Advisory label in North America. 'The Sweet Escape' is one of the tracks that has leaked onto the Internet.
