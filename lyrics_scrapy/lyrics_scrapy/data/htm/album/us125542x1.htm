Album Intro：

The Status have been recognized as one of the up and coming bands in Atlanta and were touted in Alternative Press as one of the top unsigned bands of 2007. They've toured with the likes of Bedlight For Blue Eyes, Sound The Alarm, and Tokyo Rose. The single 'Perfect Day' received regular rotation on specialty shows throughout 2007 and 2008 on 99X in Atlanta. Featured on Comcast Cable's 'Bands On Demand' program. RIYL: Bayside, Taking Back Sunday, All Time Low.
