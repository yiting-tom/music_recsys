Album Intro：

Toxik was formed around 1985, with the first lineup of Sanders, Christian, Erwin, and Dadabo, all of which wrote material for the 1986 'Wasteland' demo. Before the recording took place, bass player Erwin was replaced by Bonini. After three years and a lineup change, the band, comprised of Sanders, Christian, and new drummer Leger, recorded World Circus (Christian wrote all the music and lyrics), signed with Roadrunner Records. The band also wrote Think This released in 1989, also released on Roadrunner, this time with a replacement vocalist Charles Sabin and new guitarist John Donnely. Toxik broke up in 1992 after 2 albums and two world tours. The band made a huge impression with they fast guitar-based thrash metal. It's the most anticipated thrash reissue of this decade. This version includes exclusive bonus material, including unreleased songs.
