Album Intro：

2010 release. The Irish Tenors powerful vocal line-up of Finbar Wright, Anthony Kearns and Karl Scully is unquestionably the leading brand of this genre in the market. All classically trained singers, each having performed in leading concert halls across the world, together they combine to produce a sound and concert experience which touches hearts and stirs the emotions. Happy songs of celebration and humor, mixed with touching melodies and songs that warm the heart. Ireland features 13 of the most beloved Irish standards including, 'Whiskey In The Jar', 'Galway Bay' and , of course, 'Danny Boy'.
