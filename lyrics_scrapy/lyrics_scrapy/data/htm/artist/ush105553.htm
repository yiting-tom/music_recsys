Shin Hye Sung( Shin Hyesung )【 11 albums 88 lyrics 】
Jung Pil-kyo (born November 27, 1979), better known by his stage name Shin Hye-sung, is a South Korean singer-songwriter. He is best known as a member of the South Korean boy group Shinhwa.
