Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of this 1986 album from the R&B trio including bonus tracks. Hot Together featured the production of the legendary Richard Perry who is responsible for producing some of The Pointer Sisters best work. The first single to hit the charts was 'Goldmine', which shot its way into the Top 20 on the US R&B charts and made #33 spot on the US Pop Chart. BBR.
