Album Intro：

Trevor Andrew is already internationally recognised as one of the world's top snowboarders with two Olympic Games and numerous World Cup wins under his belt. And, now, Trevor - a.k.a. Trouble Andrew - is about to conquer the world of music with his debut Trouble Andrew, which was produced by John Hill/Shitake Monkey (Santogold, Lykke Li) and mixed by Mark Needham (The Killers). Trouble Andrew is a remixed and remastered edition of his self-released 2007 CD of the same name (previously available only via iTunes) with underground hits 'Chase Money' and 'Band Bang (feat. Santogold)' plus the addition of one newly recorded song, 'What's So Strange About Me'.
