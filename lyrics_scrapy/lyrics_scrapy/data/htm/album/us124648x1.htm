Album Intro：

The Originals are often described as Motown's best kept secret. Led by Freddie Gorman, The Originals took the R&B world by storm in 1969, although they had worked at Motown for years aas background vocalists. Freddie Gorman co-wrote'Please Mr. Postman' for The Marvelettes. Now for the first time on one UK CD enjoy the sound of The Originals and discover for yourself why the late great Marvin Gaye ranked among their biggest fans. 18 tracks. 2002.
