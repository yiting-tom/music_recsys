Album Intro：

Originating out of a small country town in Calhoun County, Florida in the late 90's with a straightforward post-grunge growl, Socialburn quickly took Tallahassee, Orlando, Jacksonville and all points in-between by storm. Their self released CD led to a deal with Elektra Records and a 2003 release Where You Are, a slice of post-grunge hard rock featuring Top 10 Rock/Alternative track 'Down' and SoundScan of over 90,000. Extensive touring with the likes of Default and Seether has created a strong and loyal national base of fans ready for Socialburn's followup The Beauty of Letting Go. IROCK. 2005.
