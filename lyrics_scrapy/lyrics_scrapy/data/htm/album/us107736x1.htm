Album Intro：

What began as three guys playing songs in a basement has transformed quickly into one of the most sought after bands in the country. When Promoters and Pastors are seeking out youth ministries that are not only relevant, but also willing and able to share the uncompromising Word of the Lord, Silverline is at the top of the list. After the success of their first independent single release, word started to spread throughout the music industry, and several regional rock radio stations picked up the single. Not often does an independent band have success on national radio. With their recent signing to VSR Music Group, Silverline is poised to break through the boundaries of the 「Christian Music Industry」 with their debut album Start to Believe. Silverline has had the privilege of sharing the stage with a myriad of well-known bands such as P.O.D., Hawk Nelson, Newsboys, Sanctus Real, TFK, and many others. Silverline is committed to rocking as hard as possible, and standing up for truth in our lost generation!
