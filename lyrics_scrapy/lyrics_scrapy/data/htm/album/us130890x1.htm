Album Intro：

Electro-pop group Speak presents their highly anticipated debut full-length album, I Believe In Everything. Always striving for original & innovative melodies, Speak garnered noteworthy attention with their 2010 debut EP, Hear Here, and received overwhelming industry praise. Their powerhouse synth-rock-pop anthems and infectious melodies have earned them honors like 'Best New Band' at Austin Music Award. I Believe In Everything was produced by the sought-after Chris 'Frenchie' Smith (JET, Dandy Warhols).
