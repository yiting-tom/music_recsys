Album Intro：

French-only expanded reissue of the American rocker's sophomore album originally released in 1968, now including six bonus tracks. The American Breed, best known for the title track, were also the creators of the song 'The Brain', used on the soundtrack for the French film Le Cerveau in 1968. This spectacular song is here in three different versions as bonus tracks to this already groovy album. Magic. 2009.
