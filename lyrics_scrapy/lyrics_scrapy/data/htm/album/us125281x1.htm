Album Intro：

2009 sophomore album from the British five piece recorded and produced in New York by none other than Mark Ronson. Welcome To The Walk Alone shows the Rumble Strips in a much more widescreen light than their previous offering with parping brass toned down and the FILMharmonic orchestra of Prague drafted in by Owen Pallet (The Arcade Fire, Final Fantasy) to fill this album with incredible cinematic textures and epic arrangements. Features the single 'Not The Only Person'. Island.
