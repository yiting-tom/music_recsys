Album Intro：

Limited edition package include bonus Large T-shirt. Digitally remastered and expanded edition of this 1976 live album from the Classic Rocker. Recorded at Cobo Hall in Detroit, MI, Bob Seger's landmark live album contains an exclusive, never before released bonus track, 'I Feel Like Breaking Up Somebody's Home', restored artwork and updated packaging. Bob Seger seemed he was destined to be a rock footnote when he recorded this live epic at Detroit's very partisan Cobo Arena in 1976. Seger had already released six Capitol LPs to little national attention at this point, but Live Bullet showcased him at his rockin' best--kind of like the Motor City's own Springsteen--and it caught on.
