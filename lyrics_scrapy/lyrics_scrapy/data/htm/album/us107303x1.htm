Album Intro：

To coincide with their appearance on the 2011 Soundwave Tour, and Sum-41 sideshows, There For Tomorrow is set to release a deluxe version of a little faster to include 5 bonus tracks taken off their self-produced remix EP , 'Re:Creations', for their Australian fans!The band describes their sound as 'a band oriented, innovative revision of our sound with incorporated use of dubstep, house, & other electro-based genres with rock'.
