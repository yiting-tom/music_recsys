Album Intro：

These three releases comprise the final wave of The Triffids' re-issue program that Domino Records started in 2006 with the re-issues of 'Born Sandy Devotional', 'Calenture', and 'In The Pines', all re-mastered and released as deluxe CDs with additional music and sleeve notes. Originally recorded in a series of midnight to dawn sessions, 'Treeless Plain' has been re-mastered and wonderfully updated.
