Album Intro：

Sundowner is an acoustic project of Chris McCaughan of The Lawrence Arms, also featuring Jenny Choi, Neil Hennessy, and Eli Caterer. Their debut album, Four One Five Two, was released on Red Scare Records on March 13, 2007.Since releasing the album, one official video has been made for the song 'This War is Noise', directed by Lawrence Arms bassist/singer Brendan Kelly.
