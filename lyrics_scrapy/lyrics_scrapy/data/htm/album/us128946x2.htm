Album Intro：

2010 album from the Christian Post-Hardcore band. Sent By Ravens team up with producer extraordinaire Aaron Sprinkle to craft an album with the perfect balance of brawn and beauty, muscle and melody. Our Graceful Words is a heavy-hitting set of emotive hard rock numbers, including 'New Fire,' 'Beautiful List,' 'Salt and The Light,' and more.
