Album Intro：

2011 release, the third album from the Hard Rockers. Justice is a theme Rev Theory has rallied around collectively. It is a concept or symbol encompassing a philosophy of rebellion and a statement towards society. Guitarist Julien Jorgensen offered, 'Every one walking this Earth has encountered an injustice in their lives. Whether it be professionally, philosophically, religiously, spiritually, politically, or even in a relationship they hold dear, this song is a statement towards standing up for one's self and taking what is yours. It is a time in society where adversity must be challenged with strength, and as a band we have rallied around the belief that our time has come and we are going to grab it.'
