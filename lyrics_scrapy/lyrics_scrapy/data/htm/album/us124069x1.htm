Album Intro：

2008 sophomore album from this UK trio, probably the biggest underground Punk band in Britain. Taking a huge step forward from the DIY scratchiness of their first album, Save The World, Get The Girl, sees the band's impressive songwriting chops given a new sheen by veteran producer (Clive Langer - Madness, Elvis Costello) as well as long time collaborator Peter Miles. The CD is augmented by enhanced bonus material - including photo galleries, videos, comics and filmed thank yous. 11 tracks including the single 'My Boulder'. Island.
