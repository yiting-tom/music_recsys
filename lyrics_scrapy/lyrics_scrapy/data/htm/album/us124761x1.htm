Album Intro：

The Plot In You's debut full length took the metalcore world by storm, making a profound splash. The band shows no signs of slowing down with their second album Could You Watch Your Children Burn. The bands angry, yet deep and inquisitive record explores many themes that the youth of today battle with on a regular basis. They approach it with a cynicism and ire that you'd expect from a metalcore band, delivered brutally, while still weaving incredible melodies and insanely catchy choruses.
