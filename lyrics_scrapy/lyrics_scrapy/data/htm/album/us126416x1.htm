Album Intro：

The 2008 album Madeline from Tickle Me Pink includes a live DVD featuring performances from the band's sold-out show in their hometown of Fort Collins, CO. Tickle Me Pink hit the road in the summer of '08 with Scary Kids Scaring Kids, Finch and Foxy Shazam, as well as play select dates on the Vans Warped Tour.
