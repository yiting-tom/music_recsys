Album Intro：

Following their 3rd album from 2006, 'Antithesis', the press dubbed Secrets Of The Moon 'THE reference of German Black Metal' (leading European magazine LEGACY), but with 'Privilegivm' the band caps it all off and is ready for their big strike. Their new album consists of pitch-black, complex, yet always stirring Black Metal beyond all comparisons that will surely set new genre standards.On US tour with Moonspell and Samael in autumn (23 dates).
