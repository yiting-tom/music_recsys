Album Intro：

Snowy White played on Fleetwood Mac founder Peter Green's In the Skies album. He has toured and/or recorded with Mick Taylor, Thin Lizzy, Pink Floyd, and Roger Waters, as well with his own White Flames.

This release combines the previously-released albums Change My Life and Blues On Me.

White is one of a few classic blues-oriented British electric guitarists whose sound, style, and passion echo the originality of the blues, and who inject it with the excitement of contemporary rock music.
