Album Intro：

2008 digitally remastered and expanded two CD edition of the debut album from the Synthpop duo featuring 10 bonus tracks on Disc One plus a bonus CD featuring 17 remixes including tracks from the Non-Stop Ecstatic Dancing mini album. Originally released in 1981, the album's critical and commercial success was bolstered by the worldwide success of its single 'Tainted Love', a cover version of a little-known R&B song by Gloria Jones which topped charts worldwide and became the best-selling British single of 1981 in the United States. The album produced two more Top Five singles in the UK with 'Bedsitter' and the unapologetically sentimental 'Say Hello, Wave Goodbye'.
