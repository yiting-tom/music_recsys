Album Intro：

Double vinyl LP pressing. 2011 album from the Blink 182 drummer, paparazzi target and Hip Hop producer. The first single 'Can A Drummer Get Some' features The Game, Swizz Beats, Lil Wayne, and Rick Ross. Additional tracks include 'Jump Down' (featuring The Cool Kids) and 'Carry It' (featuring RZA, Raekwon, and Tom Morello), 'Devil's Got A Hold of Me' (featuring Hip Hop supergroup Slaughterhouse), 'Cool Head' (featuring Kid Cudi) and more.
