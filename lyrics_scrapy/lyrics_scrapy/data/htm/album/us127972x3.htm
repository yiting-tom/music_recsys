Album Intro：

Three CD set. After four long years, Damien Saez is finally releasing his fourth album. During the break, he took the time to record not only one, but a triple acoustic album entitled Varsovie - L'Alhambra - Paris. On 29 different tracks - including the songs 'A L'Alhambra', 'Je Cherche Encore', 'Chanson Pour Mon Enterrement', 'Jeunesse Lève-toi' and many, many more - the listeners are able to see Saez in a much more intimate way than we have before. The main subject and focus of this release is the breakup with his girlfriend and how he feels about this. Furthermore, it's the first time once can see his face on the cover.
