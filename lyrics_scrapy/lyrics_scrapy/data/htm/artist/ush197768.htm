C-BooL, actually Grzegorz Cebula (born on May 30, 1981 in Pyskowice) - Polish DJ and music producer.

Curriculum vitae
Career

Grzegorz Cebula showed interest in club music from an early age. In 1998 he started working professionally as a DJ, he performed in local discos and music clubs. Four years later he became a music producer. In 2003, he collaborated with the Casteam team. Two of the most popular recordings from 2003-2004 - 'Would You Feel' and 'House Baby' - were released under the pseudonym C-BooL.

In 2010, he gained publicity thanks to the song 'Body & Soul', recorded with the vocalist Isabelle, who hit many national charts. In June 2011, he released his debut album entitled 8 Years, which featured songs made by DJ at the turn of eight years and three premiere productions. The release was promoted by the single 'Do not Waste the Time,' which was recorded with Jo's guest appearance. In the summer of 2012, the DJ single 'Do You Know' was released.

In 2016, the single 'Never Go Away' was released. The composition was ranked first on the AirPlay list, the most-played songs on Polish radio stations. The recording gained the status of a diamond single in Poland, exceeding the number of 100,000 copies sold. On October 24 of the same year, the single 'Magic Symphony' appeared with guest appearance by Giang Pham. The hit was listed in the first place on the AirPlay list. The song overlapped with quadruple platinum in Poland, spreading over 80,000 copies. On April 26, 2017, the single 'DJ Is Your Second Name' was released. The track was ranked 3rd in the AirPlay ranking.
