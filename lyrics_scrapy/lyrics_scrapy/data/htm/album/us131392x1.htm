Album Intro：

Signed to Hut Recordings on the strength of a handful of songs presented acapella & delivered by post, Stephanie Kirkham's debut album features some of the very songs that made such a huge impression on those who first heard her fledgling demo tape. Recorded in Dublin, produced by Ian Stanley (Tori Amos, Natalie Imbruglia, The Pretenders) & mixed by Danton Supple (Coldplay's 'A Rush Of Blood To The Head'), 'That Girl' provides the ideal introduction to a fresh female vocalist with a uniquely English sound. Features 10 tracks including the single 'Inappropriate'. Copy Controlled. Hut. 2003.
