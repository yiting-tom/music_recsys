Album Intro：

The Rolling Stone Album of the Year for 1970: Let It Be? Morrison Hotel? Moondance? After the Gold Rush? Nope: it was Parachute , a harmony-heavy rocker and another stunning concept album following on the heels of S.F. Sorrow . Here's In the Square; The Letter; Rain; Miss Fay Regrets; Grass , and the rest of the original LP plus period singles and recently recorded acoustic reinterpretations!
