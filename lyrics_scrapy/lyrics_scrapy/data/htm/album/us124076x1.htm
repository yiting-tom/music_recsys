Album Intro：

Euphoric and evocative, listening to the kissaway trail is an intoxicating experience. Hailing from odense, denmark, the band's self titled debut was filled with songs of immense ambition and scope. The music soared with majestic guitar lines, dramatic melodies and epic arrangements. Nme described it as 'savagely beautiful noise', while the independent titled the record 'debut album of the year'. With two lead singers and two songwriters bringing even more colour to the band's already diverse sound, the result is a remarkable twelve songs; loaded with catchy melodies, large string arrangements, banjos and mandolins, altogether creating a uniquely impressive soundscape. The kissaway trail's profile has increased considerably in 2010 via their extensive touring and incendiary live shows across the uk and us. The band performed at last month?s sxsw festival in texas, and are currently touring abroad with midlake.
