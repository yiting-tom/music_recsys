Album Intro：

2010 release from the New Jersey-based singer/songwriter, choir director, and multi-instrumentalist. Tye Tribbett has evolved into one of Gospel music's most dynamic and respected performers. With this new release Fresh, Tye is embarking on his first solo recording without Greater Anointing (G.A.). He is making a fresh start, with a new invigorated spirit of his music and ministry. The sound of Fresh is a blend of the up-tempo, inspirational songs that Tye has become known for, and some songs that take a new direction straddling the CCM/Rock fence. Fresh will expand the senses of gospel music connoisseurs near and far.
