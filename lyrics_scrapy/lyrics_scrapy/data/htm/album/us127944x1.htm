Album Intro：

2008 compilation from the British Rock outfit, that enjoyed a successful career from the mid-'70s through the early '80s. The group's leader, singer Paul Young (not the same Paul Young that scored the '80s hit 'Every Time You Go Away'), got his start with music in the mid-'60s, when he fronted a forgotten Manchester group called the Toggery Five, which included a few members that would later go on to join Prog rockers Jethro Tull. Young formed Sad Caf‚ in the mid '70s and achieved moderate but deserved success around the world. He later went on to join Mike & The Mechanics, handling lead vocals alongside Paul Carrack. This Sad Cafe collection features songs from their recordings for A&M, Vertigo, RCA and Atlantic. 16 tracks. Store For Music.
