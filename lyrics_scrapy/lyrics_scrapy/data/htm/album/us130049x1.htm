Album Intro：

2011 sophomore album from the Dutch Prog Rock band. In the summer of 2010, Sky Architect set out to record a follow up to their debut album, Excavations Of The Mind. In the lovely woods of Sweden, they found their new home, where their musical creativity felt no boundaries. The result: A stunning 76 minute long concept album telling the story of a man's flight to redemption. The album sounds much more mature, and overall more balanced. Mellow at some points and at other times a real Prog-extravaganza with distorted clavinets, soaring guitar solos and crazy odd rhythms. Surely a must have for fans of Gentle Giant, the Flower Kings, Beardfish and Pain Of Salvation.
