Album Intro：

Blake Tollison Shelton (born June 18, 1976 in Ada, Oklahoma[2]) is an American country music artist. In 2001, he made his debut with the single 'Austin'. Released as the lead-off single from his self-titled debut album, 'Austin' went on to spend five weeks at Number One on the Billboard Hot Country Songs charts. This song was the first single from his gold-certified debut album, which also produced two more Top 20 hits.

His second and third albums, 2003's The Dreamer and 2004's Blake Shelton's Barn & Grill, were each certified gold as well. Shelton's fourth album, Pure BS, was issued in 2007, and re-issued in 2008 with a cover of Michael Buble's song 'Home' as one of the bonus tracks. This cover was also that album's third single. A fifth album, Startin' Fires, was released in November 2008.

Overall, Shelton has charted a total of fourteen singles on the country charts, including four Number One hits: 'Austin' (2001), 'The Baby' (2003), 'Some Beach (2004-2005), and 'Home' (2008). Besides these, he has charted two more Top Ten hits in the #10 'Goodbye Time' (a cover of the Conway Twitty song) and the #4 'Nobody but Me'.

Startin' Fires is the fifth studio album released from country music singer Blake Shelton. The album was released on November 18, 2008, via Warner Bros. Nashville. Its lead-off single, 'She Wouldn't Be Gone', has charted in the Top 20 on the Billboard country charts.

Blake Tollison Shelton（1976年6月18日生於美國俄克拉何馬州的埃達）是一位美國鄉村樂人。2001年以其《Austin》除顯於大眾面前。他自命名的領頭單曲《Austin》一發行便連續五周榮登Billboard熱門鄉村歌曲榜冠軍之位。這首歌在他的金牌專輯中位列第一，同時獲得兩個Top 20的成績。

他的第二張和第三張專輯，2003年的《The Dreamer》和2004年的《Blake Shelton's Barn & Grill》，每張都達到金牌品質。其第四張專輯《Pure BS》發行於2007年，於2008年重新錄製，其中包含了Michael Buble的那首《Home》作為新增加的曲目。這張專輯還包含了三首新單曲。其第五張專輯《Startin' Fires》發行於2008年11月。

在其鄉村曲單中Shelton總共列了14首單曲，其中包括四首冠軍歌曲：《Austin》（2001），《The Baby》（2003），《Some Beach》(2004-2005)和《Home》(2008)。此外，他還列了兩首前十名歌曲：排名第十的《Goodbye Time》(Conway Twitty的歌)和排名第四的《Nobody but Me》。

《Startin' Fires》是鄉村歌手Blake Shelton發行的第五張專輯。這張專輯將於2008年11月18日由華納兄弟發行。納什維爾。其領軍單曲《She Wouldn't Be Gone》已經登上Billboard熱門鄉村歌曲榜前20之位。
