Album Intro：

With Roots Above and Branches Below is the third studio album by American metalcore band The Devil Wears Prada.

The album is set to be released on May 5, 2009 through Ferret Music. On their 'Sweet Brag Tour,' the band offered a download card for the songs 'Dez Moines' and 'Assistant to the Regional Manager'. With Roots Above and Branches Below will be released in both a standard CD version, and a deluxe CD/DVD version, with the full length CD, and the DVD that contains a making of the album that was featured on their myspace. There are some clips that were deleted from the clips on their myspace that are featured in the DVD.

The albums song 'Dez Moines' will be available as a free download on Guitar Hero: World Tour on May 7th.
