Album Intro：

Sarah Fimm has released two independant albums 'Cocooned' and 'A Perfect Dream' prior to 'Nexus.' Her beautiful voice draws you into albums and the electronic grooves let you get lost in her world, her vision. Whether she hooks you with her frankness or her sexuality there is always this need to hear more.
