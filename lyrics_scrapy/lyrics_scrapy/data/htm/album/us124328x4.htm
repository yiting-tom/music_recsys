Album Intro：

The McClymonts are 3 sisters from the small town of Grafton, NSW, Australia. Over the last five years The McClymonts have conquered the Australian market with 2 Gold Albums, 7 #1 hits, Awarded Best Group of the Year 4 years in a row at The Australian Country Music Awards, they took home the 2010 ARIA for Best Country Album for `Wrapped Up Good' and are one of the most successful live touring acts in Australia with sell out shows nationally. In 2010 The McClymonts were Jason Aldean's special guests on his USA Fall Tour. The McClymonts have relocated to the USA and Nashville is their new home. They will be touring extensively through summer. Discover the McClymonts - you will be Wrapped Up Good!
