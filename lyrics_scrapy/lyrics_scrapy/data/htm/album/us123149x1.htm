Album Intro：

2008 digitally remastered and expanded edition of the debut album from the '60s Bubblegum band fronted by Ron Dante, best known for being the vocalist for The Archies. The Cuff Links were a legendary studio group featuring Ron Dante and were the brainchild of Pat Rizzo (formerly of Joey Dee and the Starliters). Featuring sax, trumpet, guitar, bass, drums, organ and vocals, the seven-piece combo created an infectious sound with their innovative renditions of contemporary Pop hits and they came to the attention of legendary song-writing/production team Paul Vance & Lee Pockriss ('Itsy Bitsy Teenie Weenie Yellow Polka Dot Bikini') In addition to the debut album, we have added the subsequent Decca singles (featuring vocalist Joey Cord) and with one exception, all make their CD debut here.
