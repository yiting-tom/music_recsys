NOW AVAILABLE ON LIMITED EDITION VINYL FORMAT WITH BONUS TRACKS! Systems Officer is the solo guise of Armistead Burwell Smith IV, the prolific multi- instrumentalist songwriter and founding member of Pinback and Three Mile Pilot. Written and recorded between breaks in the rigorous Pinback recording and touring schedule, Underslept is five years in the making, a concise collection of thoughtful, complex pop songs.
