Album Intro：

Unshakable delivers energetic and passionate modern worship from the Deluge Band of Bethany World Prayer Center in Baton Rouge. Featuring lead vocalist Jonathan Stockstill, this powerful album is the follow-up to their debut CD which Christianity Today Magazine named one of their Favorite Worship Albums of 2008. Unshakable is sure to have wide appeal to both young and old as it calls listeners into a place of exuberant praise and intimate worship.
