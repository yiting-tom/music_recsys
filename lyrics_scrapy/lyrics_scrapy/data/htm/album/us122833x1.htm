Album Intro：

Album #2 from LA popmeisters after their acclaimed debut, I Can't Go In, I'll Go On. Eschewing the broader West Coast roots rock palette of its predecessor, the new album is pared down to the essentials, keeping tight pop melodies to the fore, and is replete with clever guitar lines, metronomic drum pulses and sublime harmonies. Licensed to Longtime Listener from Merge Records (The Arcade Fire, M.Ward, Lambchop), the Australian/NZ edition includes an exclusive bonus track unavailable anywhere else in the world.
