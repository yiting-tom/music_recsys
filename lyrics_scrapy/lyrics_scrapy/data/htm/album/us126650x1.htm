Album Intro：

2010 two CD release, the seventh album from the Danish singer/songwriter. Welcome Back Colour is a stunning collection of new songs, re-workings of favorites and all the classics in on package. It draws a final stroke under Dico's career to date and marks a new musical beginning. To get this far, she needed the peace and reflection that she only found when she retreated to her native Denmark after several years residing in London.
