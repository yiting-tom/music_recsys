Album Intro：

和The Fray一樣，Seabird從英倫鋼琴搖滾當中學到了那種紳士般的浪漫情懷，並將之放置在他們的兩張專輯當中，人聲部分較弱的他們在第二張專輯《Rocks Into Rivers》中將更多的功夫花在如何將鋼琴與電吉他的編織上，促使整張專輯在旋律之上既有著電吉他的狂放又不失鋼琴之優雅，專輯所唱響的情緒也偏向於令人雀躍的那種欣喜，對於喜愛流行鋼琴搖滾的人而言，年底Seabird的這張專輯可以彌補自The Fray的同名專輯之後再沒有相同類型的此類專輯的遺憾，而巧的是，這張在前半部分愉悅動人的專輯，到了中後部分也不可避免的與今年同樣毀譽參半的專輯《The Fray》那樣，出現了不少濫竽充數之作。


SEABIRD has a knack for creating songs that you can't get out of your head. Their debut release `Til We See The Shore' is an album that received critical acclaim from media for doing just that. Seabird has seen their music make its way on to hit TV shows like Grey's Anatomy, Numb3rs, and others giving them exposure on the major television networks. Their sophomore release Rocks Into Rivers reflects the experience and maturity you have come to expect from Seabird. Working with producers in LA (Matt Hales - Aqualung) and Nashville (Paul Moak) Rocks Into Rivers delivers honest songs about love and loss Seabird is known for, while merging the sonic worlds of modern piano rock and Brit-pop to create one-of-a-kind melodies. Rocks Into Rivers is sure to be another Seabird record to receive critical acclaim and is a must have for every Seabird fan and a must listen for those who have yet to experience the band.
