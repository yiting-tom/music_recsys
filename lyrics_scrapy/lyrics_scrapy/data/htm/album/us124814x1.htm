Album Intro：

2012 album from the British popsters, their first studio release in 17 years. Echoes And Rhymes is a concept covers album, on which they revisit songs from semi-obscure bands from the '60s with women lead singers. From Garage to Psychedelia, with touches of Soul and Sunshine Pop, multiple styles come together to form a special type of compilation, revisited with all the love and affection and energy of The Primitives.
