Album Intro：

'Alpha' is scheduled for release on March 6 via SEVENDUST's own label, 7 Bros. Records, which recently entered into a partnership with Asylum/Warner Music Group.

Produced by Joe Miller, John Connolly, Morgan Rose and Shawn Grove, 'Alpha' will be the group's first effort since 2005's 'Next'.
