Album Intro：

Deluxe CD/DVD edition of this 2009 live release from the Alt-Rock band. Somebody's Gonna Miss Us is an amazing live performance of career-spanning material from The Starting Line's final hometown show in Philadelphia. This set features a full-length concert and an exclusive documentary following the band through the tour to just after their final show -- filled with open and honest interviews with band members, friends and families sharing their thoughts on TSL's career.
