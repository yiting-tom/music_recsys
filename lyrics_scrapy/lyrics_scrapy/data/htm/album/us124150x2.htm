Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded three CD edition of this 1997 album from the British Alt-Rock/Folk band. Produced by Jon Kelly, the album features more of the band's biggest hits in 'What A Beautiful Day', 'Celebrate', 'Dog Train' and 'Too Real'. Expanded to three CDs, this reissue contains the non-album b-sides (including many covers) and remixes, two songs exclusive to the `Best Of' compilation, a newly written note Alan Robinson and all the lyrics. Edsel.
