Album Intro：

2008 sophomore album from Starbreaker featuring world renowned musicians such as singer Tony Harnell (TNT, Westworld, Morning Wood), guitarist Magnus Karlsson (Allen/Lande, Primal Fear) and John Macaluso (Malmsteen, TNT, Ark) on drums. The album was written and produced by Harnell and Karlsson and was mixed by respected producer Tommy Hansen (TNT, Jorn, Pretty Maids, Helloween). New bassist Jonni Lightfoot (a touring and recording veteran with extensive credits to his name) has been added to the line up, adding a tasteful but aggressive touch and complimenting Macaluso's flashy drumming perfectly. Love's Dying Wish is a very special album that progresses from where the Starbreaker debut left off. With a fresh diversity that spans from pounding Metal, to deep moody passages wrapped in a dark and beautifully melodic package. Frontiers.
