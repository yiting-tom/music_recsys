Album Intro：

2012 studio album from the Glam rockers. This collection of covers and classic tracks is full of little gems and surprises, stamped right through the middle with a vibe and sound that is unmistakably Sweet. From Velvet Undergrounds 'Sweet Jane' or Russ Ballard's 'New York Groove' (a smash hit for Hello in the 70s) up to punk anthems like Ramones 'Blitzkrieg Bop' or 'It's All Moving Faster' (originally from Electric Frankenstein). Not to forget a great version of the '80s pop classic 'You Spin Me Right Round', The Sweet has turned every single track into their own.
