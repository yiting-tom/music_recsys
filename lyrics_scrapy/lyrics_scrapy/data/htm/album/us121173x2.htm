Album Intro：

VAUX's total commitment to their art dates back six years, to their humble origins in a basement in Denver, Colorado. Since then, they've taken punk's D.I.Y. ethics straight to heart, scrimping, saving, & struggling to release their own records, finance their own tours & orchestrate their own dazzling & unique light show. Amazingly, the band's energy & intensity isn't lost in the translation onto tape, as their recordings prove to be an adrenaline rush just as the VAUX live experience.
