Album Intro：

Emmy-Nominated songwriter/lyricist & Los Angeles based performer Rie Sinclair, has been writing music for ABC for the last two years. Her yet to be released song, Where You Are (co-written with producer/guitarist Bob Hartry), was nominated as Outstanding Original Song for Daytime Television this year. While still waiting for release of some of her more popular songs from ABC, she's released her current EP, Glow , and now working on several short-cut Singles.
