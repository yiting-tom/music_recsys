Album Intro：

Under The Influence by Tony Lucca is an album that includes a nod to Lucca's key influences (Simon, Jagger/Richards, CSN, Springsteen, Petty, Withers, Page/Plant) in a life spent in music. Raised in Michigan, Lucca began singing at age 3; by 12 he was writing and playing. Over the course of his career, Lucca has shared stages with Macy Gray, Johnny Lang, the late Chris Whitley, Sara Bareilles, Tyrone Wells, and Jay Nash/Matt Duke (which resulted in TFDI)
