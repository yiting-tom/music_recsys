Album Intro：

Originally from Santa Cruz but now based not far away in the spiritual home of psychedelia, San Francisco, this six piece (all aged 22-23) presents their soulful take on psychedelia. Drawing musical inspiration from BLACK SABBATH, CAN and CREATION RECORDS, they forged their sound in intense jams. But mellower NEIL YOUNG / MMJ moments are as much a part of their approach, giving the heavier moments even more impact. Their debut was produced by COLIN STEWART who did an amazing job on BLACK MOUNTAIN's last opus.
