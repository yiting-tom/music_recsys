Album Intro：

Double ten inch vinyl pressing of this 2006 EP from the highly original band from Sheffield, one of the city's best kept musical secrets. Produced by the legendary John Leckie (XTC/Stone Roses/etc), this five track release will keep you hooked with it's instantly hummable melodies and daring imagination. Features '20 To 9', 'Hemsworth Hallway', 'Russian Snow', 'Sun Goes Down' and 'Going Away'. Parlophone.
