Album Intro：

Continuing their climb to prominence from modest Northwest roots, The Lonely Forest presents its Trans Records label debut Arrows. The album release follows a year filled with recording, touring and the release of the band's eponymous digital-only EP. With multiple tours throughout 2010, The Lonely Forest enjoyed the steady journey from Northwest D.I.Y. favorites to national touring act.
