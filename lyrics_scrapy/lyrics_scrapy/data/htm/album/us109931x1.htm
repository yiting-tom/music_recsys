Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded three CD edition of the Liverpool band's 1988 album. Blind contains some of the Icicle Works' best individual tracks, including the singles 'High Time' and 'Little Girl Lost'. Features the complete original album plus two discs filled with rare cuts, non-album tracks, live versions and more. Cherry Red.
