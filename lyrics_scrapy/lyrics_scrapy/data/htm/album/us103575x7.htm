Album Intro：

Todd Rundgren, founding member of the late 60's Philly rockers Nazz, multi-instrumentalist, vocalist, songwriter & producer, released his 1978 masterpiece Hermit Of Mink Hollow during his years in Woodstock, NY. Previously unavailable on vinyl for several decades, the album was mastered from the original Bearsville tapes by Joe Reagoso on 180 Gram Audiophile Vinyl & packaged in a deluxe gatefold cover. Album includes the hit 'Can We Still Be Friends' (covered a year later by Robert Palmer) which reached #29 in the U.S. and helped make the album Rundgren's 2nd best seller of his career.
