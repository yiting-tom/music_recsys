Album Intro：

A filthy dub, punk and reggae band, wrapped up in a very accessible pop-package. Think Sublime meets No Doubt! With this formula they have become one of Toronto's most popular offspring, drawing huge crowds everywhere they play!

IllScarlett has performed/toured with The All-American Rejects, Cypress Hill, Buckcherry, Yellowcard, Pepper, Badfish, Pepper, Finch, Stae Radio, Goldfinger, Swollen Members, Gnarls Barkley and Dropkick Murphy's.

The band set up generators in the parking lot of of the Warped Tour to entertain festival fans waiting in line. Warped Tour founder Kevin Lyman invited them backstage to play a private barbecue for all of the other bands on the tour. The band's 2 guerilla performances that day impressed Lyman so much that he invited them out to play on the past 3 Warped Tours in the United States & Canada.

The album 'All Day With It' released in 2007. And now, its Limited Edition has turn up!
