Album Intro：

The JaneDear Girls is an American country duo, consisting of Susie Brown (vocals, mandolin, fiddle) from Alpine, Utah, and Danelle Leverett (vocals, guitar) from Amarillo, Texas. The duo has signed with Warner Music Group Nashville's Reprise label and released its debut single, 'Wildflower.' This song has charted on Hot Country Songs.
