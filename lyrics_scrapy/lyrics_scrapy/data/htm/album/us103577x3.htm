Album Intro：

2007 Japanese release of the fourth album by the singer/songwriter produced by Sean Genockey (Manic Street Preachers, Kula Shaker, Suede). All songs were written by McRae. His most accessible work to date, it's a stellar collection of instantaneous melodies and heartfelt lyrics, all driven by his unique voice. This edition includes 2 bonus tracks: acoustic versions of 'Got A Suitcase, Got Regrets' and 'Sound Of The City'.
