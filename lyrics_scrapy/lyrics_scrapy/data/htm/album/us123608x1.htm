Album Intro：

The Frozen Autumn is an Italian darkwave band, formed in May
1993, characterized by a blend of melancholic vocals, atmospheric
gothic-wave, and 80s-style electro-pop. Having established a name
for itself with classic albums such as Fragments of Memories (1997)
and Emotional Screening Device (2002), The Frozen Autumn now
presents its latest masterpiece, Chirality. An artistic collage of
seamlessly blended styles and genres, Chirality is a beautiful addition
to The Frozen Autumn s legacy.
