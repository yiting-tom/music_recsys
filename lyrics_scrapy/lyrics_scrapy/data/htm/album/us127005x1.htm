Album Intro：

After the great success of To-+o Rosario's release To-+o En America, Warner Music Latina is proud to present To-+o's newest production entitled Amigo Mio...To-+o y Sus Exitos. This album contains 13 songs of To-+o's greates hits and two never before released songs. The song 'Kulikitaka Ti' was re-mastered live and released for the first time and 'Amigo Mio' a reggaeton duet with Tego Calderon and To-+o himself. Thanks to the collaboration of Tego Calderon (known as one of the biggest reggeaton artists of Puerto Rico), Amigo Mio...To-+o y Sus Exitos will definitely be one of To-+o's best ever.
