Alvaro Soler( Álvaro Soler )【 4 albums 28 lyrics 】
Álvaro Tauchert Soler (Spanish: [ˈalβaɾo tauˈtʃeɾt soˈleɾ]; born 9 January 1991) is a Spanish-German singer. He rose to prominence across Europe and Latin America with his 2015 hit 'El Mismo Sol', primarily hitting success in Italy, Switzerland and Mexico. A special bilingual Spanish-English version of 'El mismo sol', featuring Jennifer Lopez, was also recorded for international release in the United States, in the United Kingdom, and worldwide. His follow up single 'Sofia' in 2016 also achieved chart success in European countries, reaching number 1 in Poland, Italy and Switzerland.
