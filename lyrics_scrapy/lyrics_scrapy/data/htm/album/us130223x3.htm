On the strength of their refreshing and versatile sound and intoxicatingly energetic live show, Cape Breton's Slowcoaster releases 'Future Radio', a follow-up to their 2005 Debut LP 'Where Are They Going,' winner of 'Alternative Recording of the Year' (2006 East Coast Music Awards).
