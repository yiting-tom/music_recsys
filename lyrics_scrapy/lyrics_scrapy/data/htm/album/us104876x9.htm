Album Intro：

娜 喊 世 界 唯 一 蕾 哈 娜



★2000年以來締造8首告示牌排行冠軍，本世紀最多冠軍單曲女歌手★

★首發主打“Only Girl (In The World)”榮登英國金榜+美國舞曲榜冠軍，摘下歐美14國No.1后冠；第2波Drake跨刀單曲“What’s My Name?”空降全美流行榜冠軍★

★超值追加與痞子阿姆攜手蟬聯7週冠軍作“Love The Way You Lie (Part II)”二部曲，DVD精彩收錄專輯錄製幕後花絮



不到5年時間，全球唱片銷售飆破1200萬張，囊括8首告示牌冠軍單曲，兩度獲得葛萊美獎榮耀加冕，以及全美音樂獎、世界音樂獎的多次肯定，這位來自加勒比海小島的陽光女孩已經成功蛻變為樂壇流行天后。小娜妹Rihanna/蕾哈娜始終以百變魅力讓全球樂迷驚豔不已，她最新的鮮豔紅髮造型，可以說是呼應了她2010全新專輯名稱【Loud】，大膽、原創、勇敢表達自己的主張，這就是蕾哈娜。量身打造這張新輯的超級製作人L.A. Reid形容：「蕾哈娜擁有屬於她獨有的風格和音樂天賦，而這張專輯完全捕捉到蕾哈娜的迷人神采和精髓。」



蕾哈娜05年以充滿雷鬼島國風情的首張專輯『Music Of The Sun太陽之歌』初試啼聲；06年白金認證專輯『A Girl Like Me娜妹天下』集結了所有新潮舞曲元素；07年空降英國冠軍+美國亞軍的『Good Girl Gone Bad娜妹好壞』，挑戰電子與搖滾風格，並締造3首冠軍曲；09年的『Rated R限制級』，黑暗的音樂風格挑戰自我尺度，是她赤裸的真實告白；由此可見蕾哈娜對音樂的勇於挑戰與強烈企圖心。



第五張錄音室大碟【Loud】，蕾哈娜回歸愉悅、歡樂、舞曲的一面，從選曲到製作全程參與，展現對音樂的自主性。專輯中大聲吶喊出對愛的渴望、對音樂的執著、對生活的認真，展現新都會女性自信成熟的丰采。碧昂絲、亞瑟小子暢銷作幕後金手--挪威籍組合Stargate操刀“S&M”，包圍高壓Electro-Pop音線，小娜妹以堅韌放肆聲頻詮釋，十分過癮；甫出道即獲葛萊美獎提名的饒舌天才Drake站台“What's My Name?”，火速登上告示牌流行單曲榜冠軍；當紅的The Runners(Fat Joe、Lil Wayne)執掌“Cheers (Drink To That)”，於順暢的Mid-Tempo線條裡，取樣艾薇兒的抒情曲“I’m With You”；“Fading”則併入Enya的“One By One”動人橋段，好聽滿分；率先曝光主打“Only Girl (In The World)”，混搭Euro-Pop/ Dance-Pop與紮實的貝斯頻率，搶下英國金榜+美國舞曲榜冠軍，這是蕾哈娜的第8首舞曲榜冠軍曲，打破瑪丹娜多年來保持的紀錄，同時也寫下德+法+澳+加等14國No.1紀錄；Bangladesh(Ciara、Missy Elliott)加持“Raining Men”，邀來說唱女將Nicki Minaj幫腔；超值追加與痞子阿姆攜手蟬聯7週冠軍作“Love The Way You Lie”的Part II二部曲。影音盤更精華記載【Loud】錄製過程、幕後花絮、拍照片段，貼身捕捉最真實自然的蕾哈娜。

Loud is the upcoming fifth studio album by Barbadian recording artist Rihanna, scheduled to be released on November 16, 2010 through Def Jam Recordings. Recording sessions began in February 2010 and continued over a period of six months, concluding in August 2010. The album's lead single 「Only Girl (In the World)」 was released on September 10, 2010 as a digital download. 「What's My Name?」 is confirmed for release as the second single, while Rihanna has stated that current plans will see 「S&M」 released as the third single，In June 2010, it was revealed that Rihanna was planning to release the fifth studio album soon.Singers, songwriters and producers Taio Cruz, Alex da Kid, Sean Garrett, Ne-Yo, Rico Love, Timbaland, Shontelle, David Guetta, and Drake worked on the album. 「I'm gonna miss the Rated R era too, but nothing compares to the album I just made,」 Rihanna said, adding,」I made sure not to let you down with my music. You guys are always defending me, so now you've got some great songs to justify it. I didn't want to go backward and remake Good Girl Gone Bad. I wanted the next step in the evolution of Rihanna, and it's perfect for us」. In early September 2010, she announced that the album would be called, Loud saying that 「get Loud everybody, get crazy, get excited, because I'm pumped. I'm just gonna be me, because that's what you guys love the most, and that's what makes me feel best. Just being normal, normal for me is Loud! Sassy, fun, flirty, energetic.」 While Rihanna was on set filming for the movie Battleship, she explained in an interview with Entertainment Tonight, 「Loud is, the word, the name of the album definitely reflects the attitude of it, it's really sassy and flirty and it grabs your attention and that's why I enjoy it. It takes you through a really really interesting ride. So colorful the album.」 The artwork of the album was revealed on September 28, 2010
