Album Intro：

This Providence has exploded onto the scene with their debut full-length, 'Our World's Divorce', out November 16th on Rocketstar Recordings. The album is in a word - astounding. Beautiful interlacing guitar lines weave thick bass into crunchy choruses. Memorable pop melodies infiltrate every verse and every chorus. This is more than just another pop-punk album. This is an entirely new and refreshing twist on an over-populated genre.
