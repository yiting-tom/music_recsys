Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2013 release from John & Yoko's son. Sweeping orchestral arrangements, surf guitar, epic superhero themes & a touch of eerie that recalls Twin Peaks. All of these diverse elements blend seamlessly together on Sean Lennon's Alter Egos film score - the musical backdrop for a comedic alternate reality that flips the superhero genre on its head. Lennon acts as both composer and performer on the 20 song soundtrack. Miho Hatori (Cibo Matto) lends her voice talents on a few tracks.
