Album Intro：

2009 enhanced edition features two additional tracks: 'Every Word She Said' and 'Streets Of Steel'. Made In Sheffield is the 2008 album from the long-serving British balladeer, more popular in the new millennium than in his supposed '60s heyday! Made In Sheffield is exactly that, an album that was recorded and produced in Sheffield, utilizing the highly talented musicians born and bred in the city: Richard Hawley, Jarvis Cocker, Phil Oakley, Alex Turner and Roisin Murphy are some of the collaborators on this remarkable album. Features 13 tracks, including a beautiful remake of 'Louise' (originally by The Human League), 'Only Ones Who Know' and the hauntingly poetic 'Danger Is A Woman In Love'. Universal
