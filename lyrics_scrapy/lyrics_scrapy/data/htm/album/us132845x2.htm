Album Intro：

Motown's first white rock group was every bit as funky and soulful as their Motor City labelmates, yet Rare Earth has been far too rare in the CD era-until now! Get Ready for this awesome 3-CD set from Hip-O Select that collects their first 5 charting studio albums: Get Ready ('69), Ecology ('70), One World ('71), Willie Remembers ('72) and Ma ('73), featuring classics like (I Know) I'm Losing You; I Just Want to Celebrate; Born to Wander , and Big John is My Name . You also get 9 bonus single sides including the hit edit of Get Ready and the Top 20 non-lp cut Hey Big Brother . 43 massive tracks plus a 32-page booklet...and it's all housed in a curved-top box that mimics the unique design of those original Rare Earth label releases!
