Album Intro：

Inhabiting their own beautifully dark, ethereal corner of the musical universe, poised somewhere at the crossroads between the nocturnal, rolling soundscapes of Angelo Badalamenti and the sublime, naive melodies of 50s pop, Trailer Trash Tracys are a deliciously unique proposition. Recorded by the band themselves on a 'solfeggio scale' a scale to which, apparently, western guitars and pianos are not pitched to Ester is an ambitious achievement that marries an experimental urge with a love for the standards and classics of pop eternal.
