Album Intro：

Limited edition digipak edition includes three bonus tracks. 2010 album from the Australian Prog/World Music outfit. Artificial uses symphonic, Progressive, World, Jazz and Art Rock elements to illustrate the dichotomy of an artificial world that is developing within our real world and how we as human beings relate to each of these worlds. The follow-up to The Garden, Artificial is the band's third album overall. Century Media.
