Album Intro：

Formed in 2009, We Are The In Crowd career started early, signing to independent label Hopeless Records and having their first single 'For The Win' featured on MTV's The Hills that same year, which in turn landed them a spot on the entire 2010 Vans Warped Tour. Armed with an irresistible combination of both male and female vocals, We Are The In Crowd's debut EP is filled with honest, emotional pop rock songs that tell stories about life and relationships from differing views. The EP was recorded and produced by Zack Odom and Kenneth Mount (All Time Low, Jimmy Eat World, Cartel).
