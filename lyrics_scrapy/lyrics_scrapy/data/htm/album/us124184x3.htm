Album Intro：

Limited Australian two CD collectors edition of the band's 2008 album including a bonus disc that contains 11 rare, previously unreleased tracks. White Noise was recorded with producer John Agnello at Water Music Studios in Hoboken, New Jersey. Agnello forged a name for himself in the heady days of the '90s, working with Sonic Youth, Dinosaur Jr. and The Hold Steady. Legendary producer Brendan O'Brien mixed the album in between his commitments recording new albums by AC/DC and Pearl Jam. 22 tracks including the first single 'White Noise'. Dew Process. 2009.
