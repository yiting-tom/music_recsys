Album Intro：

'The Dawnseeker' is the realization of artist/producer Justin Elswick's vision to unite an all-star cast of electronica's most dynamic female vocalists, performing breathtaking and melodically luminous compositions. These singers are familiar for their work with Tiesto, BT, Ferry Corsten, Massive Attack and many other marquee electronica artists. Angelic songstresses, ethereal soundscapes, exotic electronic... those seeking an escape from the mundane will find Sleepthief a rich and varied experience.
