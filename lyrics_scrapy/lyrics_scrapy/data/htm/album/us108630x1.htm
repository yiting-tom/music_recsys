Album Intro：

Welcome to the Dollhouse is the second studio album by American R&B and pop group Danity Kane, set to be released on March 18, 2008. Danity Kane recorded the album in under five weeks, while filming the second season of Making the Band 4 with fellow label mates Day26 and Donnie J in Miami, Florida. 　　

　　

The first single, 'Damaged', was chosen through an on line fan poll. In January 2008, Danity Kane had posted a bulletin on their respective MySpace page expressing that they were giving their fans the chance to choose their newest single. Fans were given two choices, 'Damaged' and 'Pretty Boy'. Of the two choices, 'Damaged' dominated the poll by a vast majority, and thus, became the first single off Welcome to the Dollhouse. It was released on January 29, 2008
