Album Intro：

As founding member of the premiere late sixties power pop rockers Nazz, multi-instrumentalist, vocalist, songwriterand producer Todd Rundgren was barely in his 20's when he began his long solo journey. Runt, his first solo album, showed a dazzling array of songs, displaying not only his rock and roll side but ventures into jazz, R&B, eastern and blues. With help from his friends Hunt and Tony Sales, the power trio nailed one of the finest albums of the classic rock era and catapulted Todd Rundren's marquee as a singer/songwriter with his smash hit single 'We Gotta Get You A Woman'.
