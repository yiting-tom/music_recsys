Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2013 release, the eighth album from the Welsh Alt-Rock band led by Kelly Jones. Graffiti On The Train was written by Jones and produced by the frontman and Jim Lowe, who has now worked on every Stereophonics album since 2005's Language. Sex. Violence. Other?. Bond composer David Arnold also collaborated with the band, providing string arrangements for several tracks. The album features the single 'Indian Summer'.
