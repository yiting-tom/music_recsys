Album Intro：

'The Little Stevies' journey since the launch of their 'Grow Up' EP has been an incredible one. Expanding the band instrumentally and working alongside producer Jimi Maroudas (Tim Rogers, Pete Murray), the recording of their debut album 'Love Your Band' allowed them to grow fromfolk-cherubs into magnificent birds of pop. 'Sunshower', the first single, and its film clip, were both rapturously received. Beautiful stories and soaring melodies are abundant on this highly-anticipated record, and with an enthralling live energy, the band are ready to launch it into the stratosphere.'
