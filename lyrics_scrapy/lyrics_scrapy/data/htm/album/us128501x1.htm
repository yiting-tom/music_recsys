Album Intro：

2009 two CD set containing a pair of digitally remastered albums (plus bonus tracks) from the British singer/songwriter: Here (1979) and Living In A Fantasy(1980). After three albums with producer Richard Perry, in 1979, Leo reunited with his original collaborator David Courtney to produce Here, which features Leo's cover of the Chi-Lites' 'Oh Girl'. There are two bonus tracks per album including a non-album single A-side, and Leo's version of 'Unchained Melody'. The booklet features brand new sleeve notes by Leo himself for each album, as well as photos from his own collection. Edsel.
