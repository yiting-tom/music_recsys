Album Intro：

The third full-length album by indie rock artist Feist.

Hey, remember Leslie Feist? The girl who used to do raunchy electro with Peaches and is now a contributor to Broken Social Scene and a folky, discoy solo artist in her own right? Yeah, she was all the rage in 2005, wasn't she?

Leslie Feist (born February 13, 1976) is a Canadian singer-songwriter. She performs as a solo artist under the name Feist, and as a member of Broken Social Scene.
