Album Intro：

The First Step is the debut solo album from the legendary Faces, originally released in 1970. Featuring a post-Jeff-Beck-Group Rod Stewart, along with Ronnie Lane, Ronnie Wood, Ian McLagan, and Kenny Jones, The First Step includes the now classic Three Button Hand Me Down, and Flying. Reissued on 180 gram vinyl.
