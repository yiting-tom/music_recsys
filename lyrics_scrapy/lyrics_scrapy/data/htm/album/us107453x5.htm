Album Intro：

After selling hundreds of thousands of records across the world, French popsters Tahiti 80 return with their third album, `Fosbury'. Available domestically for the first time, `Fosbury' picks up where the band left off by blending the sounds of fellow French contemporaries Phoenix and Air with the funkier elements of Prince. The sound is dominated by sub-bass, sturdy beats, philly string flourishes and quirky electronic noises. To keep with the band's sound evolution they chose to work with Neal Pouge and Serban Ghenea who fittingly are best known for their work Outkast and Nerd. ''Lemon fresh, sugar sweet French popsters...absolutely irresistible...Tahiti 80 are refreshingly unlike anything else out there right now'' Rocksound.
