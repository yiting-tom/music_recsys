Album Intro：

The Murder Of My Sweet is something special, it was so obvious right from the start: Swedish female-fronted Rock/Metal outfit that offers new stylistic facets within the Rock genre. Their mixture of gothic-like melancholy, catchy melodies, heavy grooves and almost soundtrack-like elements is outstanding and surely will appeal to fans of Within Temptation, Evanescence, Nightwish and many more. Towering over this sound is charismatic vocalist Angelica Rylin, one of the best talents to surface in recent years. Bye Bye Lullaby also features guest appearances by Jesper Strömblad (IN FALMES), Peter Wichers (SOILWORK) and Fredrik Åkesson (OPETH). No matter if you call it Cinematic Rock , Gothic Metal or whatever, The Murder Of My Sweet rocks! Bye Bye Lullaby is a pleasure for all fans of all heavy music!
