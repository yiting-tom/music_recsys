Album Intro：

Described as 'the perfect hybrid of LA rock and classic British rock'...Southern California's 21 year old The Shys are a blistering 4 piece featuring Iggy style vox and hints of all the stones...Rolling Stones..Stone Roses and Sly and the Family!! Band is currently recording debut album for Sire Records in Hollywood with producer Dave Cobb (Shooter Jennings, Rock n Roll Soldiers, The Years). Having already performed with the likes of the Color...Redwalls...Killers....they are quickly making a name for themselves on the live circuit. After recently finishing a headlining residency at Moscow in LA, a national tour with The Subways is underway in March 1 as well as SXSW appearances. Early press includes Filter Magazine..Newbeats..Sugar Buzz..Myspace featured act..Music Connection.. Popmatters, among others.
