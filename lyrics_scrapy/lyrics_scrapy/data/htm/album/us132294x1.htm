Album Intro：

Independent performance artist, Svoy, offers third studio music release Grow Up. The new ten track title, given Svoy's Classical/Jazz background, is an Electro based Emo/Pop title. From beginning track 'Never Grow Up' a calm and yet youthful direction is taken that is filled with a set of cohesive temperate and swooning sonic palates. Lyrical themes that are whimsical, delicate, and emotive, are sung in reflective tones. The singles Never Grow Up and Right Here, released in support of the new album, have already garnered international success. Other stand out tracks: 'Help Me Remember', 'Crocodile Tears (Say Goodbye)', and 'When I Think of You' The album, which is Svoy s third studio recording following releases Eclectric and Automatons, is self produced. Svoy's release, Grow Up, is a sonic adventure that has a place in today s Electronic/Pop landscape. It is available now, from various retailers, as an import. --TheCelebrityCafe.com
