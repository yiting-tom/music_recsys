Album Intro：

Famous for her sublime symbiosis of hypnotic beats and deep lyrics Sara Noxx releases one of the most spectacular independent 'Best Of' collections of the past few years.

This triple CD Box Set comes recommended to every collector and lover of electronic music, containing as it does numerous new interpretations, remixes and duets on an exceptional collection of multi lingual interpretations. Disc 1 includes remastered original digital Sara Noxx songs while Disc 2 contains sensational Remixx / duets with secene heavyweights such as ASP, Feindflug, Kirlian Camera, PAL, Armageddon Dildos, Seabound and more. Disc 3 is international : Artists from different countries of the world interpret selected Noxx songs partly in their original language but also in Italian, Russian, French, Dutch, Bulgarian, Arabic, Japanese....by bands such as The Frozen Autumn, Auto-Auto, Le Visage, Dark Territory and ore...

Additionally the first 5000 copies of 'XX-Ray' come complete with a special code for TWO additional complete Remix CD's with exclusive wallpapers, screensavers and videos.
