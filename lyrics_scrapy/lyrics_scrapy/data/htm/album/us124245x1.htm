Album Intro：

Poppy punk influenced songs with a metal edge. Forget wrestling, Amy Dumas fronts The Luchagors with more raw power and energy that could ever be contained in a ring.The Luchagors formed in October of 2006. With their CD out now, they couldn't be more excited about their future on the road. All of the Luchagors are veterans of the entertainment world. Shane Morton, co-writer of their self titled release, has been in such notable bands as Super-X 13, and Gargantua, where he shared a stage with the current drummer of The Luchagors, Troy King. Bassist Jay Hedberg joined the outfit shortly after the first few shows to add the higher intensity the band was searching for. Amy Dumas fronts the band. Although she is most widely know for her past efforts with a long career performing with the WWE as her character, 'Lita', her longtime passion for music is now emerging in the form of The Luchagors.
