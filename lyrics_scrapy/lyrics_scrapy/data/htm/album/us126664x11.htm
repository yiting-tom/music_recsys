Album Intro：

Grime crew Ruff Sqwad's breakout MC Tinchy Stryder's 2007 debut is both the best UK garage and UK hip hop album since Dizzee Rascal's Boy In Da Corner. With his skillful, yet wounded and soulful flow, Tinchy spits touching, insightful tales from the street over productions by DaVinche and fellow Ruff Sqwad members Rapid and Dirty Danger. Standouts include the r&b-flavored singles 'Breakaway' and 'Something About Your Eyes' (featuring sweet vocals by Cyleena). Takeover Entertainment.
