Album Intro：

U.S. pressing of the sophomore album from this eccentric UK outfit formed from the ashes of the beloved Beta Band. Following on from their critically acclaimed debut, Luna is more experimental than its predecessor. 10 minute Prog-Rock epics interspersed with Gregorian chanting and Trip Hop beats make Luna a very eclectic listen, although the quality of the songs consistently shines through. Contains the single 'Magic Man'.
