Album Intro：

Tantric's self-titled 2001 debut album, gold certified and climbing (700,000+ scans), featured the #1 Rock/#5 Modern Rock hit 'Breakdown,' the Top 10 'Astounded' and Top 20 'Mourning.' Now the eagerly anticipated follow-up, After We Go, again produced by Toby Wright (also Korn, Alice In Chains), goes beyond all expectations for the Louisville, KY, quartet. After touring for two years with everyone from Staind, Lifehouse and Creed to Kid Rock, Stone Temple Pilots and 3 Doors Down, Tantric has a fan base ready to go for After We Go.
