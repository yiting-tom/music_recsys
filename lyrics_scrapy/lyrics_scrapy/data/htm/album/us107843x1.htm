Album Intro：

Mumford & Sons are a folk band from London. They formed in late 2007, rising out of London's folk scene with other artists such as Laura Marling, Johnny Flynn, Jay Jay Pistolet and Noah and the Whale. The band have often supported Laura Marling at concerts, while their association with Noah and the Whale can be traced back to King's College School, Wimbledon, attended by Mumford and Lovett of Mumford & Sons, and Noah and the Whale bass-player Matt Owens.

Mumford and Sons won Best Band at the 3rd Annual BalconyTV Music Video Awards in Dublin on July 3rd 2009. . The award for a balcony performance on the award winning music site BalconyTV.Com, was previously won by Royseven and The Script.
