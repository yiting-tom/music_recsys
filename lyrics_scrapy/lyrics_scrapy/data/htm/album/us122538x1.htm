Album Intro：

This 2009 release is the darkest and angriest record they have ever recorded. Requiem is a brooding, furious Metal masterpiece that blends the incredible hooks and melody of their last opus, Fear Will Cast No Shadow and their first two Southern thrashers Revelations Of The Unsung and Embrace The Gutter. The biggest standout from the previous record is the fact that Requiem is a pure adrenaline rush of confrontationally spiteful lyrics and sinister musicianship. The band's technical prowess is top-notch and they are not here to mess around. This is unadulterated, aggressive Metal that will level the Hard Rock world.
