Album Intro：

The Downtown Fiction have announced the release of their hugely anticipated debut album, Let's Be Animals, which will arrive in stores and at all DSPs on April 26th. Let's Be Animals is highlighted by the breakthrough hit single/video, 'I Just Wanna Run.' The track is currently a top 20 smash at Sirius Hits 1, while its companion video clip has scored major play on MTV2, MTV Hits, and mtvU.
