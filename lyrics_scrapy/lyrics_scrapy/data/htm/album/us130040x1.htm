Album Intro：

Since these young Canadians scored the Rock The Nation Award in 2010, the career of singer Jackie Slaughter and his gang has skyrocketed: their debut Head of the Pack was unleashed on the hungry metal masses by NoiseArt Records in 2011 and collected rave reviews worldwide. SKULL FIST went on tour with BULLET, ENFORCER, SABATON and POWERWOLF and have escaped the underground scene for good - ready to claim the metal throne. Their weapons? Authenticity, 80s spirit and classic metal tunes with singalong character enter Chasing the Dream. For fans of Amon Amarth, Testament and Enforcer.
