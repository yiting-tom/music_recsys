Album Intro：

The music of Toronto-based instrumental band The Hylozoists lends itself well to lofty analogies. One listener might liken it to a soundtrack in search of an arthouse film. Another might find it ideal accompaniment for a macabre carnival carousel. Perhaps if Fellini and Leone had started a circus, their organ grinders would've churned out music akin what you will hear on The Hylozoists long-awaited sophomore album La Fin Du Monde.
