Album Intro：

''I Want That You Are Always Happy'' is the debut album from Queensland, Australia based musical collective The Middle East. The release is the long-an- ticipated follow-up to their debut EP, ''Recordings of the Middle East'', which earned accolades and fans the world over and led to extensive touring all over the world, shows with Mumford & Sons and Laura Marling, and slots on the Coachella, Bonnaroo, Sasquatch!, Glastonbury and Latitude festivals. In their home country, TME have performed at major Aussie fests including Big Day Out and Splendour in the Grass. ''The Recordings of the Middle East'' EP, released in 2008, inspired the NME to dub them ''just about the loveliest thing we've heard in a fair bit,'' while Clash Magazine praised the ''cobweb beauty'' of their music, ''which begins in the lullaby vocals and intriguingly weird lyrics.'' ''Blood'' from the Recordings EP has been placed prominently in multiple films including ''It s Kind of a Funny Story'' and ''Accidents Happen.'' The song's most high profile placement to date is in the climatic scene of the Steve Carrell Summer comedy ''Crazy Stupid Love.''
