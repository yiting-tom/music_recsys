Vic Damone【 18 albums 176 lyrics 】
Vic Damone (June 12, 1928 – February 11, 2018) was an American traditional pop and big band singer, actor, radio and television presenter, and entertainer. He is best known for his performances of songs such as the number one hit 'You're Breaking My Heart', and 'On the Street Where You Live' (from My Fair Lady) and 'My Heart Cries for You' which were both the number four hits.
