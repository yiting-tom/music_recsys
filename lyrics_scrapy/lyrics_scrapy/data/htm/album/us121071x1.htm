Album Intro：

Produced by Bob Casale from DEVO. This is the album that put the Vandals back on the map with Josh Freese on the drums and set them up to become a main stage Warped Tour band for the next 15 years and beyond. Tracks 13-16 are bonus tracks added to the CD when the band put it out through their own label, Kung Fu Records to battle the previous version, for which royalties and statements were years past due. They won. This is the only version available now.
