Album Intro：

Steve Kilbey is the lead singer, bassist and main lyricist for Australia's legendary band The Church. As well as this he has been one of the most prolific songwriters of the last twenty years. His own creative tally outside of the band includes six solo albums and numerous collaborative projects and production credits.

For the first time ever, we have put together a collection of Steve's 6 official solo albums: Earthed, Unearthed, The Slow Crack, Remindlessness, Narcosis and Dabble in one limited edition box set. All albums have been digitally remastered, some contain extra tracks, they are all available in special slip sleeve (vinyl type) packaging and the box set will contain a large book of liner notes, artwork and tidbits on the albums that is only available in the limited box set.
