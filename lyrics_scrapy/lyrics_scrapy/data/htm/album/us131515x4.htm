Album Intro：

CD reissue of this 1975 album from the British guitarist best known for his work with Yes , Asia and GTR who is featured on all insturments excluding the kitchen sink. Guesting on this album are Yes drummer Alan White, drummer Bill Bruford, keyboardist Patrick Moraz, James Gregory on flute and Mick Eve on saxophone. This album reached #63 on the Billboard charts.
