Album Intro：

Statik Selektah is a well-known and highly respected DJ/producer from Boston, MA. He has a consistent release schedule over the years, with producer albums such as Population Control in 2011 and collaborative albums with Action Bronson (Well Done in 2011) and Saigon (All in a Day's Work in 2009) to name a few. Statik Selektah is the host of his own radio show on Sirius/XM.
