In between singing with the Twisters and working with artists like Aretha Franklin, Roberta Flack and Joan Armatrading, Sammy Turner recorded some fantastic R&B sides of his own-many of which found him turning standards into hits of his own. His hit interpretations of Lavender Blue; Always; Paradise , and Symphony are here with Honey; Goodnight, Irene; The Things I Love , and 12 more!
