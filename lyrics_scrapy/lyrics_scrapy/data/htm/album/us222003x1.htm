Album Intro：

自由落體成團三年首張EP《Drop》，數字搖滾的混沌體驗。
墜落是快速而失控，身處其中，卻又緩慢而凝結，內外矛盾感受歸於一體，抑鬱呢喃與失控吶喊都無法影響。
《Drop》即「落下」，呼應自由落體的團名，使用不規則的拍數和糾結樂句對話，一起體驗落下時的矛盾衝擊。
Let's drop.

本作品獲文化部影視及流行音樂產業局107年補助

Free Fall released their first EP 'Drop', a dope experience of math-rock. Falling seems fast and out of control, but it also makes people who is falling feels slow and condensed. The inner and outer contradictory feelings are integrated into one. Depressed whispers and unbridled screams cannot affect anything. 'Drop' means 'falling down', echoing the name of the band. By staggered beats and entangled phrases, Free Fall will let you experience the contradictory impact of falling.

Let’s drop.
