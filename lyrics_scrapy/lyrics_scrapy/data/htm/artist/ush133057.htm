Rebecca Black( Rebecca Renee Black )【 7 albums 13 lyrics 】
Rebecca Renee Black (born June 21, 1997) is an American YouTuber and singer who gained extensive media attention with her 2011 single 'Friday'. After its music video went viral on YouTube and other social media sites, 'Friday' was derided by many music critics and viewers, who dubbed it 'the worst song ever'. Black went on to release other songs; her Dave Days collaboration 'Saturday' earned more success. She currently uploads videos on her YouTube channel about various topics.
