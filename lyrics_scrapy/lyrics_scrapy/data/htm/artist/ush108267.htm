THE JAYWALK( J-WALK )【 42 albums 408 lyrics 】
THE JAYWALK is a Japanese rock band best known in the West for the intro theme of the videogame Road Avenger.

In 1991 they had a big hit with 'Nanimo ienakute... natsu' (何も言えなくて...夏?).
