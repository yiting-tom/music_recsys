BARBEE BOYS【 14 albums 209 lyrics 】
Barbee Boys were a successful 1980s Japanese rock band. The ban debuted in 1984 and were popular through the 1980s, becoming the first Japanese rock band to perform at the Nippon Budokan, until disbanding in 1992. The five members were Konta (real name Atsushi Kondou), male vocals and soprano sax, female vocalist Kyoko Sekihara, lead guitarist and songwriter Tomotaka Imamichi - who went on to have a career as a songwriter for other bands, bassist and pianist Enrique, drummer Toshiaki Konuma. The band played a reunion concert in 2008.
