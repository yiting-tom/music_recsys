Album Intro：

The twins, Jennifer and Heather, grew up in Philadelphia, and have been performing together since they were eight. They sorted through their parent's records and found themselves singing a wide range of music from the Andrew Sisters to Tammmy Wynette to the Everly Brothers. They learned how to hone their harmony craft by imitating those Everly sounds they loved so much and eventually started listening to the local country radio station. They soon became huge fans of the family harmony of The Judds. The sisters first attracted attention with their debut 'Just Between You and Me'. their first single 'Please' was an impassioned plea and remains the highest debuting single by a female duo in Country Music history, while the alburm's title track was a top ten smash. They were named the Best New Duo by the Academy of Ciountry Music and garnered nominations from the Grammys, the Country Music Association and the American Music Awards.
