Album Intro：

Stellar Kart is back with a Pop/Rock tour de force (think Modern/Pop Rock with an '80's Arena Rock flair) that includes that band's most mature lyrics yet. This album is internationally themed around topics most important to the turbulent lives of today's teenagers, touching on subjects like friendship, loss, prayer and redemption. Stellar Kart follows in the footsteps of 'youth group bands' like Audio Adrenaline and Newsboys and is blazing it's own trail.
