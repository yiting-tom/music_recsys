Album Intro：

Victor Young scored over 300 films in just over 20 years, but this is the first collection devoted to his soundtrack work! And it's a honey, featuring 22 carefully-remastered tracks, some in stereo: Around the World (single version stereo debut); For Whom the Bell Tolls; Samson & Delilah; East of Eden; The High & the Mighty; La Vie En Rose; Theme from 'The Medic'; My Mother (from The Quiet Man ); Written on the Wind , and more.
