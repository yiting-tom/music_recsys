Album Intro：

Rich Brian迄今為止最個人化的單曲“See Me”正式上線。這是他在2018年發布的第一首個人單曲，也是改名後的首個作品。在這首歌裡，我們能聽到Rich Brian對自己音樂歷程的回顧反思，和對未來的美好憧憬。
此前不久，Rich Brian在社交網絡上公佈了他的首張個人專輯發布計劃，這張萬眾期待的專輯名為“Amen”，並最終將於2018年2月2日面世。

Brian Imanuel announces his name change from Rich Chigga to simply his first name, Brian. The teen rap sensation from Indonesia shared a tweet on the first day of 2018 about him making this change: “ Yes I now go by 'Brian'. I have been planning to do this forever and I'm so happy to finally do it. I was naive & I made a mistake. new year, new beginning, happy new years.” Before the holidays, Brian announced his highly anticipated upcoming, debut project , Amen , which is set to release on February 2nd via 88rising Records. The same day he also shares “ See Me ”, his most personal song to date. It paints an introspective picture of Brian's journey up until now in music and what he plans for the upcoming future.
