Album Intro：

We all know what happens when record companies merge: Some really great music gets lost in the shuffle. Vaux's much-anticipated third album sat on the shelf for over year before getting a release date. The good news is it hardly sounds worse for the wear, delivering the palpable fury fans have come to expect from the tightly wound Denver screamo sextet, especially on tracks like 'Identity Theft' and 'Are You With Me.' But there are signs of growth as well, like the subtle electronic beats simmering under 'The Rope, The Pistol, The Candlestick' and the distinct Radiohead thumbprint on 'The Last Report From . . .' --Aidin Vaziri
