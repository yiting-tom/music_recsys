Album Intro：

If A Song Could Get Me You is a limited edition album from Norwegian singer-songwriter Marit Larsen, and was released on August 14, 2009. The information was made available via Marit Larsen's Youtube page and Maritlarsen.de. The album consists of her greatest singles from her previous two albums 'Under the Surface' and 'The Chase'.
