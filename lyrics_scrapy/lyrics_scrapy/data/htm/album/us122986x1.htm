Album Intro：

THE CHEVIN are a four piece from Leeds, UK and in the Summer of 2011 recorded their debut album
with producer Noah Shain at a remote studio desert in El Paso in South Texas (where the eventual name for the album Borderland was conceived). The Airborne Toxic Event heard it and took them on the road around the UK for a month; The Pigeon Detectives heard it and offered them a 16-date tour of Europe at the start of 2012, sharing their tour bus. And White Lies heard it, came down to catch them on their UK headline club tour towards the end of 2011 and invited The Chevin to support them on their winter arena tour, culminating at Wembley Arena. In May 2012 the band completed a sell out tour of Ireland
supporting Franz Ferdinand.
Borderland is The Chevin s debut album.
