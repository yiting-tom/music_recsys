Album Intro：

Heresy And Creed is TEN s 10th studio album following
their successful 2010 return with Stormwarning .
Lead singer and songwriter, Gary Hughes worked hard to
live up to the expectations surrounding this new album.
Such has been his prolific output of quality material over
the years, he is to many, arguably one of the greatest
British AOR songwriters of all time; 17 years later, armed
with this seemingly endless source of classic songs, Ten
continue to astound.
Akin to Whitesnake, Journey and Rainbow, TEN s music is
a must for all lovers of Classic British Hard Rock music!
The new album is a true classic Ten album while at the
same time a groundbreaking, monumental album in more
ways than one: Classic hook laden songs. Power and
melancholy with not a single filler in sight. Thanks to a
superb mix courtesy (again) of Dennis Ward, Heresy And
Creed will prove once again that Ten are at the top of
their genre. Ten are back and better than ever!
