Jun'ichi Suwabe (諏訪部 順一 Suwabe Jun'ichi, born March 29, 1972 in Tokyo) is a Japanese voice actor affiliated with Haikyo. His popular roles include Keigo Atobe in The Prince of Tennis, Victor Nikiforov in YURI!!! on Ice, Grimmjow Jaegerjaques in Bleach, Archer in Fate/stay night, Ren Jinguji in Uta no Prince-sama, Undertaker in Black Butler, Daiki Aomine in Kuroko's Basketball, Takashi Komuro in Highschool of the Dead and Dandy in Space Dandy.

He was nominated for a Best Singing Award at the 6th Seiyu Awards, and received a Best Supporting Actor award at the 7th Seiyu Awards.
