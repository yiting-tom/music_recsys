Kutless is a Christian rock band from Portland, Oregon formed in 1999. Since their formation, they have released multiple studio albums and one live album, Live from Portland. They have sold over 3 million records worldwide.

Current members

Jon Micah Sumrall – lead vocals, acoustic guitar, piano (2000–present)
James Mead – guitar, backing vocals (2001–present)
Nathan Parrish – bass guitar, backing vocals (2015–present)
Vince DiCarlo – guitar (2014–present)
Drew Porter – drums (2014–present)
