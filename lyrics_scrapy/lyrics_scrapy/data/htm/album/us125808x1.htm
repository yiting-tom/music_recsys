Album Intro：

THE UNGUIDED - the new band featuring founding brothers Richard & Roger Sjunnesson and former members of Sonic Syndicate - release a highly dynamic and electrifying Melodic Death Metal album. 'Fragile Immortality' features highly infectious melodies combined with metallic heaviness and turns songs like 'Blodbad' or 'Inception' into hymns of a new generation. The centerpiece is undoubtedly the vocal duo Roland Johansson & Richard Sjunnesson, who also branded the trademark sound of SONIC SYNDICATE. For fans of FIVE FINGER DEATH PUNCH, HEAVEN SHALL BURN, DEVILDRIVER and ALL SHALL PERISH.
