Cody Simpson【 15 albums 101 lyrics 】
Cody Robert Simpson (born 11 January 1997) is an Australian singer, songwriter, dancer and actor. He owns a record label Coast House Records. He formed a new band called 'Cody Simpson & The Tide, and debuted their debut song 'Waiting For The Tide', releasing the song in his own label.

In 2017, he signed with ICM Partners. He is now managed by Maverick Management company after parting ways with Scooter Braun.
