Album Intro：

The re-release of the album that put Trick Daddy on the map has been long overdue. Originally released in 1998 WWW.THUG.COM went on to score RIAA gold while blazing a trail through the Billboard top 10 R&B chart and eventually went Platinum. Filled with powerful rhymes taken from life on the blocks of Any hood, USA, the set stood out on the strength of its hit singles, 'Sweatin' Me' and the breakthrough 'Nann.' The success of 'Nann' effectively underscored the undeniable ability of the streets to choose its hits while shining the spotlight on the spirited lyrical skills of Trick's fellow Slip-N-Slide comrade, Trina. This release includes two new bonus tracks not on the original album.
