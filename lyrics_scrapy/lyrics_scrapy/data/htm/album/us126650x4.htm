Album Intro：

Three EPs recorded over a year of touring; Tina only planned to sell them individually as tour merch. Extraordinarily high demand pushed her to package them together for sale in stores. 3 CDs packaged in a beautiful box set with a 36 page booklet of lyrics, pictures and diaries.
