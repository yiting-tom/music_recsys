Album Intro：

Deluxe edition featuring five brand new tracks including 'Chasing Cars' and 'Poker Face', plus bonus video content! 2009 debut album from German outfit The Baseballs who became popular in their native country for releasing '50s style Rock 'n' Roll covers of Rihanna's 'Umbrella' and Katy Perry's 'Hot 'n' Cold'. The album - which includes both those singles - includes 17 more re-workings of major chart hits such as Robbie Williams' 'Angels' and Leona Lewis' 'Bleeding Love' all delivered in their classic sounding Rockabilly style. 1 7tracks.
