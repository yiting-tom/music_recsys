Album Intro：

The London-based band duo create colorful, exuberant alt-pop that celebrates their innate musical kinship and drawsupon their shared childhood memories. Layered androgynous vocals are backed by muscular drums, astral synths and shimmering guitars in a celebration of the breathless sensations of youth and coming of age.
