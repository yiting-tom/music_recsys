Album Intro：

Considered, the father of Christian Hip Hop, and a founding member of the critically acclaimed group the Cross Movement, his debut solo album Christology: In Laymen's Terms offered a triumvirate of piercing Bible-based lyrics, banging hip-hop tracks and dynamic flow--a defining work for the genre. His sophomore solo CD The Thesis received a Stellar nomination for Rap/Hip Hop Gospel CD of the Year. Together with The Cross Movement, he also released six successful CDs--including their GRAMMY® and Stellar award nominated finale album, HIStory: Our Place In His Story. He has toured globally as a solo artist and with The Cross Movement and been covered by media outlets as diverse as Time Magazine, CCM Magazine, VIBE, The Source, Billboard and The Washington Post. His television appearances include BET's '106 & Gospel' and 'Bobby Jones Gospel;' TV1's 'The Gospel of Music with Jeff Majors;' INSP's 'Mixx Masters Lounge;' TBN's'Praise the Lord' and 'Real Videos and JCTV's 'Top 3.
