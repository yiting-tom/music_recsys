Album Intro：

Griffin House is a singer/songwriter based in Cincinnati, Ohio, having also lived in Philadelphia and Nashville. He describes his musical genre as rock / folk / punk. He mostly operates as an unsigned act, releasing his albums through Evening Records; the one exception to this rule is Lost And Found, his only widely-commercial release. He has been known to tour extensively, and recently completed a successful tour opening for Mat Kearney. House attended Miami University in Oxford, Ohio.
