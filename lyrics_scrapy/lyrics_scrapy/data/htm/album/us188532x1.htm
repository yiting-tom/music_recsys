EP歌曲介紹：

《抓背》單純因為背癢的需求所寫下的一首歌曲，我們鮮少關注生活中有趣的人事物，抓背器具自己也沒想到自己得到人類的舒爽感受，而被歌頌著！

《厝內Lady》中講述身為SOHO接案創作以及文字創作者面對工作必須忍耐，達到客戶滿意成效或許得把藝術家個性收掉的一首歌曲，也很適合獻給母親的一首歌，陰柔的女性使用月亮作為代表，性格有如潮汐般難解。於是月亮因此結合中秋節的月餅，加上情歌敘事Rap的表現出這首歌曲獨特的神秘感。

《內衫蟻姑》這是一隻母螞蟻愛上修理紗窗玻璃的公螞蟻的愛情故事，一隻體重超重的母螞蟻在購物的路途返家之後發現家裡的紗窗破掉需要修補，於是記起了門外經常傳來『修理紗窗換玻璃』的廣告車聲音，就這麼地因此兩人開啓了小畫家愛情故事的樂章。

《坐咧》是ABS的前身MEMO，收錄在『用力活著』第一發，第一次嘗試台語電音創作的作品，裡面寫的人物都是經典，玩著大風吹的遊戲，無論政治上還是娛樂圈或是通告藝人們，何時何地風向球都不斷地大風吹，同音異字讓你猜猜看到底誰是誰？特別符合首張EP《ABS 解悶》的核心精神！

我們探討現實社會中溫馨有趣的人事物
我們輕鬆地看待身邊發生的大小事
我們讓台語歌曲開啓了新的無限

ABS 做自己的音樂 最爽

《抓背 Scratch My Back》is a song we wrote because of the need to scratch the itchy back. We seldom notice interesting things and people in life, the back scratching machine wouldn’t know that it got praised because of the comfortable feeling that it gives to human.

In the song《厝內LADY Lady At Home》,we talk about what being a SOHO worker is like.

When facing our occupation, we have to be patient, and sometimes we might also need to hide our artistic personality in order to satisfy our client. This song is also a great song to dedicate to mothers. The moon is used to represent introverted and gentle females, their personality are like tides, not simple to understand.
So we combined the Moon with Moon cakes that we eat on Mid-Autumn Festival, adding a love song narrative Rap to show the sense of mystery in this song.

《內衫蟻姑 E Good》is a love story about a female ant that fell in love with a male ant who fixes screen and glass windows. A really over weighted female ant was on her way back home from shopping, she found out that the screen and glass windows in her house needs to be repaired, so the advertising vehicle stated “repairing screen and glass windows” occurred to her, then the tiny artists’ love story begins.

《坐咧 Sit Down》is a memo before ABS, included in 《用力活著》.The first time trying “Taiwanese Rapping EDM”. In this song, every character is classic, playing Simon Says, no matter if they are political or entertainment notice artists, whenever or wherever they play Simon Says, using same sound but different words for you to guess who it is. Especially meets the core spirit of the first EP《ABS Relieve Bordem》
