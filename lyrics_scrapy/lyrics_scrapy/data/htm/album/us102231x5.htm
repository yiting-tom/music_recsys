Album Intro：

日語專輯

Following Fahrenheit, DA Mouth, and other popular Taiwanese artists, Vanness Wu of F4 (of drama Meteor Garden fame) will be making his solo Japanese debut! After ending his contract with Sony BMG earlier this year, Vanness signed on with PonyCanyon in preparation for his Japanese debut. His debut single, Only, will be released under the VANNESS on 2009.5.20. in CD+DVD and CD-only formats. The title track of the single is written by popular songwriter Daisuke “DAIS” Miyachi and is described as a fresh pop tune. The CD-only includes one extra song and its instrumental.
