Album Intro：

Deluxe 2 disc edition of the fifth album from the Alt-Rock/Punk band Say Anything. The album was recorded between August and September, 2011, and finds the band attempting to write their first true Punk album, lyrically. Features 'Burn A Miracle' and 'Say Anything'.
