Album Intro：

Simian Mobile Disco release a brand-new album `Temporary Pleasure, which includes the single `Audacity Of Huge' featuring vocals from Chris Keating from the critically acclaimed Yeasayer. `Temporary Pleasure' was recorded at the bands studio in east London throughout 2008 and 2009, when the band weren't busy touring their live show, DJing and producing. As well as representing the best of UK dance music the record contains some staggering pop anthems, featuring vocal contributions from the likes of Jamie Lidell, Telepathe, the Gossip's Beth Ditto, Super Furry Animals' Gruff Rhys and Hot Chip's Alexis Taylor. Wichita. 2009.
