Album Intro：

'The music is very elaborate gothic doom metal, more doom than gothic, to be precise, with beautiful melancholic melodies, slow to mid fast tempo, strong, heavy and often melodic guitar riffs and ponderous rhythm section with amazing passages. They always keep the right dose of heaviness in their drive, never going too extreme and never too sweet. In a way it reminds me to the glorious days of the British gothic doom giants from 90's like Paradise Lost, with deep emotions that can compete even with Anathema's Judgment or similar acts. - Metalstorm.com
