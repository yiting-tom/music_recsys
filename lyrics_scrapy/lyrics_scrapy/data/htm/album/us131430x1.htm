Album Intro：

Stephen Pearcy was (and still is) the lead vocalist for the multi-platinum successful heavy metal band Ratt who have just completed a sell out European tour. Under My Skin is the follow up album to the all acoustic Stripped released in 2006 and as such is his fourth proper solo album and is a return to the Heavy Metal/Rock style of Ratt but with a harder edge.
