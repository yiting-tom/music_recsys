Ayaka【 38 albums 201 lyrics 】
Ayaka Iida (飯田 絢香 Iida Ayaka?, born December 18, 1987), known mononymously as Ayaka, is a female Japanese singer, songwriter and record producer formerly signed to Warner Music Japan. Born in Osaka, she moved to Tokyo to pursue a singing career. She married actor Hiro Mizushima on February 22, 2009.
