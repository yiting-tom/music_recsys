Album Intro：

Atlanta' s newest superstar-in-the-making,
V.I.C. follows in the footsteps of rap
phenomenon Soulja Boy Tell em for his
debut album, Beast. From the club-jumping
lead track,Get Silly, produced by
Soulja Boy, and other danceable jams to
introspective, poetic parables, Beast is a
cleverly crafted hybrid of witty battle
rhymes and energetic southern party
music.
