Album Intro：

As a result of the Vodafone Live advertising campaign, 'Can You Dig It?' (which originally peaked at no. 18 in the singles chart in 1991) has brought back deserved attention to this Madchester five-piece band. For the first time, this 'Best Of' features the best tracks from the band's three (deleted) albums with tracks from Imaginary Records & Virgin Records. Unusually for a hits collection, 'The Best Of The Mock Turtles' also includes 8 brand new songs, penned by original frontman Martin Coogan. Includes 'Can You Dig It? (Fatboy Slim & Simon Thornton 2003 Remix)'. Copy controlled.
