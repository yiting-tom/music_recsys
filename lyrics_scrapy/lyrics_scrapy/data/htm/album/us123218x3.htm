Album Intro：

Limited Edition vinyl LP pressing of this US release, which contains three bonus tracks, 'The Highest', 'Whites Only Party' and 'There Goes My Outfit' (Acoustic Version). 2006 release from this loose collective of Montreal-area musicians led by the charismatic Murray Lightburn, who combine cabaret-style vocals with moody, intense orchestral Pop/Rock. 12 tracks including the first single 'Ticket To Immortality'. Arts and Crafts Records.
