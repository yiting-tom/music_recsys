Album Intro：

Hostage Calm's self-titled sophomore album delivers an expansive and deranged pop-music palette, imprinting The Smiths' reluctant resolve and early-Beatles pop sensibilities on a high-energy punk landscape. The shimmering jangle of electric and acoustic guitar layers meet adventurous bass lines, intricately woven together and backed by a percussion section. Soaring choruses and smooth vocal harmonies surge to the forefront, driving political and personal anthems with infectious melody and poetic elegance. Instantly accessible yet ambitious in depth, Hostage Calm's Run For Cover debut is destined to be your summer infection/affection.
