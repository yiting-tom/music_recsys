Album Intro：

Oh hell yes.............they're back.......................! Seance return from Sweden to blow the roof off with their third full length. Classic, heavy as hell swedish Death Metal that isnt a let down from their previous L.P.s. Gone is guitarist Jensen, (The Haunted) also gone is the evil bass sound from the Saltrubbed Eye's release, but i dont even know if that sound can be reproduced! Line-up still features members of Witchery, In Flames, and Satanic Slaughter. Killer album...............
