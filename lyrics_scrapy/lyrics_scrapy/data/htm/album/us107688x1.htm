Album Intro：

Bethany Dillon has built quite the resume over the last several years taking fans with her on the journey and gaining new fans every step of the way but all along delivering songs that not only encourage and inspire, but deal with real spiritual truths in her life and the listeners'. Through success at Christian radio, major tours across the country, major national press, and TV placements on block-buster movies Bethany Dillon has always remained core to writing songs with lyrical and spiritual depth that incorporate catchy hooks radio airwaves and car stereos love. Bethany's highlight anticipated new studio album Stop & Listen delivers what fans know and love about Bethany while also taking her craft to a new level both lyrically and sonically. Stop & Listen releases September 8th and is sure to be one of 2009's best.
