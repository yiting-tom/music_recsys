Album Intro：

My Dinosaur Life is the upcoming fourth studio album by American pop punk band Motion City Soundtrack, due to be released on January 19, 2010. It will be their first album on a major label, Columbia Records, after the band moved from Epitaph in June 2008. As with their second studio album, Commit This to Memory, the album was produced by Blink-182 bassist and co-frontman, Mark Hoppus.
