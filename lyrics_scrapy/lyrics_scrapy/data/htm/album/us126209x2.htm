Album Intro：

Don't be surprised if 2010 is the year real pop punk re-emerges, with This Time Next Year leading the charge. TTNY will be your new Taking Back Sunday, Movielife, and New Found Glory rolled into one. Anthemic, catchy, want-to-sing-along-to songs are the band's specialty and their debut full length, Road Maps and Heart Attacks, features 11 tracks of their best material to date. The band has previously released two EPS and toured the U.S. on self-booked tours since their formation in 2006.
