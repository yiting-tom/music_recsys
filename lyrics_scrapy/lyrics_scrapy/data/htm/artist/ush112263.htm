パスピエ( Passepied )【 33 albums 140 lyrics 】
Passepied (パスピエ, pasupie) is a five-member Japanese pop-rock band formed in Tokyo in 2009. They are signed with Warner Music Japan.

Passepied was formed by Tokyo University of the Arts graduate and keyboardist, Narita Haneda.

Members
Haneda Narita (keyboard)
Natsuki Ogoda (vocals)
Katsuko Misawa (guitar)
Yoshikuni Tsuyusaki (bass)
Takuya Yao (drums)
