Britney is the third studio album from American pop singer Britney Spears. The album was released on November 6, 2001 in the United States and Canada, on November 12, 2001 in Japan, and on November 5, 2001 around the world. Spears supported the album with the Dream Within a Dream Tour. The album has sold 15,000,000 copies worldwide.
