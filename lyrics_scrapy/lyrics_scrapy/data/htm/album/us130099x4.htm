Album Intro：

As a member of the super group La Coka Nostra (along with Everlast and Danny Boy of House of Pain and Ill Bill) Slaine is fresh off the success of his hit album A Brand You Can Trust. The album featured Snoop Dogg, Cypress Hill, Bun B, Immortal Technique and Psycho Realm and has scanned 30,000 units to date and made the following impressive chart debuts in July 2009: #84 Debut on the Billboard Top 200, #6 Rap Chart, #11 Independent Chart, #17 R&B Chart, #21 Internet Chart.

After success on the mixtape circuit and then with Special Teamz and finally La Coka Nostra, Slaine has laid a solid foundation for his first solo project. The album features La Coka Nostra alumni Everlast and Ill Bill along with B-Real from Cypress Hill.
