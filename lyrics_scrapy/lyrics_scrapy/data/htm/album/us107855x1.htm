Album Intro：

Matthew 'Matt' Maher is a Roman Catholic singer/songwriter/worship leader originally from Newfoundland, Canada, who later relocated to Mesa, Arizona. He has written and produced five solo albums to date. His most well-known song is 'Your Grace is Enough.' Matt Maher was born and raised in Newfoundland, Canada. His parents realized his musical talent. Matt took piano lessons, and later immersed himself in many different styles of music.
