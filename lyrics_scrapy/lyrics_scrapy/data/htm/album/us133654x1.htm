Album Intro：

Richard Smallwood is indeed a man of vast talents. A singer, songwriter, arranger and producer, his stellar career has spanned decades. His latest recording, Promises, is sure to be met with enthusiasm. Richard Smallwood fans are going to love his newest collection of songs. Recorded live in studio, Promises has the same magical quality that is a signature of all Richard Smallwood recordings. Backed by his incomparable choir Vision, Richard delivers powerful songs that will make you jump to your feet! And as always, each song is laced with the lush harmonies, melodies and orchestral arrangements that have always been a Smallwood staple.
