Album Intro：

In an age where music has become increasingly predictable and computer driven, The Black Dots of Death have formed to breathe new life into the dying scene. Founded by Clown from the Grammy winning, cultural force that is Slipknot, The Black Dots of Death are unlike any other band that you have heard with an approach focused on killing all of your pre-conceived notions on what music can be bringing a devastating tidal wave of sound crashing down on you.
