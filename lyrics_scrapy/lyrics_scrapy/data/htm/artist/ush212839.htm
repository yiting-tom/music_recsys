Paulo Mendonca( Paulo Mendonça )【 3 albums 5 lyrics 】
Paulo Mendonça is a Swedish funk guitarist of Portuguese origin. He has made 4 albums, the first 3 in the 1990s to limited success. He toured with Tina Turner among others. In 2008, he collaborated on Jeff Scott Soto's album, Beautiful mess. In 2013, he released Does anyone wanna funk? which featured the song Birds and the bees, a moderate success in Sweden and Germany

He was one of the first musicians ever to collaborate with the Swatch Group to provide tunes for its Melody line of wristwatches. His latest collaboration with Swatch is the PAULO MENDONCA-11 PM model, from the Winter 2013 collection.
