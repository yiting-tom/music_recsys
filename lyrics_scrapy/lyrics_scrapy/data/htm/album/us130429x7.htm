Album Intro：

A long overdue reissue of the classic 1975 album by the celebrated jazz and rock group Soft Machine - remastered from the original tapes!

The album was the band s first for EMI s Harvest label and the first to feature fusion guitar icon Alan Holdsworth, along with Mike Ratledge (keyboards), Karl Jenkins (oboe, piano, soprano sax), John Marshall (drums), and Roy Babbington (bass)!

Critically acclaimed upon its release, Bundles opened a new chapter for the band but has been unavailable on CD for 15 years - until NOW! --Official Press Release
