Album Intro：

Scooter Lee is the World's Leading Artist for recorded country line dance music. This is her 4th collection of some of the best country dance music in the world. She continues to travel to over 23 Countries promoting line dancing for the health and fun of it. Celebrating her 10th year on the road with dance music, Walking On Sunshine once again proves why she is the world's leader.
