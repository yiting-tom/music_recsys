Album Intro：

1997 debut album for London based trip-hop act with a distinctively South American flavor inspired in part by vocalist Nina Miranda's childhood. Best known for the track 'Underwater Love' that was featured in a successful Levis' ad campaign, the album's other highlights include, 'Numbers' and 'DarkWalk' and Miranda's sultry vocals in English, French and Portuguese. Standard jewelcase.
