Album Intro：

Vomitory hails from Sweden, and plays good old school death metal. 'Redemption' is a gathering of uncompromising, coherent, monolithic compositions, and a boiling surge of fluid technical and melodic guitar riffs, skillful drumming, and a chest-pounding bass sound.
