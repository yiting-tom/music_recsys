Album Intro：

The Blind Boys of Alabama explore the sounds of the birthplace of jazz and R&B on this studio album: Down In New Orleans. One of the longest-running groups in the world since the 1930s, the Blind Boys came back stronger than ever at the turn of the 21st century after giving a series of rock numbers a gospel makeover. This rousing, optimistic and spiritually-driven record features high-profile New Orleans guest artists Allen Toussaint, The Preservation Hall Jazz Band and The Hot 8 Brass Band, as well as session musicians from New Orleans. 12 tracks. Time Life Records. 2008.
