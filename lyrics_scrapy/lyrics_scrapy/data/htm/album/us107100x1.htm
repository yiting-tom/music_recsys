Album Intro：

Be human is the third studio album from UK rock quartet, Fightstar, and is due for release on April 20, 2009.

The album was co-produced by the band along with Carl Bown and was recorded between a period of August through to December 2008 at Treehouse Studios, Chesterfield, Derbyshire

The band then worked with composer Audrey Riley in Air Studios, London with a full 16-piece orchestra and they cite this as the biggest progression in terms of sound from their previous releases. Lead vocalist and guitarist, Charlie Simpson, commented 'It was great to bring that element, that classical side, into it. It adds so much.'
