Album Intro：

Tour edition of the group's 2009 release. BONUSES include 'Empty' (which was the bonus cut on the limited digipak edition) and a 10 track EP 'Adennda' which features rare/unreleaded tracks: The Breaking (only found on the vinyl pressing of FITW), Beauty in Deconstruction (original version from the 'Storm' sessions), Frozen (Ambrosius remake), Empty (extra album track), Deadland (Tommy Olsson remake), Revolution (Flip Wicked remix), Highlights (bonus track on 'Storm'), Illusions (Zensor remix), Empty (E&A wedding mix), Forever is the World (Chamber version). 21 tracks total.
