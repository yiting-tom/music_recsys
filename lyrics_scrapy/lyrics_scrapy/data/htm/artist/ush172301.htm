Neo Jessica Joshua (born in December 1987), better known as Nao (/ˈneɪ.oʊ/), is an English singer-songwriter and record producer from East London. Her sound has been described as soul combined with electronic music, funk and R&B. Nao coined the term 'wonky funk' to describe her style. She released her debut album, For All We Know, in 2016, and her second album, Saturn, in 2018.
