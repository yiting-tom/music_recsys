Album Intro：

首先，給所有購買這張EP的朋友們:
感謝您花錢買了這張看起來沒什麼卵用的EP，雖然可能不是你這輩子買過最好的專輯，但是相信你一定會在我們的歌裡面找到一些發自內心的笑容，很好，就是這樣，請掛著這樣的笑容然後帶著厭世又有點小叛逆的心跟著老破麻們一起嘲笑這個世界吧。
這張專輯是老破麻樂團自2016成軍以來的第一張EP，算是一個老破麻樂團的里程碑，搞了兩年才完成的這張EP，過程歷經了各種不同的考驗，當中也包括樂手的替換跟創作理念上的意見分歧，與其說是EP更像是老破麻樂團漸漸完型的證明，整張專輯包含了老破麻樂團的生活態度跟嘲諷主義風格，”我們怎樣都隨便啦，我們是老破麻”，輕浮又無所謂的一句話就能說明，我們寫我們看到的事情，我們唱我們覺得好聽的歌，我們用最直白的方式講給你聽，不阿諛不做作的做自己，反正你想聽我就唱，只要你喜歡其他都隨便啦，就像這張EP的名字乾脆就叫做EP是一樣的意思。

For all our friends that are buying this album
Thanks for wasting your money on this shit; though it might not be the best album you'll ever own, we still believe you'll find a bunch of reasons to break out into smile from those songs. Well... if not, we're not sorry either.


About 'Extended Play'
Since 2016, the year that Old Slut Distortion was formed, this is the first album we ever accomplished, two years in the making and it's been a tough ride, including changes to our musician lineup in between. So you bet your sweet ass this album is just like a milestone to our band.
The album to its core is representative of the lifestyle attitude and taunting attitude Old Slut Distortion is proud to boast, like we usually says 'Rock hard or Get Stoned'.
Straight from the heart, we wrote what we saw, sing what we heard, live that glory all out that's all how we roll.
Hence why this album's name is called Extended Play. Yeah, we don't give a fuck, simply all we care about is whether our tunes managed to inspire its fans.
