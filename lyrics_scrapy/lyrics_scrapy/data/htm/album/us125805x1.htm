Album Intro：

The Underneath bring a thrilling and sexy high fashion electro-rock-fusion that blends heaviness and romanticism. They're the next generation of Japan's rock superstars who represent the new and powerful J-rock revolution culture; a new era that combines fashion, music, rock, and technology, where each member is involved in composing the music and creating the visuals, making a truly unique live experience. Look for them on the 35-city Taste Of Chaos Tour from February 29 through April 15 with Avenged Sevenfold and Atreyu.
