Album Intro：

Critically acclaimed but never receiving their due, The Runaways were formed in Hollywood in 1975, with the help of music svengali/producer Kim Fowley, becoming the first all female Rock 'N' Roll band to actually make an impression on the record buying public & musicians alike - although more so now than then! Originally released on vinyl by Cherry Red in 1980, due to the popularity of our recent Runaways re-issues, & demand from the public, 'Flamin' Schoolgirls' will now be re-issued on CD for the first time!
