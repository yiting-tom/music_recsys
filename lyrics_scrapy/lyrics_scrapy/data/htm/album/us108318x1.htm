Album Intro：

Limited deluxe two CD edition includes a bonus CD containing five bonus tracks. 2010 sophomore album from the Manchester Alt-Rock/Pop act fronted by Liam Fry. Falcon was produced by Ed Buller (Suede, Pulp) and mixed at the famous Electric Ladyland studios with the legendary Michael Brauer (The Rolling Stones, Bob Dylan). The band have gained notoriety not only for their cracking tunes, but also for Fry's mouthy arrogance, which always makes for good press no matter how you look at it. Features the tracks 'You Overdid It Doll' and 'Cross My Heart And Hope To Fly'. Polydor.
