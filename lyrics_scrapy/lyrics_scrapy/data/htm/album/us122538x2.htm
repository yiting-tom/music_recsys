Album Intro：

2007 album from the old-fashioned hard rocking Metal band. The Autumn Offering have a new vocalist and a burning mission to spread the Metal gospel across the world! With Fear Will Cast No Shadow, the band continues to carry the flag of classic Heavy Metal in the vein of early Metallica, Pantera and Ozzy Osbourne into battle against the mainstream trends. Fear Will Cast No Shadow, does not offer crossovers, new fashions or trendy revivals. Fear Will Cast No Shadow, is all about the celebration of true Heavy Metal and having a damn good time doing it. It's high time you get off your ass, grab a cold beer and join them in the insanity! Victory.
