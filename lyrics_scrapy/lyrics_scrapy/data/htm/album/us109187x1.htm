Album Intro：

One Night Only is the self-titled second album from the young Yorkshire band One Night Only; the follow-up to 2008's Started a Fire, which spawned the huge hit 'Just For Tonight'. Produced by Ed Butler (Suede, White Lies, Pulp), One Night Only sees the band explore a more 80s-influenced, synth-driven sound while hanging on to the big anthemic choruses that characterised their debut. Includes the single 'Say You Don't Want It'.
