Album Intro：

Black & White is the sophomore album by The Maine and was released on 13, July 2010. Recorded in California with Grammy-nominated producer Howard Benson (My Chemical Romance, The All-American Rejects) 'Black & White' sees The Maine stepping out of their comfort zone and creating a cohesive body of work that is an 'album' in the most traditional sense of the word.
