Album Intro：

Rare professional studio recordings that predate the band's classic 1966 album Black Monk Time. Also features two tracks from the Five Torguays, the band's early '60s incarnation as a covers band. Now often referred to as one of the first Garage Punk bands, The Monks' '60s recordings are more popular 41 years later than ever before. Munster. 2007.
