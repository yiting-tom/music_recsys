Album Intro：

TONIGHT ALIVE is a young pop/punk band from Sydney Australia for fans of Paramore, New Found Glory and Good Charlotte. Since late 2009, the band has already shared the stage with prominent Australian bands and international acts, including Lost Prophets, 3oh!3, The Flatliners, Mest, No Fun At All, The Blackout, Ivoryline, and soon to be touring buddies 'The Wonder Years'. Fronted by 17 year old Jenna Mcdougall, the band formed completely in early 2009, after 2 long years of writing, creating and becoming great friends. Along with Jenna, guitarists Whak and Matt, drummer Jake and bass player Cam, started writing the songs and have created a massive buzz that is hitting the streets of Sydney, as well the rest of Australia and soon the entire world!
