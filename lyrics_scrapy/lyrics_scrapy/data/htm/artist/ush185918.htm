VROMANCE【 9 albums 13 lyrics 】
Vromance (Hangul: 브로맨스), stylized as VROMANCE, is a South Korean boy band formed by Rainbow Bridge World in 2016. Dubbed as label mate Mamamoo's 'brother group', the group debuted in July 2016, with the extended play The Action and the single 'She'.

Prior to their debut, the four members worked as vocal trainers for Korean pop idols and recorded guide songs for singers. Members Park Jang-hyun and Park Hyun-kyu previously recorded the song 'Love Is...' for the OST of the 2013 SBS drama The Heirs. In February 2016, Park Jang-hyun collaborated with Huh Gak for the song 'Already Winter'. Their label described them as a versatile traditional vocal group.
2016-present: Debut with The Action and Romance

The group debuted on July 12, 2016, with the release of their first mini-album The Action and its title track 'She', which they described as swing-inspired and funky.

Vromance held a comeback showcase on January 5, 2017, for the release of their second mini-album Romance and its title track 'I'm Fine', which has been described as heartwarming and traditional. The album was released the next day accompanied by a music video for the single.
