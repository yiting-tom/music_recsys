Album Intro：

The Panda Band is a collision of layered vocal and keyboard melodies, guitars riddled with a menagerie of effects, textured drumming and an array of other sounds that tip the hat to some of the coolest studio and live bands over the past forty years. This first album from Australia's The Panda Band is the final product of songwriting, playing and creating in the most isolated city on the planet: Perth,Western Australia. The Panda Band is fronted by multiple award-winning songwriter Damien Robert Crosbie, who has penned the songs currently earning his five-piece an ever-growing popularity with fans, radio, press and the music industry.
