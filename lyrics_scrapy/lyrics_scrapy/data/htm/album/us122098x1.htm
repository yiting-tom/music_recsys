Album Intro：

2010 release from the Scottish Popsters. It has been five long years since their last album, Man-Made, was released to universal acclaim, so it is no overstatement to say Shadows is keenly anticipated. While most bands are lucky to have one great songwriter, Teenage Fanclub are blessed with three, hence Shadows is overflowing with the kind of gorgeous, harmony-driven classics you'd expect to find on a greatest hits album. 11 tracks.
