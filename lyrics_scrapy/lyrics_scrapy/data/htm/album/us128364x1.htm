Album Intro：

2007 release from the former vocalist from Australian Alterna-Rock band The Superjesus. Just as the title says, this is Sarah McLeod recorded live and acoustic. Includes hits and surprises, including 'Second Sun' (The Superjesus song from Jet Age), 'Private School Kid' (from her solo debut Beauty Was A Tiger), 'All But Gone', a cover of the AC/DC classic, 'You Shook Me All Night Long' and many more.
