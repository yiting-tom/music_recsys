Album Intro：

Aja is the second in a trilogy of releases from Toronto art rock quartet The Darcys. An interpretation of Steely Dan's 1977 studio masterpiece, the album was arranged, produced and recorded by the band at home during the summer and fall of 2010. Moody, dense and textured, Aja is an exploration of physical and emotional space and the real and imaginary divisions within it.
