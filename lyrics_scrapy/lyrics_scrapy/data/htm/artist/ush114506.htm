REIMY【 26 albums 233 lyrics 】
Reimy Horikawa (堀川 麗美 Horikawa Reimi?, born 6 January 1965 in Ginowan, Okinawa), better known by her stage name Reimy, is a Japanese singer-songwriter. She has collaborated with Japanese movie producer Shunji Iwai on several soundtracks.
