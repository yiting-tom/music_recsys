Album Intro：

2013 release, the fourth album from the Buffalo, New York-based post-Hardcore band. Formed in 2008 by Matthew Tybor, also referred to as 'The Bunny' hence the rabbit mask and persona he incorporates in the group. Tybor was immediately joined by Chris Hutka, known as 'The Bear' whom likewise, wears a bear mask. Tybor employs screamed vocals for the band while Hutka provides clean vocals.
