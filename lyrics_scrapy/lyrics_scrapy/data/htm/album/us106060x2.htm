Album Intro：

Butch Walker's 2008 solo album, Sycamore Meadows features the single, 'Weight of Her', and an additional track, 'Ships in a Bottle.' The video for 'Ships in a Bottle' documents Butch walking on the grounds of his home in Southern California after it was destroyed by the wildfires of November 2007. Butch is a singer,songwriter, producer who has written hits for other artists, including Bowling for Soup, Avril Lavigne, Family Force 5, Pink, Lit, Simple Plan, and more.
