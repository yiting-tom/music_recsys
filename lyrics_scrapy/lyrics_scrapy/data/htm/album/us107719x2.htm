Album Intro：

Three Days Grace 新專輯One-X將於六月十三號推出. 樂隊的新單曲'Animal I've Become'現已進入了Active Rock, Alternative and Rock 的TOP10排行榜！

Three Days Grace is set to release their new album, One-X, on June 13. The band's new single 'Animal I've Become' has now moved into the Top 10 in the Active Rock, Alternative and Rock chart.

It's the band's first collection of new tunes since their self-titled debut, which spawned hits 'Just Like You' and 'I Hate Everything About You.' The band had been on tour supporting the 2003 release, and vocalist Adam Gontier and drummer Neil Sanderson said the lonely and isolating experience proved to be the inspiration behind One-X.
