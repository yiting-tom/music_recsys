Album Intro：

Import pressing of the 2008 album from one of Finland finest Rock exports of recent years. The band that gave us the international smash hit 'In The Shadows' in 2003, taken from the platinum-selling album Dead Letters, have now teamed up with legendary producer and songwriter Desmond Child (he that wrote the likes of 'Livin' On A Prayer' by Bon Jovi, 'Poison' by Alice Cooper and 'Livin' La Vida Loca' by Ricki Martin) on an album that boast a score of potential hit singles.
