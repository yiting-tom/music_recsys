Album Intro：

From this Omaha band come ten perfectly formed songs bursting with hyperactivity, while emanating a bittersweet melancholy of long lost summers and misguided first loves. These are songs about loving and kissing, dancing and drinking, staying and leaving, driving and talking, and sleeping and dreaming. Tilly And The Wall celebrate the petulant, determined, feisty nature of youth, recorded in the style of classic 60's pop, boy/girl harmonies, and Americana folk records.
