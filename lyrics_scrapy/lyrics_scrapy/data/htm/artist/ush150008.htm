Jon Bellion( Jonathan David Bellion )【 9 albums 70 lyrics 】
Jonathan David Bellion (born December 26, 1990) is an American singer, rapper, songwriter and record producer. He was born and raised in Lake Grove, New York on Long Island. Bellion has released four mixtapes and one studio album, the most recent of which, The Human Condition, was released on June 10, 2016, and it debuted at number five on the US Billboard 200. He is currently signed to Visionary Music Group and Capitol Records. Bellion also provided vocals for the 2015 EDM song 'Beautiful Now' by Zedd and opened the concerts from the third leg of Twenty One Pilots' Emotional Roadshow World Tour.
