Album Intro：

專輯以ØZI出道後三年間的人生變化為核心，以全英語打造出真正屬於ØZI 的正統R&B世界觀，也宣告正式投入海外音樂戰場。

整個計畫發展出全新製作方式，全作品飛往韓國與美國，與當地最強R&B音樂人合作，製作出屬於這時代R&B應有的味道和標準，出現在合作名單的音樂製作人Ian Thomas是製作過PBR&B天王 Bryson Tiller及Ian Dior的製作人！歌手則有Arin Ray 是與Kehlani, Ty Dolla Sign, Meek Mill, Babyface合作過的同等級代表性歌手！加上前Jay Park旗下H1GHr Music的韓國R&B天王GSoul都出現在合作名單！

以此規格製作與發行的「新樂園Forbidden Paradise」與國際音樂公司Transparent Art 結盟合作，在米奇林和剃刀蔣的運作下發展成更完整的音樂廠牌，將ØZI的音樂正式在海外扎根！

而你現在聽見的是由台灣出發，但無時差與世界同步的國際音樂作品《PEDESTAL》


＊本作品 獲文化部影視及流行音樂產業局108年補助

Ever since ØZI emerged in 2018 with his self-titled debut album, the Taiwanese-American singer has kept us on our toes. Shortly after his debut, he managed to secure six Golden Melody Awards nominations as well as the widely sought after Best New Artist title. With an industry buzz throughout Asia, ØZI launches his label/collective “Forbidden Paradise”, bridging his Mando-pop and Western musical background to introduce his next body of work: PEDESTAL.

PEDESTAL is ØZI’s eight-track sophomore album, a project two years in the making as the singer-songwriter tirelessly hones in on his craft while in the limelight as one of the most promising rising Asian American artists in recent years. In this album, ØZI takes us on a tumultuous journey about love - from heartbreak to lust to romance. Packed with crossover genres such as Nu Metal and Alternative R&B and international features from Arin Ray and G.Soul, PEDESTAL proves the range that exists within ØZI’s musicality. This is also the first time the rising star is co-producing an album with American producer Ian Jeffrey Thomas, who most notably produced for artists such as Bryson Tiller, Kevin Gates, CL and Iann Dior, as he continues his mission to open the world to a forbidden paradise.
