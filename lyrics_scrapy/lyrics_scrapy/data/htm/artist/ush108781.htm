Pay Money to My Pain (stylized as Pay money To my Pain and abbreviated as P.T.P.) was a Japanese alternative rock/metal band. All of the band's lyrics are in English.

Members

Former members

JIN – guitar
Jin is currently vocalist for High Speed Boyz, he has also produced for groups such as Greeeen, the band of his brother Hide, and Bareeeeeeeeeen, the combination group of both Greeeen and Back-On.
PABLO – guitar
Pablo is a Hispanic-Japanese guitarist and is a support guitarist for Fake?, Oblivion Dust and Takui Nakajima.
T$UYO$HI – bass
T$UYOSHI now plays bass in the band THE BONEZ
ZAX – drums
ZAX now plays drums in the band THE BONEZ
K – vocals
K, real name Kei Goto (後藤慶?), was often in poor physical condition and suffered from mental health issues (he talked about depression in some of his songs). On January 10, 2013, Pay Money to My Pain announced that K died on December 30, 2012 at age 31 due to acute heart failure at his home in Yokohama.
