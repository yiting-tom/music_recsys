Album Intro：

One of australia's most innovative hip hop acts returns. Tzu's new album 'computer love' is an immaculate mash-up of hip hop, soul, funk and electronica, which takes their music to a whole new level. Tzu's last release 'smiling at strangers' saw the band break boundaries and reach new heights with their music, culminating in being named finalists for the prestigious australian music prize. Mixed by tony espie (cut copy, the avalanches) at melbourne's sing sing studios, 'computer love' is a stunning 14 track album that represents tzu's musical coming of age.
