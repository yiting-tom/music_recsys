Album Intro：

Act III: Life and Death is the second full-length studio album by American rock band The Dear Hunter, set to be released on June 23, 2009. According to lead vocalist Casey Crescenzo, it is the third part of a six-act story, following the original in 2006, Act I: The Lake South, the River North, and 2007's Act II: The Meaning of, and All Things Regarding Ms. Leading. The album was produced by Crescenzo and Andy Wildrick in the band's own studio in Providence, Rhode Island, with a number of musicians making guest appearances.

Limited deluxe edition Includes a picture book that tells the story of 'the boy''s journey throughout the preceding album, 'Act 2: The Meaning of, and all Things Regardings Ms. Leading.' The illustrations were done by artist Kent St. John, with the coloring done by Synapse's Glenn Thomas. For those unfamiliar with the first two acts, the book will serve as a prologue of sorts to 'Act 3: Life and Death.' Includes 4 additional tracks not on the standard version.
