Album Intro：

When legendary post-punk outfit Hot Water Music called it a day in 2005, three-quarters of the band weren't ready to roll over. Frontman/guitarist Chris Wollard, bassist Jason Black and drummer George Rebelo recruited new guitarist Todd Rockhill and formed The Draft - their debut In A Million Pieces is a victorious shout at the world. Taking time to step back from years of constant touring, The Draft dug deep to find the inspiration for 12 tracks that move beyond the legacy of Hot Water Music into new territory - from the catchy, hook-laden 'Bordering' to the ska-inspired thump of 'Let It Go.' With rave press from tour dates this summer that introduced The Draft to a waiting world, the band is set to build on what they have already achieved as individuals, coming together with a cathartic, defiant voice of their own.
