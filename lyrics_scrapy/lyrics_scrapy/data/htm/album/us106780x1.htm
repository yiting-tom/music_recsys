Album Intro：

Mando Diao is a garage rock band from Borlänge, Sweden. The band got their breakthrough with the release of the album Hurricane Bar. The song God Knows was featured in FIFA 06. Their main fan base is in Sweden, Germany, and Japan.

Give Me Fire is the fifth studio album by Swedish band Mando Diao. Produced By Salla Salazar of hip hop group group The Latin Kings and his brother Masse.

One of Mando Diao』s roadies knew the hip hop producing brothers and introduced Gustaf Noren to them. Noren says he originally approached The Salazar Brothers as he is a massive Latin Kings fan and wanted to get an autograph, but the visit ended with a recorded song. After sitting on the song for a while he played it for the other members of the band and they decided to record more tracks with Salle & Masse.
