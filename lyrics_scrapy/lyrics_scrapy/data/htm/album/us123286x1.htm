Album Intro：

After several years together, road-tested, club-polished, The Dirt Drifters signed to Warner Bros. Records, who turned them loose on a full-scale national audience with This Is My Blood. Produced by Justin Niebank, the phenomenal musical talent known for work with Vince Gill, Brad Paisley and Keith Urban, among others, it comes from that place where world-class songwriting weaves compelling stories from life's good and bad-stories all the more riveting for their truth.
