Album Intro：

Low's visionary Alan Sparhawk cranks the amps for a radical stylistic departure. Crude, mangy guitars collide with trudging stoner-rock rhythms and some catchy melodies - for modern-day spirituals that defy all preconceptions. Produced by Red House Painters/Sun Kil Moon's Mark Kozelek. Individually numbered limited edition of 1,500 in a deluxe gatefold sleeve.
