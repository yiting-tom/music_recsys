Album Intro：

Anthem Lights is an innovative, high-energy pop foursome with soaring vocals and hearts bent on ministry. The group's infectiously melodic self-titled debut, set for a May 2011 release on Reunion Records, delivers 10 powerful anthems sure to leave a ripple effect long after the CD stops spinning. Anthem Lights 'Alan Powell says this about the new album, 'These songs were born out of what we've experienced over the past couple of years, spiritually. We've had to learn some very difficult lessons about who God is and who we are. We've gone through moments where we felt really close to the Lord, really felt Him at work in our lives, and then very real moments when you feel you re going around in circles and can t find Him at all. I think we've just tried to be really honest about what that darkness feels like and yet make it clear that 'the Light always wins.'
