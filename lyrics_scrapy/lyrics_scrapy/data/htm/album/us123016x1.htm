Album Intro：

The City Drive is a hook-driven indie rock band with mainstream sensibilities and catchy songs that hearken to the All American Rejects and Green Day. Their debut 12-track album, Always Moving Never Stopping, produced by Chris Fudurich (Nada Surf, RX Bandits, Ozma) will be released Summer 2006, and will be supported by new media and sampler campaigns along with touring, press support and home-label We The People Record's grassroots branding. Additionally, The City Drive's long standing publishing deal with Famous Music has landed TCD tracks in American Pie: Band Camp, Yours, Mine and Ours and a spot for The World Poker Championship.
