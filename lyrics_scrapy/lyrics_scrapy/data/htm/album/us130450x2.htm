Album Intro：

2010 release from the Irish duo. Solar Bears' music is the soundtrack to a good life. Their songwriting and production has a languid, pastoral grace that intertwines Rock and Electronics, twisting melodies through Prog Rock's structures and occasionally making sharp turns into more tense post-rock territory. She Was Coloured In mixes analog Electronica with the quietly epic perspective and shimmering neon synths of late-'70s/early-'80s kosmische music and the Synth pop it informed.
