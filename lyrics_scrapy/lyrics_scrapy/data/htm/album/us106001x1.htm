Album Intro：

Academy Award winner actor and Grammy nominated songster Jamie Foxx will release his J Records sophomore album Intuition on December 16. The first single, 「Just Like Me」 featuring T.I., will impact multiple radio formats on November 10 and will be available via all digital music providers on November 11.

「Just Like Me」 was co-written and co-produced by The Dream and Christopher 「Tricky」 Stewart, the same duo responsible for such hit songs as Rihanna's 「Umbrella,」 Mary J. Blige's 「Just Fine,」 and Beyonce's latest hit 「Single Ladies (Put a Ring on it).」

The follow-up to his RIAA-certified double platinum release Unpredictable, Intuition is slated to feature additional production by Timbaland, Ne-Yo, Sean Garrett, Salaam Remi, Carlos McKinney and more.
