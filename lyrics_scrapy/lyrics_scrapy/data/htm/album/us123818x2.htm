Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2008 release, the seventh and final studio album from the Swedish rockers, appropriately titled Head Off. The album features a little sonic taste from every phase of The Hellacopters musical past, from the feed-backed and hum-bucked mayhem found on 1996's Grammy winning Super Shitty To The Max! to the refined songwriting and production found on their last studio album Rock & Roll Is Dead. Sayonara, my brothers in Rock! 12 tracks including 'Electrocute', 'I'm Watching You' and 'I Just Don't Know About Girls'. Wild Kingdom.
