Album Intro：

在廣大樂迷的千呼萬喚之下，Hollywood Undead的新專輯終於露出水面，這次所帶來的音樂依然強勁有力，而你所做的不僅是排著隊手捧鈔票迎接它的到來，Hollywood Undead更需要身為歌迷的你為他們吶喊與尖叫，讓我們一起嗨翻天吧。

Hollywood Undead will return on March 15th with their second album, the ominously titled American Tragedy. The album is the follow up to their controversial but massively successful Gold-selling debut, Swan Songs, which spent 53 consecutive weeks on the Billboard Top 200 Album chart. So it was selling well for a year and a week!

Hollywood Undead's first single from the album, Hear Me Now is at radio now.

Been a long time, were out of the studio, done recording, beat the shit out of ourselves to make sure our second record is better than our first, said J-Dog. It was hard but we finally finished it and we're excited our first track is out there.

The group is an explosive cocktail of hip hop, rock and metal, forging an alternative style that carved out a loyal and die-hard fan base since their debut on the LA scene in 2005. They craft incisive, sharp and sarcastic raps that slash the jugular of mainstream pop culture. Donning individual masks, Hollywood Undead possess a dark aesthetic that's connected to their hometown's depravity.
