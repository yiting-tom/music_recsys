Album Intro：

2006 reissue of the Californian thrash metal band's debut full length album that orginally issued in 1988 under the title 'Illusions'. In 1991, the Roadrunner label released it on compact disc with the title of 'Chemical Exposure', which it retains. The lineup included Steve DiGiorgio on bass, Jon Allen on drums, Darren Travis on guitar and vocals and Rob Moore on guitar.
