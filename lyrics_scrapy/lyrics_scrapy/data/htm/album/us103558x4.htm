Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded deluxe two CD edition of the Irish rockers' 1975 album containing an additional 15 rare and live tracks. After spending four albums trying to find their niche, Thin Lizzy finally forged an identifiable sound featuring the twin guitars of Scott Gorham and Brian Robertson. This sound draws from Hard Rock, Folk, Pop, and Rhythm & Blues. It set the stage for the big commercial breakthrough of the follow-up album, Jailbreak. This deluxe version of the album contains an expanded booklet written by Malcolm Dome and contains new interviews with Scott Gorham and Brian Downey. The bonus CD's contains many rare and unreleased tracks available for the first time. Universal.
