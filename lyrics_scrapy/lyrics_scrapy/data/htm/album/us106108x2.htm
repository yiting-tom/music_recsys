Album Intro：

June 29th sees the release of 'California Kicks', the first full length release from San Diego based pop-rockers Fight Fair under the Triple Crown label. 'California Kicks' is ten tracks packed with pop-laced, beach party feeling, sing along music. Confirmed for the entire Warped Tour 2010, their first single 'Party Girl' will be featured on the Warped Tour comp on Side One Dummy.
