Album Intro：

Through driving rhythms, catchy and anthemic choruses, moving melodies and rock solid bottom end, Since October have connected with listeners in clubs across America, on the radio dial landing a Top 20 Active Rock single ('Guilty') and online with an authentic and pure voice. 'Life, Scars, Apologies' -- the band's sophomore album -- will undoubtedly kick down even more doors for Since October, spreading their huge riffs and big harmony vocals further across the globe. Featuring the hit songs 'The Show' (featured on WWE Wrestlemanina), 'Life, Scars, Apologies', and 'The Way You Move'.
