Kenshō Ono (小野 賢章 Ono Kenshō, born October 5, 1989 in Fukuoka Prefecture) is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He was formerly represented by Sunaoka Office and is now represented by Animo Produce. In Japan, has also appeared in numerous anime and Japanese television drama series. He is also known for playing Muromachi Tōji from the well-known Prince of Tennis Musical.
