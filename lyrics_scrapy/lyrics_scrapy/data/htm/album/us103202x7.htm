Album Intro：

Keep Calm and Carry On is the upcoming seventh studio album by Stereophonics, due to be released in Ireland, Belgium, Austria and Germany on 13 November 2009, and in the UK on 16 November 2009. The lead single from the album will be 'Innocent', released on 9 November. The album will be available in three different formats; a single disc version, special edition with a Bonus DVD and vinyl.

The album title reflects a British World War II poster bearing the same phrase.
