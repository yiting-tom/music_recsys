Album Intro：

The British heavy metal legends produce a series of alternative takes on classic favorites to herald a worthy new entry to the Saxonian Institute of Metal. There is always feral beauty in a raging beast...and with Unplugged and Strung Up, Saxon's selection of unique, fresh and adventurous interpretations of material previously heard only with raw heavy metal thunder, the point is triumphantly underscored. As if this reinvention wasn't enough, a digipak version of the release will include 2002's storming set of full-volume, pedal-to-the-metal re-recorded hits 'Heavy Metal Thunder'!
