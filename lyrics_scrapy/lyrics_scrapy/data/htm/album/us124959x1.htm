Album Intro：

Raw, funky deep soul from Brooklyn based Tre Williams and the Revelations -- with a sound rooted in the classic southern sounds of Hi Records and others, but feels fresh and current -- not so much in the throwback vein of others in the soul and funk underground -- but a timelessly gritty sound! --Dusty Groove, 2009

They play with the intensity of those who have nowhere to look but forward, and will fight to make sure that s the direction they re going.
Williams voice is close to the bone but full of fire and longing, the kind that comes along once every generation if we re lucky. --Sonic Boomers, 2009
