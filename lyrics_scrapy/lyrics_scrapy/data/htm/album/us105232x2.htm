Album Intro：

It's Not Me, It's You is the upcoming second studio album by British pop singer-songwriter Lily Allen. The album is expected to be released in the United Kingdom on February 9, 2009, and on February 10 in North America. Despite some profany on tracks such 'Fuck You' and 'The Fear', the album does not care a Parental Advisory sticker.
