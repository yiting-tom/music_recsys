Album Intro：

Internationally renowned singer, songwriter, producer and multi-instrumentalist Teena Marie, the 'Ivory Queen of Soul,' presents her 2009 album, a 16 song set showing she hasn't lost one bit of her edge. Like most of her classic albums, Congo Square cruises smoothly from Southern Soul and smoky Jazz to Dancefloor Funk. Features guest appearances from Faith Evans, George Duke, MC Lyte, Howard Hewitt and Shirley Murdock. Includes the new hit single 'Can't Last A Day' featuring Faith Evans.
