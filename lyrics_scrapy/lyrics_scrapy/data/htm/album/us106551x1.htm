Album Intro：

2009 album from the hotly-tipped outfit. Since opening the NME New Noise Tour in '08, White Lies have played well-received festival slots at Latitude, T In The Park, Fuji Rock Japan and a main stage performance at Lollapalooza festival in the US. Recorded at ICP studios in Brussels during the summer of 2008 with acclaimed producers Ed Buller (Pulp, Suede) and Max Dingel (The Killers, Glasvegas), the album will be features the singles 'Death' and 'To Lose My Life'. Polydor.
