Album Intro：

If life is what you make it, then Johnny Ray and Jimmy James have made a good living drifting in and out of heartache, then moving on. And now that the statute of limitations has run out, they are finally free to release the album of music they have lived through. With voices that were born to harmonize, this record takes you on a ride into a world where the torch never dies, lost highways moan, everybody needs someone to love.
