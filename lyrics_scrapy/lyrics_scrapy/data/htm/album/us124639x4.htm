Album Intro：

Newly remastered, this CD runs for 70 minutes and includes 9 bonus tracks as well as liner notes by Alistair Fitchett. Stand-out tracks include 'Dirty Clothing', 'Bringing You The Love', 'The Sadness Of Sex Pt. 1' and the should have been single 'Peaches'. Among the pick of the bonus cuts are the singles 'Something For The Lounging And Bemused', 'Confused' and 'Bedraggled'. LTM. 2005.
