Album Intro：

2009 studio album from this outfit formed by former Bananarama member Siobhan Fahey. Songs From The Red Room features writing and production collaborations with contemporary hitmakers Whitey, Jagz Kooner and Death In Vegas to musical legends Marco Pirroni (Adam & the Ants, Wolfmen) and Terry Hall (Specials / Fun Boy 3). The albums first single, 'Bitter Pill', has already been covered by The Pussycat Dolls for inclusion on their debut album and renamed 'I Want You Back'. 14 tracks.
