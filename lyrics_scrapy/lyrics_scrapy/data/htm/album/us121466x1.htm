Album Intro：

Those who hold the Peanuts music close to their heart will never forget Christmas Time Is Here; Charlie Brown Theme; Linus and Lucy , and all those others. But many have never heard Vince's extra- Peanuts work; this spotlight on his jazz-piano mastery includes the aforementioned plus Mr. Lucky; Moon River; Corcovado; Fascinating Rhythm; Work Song , and more including unissued tracks!
