'Like Only A Woman Can' is a 2007 single from the Irish singer, Brian McFadden. It is an Ireland-only release, where it reached the top of its singles chart.

It is an open and frank love-song to someone who has helped the writer find redemption. 'It is a song about Delta', explains Brian, 'but equally it』s me admitting the mistakes of my past, while looking forward to the future.'
