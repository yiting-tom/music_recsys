Album Intro：

2009 four CD set containing a quartet of quality sets from Sanchez recorded for top producers King Jammy, Fattis the Xterminator, Dennis Star. The four original albums collected together in reproduction sleeves give an extensive overview of Sanchez's successful hit-making career. Includes the hit singles 'Can We Talk', 'Searching', 'Brown Eye Girl', 'Wild Sanchez' and more. Special guest appearances from Bounty Killer, Marcia Griffiths, Macka B, Sweetie Irie, Flourgon and Shaka Shamba. VPD.
