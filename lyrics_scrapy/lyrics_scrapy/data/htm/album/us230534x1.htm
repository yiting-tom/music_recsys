Album Intro：

上古時代，人類說著一樣的語言、有著一樣的信仰
而自傲的人類為了與神平起平坐，蓋了一座通天的塔 - 巴別塔
因而觸怒神，降下了天罰將人們的語言打亂，並且流落到了世界各地。
人類度過了數千年不同語言、不同文化、不同信仰的時代
最後在地表種下了無數的'花朵'，結束了這個紀元。
我們從出生以來認知到這個世界的時候就是如此了...
一群在末日後的人們，為了再次挑戰那個神曾經給予的試煉
給自己許下了誓約，那是鑲嵌在靈魂中的話語，也是改變世界的開端。
在未知的世界中，收集那些數百年前的詩篇，將末日前那無法被傳達的故事傳頌下去，這是一個曾經有著各種不同語言、不同文化、不同信仰卻又共榮的時代。

At the ancient times, the whole earth was of one language, one speech, one faith
But they were pride. For touching the sky and reaching God, human started to build a tower-The Babel.And the God was rage, punished them by confusing their language,and scattered them abroad from thence upon the face of all the earth.For thousand years, human been through different languages, different cultures, and different dynasties. By planting countless “bloom” on the whole earth, it is the end of last era.

This is how we recognize the world and have been told that how it should be since we were born. We,the survivor of the apocalypse, challenging the same trial of the ancestor and the old God again.Thus we made a vow which the words embed in souls and starting point of changing the world. Of the unknown territories, we shall collecting the “psalm” that grows from the “bloom” and reveal the unseen world by singing the poem.
This were an era of different languages, cultures and religions but also the prime time of human.
