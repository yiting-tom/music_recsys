Album Intro：

With over 10 million in career sales, TobyMac's first three solo projects received RIAA Gold status and GRAMMY® nominations, his live CD/DVD took home a GRAMMY® award in 2009, and this year the much-celebrated and acclaimed artist celebrated his seventh consecutive GRAMMY® nomination for his latest album TONIGHT. The GRAMMY ® nominated single 'City On Our Knees' topped the sales and radio charts faster than any of his previous singles, spending 8 weeks at #1 on the Christian Audience Chart. TONIGHT also garnered him the highest sales debut of his solo career with over 78,000 units and a No. 6 Billboard Top 200 debut. The album was also named as iTunes® Best Christian Album of 2010 and recently took home the Dove Award for 'Best Rock Contemporary Album of the Year.'

In addition to being nominated for 'Favorite Contemporary Inspirational Artist' at the 2010 American Music Awards and nominated for three Billboard Music Awards including 'Top Christian Artist,' the title-track of TobyMac's latest album was also chosen as the theme song for MLB's 30 Clubs in 30 Days Spring Training Series. His music has been heard in major motion pictures including 'Transporter 2' and 'Hancock', and has been used extensively on television including the NFL Network, Fox Sports, Fox College Football, ESPN's the ESPY Awards and WWE's Fatal 4 Way Pay-Per-View. Most recently, his song 'Showstopper' was chosen to launch the new season of CBS's hit show CSI: NY.
