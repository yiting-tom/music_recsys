Album Intro：

UK compilation for Scottish punk act who formed in 1977. Includes all ten of their UK hits for Virgin, including the classic new wave anthem 'Into The Valley', seven inch B-sides, and album tracks from their three Virgin albums. The Skids were led by the late Stuart Adamson of Big Country. 18 tracks, EMI. 2004.
