APRIL【 5 albums 27 lyrics 】
April (Hangul: 에이프릴) also stylized as APRIL is a South Korean girl group formed by DSP Media in 2015. They debuted on August 24, 2015, with their mini-album Dreaming and its title track 'Dream Candy'. The group is currently composed of six members: Chaekyung, Chaewon, Naeun, Yena, Rachel and Jinsol.
