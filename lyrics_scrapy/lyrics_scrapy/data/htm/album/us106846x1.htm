Album Intro：

Recorded with Matt Squire (Boys Like Girls, All Time Low) in Los Angeles, the album is a collection of extremely varied rock songs that were influenced by everyone from the Cure to the Beatles and showcase This Providence's rock edge as much as it does their stripped-down sensibilities.
