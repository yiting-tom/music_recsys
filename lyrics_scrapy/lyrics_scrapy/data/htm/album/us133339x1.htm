Album Intro：

Carosone was One of the Most Innovative Composers and Signers of the Neapolitan Tradition of the 50's and 60's. In the 50's, Together with his Orchestra, He Had a Huge Success Both in Italy and Abroad with a Special Style in which Mixed Neapolitan Music with a Kind of Primordial Rock'n'roll, Swing, Mambo, and Jazz. This Collection Includes 54 Hits and Some Rarities Previously Unreleased on a Remastered Version of a CD.
