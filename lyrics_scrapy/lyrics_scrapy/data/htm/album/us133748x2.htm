Album Intro：

Something of a compilation of Ricky Nelson's 1950s Rock 'n' Roll songs, which enabled Ricky to establish himself alongside such contemporaries as Fats Domino, Bill Haley, Elvis Presley and Little Richard. Major commercial success was just around the corner for Ricky, who recorded most of these sides for the Verve label but moved after royalties weren't forthcoming. Hallmark.
