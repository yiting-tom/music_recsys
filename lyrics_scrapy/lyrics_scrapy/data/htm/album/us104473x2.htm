Album Intro：

While She was Initially Presented to the World as a Protege of Missy 'Misdemeanour' Elliott and Timbaland, Charlene 'Tweet' Keys Immediately Proved Herself No Mere Follower, with the Delectable and Diverse Debut 'southern Hummingbird'. After a Three-year Hiatus, Tweet is Back, and Ready to Unveil the Long Overdue 'it's Me Again', an Exciting, New Chapter in her Life, which She Refers to as 'Southern Hummingbird Times Two.' On 'it's Me Again', Tweet Confirms her Independence with a Remarkably Adventurous Record that is Smoothly Seductive and Giddily Fun at the Same Time. Here Tweet Creates Clever, Original, and Moving Songs that Straddle the Line Somewhere Between Neo-soul and Modern Mainstream R&b, Wrapping her Sweet-and-sultry Voice around Each Tune with Wicked Effect. And, of Course, Having Elliott's Able, Artful Production on Most Songs Certainly Doesn't Hurt. Includes a Bonus Remix of 'turn Da Lights Off'.
