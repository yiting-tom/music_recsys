Album Intro：

Downhere's newest effort, At The Altar of Love, will catch you off guard. In a time when many are turning and questioning the roots of their faith, Downhere is running away too – into the past. Embracing the roots of their faith and seeking to inspire others to do the same, their new effort lays down the foundation for faith in a new and inspiring way. Rediscover You is arguably one of the best tracks and you'll find yourself singing it over and over again. Give this release your full attention, not just a passive listen, and go buy a copy. Heck, go buy two and give one to someone to bless them and encourage their faith. That's exactly what Downhere would want you to do.
