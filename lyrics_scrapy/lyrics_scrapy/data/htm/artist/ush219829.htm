Melody Day (Hangul: 멜로디데이) is a South Korean girl group formed in 2012 by Cre.ker Entertainment (formerly known as Viewga Entertainment). The group officially debuted with their single album Another Parting in February 2014 and with three members: Yeoeun, Chahee, and Yein. The fourth member, Yoomin was added to the group in October 2014.
