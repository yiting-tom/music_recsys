Album Intro：

The Wombats Proudly Present: This Modern Glitch is the second studio album by British indie band The Wombats. The album was released in the United Kingdom on April 25, 2011, with three singles preceding it: 'Tokyo (Vampires & Wolves)', 'Jump Into the Fog' and 'Anti-D', released on September 24, 2010, January 24, 2011 and April 11, 2011 respectively. 'Techno Fan' has been revealed as the fourth single off the album set to be released on June 22, 2011.
