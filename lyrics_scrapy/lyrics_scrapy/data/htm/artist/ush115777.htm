Ikue Otani【 2 albums 25 lyrics 】
Ikue Ōtani (大谷 育江 Ōtani Ikue?, born August 18, 1965) is a Japanese voice actress from Kashiwazaki, Niigata, affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She is best known for her roles in the Pokémon series (as Pikachu), One Piece (as Tony Tony Chopper), Naruto (as Konohamaru Sarutobi), Detective Conan (as Mitsuhiko Tsuburaya), Uchi no Sanshimai (as Fu), and Konjiki no Gash Bell (as Gash). She is currently attached to Mausu Promotion. Her pet name is 'Iku-chan'. She is known for playing both male and female roles, and sometimes plays multiple roles in one production. She is a native of Tokyo, but grew up in Niigata Prefecture.
