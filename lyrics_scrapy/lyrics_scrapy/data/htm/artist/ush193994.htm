Melissa Jefferson (born April 27, 1988), known professionally as Lizzo, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. She is a founding member of indie hip hop groups The Chalice, Grrrl Prty, The Clerb, Ellypseas, and Absynthe. Her debut album, Lizzobangers, was released in 2013. In 2015, she released her sophomore album, Big Grrrl Small World.

After the release of Big Grrrl Small World, she worked with Ricky Reed, who helped her to improve her voice and later signed her to Atlantic Records. Soon after, she released the 2016 major-label EP Coconut Oil. In 2019, she will release Cuz I Love You, her third studio album.

She will be doing an international tour in 2019 for Cuz I Love You to be released on April 19th, 2019.
