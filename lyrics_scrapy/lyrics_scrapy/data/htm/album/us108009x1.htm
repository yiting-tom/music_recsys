Album Intro：

All or Nothing is the third studio album by British R&B singer Jay Sean and is set to be released on 23 November 2009 in the United States as the singer's American debut album as part of the Super Monday mass album release in the US. The album will be later released in the United Kingdom on 30 November 2009 under Jayded Records and distributed by Island Records.

'Down', featuring label-mate Lil Wayne, was officially released on 30 June 2009 in the U.S. for digital download as the album's lead single. It took the number-one spot on the U.S Billboard Hot 100 at the week date of 17 October 2009. The album's second single, 'Do You Remember', has debuted at #27 in the Hot 100.
