Album Intro：

Best known for their massive disco smash 'Fly Robin Fly', which occupied the #1 slot on Billboard’s Disco, Pop and Black Album charts and won the Grammy for best R&B Instrumental Performance in 1975, Silver Convention left behind an impressive string of extremely successful and highly influential hits that provided the blueprint for some of the best dance music of the seventies and eighties. In just a few short years, Munich, Germany’s Silver Convention racked up six #1 singles in the United States and were the toast of the disco era worldwide. This compilation contains all their massive hits expertly remastered, extensive liner notes and, as anadded bonus, band member Penny McLean’s solo worldwide disco smash, 'Lady Bump'. All the classics are here including 'Get Up and Boogie', 'Save Me', 'San Francisco Hustle' and many more.
