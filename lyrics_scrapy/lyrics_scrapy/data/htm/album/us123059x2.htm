Album Intro：

The Color Morale stepped it up big time for their sophomore album, 'My Devil In Your Eyes'. Produced by Joey Sturgis (The Devil Wears Prada, Attack Attack!), their faith based songs are delivered with the heaviest of the heavy and the catchiest hooks you've heard in a long time. Similar to A Day To Remember and Attack Attack!, TCM are beloved in the scene due to their non-stop touring and humble personalities.
