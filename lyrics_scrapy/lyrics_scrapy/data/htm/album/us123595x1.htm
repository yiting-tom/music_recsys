Album Intro：

The debut album Audio & Murder from Chicago pop-punks The Frantic features lead vocalist Kyle Dee (of American Idol 2012 fame). The catchy sounds of Audio & Murder find that fragile balance between pop and punk that can simultaneously cause a nose-bloodying circle pit and mass sing-a-longs.
