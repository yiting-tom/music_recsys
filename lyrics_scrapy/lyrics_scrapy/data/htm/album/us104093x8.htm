Album Intro：

When one of the loudest bands in rock switches gears and shows its not-so-loud side, you can't help but listen. Highlighting the acoustic-flavored songs from disc two of the platinum-plus 2005 double album In Your Honor, Foo Fighters' new live CD also features unqiue interpretations of favorites from the band's entire career.

Recorded at the Pantages Thearer in Los Angeles, Skin And Bones features the Foo Fighters performing with guest players Petra Haden (violin) and Rami Jaffee (keyboards), as well as Drew Hester (percussion) and former Foo Fighter Pat Smear (guitar).

This LIVE CD release will capture the intensity of this unique concert experience which drew rave reviews!
