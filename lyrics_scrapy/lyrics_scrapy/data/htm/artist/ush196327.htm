Heize( 헤이즈 )【 9 albums 41 lyrics 】
Jang Da-hye (born August 9, 1991), better known by her stage name Heize, is a South Korean singer-songwriter and rapper signed to CJ E&M. After making her debut in 2014 with her mini album Heize, she raised her profile by appearing in the second season of Unpretty Rapstar. Since, Heize has released two additional albums And July (2016), ///(You, Clouds, Rain) (2017) and several non-album singles to critical acclaim. The singles Don't Come Back (2016), And July (2016), Star (2016), You, Rain, Clouds (2017) were huge sucesses and swept through the number one ranking on every Korean music chart (All-Kill). She appeared on the soundtrack for the hit drama Guardian: The Lonely and Great God with the song 'Round and round', which also attained aclaim and ranked high on charts.
