Album Intro：

2009 album from the Swedish all-girl Alterna-Rock quartet. Produced by Bjorn Yttling, Sparks is a covers album that includes the gals tackling songs originally recorded by Dusty Springfield, Janet Jackson, Suzi Quatro, Redd Kross, Foo Fighters, Steve Miller Band, Pentagram and others. 10 tracks including the first single 'In Private'.
