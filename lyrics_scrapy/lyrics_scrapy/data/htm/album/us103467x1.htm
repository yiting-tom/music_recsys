Album Intro：

Newcomers Scene 23 got their well publicized start on the TV show 'Popstars 2'. This diverse group includes Monika Christian, Laurie Gidosh, Donovan Green, Josh Henderson, and Dorothy Szamborska. Their debut album 'Popstars 2: Introducing Scene 23' was full of an eclectic choice of tracks. The first half of the album includes songs recorded by Scene 23, while the second half describes Popstars 2's experience including songs from R. Kelly, Mariah Carey, and Joe. 'He Said She Said' is a song that makes you just want to get up and dance. 'I Really Don't Think So', 'What She Got', and 'Respect Me' are also outstanding pop songs not to be overlooked. A taste of Europe's two-step beats is brought out in the song 'Another Night'. 'The Greatest' is a sweet pop ballad with a soulful sound. And finally, 'All This Love' shows an R&B side to Scene 23. This album is also enhanced and when put into your computer, you to can learn dance steps, get singing tips, have fashion guidance, and a lot of other points essential to becoming a popstar. These young men and women are definitely talented and I am looking forward to their next release.
