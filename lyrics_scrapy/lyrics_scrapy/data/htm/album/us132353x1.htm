Album Intro：

Sophomore album featuring three long-standing members of Sade's backing band, Stuart Matthewman, Andrew Hale and Paul Desman. The album features Aya (Naked Music) on five tracks and Mecca Lady Bug who once opened for Sade and was the former vocalist for Digable Planets. This Japanese version features one bonus track, 'Lama Vs. Shaolin'. EPIC. 2004.
