Album Intro：

2009 sophomore release from the Britpop quartet. The Holloways exploded onto the scene in 2006 with their debut album So This Is Great Britain?. The unfortunate demise of their old record label midway through recording their second album followed by a fire which destroyed their equipment, rehearsal space and spiritual home may have been enough to crush a lesser band but now The Holloways return with No Smoke, No Mirrors. 11 tracks including the single, 'Jukebox Sunshine'. Snapper.
