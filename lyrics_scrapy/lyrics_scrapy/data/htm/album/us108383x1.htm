Album Intro：

The Classic Crime is set to release their third studio album, 'Vagabonds' on Tooth & Nail Records. The album was recorded in just three weeks and incorporates the most basic elements found in The Classic Crime's live sound. However, it's not all about the music. This Seattle, WA five-piece also hopes that their fans see their passion and love for charity. Not only has the band taken a trip to help orphanages in India, but late in 2009 they embarked on a trip to support an orphanage in Haiti. The eye-opening experience brought further shock to the band in 2010 when a massive earthquake hit the area, greatly affecting the orphanage and the friendships they had built. 'Vagabonds' is not just an album but rather a musical platform that enables the band to support those in need, who are often overlooked.
