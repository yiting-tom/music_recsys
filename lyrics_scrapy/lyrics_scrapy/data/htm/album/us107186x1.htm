Album Intro：

Boomtown Records has announced the release of 「2004-2009」 – a 21 track CD that will span each non-album release across the five year career of The Getaway Plan. The CD includes the 「Hold Conversation」 EP, the rare 2004 demo, and the 「Shadows」, 「Where the City Meets the Sea」 and 「Streetlight」 singles complete with exclusive B-Sides.
