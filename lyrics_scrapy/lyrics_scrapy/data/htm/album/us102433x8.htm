Album Intro：

Six-song vinyl EP containing mostly unreleased recordings from the 'Jukebox' sessions. In keeping with the 'Jukebox' theme (scanned 110K RTD), all the songs here are covers: 'Auld Triangle', 'Dark End Of The Street', 'Who Knows Where The Time Goes', 'Fortunate Son', 'I've Been Loving You Too Long', and 'It Ain't Fair'. Limited deluxe double gatefold 10-inch vinyl.
