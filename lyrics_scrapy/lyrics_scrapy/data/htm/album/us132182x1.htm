Album Intro：

2013 release, the third installment in the French Prog Death Metal band's The Cube series. Born in Valenciennes in the North of France in 1989, Supuration have been releasing numerous amounts of records over their 20 year career, developing a series of conceptual albums called The Cube. This third entry comes 20 years after the debut album release initiating the story about The Cube and 10 years after the similar in concept and powerful Incubation in 2003. Popular for their massive wall of guitars and tasteful vocal delivery making them sound like an unexpected mixture of a crushing Carcass and an intimate Voivod, Supuration have made themselves a cult recognition in the live circuit for not playing too often in the recent time but delivering mesmerizing and unique performances.
