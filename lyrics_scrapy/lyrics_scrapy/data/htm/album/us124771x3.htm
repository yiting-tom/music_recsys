2010 live release from one of the most popular bands to come out from Sweden in the past few years. With appearances at the Eurovision song contest, gold and platinum records won in their home country and a few hundred shows held in the entire Europe, The Poodles close the first chapter of their history of success with the release of their live opus, No Quarter.
