Album Intro：

2007 Japanese edition of the solo debut release from the Punk legend, formerly of Rancid and his side-project the Transplants. Armstrong was never a stranger to having his music suddenly and widely embraced, as evidenced by the hit songs Armstrong has created for both, it was clear that the iconic singer/songwriter had another bonafide hit with the song 'Into Action.' The undeniably catchy dance song became an instant favorite of fans and critics alike. This edition includes a special remix of 'Into Action' as a bonus track and comes with the special bonus DVD (NTSC/Region 2).
