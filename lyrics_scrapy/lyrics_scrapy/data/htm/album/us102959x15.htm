Album Intro：

UK edition of this 1977 album from the British Rock institution. This reissue is complemented with some great unreleased material including a B-side that would otherwise be completely lost. The song in question, 'Prince Of The Punks', not only boasts an energetic melody, but also features a magnificent set of lyrics dealing with the Punk 'revolution'. Universal.
