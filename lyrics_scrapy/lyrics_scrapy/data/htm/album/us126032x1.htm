Album Intro：

The view from the bottom of a glass can be a glorious place to find yourself. The colorful glow of tarnished senses can lead to all manner of creative abundance. Welcome, then to the plentiful sadness of The Wyos (Wasted Youth Orchestra). Your new favorite sense of contemplation all packaged in Dublin via Limerick. 'Throw Your Arms Up To Heaven' is a starting point for a band that have observed the fragility of Sparklehorse, the threats of Bright Eyes and the beauty of Calexico. Indeed the opening track 'A Letter From St Jude' will have you weeping into your Laphroig and by the end of the album the bottle will be empty.
