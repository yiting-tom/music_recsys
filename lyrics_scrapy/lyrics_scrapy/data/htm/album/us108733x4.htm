Album Intro：

2010 release, an acoustic re-recording of their critically successful album Butterfly House. It was their first release since 2007's Roots And Echoes, and the album took two years to write, as the band decided to heavily road-test their new material before recording it. The Coral decided to re-record the whole album acoustically after receiving a hugely positive reaction to a string of acoustic performances by the band while promoting the original record. 12 tracks.
