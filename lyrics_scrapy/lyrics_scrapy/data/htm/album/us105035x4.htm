Album Intro：

'Be Good to Me' is Ashley Tisdale's first single from her debut album, Headstrong. The single was released in the U.S. on December 25, 2006 and in South America on January, 2007. The song was included in the album Radio Disney Jams, Vol. 10 on January 22, 2008 and is included in the 2007 hit sales brazilian compilation album CD Luv! - Vol 3. It is also heard in the video game Thrillville: Off The Rails and in the multiplayer online casual rhythm game DANCE! Online. This song is also featured in the 2008 film Bring It On: In It to Win It
