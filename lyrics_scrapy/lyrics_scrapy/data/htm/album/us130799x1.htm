Album Intro：

When you first hear the single, 'Dear Claudia,' you assume that SouthFM is one of those LIFEHOUSE, CALLING, CREED type bands. Not to say that 'Dear Claudia' isn't as good, if not better than any of those aforementioned band's hits. However, the meat of the matter is the rest of the band's debut album which conjures up Tool, A Perfect Circle and bands of that ilk, all with far more substance than the occasional hit single.
