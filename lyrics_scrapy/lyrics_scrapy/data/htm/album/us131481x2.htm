Album Intro：

Steve Azar , singer-songwriter delivers Indianola as a soulful blend of country, blues and rock that reflects his roots in the Mississippi delta and his life now in Nashville. Critics have proclaimed that Azar has the inflections and rhythms of a young Bob Dylan or a Delta version of Springsteen. Indianola is Steve s
much anticipated release following his critically acclaimed and Pollstar s #1 tour in 2007 with Bob Seger and the Silver Bullet Band. Steve s last CD, Waitin on Joe, produced a radio
phenomenon with I Don t Have To Be Me ( Til Monday) and his title track yielded the #1 video featuring academy award winner Morgan Freeman.
Indianola showcases Steve s multi-faceted talent as a performer, musician and songwriter (he wrote or co-wrote all fifteen tracks). He also makes his debut as a producer on this
project and claims it s his most personal CD to date. This record has all of me in it, from the emotion to the sound and
feel of every song on it. It s been a long time comin and we re lookin forward to getting back out on the road to play
it. says Azar.
