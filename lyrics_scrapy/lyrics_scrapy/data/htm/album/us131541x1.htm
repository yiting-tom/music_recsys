Album Intro：

For ToGetHer, Nieve recruited friends like Costello, Sting, Vanessa Paradis, Laurie Anderson, Ron Sexsmith and many more to duet on an album of all original material. As the original keyboardist for Elvis Costello & The Attractions, Steve Nieve is no stranger to working with top talent. A Royal College Of Music student, Nieve was an integral part of The Attractions, playing on some of Costello's most influential albums of the era including This Year's Model, Blood And Chocolate, and Goodbye Cruel World. In the years since, he has been inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall Of Fame and racked up more than 6 solo albums.
