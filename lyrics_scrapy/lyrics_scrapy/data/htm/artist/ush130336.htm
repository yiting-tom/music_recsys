Snap! are a German Eurodance group formed in 1989 by producers Michael Münzing and Luca Anzilotti. The act has been through a number of line-up changes over the years. Their best known hits in the US are their 1990 #2 US Billboard Hot 100 hit 'The Power' and their 1992 #5 hit 'Rhythm Is a Dancer.'
