Album Intro：

Special Australian edition includes two bonus tracks. 2011 studio album from the influential Kansas City Alt-Rock quintet. Seven years after what looked to be their final album Guilt Show, The Get Up Kids returned to the studio in 2009, sparked by a spur-of-the-moment decision to start writing songs together. And so There Are Rules came to be - 12 new songs that will prove to be both surprising, and familiar. The songs were all written spontaneously and then fleshed it out with long time producer Ed Rose in the studio, resulting in a collection of tracks that are fresh and inspired, created by a band that has been given new life and isn't looking back.
