Album Intro：

2013 collection from the Smooth Jazz saxophonist and former Tower Of Power member. Number Ones features 12 tracks, taken from six top-selling albums. Making his solo debut in 1986, the Scottish born saxophonist blew into the 1990s with several releases that sold over 100,000 copies and he landed his fourth #1 album with 1997's 'Jumpin' Off.' In addition to the studio recordings of 'Island Style' and 'Inner City Blues', Number Ones includes live versions of both tracks.
