The Veronicas return with the brand new Revenge Is Sweeter Tour; a CD/DVD package, whose footage was shot at the Melbourne show of their Australian Revenge Is Sweeter National Tour. Features full live concert plus a behind-the-scenes look at the tour. Over 20 minutes of behind the scenes backstage footage leading up to the concert performance.
