Album Intro：

First time on CD for this legendary Oi! / Punk Pathetique album which was first issued by Deram back in 1981. Now comes complete with 14 bonus tracks and rounds up all of the bands recordings in the early 80's including the hit singles 'Two Pints Of Lager', 'Two Little Boys' and 'Cowpunk Medlum' as well as the bands contributions to 'Strength Thru Oi!'. Get this and get well Splodged!
