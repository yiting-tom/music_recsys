Album Intro：

2013 release from the Hip Hop superstar. Raekwon is not just a part of, but is the backbone of, the greatest rap group of ALL TIME!, The Wu-Tang Clan. This platinum selling artist has made the soundtrack to his cult followers lives for over 20 years. Even in today's market, with overly picky fans and dissipated physical CD shelf space, he's continued to stay relevant. His 2009 release of Only Built 4 Cuban Linx, Part.2 received critical acclaim and was an independent release but still managed to sell over 300,00 copies. Rae, is a steadying influence on today's 'real hip-hop' culture and has sold more than 20 million classic albums, now he's at it again,
