Album Intro：

This 1976 LP didn't chart in the U.S., but reached #50 in the UK and gave Bolan and co. a UK Top 20 hit, New York City . Explosions of feedback accompany sleazy disco and stomping rockers on this freewheeling gem: the aforementioned hit plus Jupiter Liar; Chrome Sitar; My Little Baby; Calling All Destroyers; Ride My Wheels; Dreamy Lady , and more!
