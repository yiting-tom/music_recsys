Album Intro：

THE HOUSE OF LOVE were one of the most critically acclaimed Indie Rock bands of the late 80s/early 1990s. SHE PAINTS WORDS IN RED is a brand new studio album by the band, still fronted by the band's singer/songwriter/guitarist Guy Chadwick with lead guitarist Terry Bickers, drummer Pete Evans and bassist Matt Jury.
