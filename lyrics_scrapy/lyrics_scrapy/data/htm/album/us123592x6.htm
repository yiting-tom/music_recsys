Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of this 1992 debut album from the Irish band led by singer/songwriter Glen Hansard (Once) including eight bonus tracks. Their joyous, energetic debut (which has been described as 'Nick Drake meets The Pixies') firmly illustrated The Frames' undeniable talent, particularly on singles 'The Dancer' and 'Masquerade'. Elsewhere, on songs like 'Downhill From Here' and 'Picture Of Love' for example, they flexed the more gentle atmospherics that would characterize much of their later work. Salvo. 2010.
