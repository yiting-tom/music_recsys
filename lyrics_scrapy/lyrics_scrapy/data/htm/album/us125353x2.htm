Album Intro：

We all know it's the music that makes a band what they are, but work ethic and drive is what gives a band the chance to succeed and be the next big thing. San Diego's Secrets laid the groundwork with their debut LP, selling over 15,000 copies and touring non-stop. They've spent 3 months working on their new album, Fragile Figures with producer extraordinaire Tom Denney (A Day To Remember) to create what are clearly their best songs yet. These incredible songs paired with their drive and desire to work harder and be better than anyone else are a recipe for success.
