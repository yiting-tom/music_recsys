Album Intro：

The Mary Onettes return with their first new material since 2009 years critically acclaimed album.

'Islands tilts unabashedly back towards the 1980s, with a lush keyboards- and-guitar sound that makes the Mary Onettes sound like a less downbeat, more hopeful version of the Cure.' POPMATTERS (The Best Indie Pop of 2009).

Love Forever is their first collaboration with an outside producer. Mastermind Dan Lissvik (Young Galaxy, Taken By Trees, Studio) has taken the band to a new level. Exquisite!

Vinyl only. Limited edition of 1000 copies worldwide
