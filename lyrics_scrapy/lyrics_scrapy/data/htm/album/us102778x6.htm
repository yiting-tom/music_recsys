Album Intro：

No Baggage is the upcoming sophomore solo album from Dolores O'Riordan. It is scheduled for release on 21 August 2009 in Ireland, globally on 24 August 2009 and on August 25th, 2009 in North America. The first single, 'The Journey' is set to be released to radio in July in North America and August 10th in Europe.
