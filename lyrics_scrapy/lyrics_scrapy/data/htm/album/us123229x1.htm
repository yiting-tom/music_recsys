Album Intro：

The DeFranco Family burst onto the music scene in 1973 with the biggest selling single of the year titled Heartbeat it's a lovebeat selling 3.5. million copies and followed up with the hits Abra Ca Dabra and Save the Last Dance for Me with numerous appearances on national t.v. shows including American Bandstand, Sonny and Cher and Jack Benny.
