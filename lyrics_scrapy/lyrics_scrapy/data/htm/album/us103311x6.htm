Album Intro：

Greatest Remixes is a greatest hits compilation album with a dance remix to the original songs by Good Charlotte. The compilation is set to be released on November 25th, 2008. Junior Sanchez, Benji and Joel Madden's older brother Josh Madden, and Good Charlotte are the executive producers for the album. The track listing includes songs from all four Good Charlotte studio albums and three unreleased songs, 'Anxiety', 'Fight Song', and 'War'.
