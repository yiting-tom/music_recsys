Album Intro：

The Death Set's second album on Ninja Tune's Counter Records imprint Michel Poiccard, is an album which loses none of the charm of the bands previous ''punk spazz'' work but adds considerable depth, both in terms of the punch and bottom end that comes form enlisting long time friend and Baltimore associate XXXchange (Spank Rock, Kele, The Kills) to man the desk.
