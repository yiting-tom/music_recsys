Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded two CD edition of the 1969 album from this duo featuring Mike and Sally Oldfield. Released by Nat Joseph's Transatlantic label, the album was the first vinyl outing for both musicians. Although embryonic Folk influenced music, the album has an innocent charm which has endeared it to many collectors of late 1960s Folk Rock and of the solo work of both Mike and Sally. This reissue no includes eight bonus tracks. Esoteric.
