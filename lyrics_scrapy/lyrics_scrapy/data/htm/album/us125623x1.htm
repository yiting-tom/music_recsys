Album Intro：

2008 release. The Sunny Cowgirls burst on to the Australian Country Music scene when they signed with Compass Bros Records in 2005. Now, after two albums with cumulative sales of more than 30,000 units, sold out national tours and many hit singles, the girls release their third and finest album to date. Dust Will Settle was recorded in Sydney and Tamworth with producer Graham Thompson. The Sunny Cowgirls trademark themes are unmistakably and unashamedly present but there is also a new maturity in their writing. Vocally the girls are stronger than ever and their shared lead vocals and unique harmonies set them apart as true stylists. Sony/BMG. 2008.
