Album Intro：

2009 album from the Country superstar who already has 25 #1 Country hits to his credit, making him one of the premier singer/songwriters of any music genre. American Ride features 12 new tracks, all but one written or co-written by Toby. Features guest appearances from the Hogliners plus Jazz greats Dave Koz, Marcus Miller and Arthur Thompson on 'Cryin' For Me (Wayman's Song)'.
