Album Intro：

The Phenomenauts are back with their third full length, For All Man Kind. Inspired by new wave and 70's punk, this is the third full-length from the world's bravest space band. A call to arms to defenders of science and honor across the galaxy, For All Man Kind deals with the serious implications of science fiction, science fact, and the future of humanity.
