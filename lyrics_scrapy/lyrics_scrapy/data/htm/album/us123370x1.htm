Album Intro：

Two CD set from the Australian '60s Rock legends who were just as popular Down Under, if not more so, than The Beatles and The Rolling Stones. Fronted by vocalist Stevie Wright, The Easybeats achieved great success outside their home country as well: 'Friday On My Mind' is still considered a classic and achieves airplay on radio stations all around the world on a daily basis. Guitarists Harry Vanda and George Young would eventually find more success in the '70s and '80s under the name Flash & The Pan. And George's younger brothers Angus and Malcolm would later conquer the world in AC/DC! Contains 56 of The Easybeats finest including the classic 'Friday On My Mind'. Albert Productions.
