Album Intro：

Bomb The Rocks The Early Days Singles is, as the title suggests, a glorying cull through The 5,6,7,8's early singles, throwing in twenty-seven prime cuts of cross-translated rock and roll. Ranging from the years 1989 to 1996, Bomb The Rocks is a fun collection of garage rock and roll from a nation overtly obsessed with western popular culture. Sweet Nothing. 2006.
