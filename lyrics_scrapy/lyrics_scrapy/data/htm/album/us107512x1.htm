Album Intro：

Between the Trees is an American indie rock band from Orlando, Florida, formed in 2005.

Between the Trees released their debut album through Bonded Records in 2006, entitled The Story and the Song. In 2007, they signed to The Universal Motown/Universal Republic Group, but it was later announced that this relationship had fallen through and their next album would be self-released. Their second album will be entitled Spain and released on 30 June 2009.
