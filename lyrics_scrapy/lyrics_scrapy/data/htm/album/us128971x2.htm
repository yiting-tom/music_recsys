Album Intro：

2009 release from the new star of Pop Dancehall. Serani, first emerged on the scene as a musician/producer. As one third of production team Daseca, Serani is responsible for delivering hits to many Dancehall artists (Sean Paul, Mavado, Wayne Wonder, etc.) No Games is the body of work by one of the most prolific Jamaican artists of today who has proven that Dancehall music can cross all ethnic barriers and can easily take over air waves and dance floors everywhere.
