Album Intro：

'Lollipop' is a song by Mika from his 2007 debut album, Life in Cartoon Motion and will be released as a single in the UK on 31 December 2007 as a double A-side with 'Relax, Take It Easy'. The single was available for digital download on 24 December 2007. Due to high amounts of downloads for 'Lollipop' at the time of the album release, before it had been released or promoted as a single, it charted in the Top 75 UK Singles Chart due to new rules allowing songs to chart based on download sales without a physical single release. Mika has said in interviews that the song is his favourite on the album and that he wrote it for his sister.
