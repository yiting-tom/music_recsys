Album Intro：

The Trolleyvox's new release is a two album set that is comprised of Your Secret Safe, a full-band electric album produced by Brian McTear (Danielson, Espers, Mazarin) featuring nine new originals and a ripping version of The Who's 'Our Love Was'; and Luzerne, a gorgeous acoustic album featuring songwriter-guitar player Andrew Chalfen and lead singer Beth Filla. The two albums are being packaged together for the physical release and sold at the retail and fan-friendly price of $14.99. The albums will be sold separately for digital download.
