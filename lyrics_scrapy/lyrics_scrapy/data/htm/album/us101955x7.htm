Album Intro：

2013 album from the Scottish Alt-Rock/Pop band. Travis return after a five year hiatus with their seventh album Where You Stand. Sounding and looking more invigorated than ever, the band has delivered an album of effortless, melodic Pop gems produced by 'Super Swede' Michael Ilbert (The Hives/The Cardigans/The Wannadies). Written and recorded across London, Norway, New York, and taking in a production stint at Berlin's legendary Hansa Studios, the band recorded the songs on the same sound deck that produced both OK Computer and their own breakthrough album The Man Who.
