Album Intro：

2009 two CD set. When someone says 'you had to be there', never was a truer word spoken than in the case of The Sensational Alex Harvey Band. As good as the studio albums were, they couldn't possibly compete with seeing the band live SAHB had only released one album, Framed, when they went into the BBC's Paris Theatre to record their first In Concert performance in 1972. This was the start of a series of recordings for the BBC radio and BBC television that captured the band at their very best. This set features unreleased versions of tracks from the peak of the band's success and includes an unreleased version of the hit single 'Delilah' alongside a storming cover of Sly Stone's 'Dance To The Music'. Universal.
