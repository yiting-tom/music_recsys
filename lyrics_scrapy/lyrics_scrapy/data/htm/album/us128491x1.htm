Album Intro：

2013 three CD collection from the American Alt-Rock band consisting of their independently released debut album plus other rarities from the band's early days. Disc One includes the album Baseball: An Album by Say Anything, Disc Two contains the Menorah/Majora EP and the Dormroom Demos, and Disc Three contains Junior Varsity and Other Early Rarities. Includes detailed personal essays about the band and each album, written by frontman Max Bemis.
