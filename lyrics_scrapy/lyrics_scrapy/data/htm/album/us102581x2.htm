Album Intro：

2009 release from the New Zealand Alt-Rockers. Headstunts was recorded in Sweden, and is self-produced. From the full-throttle opener 'Human Error' to the dirt bike guitars and frenzied pummeling of 'Yeah, Yeah' and the '70s stomp rock of 'Highschool Hoodlums', the album finds the band unleashing their finest work to date.
