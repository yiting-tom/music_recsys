Album Intro：

'rowdy' was steve forde's 3rd independent album, originally released in january 2006 now re released by abc/warner music. Steve forde is a great success story - from life on the farm in rural nsw to being a rebel-rousing country music star, hosting his own television series on the country music channel and touring nationally with his own staging and tour bus. Recorded in dallas, arlington, nashville and australia, self produced by steve, 'rowdy' is an album which reflects what's happening today in the world of contemporary country music and he's joined by a couple of cool dudes including vanilla ice and sean 'barney' thomas. Steve pushed the boundaries of country music with this album. The 'country crowd' love country music but they are just as happy dancing to eminem as steve forde and this album dishes up a delicious dose of both!
