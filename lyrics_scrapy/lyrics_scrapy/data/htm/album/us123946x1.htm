Album Intro：

The Impressions have been musical ambassadors for love, peace, and justice for all people. Fred Cash and Sam Gooden consider it an honor that the songs they have recorded along with Curtis Mayfield have had such an impact on the world. The Impressions, which now consists of Fred Cash, Sam Gooden, and Reginald Torian are still successful and performing over 100 concerts every year and still selling out venues all over the world. This is their very first Christmas CD and are proud to share it with you and hope you enjoy.
