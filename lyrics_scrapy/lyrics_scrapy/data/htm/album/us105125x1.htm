Album Intro：

★英國樂壇跨越世代全菁英夢幻組合：Blur+The Clash+The Verve+Africa 70=The Good，The Bad & The Queen ★Blur主唱Damon Albarn領軍，集結英國傳奇貝斯手Paul Simonon﹝The Clash)、天才吉他手Simon Tong ﹝The Verve﹞、西非頂尖鼓手Tony Allen ﹝Fela Kuti﹞與製作鬼才Danger Mouse ★收錄最新單曲【Green Fields】，外加兩首B-Side單曲【England， Summer (In Black & White) Polling Day】和【Green Fields】原始Demo版本，以及【Kingdom Of Doom】音樂錄影帶。

Limited Edition UK two disc (CD + NTSC/Region 0 DVD) pressing of the debut album from Britpop supergroup The Good, The Bad And The Queen featuring a bonus DVD TGTBTQ is a new album featuring a bonus DVD that features three tracks filmed Live at the Tabernacle ('Nature Springs', 'The Bunting Song' and 'The Good The Bad & The Queen') rehearsal footage ('A Soldier's Tale) and an interview with the band. Damon Albarn (Blur/Gorillaz), Paul Simonon (The Clash), Tony Allen (Africa 70/Fela Kuti) and Simon Tong (The Verve). The Good, The Bad and The Queen began life in the Aphrodisia Studios in Nigeria in 2004 and traces a journey from the English music hall tradition, over to West Africa for Afrobeat, zigzagging through the West Indies and its reggae and dub, back to England and London's punk scene, all the while taking in a strand of British beat music from the '50s right through to Britpop. Produced by Brian `Danger Mouse' Burton. EMI. 2007
