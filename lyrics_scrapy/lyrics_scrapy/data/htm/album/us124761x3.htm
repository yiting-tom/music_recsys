Album Intro：

The Plot In You's debut full length will hit you like a ton of bricks. The dark overtones on this album will affect you. It's supposed to. That's part of the plan. Have you ever felt unloved…not loved by a single person? This album is for the unwanted, the forgotten. First Born is the soundtrack to a life gone awry, not by choice, but by circumstance and fate. This is the new breed of heavy music: a new era in metal-core. It's time for the world to meet The Plot In You. For fans of Whitechapel and The Devil Wears Prada.
