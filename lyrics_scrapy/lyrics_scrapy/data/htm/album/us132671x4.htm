Album Intro：

Railroad Earth exploded onto the scene with its critically acclaimed debut record The Black Bear Sessions. Since then, the band has released 5 albums and established itself as a successful touring band. Railroad Earths new record Amen Corner showcases their remarkable songwriting and uniquely crafted arrangements. Railroad Earth is touring the United States in support of the record, which includes major festivals and is supported by in-store performances, a slew of positive press, and a 're-mix' campaign that allows fans to digitally remix three of the albums tracks through their personal computer, with the help of some simple editing software. With 5 records to their credit, opinions vary as to which is their best. But fans agree that The Black Bear Sessions has stood the test of time and is a must have for any fan.
