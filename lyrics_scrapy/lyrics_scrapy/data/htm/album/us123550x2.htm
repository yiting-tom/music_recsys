Album Intro：

Floetry founder Natalie 'The Floacist' Stewart takes the Floetry concept she invented to the next level with her solo debut release. Trend-setting Floetry made its mark as a unique gold-selling duo featuring poet/rapper Natalie Stewart aka 'The Floacist' and singer Marcia Ambrosius, hitting both Urban and Urban AC radio charts and selling over 1.5 million copies of three albums of their ground-breaking mix of soul, hip-hop beats, singing and poetic rap. 'Floetic Soul' delivers the same unique concept that brought Floetry a devoted worldwide fan base, this time with a stellar list of guest vocalists and producers JR Hutson and Chris 'Big Dog' Davis.
