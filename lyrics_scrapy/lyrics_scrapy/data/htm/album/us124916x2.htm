Album Intro：

UK two disc (CD + PAL/Region 0 DVD) pressing of this 2009 album from Australia's premiere purveyors of technical Death Core. Includes a bonus documentary DVD featuring a professionally filmed full concert filmed in 2008 while on tour with I Killed the Prom Queen. Also included is the bands 'stop gap' release Lost Verses. Unconsecrated was recorded under tragic circumstances, the bands original vocalist Damien Morris lost his life when the band's tour van crashed in December 2008 while on tour with All Shall Perish. Damien Morris features on three tracks and Jamie Hope handles vocals for the remainder with the exception of 'Architects Of Repulsion', which features guest vocals from Brook Reeves of Impending Doom, Brandan Schieppati of Bleeding Through, Hernan Hermida of All Shall Perish, Karl Schubach of Misery Signals and Dan Weyandt of Zao. Listenable.
