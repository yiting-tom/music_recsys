Album Intro：

New full length album from this band of costume clad crime fighters who battle all things lame in the pursuit of ultimate radness! The new album includes single 'RADIO DOWN!' featuring guest vocals from hip hop legend Biz Markie. Singer Christian Jacobs is also the co-creator of award-winning hit children's TV series Yo Gabba Gabba!, which has featured songs by The Aquabats!
