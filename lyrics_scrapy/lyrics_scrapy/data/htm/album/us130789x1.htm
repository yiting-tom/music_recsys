Album Intro：

UK pressing of their 2008 album features one bonus track: 'Beautiful Freaks'. South's methodology is a continued sonic exploration of melody refracted through an eclectic palate of disparate genres and atmospherics that blow by any and all artificial distinctions. Their sound is inspired, and their shimmering live show is unlike anything you may have experienced before. True to their mission of exploring all genres, the band has outdone themselves by creating You are Here, a record that lilts through swirling psychedelic pop, electrifying dance beats and unhinged space rock. Genepool. 2008.
