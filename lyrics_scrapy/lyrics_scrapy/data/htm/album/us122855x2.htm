Album Intro：

Dance-infused experimentalist The Bunny The Bear turned heads and opened eyes with their Victory Records debut, If You Don't Have Anything Nice to Say..last summer. Their electrifying mash-up of pulsating synths, chugging metal, and prominent visuals sparked an underground sensation that had everyone talking. Additionally their two music videos for 'Ocean Floor' and 'C'est Pas Si Loin' experienced viral success by garnering hundreds of thousands of views. Less than a year later, The Bunny The Bear are building on their current momentum by releasing The Stomach For It. The Stomach For It Highlights the dynamic duo's contrasting yet uniquely complimentary, vocals over their fusion of dance and extreme metal. Known for their captivating and theatrical live performance The Bunny The Bear will be hitting the road this spring with Blood on the Dance Floor, Brokencyde, and Duece to support The Stomach for It. The Bunny The Bear will be one of the most talked about bands in 2012.
