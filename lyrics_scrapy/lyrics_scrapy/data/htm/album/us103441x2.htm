Album Intro：

The Proclaimers second & best known album first released September 1988 reached no. 6 in the UK. Includes hit singles: 'I'm On My Way,' and 'I'm Gonna Be (500 Miles)' which was a hit in the US five years later when it was included in the soundtrack of Benny & Joon. Special Edition contains: Newly remastered album plus remastered bonus disc containing: - B sides from singles: 'I'm Gonna Be', 'Sunshine On Leith', 'King Of The Road' Previously unreleased radio sessions Nicky Campbell BBC Radio Session, 6 January 1989, BBC Going Live On Radio 1 Christmas Extravaganza, 23 December 1990, & BBC Live at Glastonbury, June 1989. EMI. 2011.
