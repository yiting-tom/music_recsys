Album Intro：

With the 'Feel Free' single, Somegirl introduces the vocal talents of new front-lady Anada. Somegirl's dreamy, atmospheric backdrop remains the same while the vocals take on a new edge and spirited, seductive lyrical approach. The single features several remixes which push the tempo upward for the dance floor. Plus, there are a couple of exclusive tracks which present a bouncy synthpop sound, including a cover version of Kraftwerk's classic song 'Sex Object'.
