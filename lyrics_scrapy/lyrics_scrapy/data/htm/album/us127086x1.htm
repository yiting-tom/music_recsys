Album Intro：

Straight out of Slipknot country, Des Moines, IA many people don' t know the story behind Too Pure To Die. The band established an extremely loyal fan base in the underground community with independent release ' Confidence And Consequence' which Trustkill later re-released. Speaking on the new album 'Confess', Jordan Peterson comments The new songs are much heavier than before and at the same time they feel more like a collective record. Previously we wrote a lot of songs that might not have felt like a record when you listen to all of them. That' s why we' re so excited about the new release, it' s the best of both worlds from what we wanted to accomplish, more polished on one end and heavier than ever at the same time.
