Album Intro：

2010 release from the Danish musician, producer, remixer, DJ and live performer. Anders Trentemoller has risen to the heights of international success usually only connected with artists in more mainstream Pop realms. His outstanding skills and talent for heart-wrenching melodic moments, fused with his trademark sound, have helped him gain fans across the globe, and blur the boundaries between cutting-edge, underground quality and mass appreciation. This album offers as much depth and soul as its predecessor, The Last Resort, yet sounds ultimately fresh and different. Above all, the 10 tracks have a glorious Indie Rock sound, with a lot of dynamics, distorted driving twang-guitars and haunting synths alongside four beautiful melodic vocal tracks.
