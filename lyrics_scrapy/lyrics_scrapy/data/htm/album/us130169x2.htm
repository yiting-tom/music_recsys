Album Intro：

Phenomenal new band by En Esch and Guenter Schulz of seminal industrial outfit KMFDM!

Esch and Schulz have performed, recorded and co-produced 11 albums and 20 singles with KMFDM that have combined to sell over 1.5 million copies!

Slick Idiot builds on the immensely successful KMFDM formula while adding several distinctive elements to make for an exciting new sound in industrial/EBM music!

Includes a bonus track remix by German industrial deities Rammstein!


Full service to press, radio and DJ pools plus consumer ads in all the major industrial/EBM publications!

The excellent guitar work of Guenter Schulz coupled with the dark, unique voice and expressive stage presence of En Esch gave KMFDM a distinctive style that separated the group from their peers and made KMFDM one of the most successful and well-known industrial bands of all time. After the group s breakup in 1999, Esch and Schulz decided to continue their work together and create a new project that would bring the idea behind KMFDM to another level. Slick Idiot s DickNity, the resulting project, will immediately remind one of the golden days of KMFDM but with a unique twist of modern glam-techno, trance and hip-hop as well as an ever present humorous touch. Fellow German superstars Rammstein have already given Slick Idiot their seal of approval by contributing a brutal, uber-heavy remix of one of the tracks. Besides the built-in audience of KMFDM fans, Slick Idiot will appeal to anyone interested in hearing cutting-edge industrial music. --Cleopatra Records
