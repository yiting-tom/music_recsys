Album Intro：

2009 release from the Scottish Pop/Rock singer/songwriter contains six unreleased tracks (including two Christmas carols and a beautiful cover of The Beatles song 'Because') plus remastered tracks lifted from his last three albums: On A Wing And A Prayer (1993), Over My Head (1994) and Another World (2000). Though his career stretches back four decades, he is best known for his solo hit 'Baker Street' as well as 'Stuck In The Middle With You', a smash while he was a member of Stealer's Wheel.
