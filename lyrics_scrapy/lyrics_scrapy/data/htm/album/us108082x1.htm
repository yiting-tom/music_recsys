Album Intro：

In 2009 the Bouncing Souls celebrated their 20th anniversary as a band by releasing a new song on the first of each month throughout the year, via digital download. They also pressed these songs onto a series of 7' vinyl records which they sold everywhere they went during a year-long tour. Here finally, they've gathered those 12 songs into a complete CD collection. Enjoy!
