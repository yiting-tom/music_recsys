Album Intro：

A living legend with a cult-like core following, Ras Kass stormed the
scene with 1996's Soul On Ice, garnering massive critical acclaim for
his complex lyricism, sharp battle rhymes and witty punchlines.
1998's follow-up, Rasassination, featured guest appearances by Dr. Dre,
Xzibit, Twista & RZA (of Wu-Tang). In 2000, he formed the Four Horsemen,
consisting of Canibus, Killah Priest and Kurupt to massive critical
accolades and a fiery commercial reception. Currently serving time,
Ras Kass now unveils the highly anticipated Institutionalized
Vol. II, a testament to both Ras' resilience and the raw skill that
underlay his longevity within the ever changing hip-hop landscape.
