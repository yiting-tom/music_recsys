Album Intro：

Bringing people together. All shapes and sizes, creeds and colors. Unified under one purpose, to worship a living Lord. This is the vision and passion of Travis Cottrell. Known as a bridge that can overcome the issues that separate the Body of Christ, his love for bringing Christians together quickly caught the eye and ear of Beth Moore, who has, for the past 10 years, had Cottrell as the worship leader for her world-wide events. With his last record 'Jesus Saves Live' pushing to the top of the charts as a #1 Praise and Worship record, this North Carolina native continues to hold strong as a staple in the worship community both as a worship leader, artist and songwriter.
