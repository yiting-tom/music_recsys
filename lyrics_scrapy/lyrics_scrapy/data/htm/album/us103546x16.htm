Album Intro：

The undisputed KING of the indie rap world is back and he brought his whole crew with him on the new release from Strange Music, TECH N9NE Collabos Misery Loves Kompany. TECH N9NE continues to break down barriers and set new industry standards for independent rap artists. TECH N9NE has graduated from urban legend to mainstream superstar with sales of over 775,000 units sold over the three Tech studio albums: Anghellic, Absolute Power, and Everready. TECH N9NE has proven to be the biggest truly independent rapper in the country as measured by Soundscan!!! The album will feature all new music from Tech and his cohorts including Big Krizz Kaliko, Kutt Calhoun, Skatterman, Snug Brim, Yukmouth, Prozak, and more. TECH N9NE is consistently in the Top 20 Hip-Hop artists worldwide on the Myspace charts with over 191,624 friends, 3,560,728 page views, and 9,519,086 song plays. TECH N9NE has a fiercely loyal fan base which is achieved through his almost unheard of touring schedule. TECH N9NE is now doing over 150 shows annually to stay closest to those who have made him successful and has rightfully been referred to as the hardest working artist in the rap game. 'Misery Loves Kompany' is the follow-up release from Strange Music after the massive success of Tech's last studio album, 'Everready: The Religion', which debuted at #2 on the indie chart scanning almost 22,000 units in the first week.
