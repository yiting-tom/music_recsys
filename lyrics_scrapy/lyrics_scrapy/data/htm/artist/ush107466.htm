Maxwell (musician)( Gerald Maxwell Rivera )【 13 albums 126 lyrics 】
Gerald Maxwell Rivera, (born May 23, 1973), better known by his stage names Maxwell and Musze, is an American (of Caribbean descent) singer-songwriter, record producer, and actor . Along with fellow musicians D'Angelo and Erykah Badu, Maxwell has been credited with helping to shape what has been termed the 'neo soul' movement that rose to prominence during the late 1990s.
