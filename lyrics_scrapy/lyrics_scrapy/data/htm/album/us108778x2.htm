Album Intro：

The Ghost Inside are one of the most exciting bands on the melodic hardcore scene. For their Epitaph debut, Get What You Give, The Ghost Inside team with A Day to Remember's Jeremy McKinnon in the producer's chair to create an onslaught of blistering breakdowns matched with anthemic choruses.
