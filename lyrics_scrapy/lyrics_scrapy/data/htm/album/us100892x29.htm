Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of this 'tribute' album to the legendary rocker from the British Pop group. Originally released in 1980, this reissue features two bonus tracks: 'Take Your Time' and 'Peggy Sue Got Married', which is actually a duet with Buddy Holly (through the wonders of technology of course). On this album Allan Clarke was back on vocal duty alongside longstanding members Bobby Elliott, Bernie Calvert, Terry Sylvester and Tony Hicks. EMI.
