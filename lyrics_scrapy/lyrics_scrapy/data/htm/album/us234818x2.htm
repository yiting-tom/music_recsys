王一橋Blair全新創作(Surprise)(Winter.Ver)“驚喜”公開
Surprise (Winter.Ver), a song created by Wang Yiqiao (Blair), comes as a “surprise”.

王一橋Blair在(創造營2020)賽後持續向華語樂壇輸出著她富有氧氣感和時令感的新鮮創作。(Surprise)是應約而來的冬日限定。首次嘗試SynthWave曲風，90s復古R&B像熱牛奶勾兌著百利甜，捧著一杯，奶音熱氣般上頭彌漫。
After the competition in Chuang 2020, Wang Yiqiao (Blair) continues her contribution to Mandopop by creating soothing music with the theme of seasons. Surprise, as promised, is a song dedicated to winter. It is Blair's first attempt to go SynthWave and 90s retro R&B, just like the mix of hot milk and Baileys, with the mesmerizing texture of milk and wine.

“玫瑰花香散著，蠟燭餘有溫熱”，啟動全身上下每一處感官細胞，在微醺又迷離的氛圍裡，讓所有心之所向的浪漫儀式感盡情揮發。寫一首歌給冬日幻想裡的那場雪，那個他。
“The scent of roses was in the air. The candles were still warm.” The lyrics depict a sensual ambiance where every single sensory cell is activated, letting out the romantic, ceremonial feelings which are irresistible for all human beings. It is a song dedicated to the winter wonderland, the snow, the right person.

轉角有一場告白，他在等你到來。
Love is around the corner. The right person is right there waiting for you.

我們，下個季節見。
See you next season.
