Album Intro：

Repress. 2009 album, the 30th release from the British Progressive Thrash/Folk Metal outfit. Recorded and mixed by Dario Mollo at Damage Inc. Studio, in Ventimiglia, Italy, In The All Together mixes the usual Thrash/Speed Metal influences with the extraordinary Folk melodies that made the band famous all around the world, maintaining a fresh and dynamic approach which gives a strong live dimension to the new songs. 10 tracks. Scarlet Records.
