Album Intro：

Like fellow Scots Aztec Camera, the Bluebells crafted impeccable, jangly guitar pop, only with better melodies and stronger hooks. Even the most despondent soul will not be able to resist the Bluebells' charms on The Singles Collection. 18 tracks of more than an hour of uplifting, buoyant, and toe-tapping jangle pop, The Singles Collection is an irresistible career retrospective. Warner. 2005.
