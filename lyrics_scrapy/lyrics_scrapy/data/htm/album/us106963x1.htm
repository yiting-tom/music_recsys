Album Intro：

Art Brut is an English and German indie rock band. Their debut album, Bang Bang Rock & Roll, was released on 30 May 2005, with its follow up, It's a Bit Complicated, released on 25 June 2007. Named after French painter Jean Dubuffet's definition of Outsider Art -- art by prisoners, loners, the mentally ill, and other marginalized people, and made without thought to imitation or presentation -- South London's Art Brut were tagged by NME as part of the 'Art Wave' scene that also includes bands such as Athlete, Franz Ferdinand and Bloc Party.

Art Brut vs. Satan is the third album by Art Brut, due out April 20th in Europe and May 12th in the US. The lead-off single from this album is 'Alcoholics Unanimous.' The album is being produced by Frank Black, best known as the frontman of Pixies.

The album tracklisting was confirmed in early February 2009.
