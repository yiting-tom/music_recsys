Album Intro：

2008 album, the first release from Interscope/DGC/Van Howes/Maloof Music Records, produced by Brian Howes and Josh Abraham. Brian Howes past projects include producer/co-writer on Hinder's Extreme Behavior and co writer on Daughtry's Daughtry. Josh Abraham has worked as a producer for many high profile Rock acts such as Velvet Revolver, Linkin Park, and Staind. Features the hit 'Hell Yeah' and 'Light It Up' (both are included on the soundtrack to popular video games). Universal.
