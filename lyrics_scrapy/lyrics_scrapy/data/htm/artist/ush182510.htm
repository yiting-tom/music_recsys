Pentagon (Hangul: 펜타곤; stylized as PENTAGON; abbreviated as PTG) is a South Korean boy group formed by Cube Entertainment in 2016. The group consists of 10 members: Jinho, Hui, Hongseok, E'Dawn, Shinwon, Yan An, Yeo One, Yuto, Kino and Wooseok. They were introduced through the Mnet survival show Pentagon Maker. Pentagon released their self-titled debut EP on October 10, 2016.

Members

Jinho (Korean: 진호) was born Jo Jin-ho (Korean: 조진호) on April 17, 1992 in Daejeon, South Korea. He is a former S.M. Entertainment trainee and an ex-member of SM the Ballad.
Hui (Korean: 후이) was born Lee Hoe-taek (Korean: 이회택) on August 28, 1993 in Gwacheon, South Korea. He is a former trainee of JYP Entertainment.
Hongseok (Korean: 홍석) was born Yang Hong-seok (Korean: 양홍석) on April 17, 1994 in Seoul, South Korea. He is a former YG Entertainment trainee and was part of the survival show Mix & Match. Hongseok has lived and studied abroad in Singapore, San Diego, Tianjin and Beijing, and can speak English and Chinese in addition to Korean.
E'Dawn (Korean: 이던) was born Kim Hyo-jong (Korean: 김효종) on June 1, 1994 in South Jeolla Province, South Korea.
Shinwon (Korean: 신원) was born Ko Shin-won (Korean: 고신원) on December 11, 1995 in Cheongju, South Korea.
Yeo One (Korean: 여원) was born Yeo Chang-gu (Korean: 여창구) on March 27, 1996 in Daejeon, South Korea.
Yan An (Chinese: 閆桉; Korean: 옌안) was born on October 25, 1996 in Shanghai, China.
Yuto (Japanese: ユウト; Korean: 유토) was born Adachi Yuto (Japanese: 安達祐人) on January 23, 1998 in Nagano, Japan.
Kino (Korean: 키노) was born Kang Hyung-gu (Korean: 강형구) on January 27, 1998 in Busan, South Korea.
Wooseok (Korean: 우석) was born Jung Woo-seok (Korean: 정우석) on January 31, 1998 in Gwangju, South Korea.
