That 1960 smash is more than just a doo-wop classic-it's one of the greatest love songs ever, and it's here with all the Capris' original Old Town-label hits and more, 16 on one CD! There's a Moon Out Tonight; Where I Fell in Love , and Girl in My Dreams join Stars in the Sky; Love Poems; Why Do I Cry; My Island in the Sun , and more hopelessly romantic doo wop.
