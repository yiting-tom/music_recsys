Album Intro：

Joshua James hails from Nebraska and his songs are intimate and wide open at the same time. After traveling to South Amercia he now lives in Utah. The Sun Is Always Brighter is his second full-length album.

It's a dark album, but there is glimmer of brightness shimmering through all the tracks. Joshua James is more than yet another singer/songwriter. With his uneven voice he summons up his demons. He digs deep and that might hurt at times.
