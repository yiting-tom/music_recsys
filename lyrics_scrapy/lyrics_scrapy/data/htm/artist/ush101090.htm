Orville Richard Burrell CD (born October 22, 1968), known mostly by his stage name Shaggy, is a Jamaican-American reggae fusion singer and deejay. He is best known for his hit singles 'Oh Carolina', 'Boombastic', 'It Wasn't Me' and 'Angel'. He was nicknamed after Shaggy Rogers, the cartoon character from the popular television series Scooby Doo.
