Album Intro：

2012 release from the Christian artist, his first new remix record in seven years. Dubbed & Freq'd: A Remix Project features new remixes of several of the biggest songs from his albums Tonight and Portable Sounds. Toby's unique blend of Rock, Pop, Hip Hop and Urban styles is perfect for the remix treatment and brings an entirely new sound to his songs.
