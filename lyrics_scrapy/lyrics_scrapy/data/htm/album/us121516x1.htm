Album Intro：

ICP have been putting out successful albums and attracting new fans for over 15 years, over the course of their Joker's Card saga, numerous EPs and side projects, and well-received solo efforts from each member. The Shining isn't just the newest Insane Clown effort; it's a new and revealing chapter in their legacy. It focuses on the fascinating character of Violent J: both as a member of ICP's ruthless, relentless Dark Carnival, and ad the all-too-human writer/rapper behind the paint, Joe Bruce.
