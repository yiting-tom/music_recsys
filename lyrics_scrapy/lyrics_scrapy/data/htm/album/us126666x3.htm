Album Intro：

This is Tingsek's second album. He has featured for a long time in the music world: Ex member in Loosegoats, has the band Kamraterna with Timbuktu, he has taking part of Ane Brun's 'Duets' album, is musician in the band Helt Off and also he has open for Willie Nelson in Scandinavia and USA. On the album 'World of its own' have Magnus Tingsek worked with Mans Mernsten (Dawn), Marten Dahl (Kamraterna), Salem Al Fakir and Pauline. Tingsek writes and produce everything all by him self and plays also 80% of the instrumental. He has taking a huge step forward both like a song writer and a singer and the results are a smoky, soulful pop disc.
