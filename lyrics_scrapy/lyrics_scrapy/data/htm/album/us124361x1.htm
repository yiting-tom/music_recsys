Album Intro：

Sydney two-piece the mess hall return with their 3rd studio album 'for the birds'. The anticipation for this record has steadily been mounting since the band won the prestigious australia music prize (amp) for their last album 'devils elbow' in 2007. As with 'devils elbow', once again the mess hall chose to record their album at sydney's bjb studio and once again they employed the talents of burke reid (the drones, jack ladder) to steer the production helm. Why mess with a winning formula?
