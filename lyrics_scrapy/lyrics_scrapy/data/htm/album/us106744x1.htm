Album Intro：

Marianas Trench is a Juno award nominated Canadian pop punk band from Vancouver, British Columbia signed to 604 Records. The band released their first full length album Fix Me on October 3, 2006. Some of the demos the band sent out four years before appear on the album.

Masterpiece Theatre is the second studio album by Marianas Trench. It was released on February 24, 2009. It also contains their 4th and current single 'Cross My Heart'.

Masterpiece Theatre來自Vancouver, British Columbia，獲加拿大Juno獎的pop punk 樂隊提名，簽約於604 Records公司。樂隊首磚《Fix Me》於2006.10.03發行。一些demo在早在專輯發行4年前便出現了。

第二張錄音室專輯《Masterpiece Theatre》發行與2009.02.24，專輯包含，樂隊第四首單曲，也就是目前的主打單曲，《Cross My Heart》。
