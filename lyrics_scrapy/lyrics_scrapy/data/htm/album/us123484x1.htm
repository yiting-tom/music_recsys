Album Intro：

Digitally remastered and expanded edition of the British quartet's 1983 debut album featuring 10 bonus tracks including remixes and non-album ! The Farmer's Boys were a unique blend of New Wave, Country, Dance, Rock and Pop and sounded like no other band before or since. Their hook-filled tunes mix British and American influences seamlessly into a unique and joyful blend of Pure Pop. Produced by the band, Pete Hammond and Peter Collins, along with additional production from Pete Waterman and Julian Mendelsohn. 21 tracks. Cherry Red. 2009.
