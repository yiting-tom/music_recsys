Album Intro：

Trevor Hall originally from Hilton Head, S.C.He now lives in Iydilwild, Ca. Has rocked the house's of Hard Rock Cafe , House Of Blues to the Venetian and Palms in Las Vegas.

Trevor cares deeply about giving back to society and has done many and will continue to do benefit shows raising money for youth oriented programs. He's helped the children of Guatemala, kids with aids in LA, inner city school kids in LA.

He's excited about his tour this summer with Steel Pulse which coincides with his new album. Trevor is also a great long boarder and loves yoga and finds inner peace through his music and sharing it with others.
