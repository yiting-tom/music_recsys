Hitomi【 30 albums 219 lyrics 】
Hitomi (ヒトミ?, born January 26, 1976) is a Japanese singer-songwriter. She began her career as model, but after meeting Tetsuya Komuro he began managing her career as a pop singer. In 1998 she left the 'Komuro Family' and started working with other musicians and producers, oriented to other musical genres such as pop rock, and more recently to electropop.
