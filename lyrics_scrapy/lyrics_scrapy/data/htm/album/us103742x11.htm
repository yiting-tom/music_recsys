Album Intro：

In A Different Light is a collection of re-worked versions of their hit songs with the addition of two new songs, 「Here Comes the Darkness」 and 「At the End of the Day.」 The band will hit the road in support of IN A DIFFERENT LIGHT playing shows across the U.S. through the end of the year starting in the Northeast October 5th. Also, fans can expect a collection of all new songs in the spring of 2010.
