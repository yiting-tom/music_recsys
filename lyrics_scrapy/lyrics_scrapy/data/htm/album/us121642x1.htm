Album Intro：

From the very uptempo traditional song 'Long Journey Home', to the newly written 'Greenwood Mill', and story songs like 'The Rabbit Song' and 'Coal Miner's Curse', this project will be much appreciated by the Bluegrass enthusiast. This is nice followup to the earlier album 'Down in the Cell' that included 'The Baptism of Jesse Taylor'. Don't overlook that one! These guys have a good formula, pick good, sing good, harmonize good, so what more could you ask for. They've delivered! --- J. Roths, Music Director, KEOS Radio, College Station, TX and Broadcast Member, IBMA. John Roths Music Director KEOS 89.1 FM --John Roths
