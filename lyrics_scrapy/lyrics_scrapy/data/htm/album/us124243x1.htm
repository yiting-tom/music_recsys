Album Intro：

Vinyl LP pressing. 2009 release, the fourth album from this Norwegian outfit. A sharper and more focused effort without a weak track in sight, Futuro is both industrial and rough as well as polished and streamlined. There are nods to fellow Norwegians 120 Days as well as Stereolab, Can, Doors, Joy Division and even B 52's, but this is a group that have perfected their own sound from album to album and through periods of extensive touring. Founded in 2001 on the west coast of Norway, they have been called the band with one foot in Pompeii and one in space. They have developed from being a searching instrumental lo-fi Post-Rock band to a full-blown, focused and fresh sounding Rock ensemble with great songs and a mix of dark atmospheres and catchy choruses.
