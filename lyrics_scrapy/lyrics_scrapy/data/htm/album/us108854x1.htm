Album Intro：

The Dopamines have put out some really great records on the very great, It's Alive Records. I know that It's Alive helped turn a lot of ears to what the Dopamines were doing and it looks as though Vinnie from Paper and Plastick were one of those sets of ears. Dopamines have a new album coming out on June 29th, and this variant is limited to 500 copies with a CD.

If you like Lookout era punk rock or Minneapolis punk rock, you should spend a second to listen to the Dopamines.

While you are considering picking up this LP, make sure you check out their previous releases.
